                                  DC REGULATIONS

OFFICIAL OFFICES OF THE COMMISSION AND OFFICE HOURS (ยง 31-110)

110.1. The official offices of the Commission shall be 2041 Martin Luther
   King, Jr. Avenue, S.E., 2nd Floor, Washington, D.C. 20020.

   110.2. The office hours of the Commission are from 8:30 a.m. to 4:45 p.m.,
   Monday through Friday, excluding holidays.

   110.3. Public records of the Commission may be examined in the offices of the
   Commission during normal office hours. A person may make an appointment with
   the Commission to listen to a tape recorded proceeding of the Commission or
   its panels.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6707, 6713 (October 23, 1987).
