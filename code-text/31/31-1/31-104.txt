                                  DC REGULATIONS

QUORUM (ยง 31-104)

104.1. A majority of the Commissioners in office shall constitute a quorum for
   the transaction of business at all meetings of the Commission. A meeting may
   commence for the consideration of matters not requiring a vote when a majority
   of Commissioners in office are present.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6707, 6711 (October 23, 1987);
   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 1578 (February 16, 2007).
