                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORDS OF MEETINGS (ยง 31-108)

108.1. The Secretary shall cause the creation of a formal record of the
   official proceedings of Commission meetings by tape recording. All written
   documents and materials of the Commission shall be maintained by the Secretary
   as the official record of the Commission.

   108.2. The Secretary shall prepare a summary of attendance and actions
   officially taken at each meeting.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6707, 6713 (October 23, 1987).
