APPOINTMENT OF THE VICE-CHAIRPERSON AND SECRETARY (ยง 31-101)

101.1. The Chairperson may designate a member of the Commission to serve as
   Vice-Chairperson.

   101.2. The General Counsel to the Commission shall serve as Secretary to the
   Commission.
