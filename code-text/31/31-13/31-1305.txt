                                  DC REGULATIONS

TAXIMETER BUSINESS LICENSE &#8211; FEES (ยง 31-1305)

1305.1. Every application for a license to operate a taximeter business shall
   be accompanied by a non-refundable application fee of five hundred dollars
   ($500) to be deducted from the first bi-annual license fee of two thousand
   dollars ($2,000).

   1305.2. The license application fee and the license fee shall be payable by
   money order or by certified check and payable to the DC Treasurer.

   1305.3. The bi-annual renewal license fee after the first license is one
   thousand and five hundred dollars ($1,500) and is due on the anniversary of
   the issuance of the license.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 3831 (April 11, 2008).
