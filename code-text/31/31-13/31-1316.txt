                                  DC REGULATIONS

TAXIMETER BUSINESS &#8211; NOTIFICATION OF ANY LICENSE SUSPENSION OR REVOCATION
(ยง 31-1316)

1316.1. A taximeter business owner shall immediately notify the Commission in
   writing of any suspension or revocation of any license granted to the
   licensee, or any other person acting on his behalf, by any agency of the
   District of Columbia or federal government.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 3831 (April 11, 2008).
