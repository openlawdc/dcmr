TAXIMETER BUSINESS &#8211; COMPLIANCE WITH LICENSING REQUIREMENTS (ยง 31-1306)

1306.1. If at any time during the term of the taximeter business license, the
   Chairman becomes aware that the licensee no longer meets the requirements for
   a taximeter business license, the Commission may suspend or revoke the license
   or deny any application for renewal.

   1306.2. Nothing contained herein shall limit the authority of the Chairman to
   summarily suspend the license of any taximeter business where a threat to
   public health, safety or welfare exists.

   1306.3. Appeals of-actions taken by the Commission pursuant to sections
   1306.1, 1306.2 and 1331 shall be heard by the Office of Administrative
   Hearings
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 3831 (April 11, 2008).
