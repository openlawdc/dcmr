TAXIMETER BUSINESS &#8211; NOTIFICATION OF CRIMINAL CONVICTION OR OTHER CHANGE
IN LICENSE CONDITIONS (ยง 31-1315)

1315.1. A taximeter business owner, including a member of a partnership or any
   officer or shareholder of a corporation, shall notify the Commission in
   writing of his/her conviction for a crime within fifteen (15) days of such
   conviction, and he or she shall deliver to the Commission a certified copy of
   the certificate of disposition issued by the clerk of the court within fifteen
   (15) days of conviction.

   1315.2. In accordance with ยง 1307.2, a taximeter business owner shall notify
   the Commission of any material change in the information contained on such
   owner&#8217;s latest taximeter business license application or renewal.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 3831 (April 11, 2008).
