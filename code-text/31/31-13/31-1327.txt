                                  DC REGULATIONS

TAXIMETER BUSINESS &#8211; REPAIR WORK AFTER TEST FAILURE PROHIBITED (ยง
31-1327)

1327.1. No taximeter business owner shall, as a condition of performing any
   test or other work, require a vehicle driver or owner to undertake any repair
   work at his business. He shall inform the owner or driver that he may select
   another licensed taximeter business to perform a repair.

   1327.2. No taximeter business owner shall direct a vehicle owner to utilize
   any other taximeter business to perform said repair work.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 3831 (April 11, 2008).
