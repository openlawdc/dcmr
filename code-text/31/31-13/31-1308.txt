                                  DC REGULATIONS

TAXIMETER BUSINESS &#8211; COMPLIANCE WITH APPLICABLE LAWS (ยง 31-1308)

1308.1. A licensee shall obtain and keep in full force and effect all licenses
   and permits required by the District or federal laws.

   1308.2. A licensee shall comply with all applicable Occupational Safety and
   Health Act (OSHA) standards and requirements at the licensee&#8217;s place of
   business, as well as all other Federal and District laws governing the conduct
   of its business.

   1308.3. A licensee shall pay any fines, fees, and/or taxes owed by it to the
   federal or District government.

   1308.4. A licensee shall comply with all workers&#8217; compensation and
   disability benefits laws, and all federal laws regarding the withholding of
   taxes and payment of FICA and other withholding taxes.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 3831 (April 11, 2008).
