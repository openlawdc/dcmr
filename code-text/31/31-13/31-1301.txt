                                  DC REGULATIONS

UNLICENSED BUSINESS ACTIVITY PROHIBITED (ยง 31-1301)

1301.1. No person shall sell, install, repair, adjust, or calibrate taximeters
   or install or replace seals, wiring harnesses or other equipment relating to
   the operation of a taximeter or cruiser light for use upon any licensed
   taxicab in the District of Columbia without a valid taximeter business license
   issued by the Commission.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 3831 (April 11, 2008).
