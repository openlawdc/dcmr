                                  DC REGULATIONS

ENFORCEMENT (ยง 31-410)

410.1. The enforcement of this chapter shall be governed by the procedures in
   Chapter 7 of this title.  If, at the time of violation, the procedures in
   Chapter 7 do not extend in their terms to PSPs or DDSs, such procedures shall
   be applied to a PSP or a DDS as if such PSP or DDS were a taxicab owner or
   operator.

   410.2. If, at the time of a violation, the procedures in Chapter 7 do not
   extend in their terms to a person regulated by this chapter, violations of
   this chapter shall be enforced as if such person were a taxicab owner or
   operator.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 6993 (May 17, 2013); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 10975 (July 26, 2013).
