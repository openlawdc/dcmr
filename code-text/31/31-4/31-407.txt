                                  DC REGULATIONS

SUSPENSION OR REVOCATION OF APPROVAL (ยง 31-407)

407.1. The approval of an MTS may be suspended or revoked by the Office with
   reasonable notice and an opportunity to be heard if the Office learns that the
   MTS or the associated owners or operators using are not it in substantial
   compliance with this title, or if that the MTS is being used in a manner that
   poses a significant threat to passenger or operator safety, or consumer
   protection.
