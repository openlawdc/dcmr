                                  DC REGULATIONS

DENIAL OF LICENSE AND REAPPLICATION (ยง 31-1210)

1210.1 	An applicant who has been denied a license to operate under this chapter
for reasons other than for failure to complete successfully an examination may
file a new application for a license after the expiration of not less than six
(6) months after the denial.
