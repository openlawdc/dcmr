                                  DC REGULATIONS

PENALTY (§ 31-907)

907.1. A violation of this chapter shall be subject to:
   
   (a)	The fines and penalties set forth in § 825 or a section of this chapter;
   provided, that where a specific fine or penalty is not listed in § 825 or
   this chapter, the fine shall be one hundred dollars ($100);
   
   (b)	Impoundment of the vehicle pursuant to the provisions of the Taxicab and
   Passenger Vehicle for Hire Impoundment Act of 1992, effective March 16, 1993
   (D.C. Law 9-199; D.C. Official Code § 50-331) (2009 Repl. &#038; 2011 Supp.);
   
   (c)	License suspension, revocation, or non-renewal; or
   
   (d)	Any combination of the sanctions listed in this subsection.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: 14 DCRR §450.1, Special Edition (January 10, 1975); as amended by
   Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 9847, 9851 (November 18, 2011).
