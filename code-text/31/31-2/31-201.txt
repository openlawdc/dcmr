                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPOINTMENT OF CO-CHAIRPERSON AND SECRETARY (ยง 31-201)

201.1. The Chairperson may designate a member of the Panel to serve as
   Co-Chairperson of the Panel on Rates and Rules.

   201.2. The General Counsel to the Commission shall serve as Secretary to the
   Panel.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6715, 6717 (October 23, 1987).
