                                  DC REGULATIONS

OFFICIAL OFFICES OF THE COMMISSION AND OFFICE HOURS (ยง 31-210)

210.1. The official offices of the Commission shall be 2041 Martin Luther
   King, Jr. Avenue, S.E., Washington, D.C. 20020.

   210.2. The office hours of the Commission are from 8:30 a.m. to 4:45 p.m.,
   Monday through Friday, excluding holidays.

   210.3. Public records of the Panel on Rates and Rules may be examined in the
   offices of the Commission during normal office hours. A person may make an
   appointment to listen to a tape recorded proceeding of the Panel.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6715, 6721 (October 23, 1987).
