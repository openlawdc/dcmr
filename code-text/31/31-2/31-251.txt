                                  DC REGULATIONS

POST-HEARING BRIEFS (ยง 31-251)

251.1. Post-hearing briefs shall be filed at the times fixed by the Panel in
   each case.

   251.2. The applicant shall brief all issues designated by the Panel in the
   Report and Order on Pre-hearing Conference and any other issues designated by
   the Panel during the proceeding.

   251.3. All post-hearing briefs shall contain a title sheet clearly identifying
   the parties submitting the brief, including name and title, local address, and
   local telephone number.

   251.4. A subject index with page references and a list of all authorities
   cited, alphabetically arranged with page references, shall be included.

   251.5. Authorities most heavily relied upon shall be marked with an asterisk.
   Lengthy quotations from any records or from authorities shall not be included.

   251.6. The Panel may reject briefs unnecessarily prolix or which do not
   conform to the rules of the Panel.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6715, 6746 (October 23, 1987).
