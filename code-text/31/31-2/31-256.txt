                                  DC REGULATIONS

WAIVER (§ 31-256)

256.1. The Panel may, in its discretion, waive any of the provisions of §§
   220 to 254 in any proceeding after duly advising the parties of its intention
   to do so.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6715, 6748 (October 23, 1987).
