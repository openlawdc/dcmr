                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROPOSED FINDINGS AND CONCLUSIONS (ยง 31-252)

252.1. Each party to the proceeding, after the close of the record within the
   time prescribed for post-hearing briefs, or as otherwise directed by the Panel
   shall serve on all parties and file with the Panel proposed findings of fact
   and conclusions of law for consideration by the Panel.

   252.2. The proposed findings of fact and conclusions of law shall be
   referenced to the record of the proceedings.

   252.3. The proposed findings of fact and conclusions of law may be included in
   the post hearing briefs required by this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6715, 6746 (October 23, 1987).
