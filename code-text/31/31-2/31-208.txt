                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORDS OF MEETINGS (ยง 31-208)

208.1. The Secretary shall cause the creation of a formal record of the
   official proceedings of Panel meetings by tape recording. All written
   documents and materials of the Panel shall be preserved by the Secretary.

   208.2. The Secretary shall prepare a summary of attendance and actions
   officially taken at each meeting.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6715, 6720 (October 23, 1987).
