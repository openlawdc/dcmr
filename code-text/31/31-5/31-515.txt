                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTIFICATION OF ANY LICENSE SUSPENSION OR REVOCATION (§ 31-515)

515.1. An owner, employee, agent, or representative of a taxicab company,
   association, or fleet or another person acting on the owner’s behalf shall
   notify the Commission in writing no later than ten (10) business days after
   the suspension or revocation of a license, permit, certificate, or authority
   granted to the licensee by an agency of the District of Columbia, Maryland,
   Virginia, or federal government.
   
   
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8549, 8561 (July 20, 2012).
