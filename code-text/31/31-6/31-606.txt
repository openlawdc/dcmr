                                  DC REGULATIONS

LOCKED SAFE (ยง 31-606)

606.1. A taxicab may be equipped with a locked safe mounted on the floorboard
   next to the driver, and if so equipped, the taxicab may display a decal on the
   right rear door or fender consisting of letters not more than three inches (3
   in.) stating: &#8220;This driver protected by a locked safe.&#8221;
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3595, 3606 (June 1, 1990).
