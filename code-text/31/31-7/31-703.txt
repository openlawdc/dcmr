                                  DC REGULATIONS

SANCTIONS (ยง 31-703)

703.1. Any taxicab owner or operator, upon determination of liability for
   violation of any of the Commission&#8217;s Rules, may be subject to a civil
   fine, not to exceed five hundred dollars ($500), for each violation.

   703.2. The Panel, in addition to imposing a monetary fine, may suspend or
   revoke the license of any taxicab owner or operator for violation of any of
   the Commission&#8217;s Rules.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3595, 3610 (June 1, 1990).
