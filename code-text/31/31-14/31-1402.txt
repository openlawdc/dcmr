                                  DC REGULATIONS

SEDAN CLASS RATES AND CHARGES (§ 31-1402)

1402.1. Passenger rates and charges for sedan class service shall:
   
   
   
   	(a)	Be based on time and distance rates as set by the DDS except for a set
   fare for a route approved by the Office order for a well-traveled route,
   including a trip to an airport or to an event;
   
   
   
   	(b)	Be disclosed to the passenger in a statement of the DDS’ fare
   calculation method;
   
   
   
   	(c)	Be used to calculate an estimated fare that shall be offered to the
   passenger prior to the acceptance of service, which shall state whether demand
   pricing applies, and, if so, the effect of such pricing on the estimate;
   
   
   
   	(d)	Be consistent with the DDS statement of its fare calculation method
   posted on its website pursuant to Chapter 16;
   
   
   
   	(e)	Not exceed the estimated fare by more than twenty percent (20%) or twenty
   five dollars ($25), whichever is less, unless the excess is due to delays or
   stopovers en route at the direction of the passenger, or other factors beyond
   the operator’s control, such as traffic, accidents, or construction;  and
   
   
   
   	(f)	Not include a gratuity that does not meet the definition of a
   “gratuity”.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 12410 (August 30, 2013).
