                                  DC REGULATIONS

INTRAINDUSTRY COMPLAINTS AND DISPUTES (§ 31-357)

357.1. Intraindustry complaints and disputes may be considered pursuant to
   §§ 355, 356, and 368.

   357.2. If issues in proceedings before a hearing panel present matters
   described in § 357.1, the Panel may hold investigative hearings pursuant to
   § 356. At the conclusion of such hearings, additional rules may be necessary
   to protect the public and the taxicab industry. The rules shall then be
   considered pursuant to § 358.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6751, 6794 (October 23, 1987).
