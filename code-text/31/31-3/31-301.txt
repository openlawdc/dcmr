APPOINTMENT OF THE CO-CHAIRPERSON AND SECRETARY (ยง 31-301)

301.1. The Chairperson may designate a member of the Panel to serve as
   Co-Chairperson of the Panel.

   301.2. The General Counsel to the Commission shall serve as Secretary to the
   Panel.
