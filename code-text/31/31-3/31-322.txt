                                  DC REGULATIONS

HEARING ON IMMEDIATE SUSPENSION OF LICENSES TO OPERATE PUBLIC VEHICLES FOR HIRE
(ยง 31-322)

322.1. Any person whose license to own or operate a vehicle as a taxicab or a
   passenger vehicle for hire is immediately suspended by the Chairperson may,
   unless otherwise provided by law, demand a hearing before an examiner in the
   Bureau of Traffic Adjudication which shall be granted, except when the action
   taken by the Chairperson is made mandatory by law or when the person has
   previously been afforded an opportunity with appropriate notice for a hearing.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6751, 6760 (October 23, 1987).
