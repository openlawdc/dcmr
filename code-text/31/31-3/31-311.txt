                                  DC REGULATIONS

POLICY AND PROGRAMS (ยง 31-311)

311.1. The Panel, when convened in regular or special session, shall,
   consistent with law, consider and adopt Panel policy, programs, and
   objectives.

   311.2. Any Commissioner may propose policy, programs, and objectives for
   consideration and adoption by the Panel.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 6751, 6757 (October 23, 1987).
