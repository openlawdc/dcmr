                                  DC REGULATIONS

DOME LIGHT INSTALLATION BUSINESS &#8211; COMPLIANCE WITH LICENSING REQUIREMENTS
(ยง 31-1506)

1506.1. If at any time during the term of the dome light installation business
   license, the Chairman becomes aware that the licensee no longer meets the
   requirements for a dome light installation business license, the Commission
   may suspend or revoke the license or deny any application for renewal.

   1506.2. Nothing contained herein shall limit the authority of the Chairman to
   summarily suspend the license of any dome light installation business where a
   threat to public health, safety, or welfare exists.

   1506.3. The Office of Administrative Hearings shall hear appeals of actions
   taken by the Commission pursuant to Sections 1506.1 and 1506.2.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 14019 (December 7, 2012).
