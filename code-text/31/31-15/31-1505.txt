                                  DC REGULATIONS

DOME LIGHT INSTALLATION BUSINESS LICENSE &#8211; FEES (ยง 31-1505)

1505.1. Every application for a license to operate a dome light installation
   business shall be accompanied by a non-refundable application fee of five
   hundred dollars ($500).

   1505.2. The license application fee and the license fee shall be payable to
   the DC Treasurer by money order or by certified check.

   1505.3. The bi-annual renewal license fee including the first license is one
   thousand five hundred dollars ($1,500) and is due on the anniversary of the
   issuance of the license.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 14019 (December 7, 2012).
