                                  DC REGULATIONS

DOME LIGHT INSTALLATION BUSINESS – OVERCHARGES PROHIBITED (§ 31-1528)

1528.1. A licensed dome light installation business shall not charge fees for
   any work involving dome lights in excess of the fees set by its fee schedule,
   which shall be filed with the Commission and shall be publicly displayed
   pursuant to § 1509 of these rules.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 14019 (December 7, 2012).
