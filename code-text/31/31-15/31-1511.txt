                                  DC REGULATIONS

DOME LIGHT INSTALLATION BUSINESS – EQUIPMENT MAINTENANCE (§ 31-1511)

1511.1. A dome light installation business owner shall properly maintain all
   equipment required by the Commission, and any other equipment required by law
   or regulation, in good working order and in such a manner that an inspection
   or test may be conducted in conformity with these rules.

   1511.2. A dome light installation business shall not conduct any test or
   installation using equipment that is not in good working order.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 14019 (December 7, 2012).
