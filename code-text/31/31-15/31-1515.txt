DOME LIGHT INSTALLATION BUSINESS – NOTIFICATION OF CRIMINAL CONVICTION OR
OTHER CHANGE IN LICENSE CONDITIONS (§ 31-1515)

1515.1. A dome light installation business owner, including a member of a
   partnership or any officer or shareholder of a corporation, shall notify the
   Commission in writing of his or her conviction for a crime within fifteen (15)
   days of such conviction, and he or she shall deliver to the Commission a
   certified copy of the certificate of disposition issued by the clerk of the
   court within fifteen (15) days of disposition.

   1515.2. In accordance with § 1507.2, a dome light installation business owner
   shall notify the Commission of any material change in the information
   contained on such owner’s latest dome light installation business license
   application or renewal.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 14019 (December 7, 2012).
