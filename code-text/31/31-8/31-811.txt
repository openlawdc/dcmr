                                  DC REGULATIONS

UNAUTHORIZED USE OF TAXICAB (ยง 31-811)

811.1. A taxicab owner may file a written complaint with the Office of
   Taxicabs for unauthorized use of the vehicle when the lessee fails to return
   the vehicle to the owner after the expiration of the lease agreement or
   failure of the lessee to have a current insurance sticker.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3595, 3618 (June 1, 1990).
