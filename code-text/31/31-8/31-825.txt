TABLE OF CIVIL FINES AND PENALTIES (§ 31-825)

825.1. The civil infractions and their respective fine amounts set forth in
   this section are applicable to the operation of all public vehicles for hire. 
   These civil infractions and their respective fines do not include moving
   violations for which jurisdiction remains within other District, federal or
   municipal agencies and/or the Superior Court of the District of Columbia or
   other courts of original jurisdiction.

   825.3. The civil fines set forth in this section shall be doubled for the
   second violation of the same infraction within a twelve (12) month period. 
   Further the civil fine shall be doubled once more for a subsequent violation
   or violations of the same infraction within a twenty-four (24) month period.

   825.4. Where a specific civil fine or penalty is not provided in the table of
   fines, the fine shall be one hundred dollars ($100).
