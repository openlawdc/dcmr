                                  DC REGULATIONS

STORAGE OF ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES (ยง 23-2003)

2003.1. The licensee under a Caterer&#8217;s license may store alcoholic
   beverages in the District of Columbia upon the approval of the Board. The
   licensee under a Caterer&#8217;s license shall not store alcoholic beverages
   intended for use in the District of Columbia outside of the District of
   Columbia.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: As amended by D.C. ACT 15-442 published at 51 DCR 6525 (July 2, 2004);
   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12991 (December 26, 2008).
