                                  DC REGULATIONS

INVESTIGATOR REPORTS (ยง 23-1709)

1709.1. The Board shall make investigator reports available to the parties of
   a contested case at least two days prior to the date of the protest hearing or
   catered site protest hearing.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 4309 (April 30, 2004); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12991 (December 26, 2008).
