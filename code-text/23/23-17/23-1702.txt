                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPUTATION OF TIME (ยง 23-1702)

1702.1. Whenever a party to a proceeding under this chapter has the right or
   is required to perform some act within a specified time period after the
   service of notice upon the party, and the notice is served upon that party by
   mail, three (3) days shall be added to the prescribed period.

   1702.2. Except as otherwise provided by law, any time period prescribed by
   this chapter may, for good cause shown, be extended or shortened by the Board
   with notice to all parties.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 4309 (April 30, 2004); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12991 (December 26, 2008).
