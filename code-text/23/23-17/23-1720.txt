EX PARTE COMMUNICATIONS (ยง 23-1720)

1720.1. If a proceeding is a contested case within the meaning of the D.C.
   Administrative Procedure Act (D.C. Official Code ยง 2-502(8)), the following
   restrictions shall apply:
   
   
   
   (a)	A person shall not make or knowingly cause to be made to a member of the
   Board an ex parte communication relevant to the merits of the proceeding; and
   
   
   
   (b)	No member of the Board shall make or cause to be made to any interested
   persons outside the Board an ex parte communication relevant to the merits of
   the proceeding.

   1720.2. The prohibitions set forth in ยง 1720.1 shall apply upon the issuance
   of notice of an application for an original, transfer, or renewal license or a
   notice to show cause hearing.

   1720.3. &#8220;Ex parte communication&#8221; does not include a request for a
   status report on a matter, proceeding, or notice of a meeting or hearing.
