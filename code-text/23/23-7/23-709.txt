                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICE OF EMPLOYEE&#8217;S CRIMINAL CONVICTION (ยง 23-709)

709.1. Each licensee shall immediately notify the Board in writing if the
   licensee discovers that any employee who sells, gives, furnishes, or
   distributes any alcoholic beverage has at any time prior to or during his or
   her employment been arrested or convicted for other than minor traffic
   violations. For purposes of this section, &#8220;immediately&#8221; shall mean
   notifying the Board within seven (7) days of discovering the criminal
   conviction.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 4309 (April 30, 2004); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12991 (December 26, 2008).
