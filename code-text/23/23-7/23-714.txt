                                  DC REGULATIONS

OUTDOOR EVENTS ON PUBLIC SPACE (ยง 23-714)

714.1. The holder of an on-premises Retailer&#8217;s license may file a
   one-day substantial change application with the Board to receive permission to
   serve or sell alcoholic beverages on public space for a specific event that
   may involve a temporary street closing.

   714.2. The one-day substantial change application to sell or serve alcoholic
   beverages on public space shall be on a form provided by the Board that shall
   include a copy of all other licenses and permits required for the event under
   District of Columbia law. The fee for a one-day substantial change shall be
   the same as the cost of a Temporary license.

   714.3. Such a request shall not be granted by the Board more than three (3)
   times in a calendar year.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 4309 (April 30, 2004); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12991 (December 26, 2008).
