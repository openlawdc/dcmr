                                  DC REGULATIONS

MANAGER CERTIFICATION (§ 23-212)

212.1. An applicant for a Manager’s license shall submit a copy of his or
   her certificate showing completion of an alcohol training and education
   program within the previous two years from a Board approved training provider
   with his or her Manager&#8217;s license application.

   212.2. An applicant for a Manager&#8217;s license, who has been selected by a
   licensee to serve as a licensed manager pursuant to § 707, but who has not
   completed an alcohol training and education program may be issued a temporary
   Manager&#8217;s license pursuant to § 707.10 by the Board for a period not to
   exceed 30 days upon the submission of a sworn affidavit from the applicant
   that he or she will complete an alcohol training and education program and
   submit a copy of his or her certificate within the 30-day period.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 4309 (April 30, 2004); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12991 (December 26, 2008).
