                                  DC REGULATIONS

SPECIAL LICENSING PROVISIONS (ยง 23-206)

206.1. No holder of a Retailer&#8217;s license Class C or Class D shall sell
   or serve alcoholic beverages in closed containers, with the following
   exceptions:
   
   
   
   (a)	Class CH and Class DH Hotels may sell and serve alcoholic beverages in
   closed containers in the private rooms of their registered guests; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Class CX and DX Clubs may sell and serve alcoholic beverages in closed
   containers in any room or area available only to bona fide members of the club
   or their guests.

   206.2. A Retailer&#8217;s license Class DR or DT may be issued to a restaurant
   or delicatessen that is located within a pavilion, shopping mall, or shopping
   center. In this case, patrons of the restaurant or delicatessen shall use the
   same common dining area within the pavilion, shopping mall or shopping center,
   and the common dining area must be approved by the Board. The restaurant or
   delicatessen may sell only beer or light wine. Beer or wine in open containers
   shall be clearly identifiable with the business where purchased.

   206.3. The Board shall not grant a Retailer&#8217;s license Class CN or DN to
   a hotel, unless the hotel holds a license of a corresponding class, that is, a
   Retailer&#8217;s license CH or DH.

   206.4. Nothing shall preclude the holder of a Retailer&#8217;s license Class A
   or Class B from having tables provided that alcoholic beverages are not opened
   or consumed on the licensed premises. The holder of a Retailer&#8217;s license
   Class A or Class B must state its intention to have tables on the ABC license
   application.

   206.5. Nothing shall preclude the holder of a Retailer&#8217;s license Class B
   issued pursuant to the provisions of D.C. Official Code ยง 25-303(c), who is
   also the holder of a Retailer&#8217;s license Class CR or DR, from selling or
   serving wine, beer, or spirits for consumption on premises.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 4309 (April 30, 2004); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12991 (December 26, 2008); as amended
   by D.C. ACT 17-696 published at 56 DCR 1204, 1207 (February 6, 2009).
