                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRIMARY AMERICAN SOURCE OF SUPPLY (ยง 23-900)

900.1. It shall be unlawful for any wholesaler to purchase any alcoholic
   beverage for resale unless the alcoholic beverages are purchased from the
   primary American source of supply for the brand of alcoholic beverages sought
   to be resold.

   900.2. It shall be unlawful for any wholesaler to sell any alcoholic beverages
   in the District of Columbia if the alcoholic beverages have not been purchased
   by the wholesaler from the primary American source of supply.
