                                  DC REGULATIONS

DENIED OR WITHDRAWN APPLICATIONS (ยง 23-504)

504.1. The service charge fee for processing an application which has been
   denied or withdrawn shall be one hundred fifty dollars ($150) for a
   proprietorship, two hundred fifty dollars ($250) for a partnership, and three
   hundred fifty dollars ($350) for a corporation or an unincorporated entity.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 4309 (April 30, 2004); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12991 (December 26, 2008).
