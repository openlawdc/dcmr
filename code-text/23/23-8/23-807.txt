                                  DC REGULATIONS

SALE TO MINOR VIOLATIONS (§ 23-807)

807.1  	The Board may give warnings for first-time sale to minor offenses,
excluding “egregious” sale to minor violations.  Egregious shall be defined
as “a sale to minor violation where the licensee: (1) sold or served an
alcoholic beverage to a minor who was unable to produce a valid identification
after a request from the licensee to do so, or (2) intentionally sold an
alcoholic beverage to a minor.”











		Source: Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 8686 (September 24, 2010).
