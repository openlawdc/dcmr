                                  DC REGULATIONS

CITATION APPEALS (ยง 23-806)

806.1. A licensee may challenge the issuance of a citation issued by an ABRA
   Investigator or MPD Officer by requesting in writing a show cause hearing
   before the Board. The written request for a show cause hearing must be
   received by ABRA within thirty (30) days from the date that the citation was
   issued to the establishment.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 4309 (April 30, 2004); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12991 (December 26, 2008).
