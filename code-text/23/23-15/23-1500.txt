APPLICABILITY (ยง 23-1500)

1500.1. This chapter shall govern all notices to the public, government
   officials, licensees and applicants for a license, concerning the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Applications for new licenses;
   
   
   
   (b)	Applications to renew licenses or transfer licenses to new locations;
   
   
   
   (c)	Applications to substantially change the nature of operations at a
   licensed establishment; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Applications to transfer licenses to new owners.
