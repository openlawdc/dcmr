                                  DC REGULATIONS

DENIAL OF LICENSE FOR VIOLATIONS OF LAW (ยง 23-401)

401.1. The Board may deny a license to an applicant if evidence shows that the
   applicant has permitted at the establishment conduct which is in violation of
   this title.
