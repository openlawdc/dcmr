                                  DC REGULATIONS

INTEREST OF PUBLIC BODY (ยง 14-3006)

3006.1. No elected official, employee or agency of the District who exercises
   policy or decision-making functions or responsibilities in connection with the
   administration of the Program shall be allowed to have any direct or indirect
   interest in the proceeds of a loan, or in any contract entered into by the
   applicant for the performance of work, financed in whole or in part with
   Program funds.

   3006.2. District employees who meet program eligibility requirements may
   receive loans in accordance with rules and procedures of the Program.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 7621, 7626 (December
   7, 1990).
