                                  DC REGULATIONS

AMENDMENT OF RULES (§ 14-6001)

6001.1. Any revision or amendment of chapters 60 through 65 shall be
   consistent with the provisions of the District of Columbia Administrative
   Procedure Act, D.C. Official Code §§ 2-501 et seq. (2001), except as
   provided for in this section.

   6001.2. The rules under chapters 60 through 65 may be amended by DCHA as
   follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	By publication as a notice in the D.C. Register where amendments are
   required pursuant to Federal law and regulation, and where such Federal
   regulation has been issued pursuant to the Federal Administrative Procedure
   Act; or
   
   
   
   (b)	Where Federal regulation provides any discretionary element to DHCA in
   adopting a policy, amendments shall be published as rules.

   6001.3. Any amendment to the rules under § 6001.2 shall be posted in all
   appropriate management offices of the District of Columbia Housing Authority
   (DCHA) properties.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 7973 (December 26,
   1986); and Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 603 (January 22,
   1999), incorporating by reference the text of Notice of Proposed Rulemaking
   published at 45 DCR 7913 (November 6, 1998).
