                                  DC REGULATIONS

STATUS OF PENDING CERTIFICATE OF OCCUPANCY (ยง 14-1409)

1409.1. All Certificate of Occupancy applications on file as of the effective
   date of these regulations, December 17, 1982, shall be considered as having
   been received on that date and shall be processed accordingly.
