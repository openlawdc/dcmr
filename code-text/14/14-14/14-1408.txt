SEVERABILITY (ยง 14-1408)

1408.1. If any provision of these rules or the application thereof to any
   person or circumstance is held invalid, the remainder of the rules, and the
   application of such provision to other persons or circumstance, shall not be
   affected.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 29 DCR 5571, 5575 (December
   17, 1982).
