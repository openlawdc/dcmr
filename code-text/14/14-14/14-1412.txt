                                  DC REGULATIONS

PARTIAL CERTIFICATE OF OCCUPANCY FEE (ยง 14-1412)

1412.1. The nonrefundable application fee for partial Certificates of
   Occupancy filed in accordance with the D.C. Building Code shall be twenty
   dollars ($20), plus an hourly fee to be determined at a rate of thirteen
   dollars ($13) per inspector hour charged by full hour or fraction thereof.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice Final Rulemaking published at 29 DCR 5571, 5576 (December 17,
   1982).
