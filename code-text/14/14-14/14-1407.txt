                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEALS (ยง 14-1407)

1407.1. Any person aggrieved by the action of the Director granting,
   withholding, or revoking a Certificate of Occupancy may appeal the action to
   the Board of Zoning Adjustment, pursuant to D.C. Official Code 6-641.07(f)
   (2001), and the District of Columbia Zoning Regulations.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 29 DCR 5571, 5575 (December
   17, 1982).
