                                  DC REGULATIONS

HEARINGS (ยง 14-3819)

3819.1. In hearing appeals, the Commission shall sit as a body with at least
   two (2) Commission members present, and all hearings shall be open to the
   public.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 1336, 1348 (March 7,
   1986).
