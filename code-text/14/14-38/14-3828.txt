                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMMISSION PROCEDURES GENERALLY (ยง 14-3828)

3828.1. When these rules are silent on a procedural issue before the
   Commission, that issue shall be decided by using as guidance the current rules
   of civil procedure published and followed by the Superior Court of the
   District of Columbia and the rules of the District of Columbia Court of
   Appeals.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final rulemaking published at 45 DCR 684, 687 (February 6,
   1998).
