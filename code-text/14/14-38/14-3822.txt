                                  DC REGULATIONS

REMANDS (ยง 14-3822)

3822.1. The Commission shall endeavor to effect a final disposition of all
   issues in controversy.

   3822.2. Any case remanded by the Commission to the Rent Administrator shall
   receive expedited and priority treatment.

   3822.3. Remanded cases shall be returned to the hearing examiner who
   originally heard the matter unless the hearing examiner is no longer available
   or the Commission for good cause orders otherwise.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 1336, 1349-50 (March
   7, 1986).
