                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 14-3800)

3800.1. The Rental Housing Commission shall establish, with the approval of a
   majority of its membership, internal operating procedures for the handling of
   the Commission’s business and the fair distribution of work among its
   members.

   3800.2. The office of the Commission shall be open daily from 8:30 a.m. to
   4:30 p.m., except Saturdays, Sundays, and legal holidays.

   3800.3. For purposes of chapters 38 through 44, all references to “the
   Act” shall mean the Rental Housing Act of 1985, D.C. Law 6-10 (effective
   July 17, 1985).
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is Section
   202(a)(1) of D.C. Law 6-10, the “Rental Housing Act of 1985,” effective
   July 17, 1985, 32 DCR 4438 (August 2, 1985) (codified at D.C. Official Code §
   42-3502.02(a)(1) (2001).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 1336 (March 7, 1986).
