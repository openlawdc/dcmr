ANNUAL INSPECTION (ยง 14-6506)

6506.1. Each occupied unit shall be inspected annually by DCHA. A written
   notice of inspection shall be given to the tenant at least two (2) days in
   advance.

   6506.2. Tenant-caused damage discovered during this inspection shall be
   assessed the tenant after completion of the repairs in accordance with ยง 6503
   of this chapter.
