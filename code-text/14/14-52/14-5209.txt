                                  DC REGULATIONS

EXPIRATION OF THE INITIAL VOUCHER TERM (§ 14-5209)

5209.1. If a Family’s voucher term or extension expires before the Family
   has submitted an approved RTA, the voucher shall be deemed to have expired and
   DCHA shall notify the Family in writing that the voucher term has expired and
   the Family may reapply for HCVP assistance in accordance with chapter 76 of
   this title of the DCMR.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7875, 7882 (June 29, 2012).
