                                  DC REGULATIONS

ORAL BRIEFING (§ 14-5203)

5203.1. Each briefing shall provide information on the following subjects:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   How the Housing Choice Voucher Program works;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Family and owner responsibilities;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Where the Family can lease a unit, including renting a unit inside or outside
   DCHA’s jurisdiction;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   For families eligible under portability, an explanation of portability; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   An explanation of the advantages of moving to areas outside of high-poverty
   concentrations.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7875, 7876 (June 29, 2012).
