                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTIFICATION AND ATTENDANCE (ยง 14-5202)

5202.1. HCVP shall notify Families in writing, by first class mail or hand
   delivery, of their eligibility for assistance at the time that they are
   invited to attend a briefing. The notice shall identify who is required to
   attend the briefing, as well as the date and time of the scheduled briefing.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7875, 7876 (June 29, 2012); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 13363 (Nov. 23, 2012).
