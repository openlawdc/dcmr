                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESPONSIBILITY FOR LOCATING HOUSING (§ 14-5211)

5211.1. Once DCHA issues a Voucher to a Family, it is the Family’s
   responsibility to locate suitable housing that is within the Local Market Rent
   as determined by HUD, meets Housing Quality Standards requirements, and
   includes the minimum bedroom size requirements for units.

   5211.2. DCHA shall maintain and update referral lists of owners who have
   called DCHA to list their available units. The list shall be made available to
   Voucher holders upon request.

   5211.3. DCHA offers search counseling and assistance to families who are
   having difficulty locating appropriate housing within the Voucher term.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7875, 7883 (June 29, 2012).
