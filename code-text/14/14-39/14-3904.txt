PARTIES (ยง 14-3904)

3904.1. Individual tenants involved in any proceeding shall be individually
   identified.

   3904.2. Any tenant association may file and shall be granted party status to
   prosecute or defend a petition on behalf of any one or more of its members who
   have provided the association with written authorization to represent them in
   the action, or to seek on behalf of all members and any injunctive relief
   available under the Rental Housing Act of 1985, effective July 17, 1985 (D.C.
   Law 6-10; D.C. Official Code ยง 42-3501.01 set seq.).  No further inquiry into
   the membership of the association shall be permitted.

   3904.3. Any tenant association that is a party to the action pursuant to ยง
   3904.2 shall be listed in the caption.
