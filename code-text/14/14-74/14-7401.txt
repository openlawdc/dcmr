                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICATION OF REASONABLE ACCOMMODATION POLICY (ยง 14-7401)

7401.1. This chapter applies to individuals with disabilities in the following
   programs provided by the DCHA:
   
   
   
   (a)	Applicants of public housing;
   
   
   
   (b)	Applicants of all Housing Choice Voucher and Moderate Rehabilitation
   Programs;
   
   
   
   (c)	Residents of public housing developments;
   
   
   
   (d)	Participants of the Housing Choice Voucher and Moderate Rehabilitation
   Programs; and
   
   
   
   (e)	Participants in all other programs or activities receiving Federal
   financial assistance that are conducted or sponsored by the DCHA, its agents
   or contractors including all non-housing facilities and common areas owned or
   operated by the DCHA.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2468, 2469 (March 15,
   2002).
