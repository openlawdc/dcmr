                                  DC REGULATIONS

RETURN OF SECURITY DEPOSIT[COLON] INSPECTION OF PREMISES (§ 14-310)

310.1. In order to determine the amount of the security deposit or other
   payment to be returned to the tenant, the owner may inspect the dwelling unit
   within three (3) days, excluding Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays, before or
   after the termination of the tenancy.

   310.2. The owner shall conduct the inspection, if the inspection is to be
   conducted, at the time and place of which notice is given to the tenant.

   310.3. The owner shall notify the tenant in writing of the time and date of
   the inspection.

   310.4. The notice of inspection shall be delivered to the tenant, or at the
   dwelling unit in question, at least ten (10) days before the date of the
   intended inspection.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 2908,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955); as amended by section 3 of
   the Security Deposit Act, D.C. Law 1-48, 22 DCR 2823 (November 28, 1975).
