                                  DC REGULATIONS

EFFECT OF DECISION (ยง 14-6315)

6315.1. A decision of the hearing officer which is in favor of DCHA, or denies
   the complainant his or her requested relief in whole, or in part, shall not
   constitute a waiver of, or affect in any manner whatever, rights the
   complainant may have to a trial de novo in judicial proceedings which may be
   later brought in the matter.

   6315.2. In de novo judicial proceedings, neither party shall be limited to
   invoking against the other the grounds originally relied on in the
   administrative proceedings.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 7973, 8019 (December
   26, 1986); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2455,
   2459 (March 15, 2002).
