                                  DC REGULATIONS

BRIEFS IN SUPPORT OF OR TAKING ISSUE WITH THE DECISION OF THE HEARING OFFICER
(§ 14-6314)

6314.1. Any party may file a brief with the Chief of OFH in support of or in
   opposition to the hearing officer’s decision within ten (10) working days
   after service of the decision.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 2291, 2294 (April 3,
   1992).
