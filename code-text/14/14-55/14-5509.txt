NOTICE TO INITIAL PUBLIC HOUSING AUTHORITY OF BILLING OR ABSORPTION (ยง 14-5509)

5509.1 	DCHA shall send Part II of HUD Form-52665 to the Initial PHA notifying
the Initial PHA of absorption or billing arrangements.



5509.2 	Under no circumstances shall DCHA absorb families into its program
retroactively.
