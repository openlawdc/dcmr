                                  DC REGULATIONS

ABSORPTION OF A PORTABLE FAMILY (§ 14-5508)

5508.1 	A Housing Specialist shall not absorb families into the Program unless
the Director of the HCVP or his or her designee directs or approves the
absorption of the families.



5508.2 	When DCHA is absorbing on an ongoing basis, an incoming portable Family
is considered to be absorbed to into the Program once DCHA executes the HAP
contract on behalf of the Family, versus at the time of voucher issuance.



5508.3 	A portable Family may also be absorbed by terminating billing
arrangement with the initial PHA.  DCHA shall give thirty (30) days’ advance
notice to the initial PHA of the intent to absorb and the effective date of the
absorption of the Family.





SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7926, 7932 (June 29, 2012).
