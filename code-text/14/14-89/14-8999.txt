                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 14-8999)

8999.1. For purposes of Chapter 89, the following terms shall have the
   meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Good cause &#8211; is defined as an unavoidable conflict which seriously
   affects the health, safety or welfare of the family or applicant, including a
   verified illness or disability;
   
   
   
   Head of Household &#8211; The person who serves as the basis for income
   eligibility and rent determination as well as assumes legal responsibility for
   the household.
   
   
   
   Applicant (Applicant Family) &#8211; refers to a family that has applied for
   admission to a program but is not yet a participant in the program;
   
   
   
   Family &#8211; A person or group of persons, as determined by the PHA,
   approved to reside in a unit with assistance under the program;
   
   
   
   Participant (Participant family) &#8211; A family that has been admitted to
   the PHA program and is currently assisted in the program. The family becomes a
   participant on the effective date of the first HAP contract executed by the
   PHA for the family (first day of initial lease term);
   
   
   
   Housing Quality Standards (HQS) &#8211; The HUD minimum quality standards for
   housing assisted under the tenant-based programs;
   
   
   
   Landlord &#8211; Landlord and Owner are used interchangeably;
   
   
   
   Tenant &#8211; the person or persons (other than a live-in aide) who executes
   the lease as lessee of the dwelling unit;
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 7192, (July 26, 2002).
