                                  DC REGULATIONS

INSUFFICIENT FUNDING (ยง 14-5809)

5809.1. DCHA may terminate HAP contracts if DCHA determines, in accordance
   with HUD requirements, that funding is insufficient to support continued
   assistance for families within the Program.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7942, 7951 (June 29, 2012).
