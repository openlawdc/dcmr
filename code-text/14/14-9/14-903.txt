                                  DC REGULATIONS

EXIT AND EMERGENCY LIGHTS AND DIRECTIONAL SIGNS (§ 14-903)

903.1. It shall be the duty of the operator of each housing business to
   maintain in an operable condition all emergency and exit lights.

   903.2. The operator of each housing business shall maintain lighted exits and
   emergency lights from sunset to sunrise, and at other times when conditions
   warrant.

   903.3. The operator of each housing business shall maintain all exit
   directional signs.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 3209,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
