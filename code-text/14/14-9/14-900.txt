                                  DC REGULATIONS

OCCUPANCY OF RESIDENTIAL BUILDINGS (§ 14-900)

900.1. Allowable occupancy in residential buildings as provided in the this
   subtitle shall not be construed as superseding or contravening any limitations
   imposed on occupancy by applicable requirements of fire safety and egress.

   900.2. Unoccupied buildings and buildings left uncompleted shall be protected
   by barricading, or other means, as to afford protection against accident to
   persons in or about the premises.
