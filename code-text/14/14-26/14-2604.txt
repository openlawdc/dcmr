                                  DC REGULATIONS

INELIGIBLE COSTS (ยง 14-2604)

2604.1. Funds from Seed Money Loans may not be applied to the actual purchase
   or rehabilitation of the building or to expenses for services after the
   initial marketing of the units.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 27 DCR 4606, 4609 (October
   17, 1980).
