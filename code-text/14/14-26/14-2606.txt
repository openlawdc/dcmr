                                  DC REGULATIONS

WAIVER AUTHORITY (ยง 14-2606)

2606.1. The Director of the Department shall have the right to waive any and
   all of the provisions of this chapter within the limits of applicable federal
   and District of Columbia laws and regulations.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 27 DCR 4606, 4610 (October
   17, 1980).
