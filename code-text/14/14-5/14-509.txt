                                  DC REGULATIONS

OBSTRUCTIONS TO VENTILATION (§ 14-509)

509.1. If a habitation is located on the ground floor of a residential
   building, the yard of the building which lies immediately outside the
   habitation shall be maintained clear of any obstruction to ventilation for a
   distance of at least three feet (3 ft.) from the exterior wall of the
   building, or such further distance as may be required by any other law or
   regulation.

   509.2. A reasonable amount of trees or shrubbery shall not be considered to be
   a violation of this section.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 2308,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503, (August 11, 1955).
