                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 14-500)

500.1. The owner of a building used for residential purposes shall provide
   that building with adequate facilities for heating, ventilating, and lighting.

   500.2. Each facility provided and maintained to comply with this section shall
   be properly and safely installed, and shall be maintained in a safe and good
   working condition.
