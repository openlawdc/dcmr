                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTROL AND MAINTENANCE OF MECHANICAL VENTILATION (§ 14-508)

508.1. If mechanical ventilation is provided for any residential building by
   the owner or licensee, the owner or licensee shall maintain that system in
   safe and good working condition.

   508.2. If the mechanical ventilation system is not under the control of the
   occupant of any habitation, the owner or licensee of the residential building
   shall keep that equipment in constant and continuous operation.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 2408,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503, (August 11, 1955).
