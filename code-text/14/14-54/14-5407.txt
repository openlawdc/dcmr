                                  DC REGULATIONS

ASSETS (§ 14-5407)

5407.1. DCHA, under its MTW authority established Initiative 3.7.08 entitled
   “Rent Simplification and Collection” which simplified calculation of
   assets for purposes of determining income.  The following provision shall
   apply to calculating assets as imputed income:
   
   
   
   (a)	DCHA shall not require documentation of assets during the biennial
   recertification process; and
   
   
   
   (b)	DCHA shall not consider imputed income from assets in the calculation of
   total annual income.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7916, 7925 (June 29, 2012).
