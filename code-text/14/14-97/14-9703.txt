STOLEN VEHICLES (ยง 14-9703)

9703.1. If DCHA determines that a vehicle is stolen, whether on DCHA Property
   or Public Property, the DCHA&#8217;s Office of Public Safety shall report the
   vehicle stolen on the requisite Metropolitan Police Department report form or
   DCHA staff shall notify the Metropolitan Police Department of the stolen
   vehicle.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 2112, 2113 (February
   27, 2004).
