                                  DC REGULATIONS

TOWING OF VEHICLES (§ 14-9705)

9705.1. DCHA will make two attempts to contact the owner of a vehicle that has
   been issued a Notice of Infraction for being parked on DCHA&#8217;s Property
   as provided for under Section 9704 above. The attempts will be no less than
   twenty-four (24) hours apart.

   9705.2. If DCHA is unable to contact the owner of a vehicle after two
   attempts, DCHA will proceed with the removal of the vehicle from DCHA
   Property.

   9705.3. If the vehicle is not removed from DCHA Property within 72 hours of
   the issuance of the Notice of Infraction and Warning Notice, DCHA will have
   the vehicle removed by contacting either:
   
   
   
   (a)	The District of Columbia Department of Public Works, Abandoned and Junk
   Vehicle Division; or
   
   
   
   (b)	A tow crane operator licensed with the District of Columbia.”
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 2112, 2114-2115
   (February 27, 2004).
