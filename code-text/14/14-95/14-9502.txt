ELIGIBILITY GENERALLY (ยง 14-9502)

9502.1. DCHA shall ensure that initial admission to the LRSP is limited to
   Extremely-Low Income Households (including 1 person households) and all
   households served by LRSP funding shall be located in the District of
   Columbia.
