                                  DC REGULATIONS

TERMINATION OF RECEIVERSHIP (ยง 14-6909)

6909.1. The Superior Court shall terminate the receivership pursuant to
   section 507 of the Act.

   6909.2. A receivership shall not be terminated in favor of any person who was
   the owner of the rental accommodation or his representative at the time the
   petition was filed under section 503 of the Act and ยง 6902 of this chapter,
   or, in the discretion of the Superior Court, any person who is or was an
   affiliate owner, agent, lessor, or manager, unless:
   
   
   
   (a)	He or she first reimburses the District for the expenses incurred in
   creating the receivership; and
   
   
   
   (b)	If required by the Superior Court, he or she posts a bond in an amount the
   Superior Court deems appropriate as security against non-compliance with the
   law.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 7488, 7501 (August 2,
   2002).
