                                  DC REGULATIONS

MILITARY LEAVE (ยง 14-7124)

7124.1. Any employee, other than a contract employee, may be granted paid
   leave not to exceed fifteen (15) days in a calendar year. An employee taking
   military leave must present his or her military orders to the Department
   Director at least two (2) weeks before departure.

   7124.2. DCHA National Guard and Reservist employees are entitled to not more
   than twenty-two (22) work days of paid leave in a calendar year pursuant to a
   national emergency.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6928-6929
   (December 8, 1995).
