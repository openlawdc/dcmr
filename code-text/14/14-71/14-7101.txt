                                  DC REGULATIONS

THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA HOUSING AUTHORITY (§ 14-7101)

7101.1. The District of Columbia Housing Authority (hereinafter referred to as
   the “Authority”) is a corporate body that has a legal existence separate
   from the District government but which is an instrumentality of the District
   government. While the Authority is not a city agency, a close and cooperative
   working relationship between the Authority and the District is essential.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6916 (December
   8, 1995).
