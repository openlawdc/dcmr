FAMILY AND MEDICAL LEAVE POLICY (ยง 14-7129)

7129.1. It is the policy of the Authority to grant up to sixteen (16) weeks of
   family and medical leave during any twenty-four (24) month period to eligible
   employees, in accordance with the District of Columbia Family and Medical
   Leave Act of 1990 (FMLA), D.C. Law 8-181. The leave may be paid, unpaid, or a
   combination of paid and unpaid depending on the circumstances and requirements
   as specified in ยง 7118.

   7129.2. The FMLA applies to employees who have worked for the Authority for
   one (1) year without a break in service prior to requesting leave under the
   FMLA and who have worked at least one thousand (1,000) hours during the last
   twelve (12) months.
