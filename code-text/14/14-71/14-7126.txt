                                  DC REGULATIONS

UNAUTHORIZED ABSENCE (AWOL) (ยง 14-7126)

7126.1. An employee absent from duty without approval shall not receive paid
   compensation for the duration of the absence. Denial of paid compensation
   shall not affect the right of the Authority to invoke any other appropriate
   form of disciplinary action, to include dismissal.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6929 (December
   8, 1995).
