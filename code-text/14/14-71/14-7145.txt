                                  DC REGULATIONS

DRUG-FREE WORKPLACE (ยง 14-7145)

7145.1. As described in the Drug-Free Workplace Act, it is the policy of the
   Authority to provide a drug-free workplace and for all employees to abide by
   this policy. Accordingly, an employee of the Authority is prohibited from
   engaging in the unlawful manufacture, distribution, dispersion, possession, or
   use of a controlled substance in the workplace. Violations of this or other
   prohibitions on drug use by Authority employees may result in disciplinary
   action, to include removal.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6940 (December
   8, 1995).
