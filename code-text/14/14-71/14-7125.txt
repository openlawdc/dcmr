                                  DC REGULATIONS

LEAVE OF ABSENCE (LWOP) (§ 14-7125)

7125.1. Leave of absence is leave without pay (LWOP) and may be granted to an
   employee for a good cause. The request shall be considered when the
   employee’s employment record reflects fully successful performance. Leave of
   absence may be granted for a specified time not to exceed one (1) year, but
   requires the approval of the Executive Director.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6929 (December
   8, 1995).
