                                  DC REGULATIONS

MERIT INCREASES (ยง 14-7116)

7116.1. Within minimum and maximum ranges determined by the Executive
   Director, each supervisor shall recommend merit increases for deserving
   employees and submit requests to the Office of Human Resource Management. The
   Authority reserves the right to deny merit increase recommendations.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6926 (December
   8, 1995).
