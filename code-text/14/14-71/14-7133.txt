                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISABILITY COMPENSATION (ยง 14-7133)

7133.1. Employees who sustain injury, occupational diseases, or death in the
   performance of their employment duties may be entitled to disability
   compensation.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6930 (December
   8, 1995).
