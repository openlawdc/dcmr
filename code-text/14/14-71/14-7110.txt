ORIENTATION OF NEW AND REHIRED EMPLOYEES (ยง 14-7110)

7110.1. The Authority will conduct an orientation program to introduce all new
   employees to their new positions and to the Authority.

   7110.2. Photo ID cards will be processed and issued by the Office of Human
   Resource Management.
