                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROGRESSIVE DISCIPLINARY MEASURES (§ 14-7139)

7139.1. The degree of discipline shall be reasonably related to the
   seriousness of the offense, and take into consideration the employee’s past
   record. Wherever practicable, disciplinary actions shall be progressive. The
   following measures are listed in degrees of severity as a means of progressive
   discipline:
   
   
   
   (a)	Corrective Action;
   
   
   
   (b)	Written Disciplinary Action Notice;
   
   
   
   (c)	Suspension;
   
   
   
   (d)	Removal.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6934 (December
   8, 1995).
