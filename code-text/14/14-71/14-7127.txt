                                  DC REGULATIONS

BEREAVEMENT LEAVE (§ 14-7127)

7127.1. Bereavement leave with pay for employees may be granted for up to
   three (3) scheduled workdays after the death of a member of the immediate
   family. Immediate family is defined as spouse, mother, mother-in-law, father,
   father-in-law, child, brother, brother-in-law, sister, sister-in-law, or any
   other relative who resides on a regular basis at the employee’s home, as
   well as relationships characterized by permanence, duration and stability
   normally associated with family relationships or marriage.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6929 (December
   8, 1995).
