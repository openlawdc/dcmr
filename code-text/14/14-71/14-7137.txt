                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISCIPLINARY ACTION (§ 14-7137)

7137.1. Disciplinary action taken against a permanent employee shall be for
   cause, in accordance with this chapter. The incident(s) precipitating the
   disciplinary action must be documented in writing and have the concurrence of
   the employee’s Department Director. Disciplinary action may be progressive
   or immediate removal. Any action taken for suspension or removal must be
   consistent with the provisions of § 7111.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6932 (December 8, 1995).
