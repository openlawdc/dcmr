                                  DC REGULATIONS

EMPLOYEE HEALTH AND SAFETY (ยง 14-7144)

7144.1. The Authority shall designate a Safety and Occupational Health
   Coordinator to develop and administer the safety program.

   7144.2. The Authority requires employees to follow all health and safety
   policies and procedures.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6940 (December
   8, 1995).
