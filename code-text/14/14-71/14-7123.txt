                                  DC REGULATIONS

COURT LEAVE (§ 14-7123)

7123.1. Employees required to perform jury duty are entitled to their regular
   rate of pay for such absences, provided any payment received as jury
   compensation is tendered to the Authority’s payroll office, with the
   exception of remuneration for travel expenses.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6928 (December
   8, 1995).
