                                  DC REGULATIONS

RATINGS (ยง 14-7114)

7114.1. Ratings shall be as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	Outstanding;
   
   
   
   (b)	Exceptional;
   
   
   
   (c)	Fully successful;
   
   
   
   (d)	Partially successful; and
   
   
   
   (e)	Unacceptable.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6925 (December
   8, 1995).
