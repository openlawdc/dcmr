                                  DC REGULATIONS

HEALTH CARE AND LIFE INSURANCE PLANS (§ 14-7131)

7131.1. Benefits are available to all full-time employees, other than contract
   employees, as outlined in health and insurance plans, which may be obtained in
   the Office of Human Resource Management. Employees who are terminated may
   elect to continue health coverage at their own expense under the Authority’s
   group plan as specified in the plan.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 6914, 6930 (December
   8, 1995).
