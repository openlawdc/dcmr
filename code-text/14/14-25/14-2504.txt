2504.1. [Deleted]

   2504.2. Loans made to any income-eligible household under this Program shall
   require monthly Principal-Only payments in an amount equal to the loan amount
   amortized over a four hundred eighty (480) month period, and payment shall
   begin five (5) years from the date of the loan execution.
