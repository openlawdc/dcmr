                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRIVACY RIGHTS (ยง 14-4911)

4911.1. In conjunction with the HUD Form-50058, applicant and participant
   families shall be required to sign the Federal Privacy Act Statement, which
   states under what conditions HUD will release Family and owner information.

   4911.2. DCHA shall only release information to third parties under one or more
   of the following circumstances:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   With the signed and written consent of the Head of Household;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   To HUD or other Housing agencies on amounts owed to DCHA for claims paid and
   not reimbursed by a participant or landlord regardless of whether there is a
   repayment agreement; or
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   As permitted or required by law.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7856, 7865 (June 29, 2012).
