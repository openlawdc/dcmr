                                  DC REGULATIONS

OWNER OUTREACH (ยง 14-4909)

4909.1. DCHA issues invitations to owners as needed to make dwelling units
   available for leasing by eligible families in accordance with the Equal
   Opportunity Housing Plan.  DCHA welcomes the continuous participation of
   owners of decent, safe, and sanitary housing units.
   
   
   
   4909.2 	DCHA shall maintain a list of interested owners and their properties
   which are available for HCVP.  As inquiries from prospective new owners are
   received, program staff records the necessary information on units and shall
   make such information available to prospective participants upon request.

   4909.3. Owner meetings with HCVP staff shall be held periodically to maintain
   a positive relationship and to provide owners further knowledge regarding
   Program rules and operations.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7856, 7864 (June 29, 2012).
