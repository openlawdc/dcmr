                                  DC REGULATIONS

MOVE-IN REQUIREMENTS (§ 14-6116)

6116.1. Applicants from the waiting list who accept a unit shall pay one (1)
   month’s tenant rent and a security deposit prior to signing the dwelling
   lease. All rent and security deposits shall be paid by check or money order
   only.

   6116.2. DCHA shall determine if the applicant&#8217;s application is current,
   shall schedule an appointment with the applicant, and at the appointment shall
   do the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Review and complete all required certifications of eligibility and the
   dwelling lease; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Obtain the applicant&#8217;s signature on all required documents.

   6116.3. The tenant shall attend any training required by DCHA.

   6116.4. DCHA shall schedule a move-in date for the new tenant upon execution
   of the documents described in Subsection 6114.2 and shall provide the new
   tenant with copies. DCHA shall also conduct a move-in inspection in accordance
   with Section 6505 of this Subtitle.
