HOUSING QUALITY STANDARDS AND INSPECTIONS (ยง 14-5321)

5321.1. DCHA is required to inspect each unit receiving assistance under the
   HCVP to ensure that it is &#8220;decent, safe, and sanitary&#8221; according
   to Housing Quality Standards.

   5321.2. DCHA has adopted additional local requirements of acceptability,
   defined in ยง 5324 and the local District of Columbia Housing Code.

   5321.3. A unit must meet Housing Quality Standards to be eligible for a
   Housing Assistance Payment, both at initial lease-up and so long as the
   participant Family resides in the unit.

   5321.4. DCHA shall perform four types of inspections:
   
   
   
   (a)	Initial;
   
   
   
   (b)	Annual;
   
   
   
   	(c)	Complaint; and
   
   
   
   	(d)	Quality Assurance.
   
   
   
    5321.5	DCHA maintains a listing of units under contract by month to ensure
   systematic reviews of contract rent, allowances for utilities and other
   services, and housing quality in accordance with the requirement for biennial
   recertification.  Monetary changes are transmitted to the Accounting Office to
   affect a change in the next rental payment.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7890, 7904 (June 29, 2012).
