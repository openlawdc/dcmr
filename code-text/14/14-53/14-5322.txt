                                  DC REGULATIONS

ELIGIBLE TYPES OF HOUSING (§ 14-5322)

5322.1. All structure types may be utilized, including but not limited to
   single Family, duplex, triplex, fourplex, garden apartment, townhouses, and
   high-rises.

   5322.2. Families may only rent units from relatives as reasonable
   accommodation for a disability, in accordance with DCHA’s policy as stated
   in § 4900.3.

   5322.3. Housing owned by DCHA is ineligible under the HCVP, as well as units
   where the Family is being subsidized under other Section 8 programs.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7890, 7905 (June 29, 2012).
