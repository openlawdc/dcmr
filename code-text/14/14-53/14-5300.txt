                                  DC REGULATIONS

INCOME CONSIDERATIONS AND DETERMINATION OF TOTAL TENANT PAYMENT (ยง 14-5300)

5300.1. Once a participant is receiving assistance, the following regularly
   scheduled events shall occur;
   
   
   
   		(a)	Biennial recertification, in which income is calculated and total tenant
   payment is determined;
   
   
   
   		(b)	Interim recertifications when necessary; and
   
   
   
   		(c)	Annual Housing Quality inspections.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 7890 (June 29, 2012).
