                                  DC REGULATIONS

HCVP WAITING LISTS GENERALLY (ยง 14-7600)

7600.1. Objectives Generally. It is DCHA&#8217;s objective to ensure that
   families applying to DCHA for private rental housing assistance are selected
   for admission in accordance with the policies set forth in this Chapter 76.

   7600.2. Other Waiting Lists. When an applicant applies to DCHA for housing
   assistance DCHA shall offer the opportunity to be placed on all open DCHA
   waiting lists.
