                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROGRAM PURPOSE (ยง 14-9800)

The purpose of the program is to develop local strategies that link public
housing assistance with public and private resources to encourage participating
families to:







Increase earned income;  and







Make progress toward achieving economic independence and prepare for purchasing
a home.



Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 2460, 2461 (March 18,
2011).
