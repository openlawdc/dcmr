                                  DC REGULATIONS

GRIEVANCE RIGHTS (§ 14-9804)

9804.1             Any grievance of any decision by DCHA with
respect to application, eligibility, continued occupancy or terminations under
this chapter is governed by 14 DCMR chapter 63, grievance provisions.



Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 2460, 2462 (March 18,
2011); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 4346 (May
20, 2011).
