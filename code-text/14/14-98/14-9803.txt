                                  DC REGULATIONS

JURISDICTION (ยง 14-9803)

9803.1. For the purposes of this Chapter the D.C. Superior Court Landlord and
   Tenant Branch has jurisdiction over any DCHA Dwelling Lease Agreement and AYBL
   Lease addendum.
   
   
   
   Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 2460, 2462 (March 18,
   2011).
