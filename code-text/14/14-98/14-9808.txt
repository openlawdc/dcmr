                                  DC REGULATIONS

OCCUPANCY STANDARDS (ยง 14-9808)

9808.1. AYBL Families will be responsible for adhering to any and all DCHA
   occupancy standards.

   9808.2. DCHA will make all reasonable efforts to apply the DCHA occupancy
   standards to avoid transfer to a conventional public housing unit when the
   family does not meet the occupancy standards and is otherwise in compliance
   with AYBL.
   
   
   
   Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 2460, 2467 (March 18,
   2011); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 4346 (May
   20, 2011).
