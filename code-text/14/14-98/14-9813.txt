                                  DC REGULATIONS

RENT COLLECTION, RETURNED CHECKS, RETROACTIVE RENT, ABATEMENT OF RENT AND
MINIMUM RENT (§ 14-9813)

9813.1 	AYBL Families will be subject to the existing Public Housing Rent
Collection, Returned Checks, Retroactive Rent, Rent Abatement of Rent and
Minimum Rent policies found at 14 DCMR §§6120, 6206, 6207, 6208.2, 6209, and
6210 and the Public Housing dwelling lease.



Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 2460, 2470 (March 18,
2011).
