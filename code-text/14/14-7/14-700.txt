                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 14-700)

700.1. The purpose of this subtitle is to require repairs and maintenance
   designed to make a premises or neighborhood healthy and safe. These
   requirements are in addition to the basic repairs and maintenance needed to
   keep out the elements.

   700.2. Every premises accommodating one (1) or more habitations shall be
   maintained and kept in repair to provide decent living accommodations for the
   occupants.
