                                  DC REGULATIONS

FOUNDATIONS, STRUCTURAL MEMBERS, AND EXTERIOR WALLS (§ 14-704)

704.1. Foundations and structural members shall provide a safe, firm, and
   substantial base and support for the structure at all points.

   704.2. Exterior walls shall be structurally sound and free of cracks and holes
   through which rodents or the elements can enter the buildings.

   704.3. Masonry walls shall be kept pointed.

   704.4. All exterior surfacing materials shall be kept securely fastened in
   place.

   704.5. All exterior wood surfaces shall be kept painted, varnished,
   shellacked, or covered with other preservative, unless the wood is customarily
   used in its natural state.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR §§
   2503 and 2512, Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
