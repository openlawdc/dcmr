                                  DC REGULATIONS

DESCRIPTION OF THE EMPLOYER- ASSISTED HOUSING PROGRAM (ยง 14-3601)

3601.1. The Employer-Assisted Housing Program provides assistance through
   Downpayment Matching Funds and/or a Deferred Payment Loan (Loan) to eligible
   Employees, consistent with this chapter.

   3601.2. DHCD shall provide Downpayment Matching Funds towards the purchase of
   a housing unit by eligible Employees consistent with this Chapter.

   3601.3. DHCD shall provide a Deferred Payment Loan (Loan) toward the purchase
   of a housing unit by eligible Employees, consistent with this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 155, 156 (January 8,
   1993); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 9853
   (November 1, 2002).
