                                  DC REGULATIONS

RENT ADMINISTRATOR PROCEDURES GENERALLY (ยง 14-4018)

4018.1. When these rules are silent on a procedural issue before the Rent
   Administrator, such issue shall be decided by using as guidance the current
   rules of civil procedure published and followed by the Superior Court of the
   District of Columbia.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 684, 687 (February 6,
   1998).
