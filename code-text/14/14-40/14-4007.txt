                                  DC REGULATIONS

OFFICIAL RECORD OF A PROCEEDING (§ 14-4007)

4007.1. The record of a proceeding at RACD shall consist of the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	The findings of fact and conclusions of law and the decision of the Rent
   Administrator;
   
   
   
   (b)	The tape recordings or transcripts of the hearings before the hearing
   examiner;
   
   
   
   (c)	All documents and exhibits offered into evidence at the hearing;
   
   
   
   (d)	Memoranda, if any, of ex parte communications as required by §§ 4002.3
   and 4002.4;
   
   
   
   (e)	Notices of hearings and proofs of service;
   
   
   
   (f)	Landlord registration files and any other documents found in the public
   record of which the Rent Administrator took official notice; and
   
   
   
   (g)	All pleadings filed with the Rent Administrator.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 1336, 1365-66 (March
   7, 1986).
