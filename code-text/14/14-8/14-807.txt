                                  DC REGULATIONS

RADIO AND TELEVISION ANTENNAS (§ 14-807)

807.1. The owner or tenant of any premises shall not permit the horizontal
   members of radio and television antennas on the premises to be less than eight
   feet (8&#8242;) in the clear above the roof.

   807.2. No parts of any radio or television antenna shall be attached to or
   strung across any fire escape or ladder.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 1208,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
