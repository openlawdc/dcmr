                                  DC REGULATIONS

SCREENING (§ 14-806)

806.1. The owner or licensee of each residential building shall provide
   screens for all openings to the external air from March 15th through November
   15th (both dates inclusive) of each year; provided, that effective means other
   than screens may be substituted for screens when specifically approved by the
   Director.

   806.2. Screens shall have a minimum of sixteen (16) meshes to the inch or the
   equivalent effectiveness of sixteen (16) meshes to the inch.

   806.3. Screens shall be maintained to prevent effectively the entrance of
   flies and mosquitoes into the building.

   806.4. All hinged screen doors shall open outwardly and be self-closing.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 2608,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955), as amended by
   Commissioners’ Order 60-529 (March 15, 1960).
