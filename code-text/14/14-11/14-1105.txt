                                  DC REGULATIONS

ROOM KEYS (§ 14-1105)

1105.1. The entrance door to each rooming unit shall be provided with a lock.

   1105.2. A key for each rooming unit shall be furnished to each respective
   roomer.

   1105.3. A duplicate key or keys shall be retained by the proprietor or
   manager.

   1105.4. The proprietor or manager shall have access to all rooms at all
   reasonable hours.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 4106,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
