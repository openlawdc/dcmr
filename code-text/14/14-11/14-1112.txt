                                  DC REGULATIONS

REFRIGERATION (§ 14-1112)

1112.1. All perishable food or drink shall be kept at or below forty-five
   degrees Fahrenheit (45° F.), except when being prepared or served.

   1112.2. Waste water from refrigeration equipment shall discharge into an open
   sink or drain properly trapped and sewer connected; provided, that if sewer
   connections are not available, clean, adequate, and water-tight drip pans
   shall be provided.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 4213,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
