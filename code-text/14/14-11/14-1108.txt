                                  DC REGULATIONS

LIGHTING (§ 14-1108)

1108.1. All rooms in which food or drink is stored, prepared, or served, or in
   which utensils are washed, shall be provided with adequate natural or
   artificial lighting.

   1108.2. In rooms in which food or drink is prepared or served, or in which
   utensils are washed, adequate natural or artificial lighting shall be provided
   which is sufficient to produce an intensity of not less than fifteen (15) foot
   candles at thirty inches (30&#8243;) from the floor.

   1108.3. In rooms in which food or drink is stored, adequate natural or
   artificial lighting shall be provided which is sufficient to produce an
   intensity of not less than four (4) foot candles, at thirty inches (30&#8243;)
   from the floor.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 4204,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
