                                  DC REGULATIONS

POISONS (§ 14-1114)

1114.1. All preparations used for the extermination of vermin, such as sodium
   fluoride, shall be conspicuously colored and kept in containers clearly
   labeled “POISON.”

   1114.2. Containers of poison shall not be placed with receptacles containing
   spices or condiments or other food substances.

   1114.3. No article, polish, or other substance containing any cyanide
   preparation or other poisonous material shall be used for the cleansing or
   polishing of eating or cooking utensils.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR §§
   4110 and 4218, Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
