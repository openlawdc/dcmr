                                  DC REGULATIONS

LOCKER OR DRESSING ROOM FOR EMPLOYEES (§ 14-1118)

1118.1. Adequate lockers or dressing rooms shall be provided for the clothing
   of male and female employees.

   1118.2. Soiled linens, coats, and aprons shall be kept in vermin-proof
   containers provided for that purpose.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 4217,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
