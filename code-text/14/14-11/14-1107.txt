                                  DC REGULATIONS

VENTILATION (§ 14-1107)

1107.1. All rooms in which food or drink is prepared, or in which food or
   drink is served, or in which utensils are washed, shall be reasonably
   ventilated.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 4205,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
