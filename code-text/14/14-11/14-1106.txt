                                  DC REGULATIONS

FLOORS, WALLS, AND CEILINGS (§ 14-1106)

1106.1. The floors of all rooms in which food or drink is stored, prepared, or
   served shall be constructed to be easily cleaned, shall be smooth, shall be
   kept clean, and shall be maintained in a safe and sanitary condition.

   1106.2. Walls and ceilings of all rooms in which food or drink is stored,
   prepared or served, shall be kept clean, and shall be maintained in a safe and
   sanitary condition.

   1106.3. The floors of all rooms in which utensils are washed or stored shall
   be constructed to be easily cleaned, shall be smooth, shall be kept clean, and
   shall be maintained in a safe and sanitary condition.

   1106.4. Walls and ceilings of all rooms in which utensils are washed or stored
   shall be kept clean, and shall be maintained in a safe and sanitary condition.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR §§
   4202 and 4203, Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
