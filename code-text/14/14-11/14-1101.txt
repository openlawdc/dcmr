                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESIDENT MANAGER (§ 14-1101)

1101.1. If the licensee does not reside in person on the premises and does not
   superintend in person the operation or conduct of the boarding house, the
   licensee shall designate a manager or other responsible person for the
   premises.

   1101.2. The designated manager or other person shall reside on the premises,
   shall superintend in person the operation or conduct of the boarding house,
   and shall have complete charge of the premises.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 4103,
   Commissioners’ Order 55-1503 (August 11, 1955).
