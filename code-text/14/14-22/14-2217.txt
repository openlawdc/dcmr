                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESPONSIBILITIES OF INCLUSIONARY UNIT OWNERS (ยง 14-2217)

Annually on the anniversary of the closing date for an Inclusionary Unit, the
Owner of a For Sale Inclusionary Unit shall submit to the Department of Housing
and Community Development certification that it continues to occupy the unit as
its principal residence.  The certification shall be submitted on or with such
form as may be prescribed by the Department of Housing and Community
Development.



	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 3907 (May 15, 2009); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 9295, 9316 (December 11, 2009).
