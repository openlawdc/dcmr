                                  DC REGULATIONS

ACCESS TO LAND TRUST RECORDS (§ 14-3516)

The land trust shall provide the Mayor’s delegee, upon request, with access to
any and all information and records within the possession or control of the land
trust related to the land trust’s operation and administration of the land
trust program.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 7931 (July 25, 2008).
