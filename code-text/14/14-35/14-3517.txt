                                  DC REGULATIONS

REPORT ON LAND TRUST PROGRAM (§ 14-3517)

3517.1. No later than sixty (60) days after the conclusion of the land trust
   program or three years and sixty (60) days after the effective date of the
   Act, whichever occurs first, the Mayor’s delegee shall submit to the Council
   a report on the land trust program. The report shall include recommendations
   for a permanent workforce housing program.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 7931 (July 25, 2008).
