                                  DC REGULATIONS

MINIMUM RENT (ยง 14-6210)

6210.1. Based on information provided pursuant to Sections 6118, 6119, and
   6200 the rent charged shall be the lesser of:
   
   
   
   (a)	A fixed amount determined by DCHA for the Development (&#8220;market-based
   rent&#8221;) pursuant to Subsection 6200.1(b);
   
   
   
   (b)	An amount based on a percentage of household income pursuant to Subsection
   6200.1(a); or
   
   
   
   (c)	$0, for families which DCHA has determined do not have any adjusted
   income, as defined in Subsection 6099.1, as determined by DCHA at
   certification or recertification.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 9814 (October 26,
   2001); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 012309 (December 21,
   2007).
