                                  DC REGULATIONS

RETROACTIVE RENT (§ 14-6208)

6208.1. Retroactive rent charges, determined in accordance with § 6114 of
   this subtitle, shall be due in full within thirty (30) days of notification.

   6208.2. Partial payments of amounts due may be authorized by DCHA, if it is
   determined that the tenant’s failure to promptly report the change(s) in
   income, which resulted in the retroactive rent, was not willful.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 7973, 8011 (December
   26, 1986).
