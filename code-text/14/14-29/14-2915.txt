                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROGRAM MONITORING &#8211; INSPECTIONS (ยง 14-2915)

2915.1. The Administrator shall make or cause to be made those inspections
   which are necessary to determine compliance with Program requirements.

   2915.2. The Administrator shall take those additional actions deemed necessary
   to assess compliance with the provisions of the abatement agreement including,
   but not limited to, the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Reviewing the records of the Office of Tax and Revenue;
   
   
   
   (b)	Requesting verification of required insurance coverage; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Reviewing the records of the D.C. Recorder of Deeds.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 4947, 4960 (July 31,
   1987).
