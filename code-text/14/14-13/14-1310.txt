                                  DC REGULATIONS

CEILING HEIGHT IN HIGH DENSITY USE ROOMS (§ 14-1310)

1310.1. A high density occupancy room shall have a ceiling height of eight
   feet (8&#8242;) or more throughout the room, except as otherwise provided in
   this section.

   1310.2. Under beams, pipes, ducts, and other construction projections from the
   ceiling, there shall be a minimum clear head room of six feet, eight inches
   (6&#8217;8&#8243;) in each high density occupancy room.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 7302,
   Commissioners’ Order 53-1503 (August 11, 1955), as amended by
   Commissioners’ Order 63-1354 (June 6, 1963), 9 DCR 276 (June 24, 1963).
