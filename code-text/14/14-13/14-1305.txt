                                  DC REGULATIONS

POSTING OF PERMISSIBLE OCCUPANCY AND RATES (§ 14-1305)

1305.1. The operator of each hotel or motel shall post in a conspicuous place
   within each rooming unit used for the accommodation of transients a card
   stating the maximum number of occupants permitted in that room under these
   regulations.

   1305.2. The operator of each hotel or motel shall post in a conspicuous place
   within each rooming unit used for the accommodation of transients a card
   stating the maximum rates charged for that rooming unit under varying
   conditions of occupancy.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR §§
   7204-7205, Commissioners’ Order 53-1503 (August 11, 1955); as amended by
   Commissioners’ Order 63-1354 (June 6, 1963), 9 DCR 276 (June 24, 1963).
