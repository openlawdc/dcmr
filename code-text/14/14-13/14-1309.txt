                                  DC REGULATIONS

LIGHTING AND VENTILATION IN HIGH DENSITY USE ROOMS (§ 14-1309)

1309.1. Each room used for high density occupancy shall have at least
   twenty-five percent (25%) more natural light than is required by § 502 of
   this subtitle.

   1309.2. Each room used for high density occupancy shall have at least
   twenty-five percent (25%) more ventilation than is required by § 506 of this
   subtitle.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 7302,
   Commissioners’ Order 53-1503 (August 11, 1955).
