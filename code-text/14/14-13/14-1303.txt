                                  DC REGULATIONS

ROOM KEYS (§ 14-1303)

1303.1. The entrance door to each rooming unit shall be provided with a lock.

   1303.2. A key to the unit shall be furnished to the respective occupant.

   1303.3. A duplicate key or keys shall be retained by the licensee or manager
   who shall have access to all rooms at all reasonable hours.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 7203,
   Commissioners’ Order 53-1503 (August 11, 1955).
