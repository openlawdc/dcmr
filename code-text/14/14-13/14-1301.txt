                                  DC REGULATIONS

HOTEL AND MOTEL MANAGERS (§ 14-1301)

1301.1. If the licensee does not superintend in person the operation or
   conduct of the hotel or motel, the licensee shall designate a manager or other
   responsible person who shall superintend in person the operation or conduct of
   the hotel or motel.

   1301.2. The designated manager or other responsible person shall have complete
   charge of the operation and conduct of the hotel or motel.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 7201,
   Commissioners’ Order 53-1503 (August 11, 1955); as amended by
   Commissioners’ Order 63-1354 (June 6, 1963), 9 DCR 276 (June 24, 1963).
