                                  DC REGULATIONS

REGISTRATION OF OCCUPANTS (§ 14-1302)

1302.1. Each person licensed to conduct a hotel or motel shall at all times
   keep a register, which shall contain the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	The name of each person occupying a rooming unit in the hotel or motel;
   and
   
   
   
   (b)	The date of arrival and date of departure of each occupant.

   1302.2. Each room shall be numbered and the number of the room assigned to
   each occupant shall be indicated in the register.

   1302.3. The register shall be available for inspection by officials of the
   District of Columbia.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: The Housing Regulations of the District of Columbia, 5G DCRR § 7202,
   Commissioners’ Order 53-1503 (August 11, 1955); as amended by
   Commissioners’ Order 63-1354 (June 6, 1963), 9 DCR 276 (June 24, 1963).
