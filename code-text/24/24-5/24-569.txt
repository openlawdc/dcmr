                                  DC REGULATIONS

REQUIREMENT TO UPDATE INFORMATION (ยง 24-569)

569.1. If, pursuant to the provisions of this chapter, a vendor provided
   information to a District agency and that information changes, the vendor
   shall, within ten (10) business days after the change in information, provide
   updated information to the appropriate agency.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13055 (September 20, 2013).
