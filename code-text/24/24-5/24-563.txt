                                  DC REGULATIONS

MOTOR VEHICLE REGISTRATION AND INSPECTION OF VENDING VEHICLES AND CARTS (§
24-563)

563.1. All vending vehicles and vending carts:
   
   
   
   (a)	Shall be registered and inspected by the District of Columbia Department
   	of Motor Vehicles or by the motor vehicle department of another state or
   	municipality with appropriate jurisdiction;
   
   
   
   (b)	Shall display all current tags on the vehicle or cart; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Shall not vend any food, merchandise, or services if the vehicle has
   	temporary tags.

   563.2. Any vending vehicle not displaying current vehicle registration shall
   be subject to removal and shall subject the licensee to summary suspension of
   their Vending Business License and Vending Site Permit. The vendor’s
   vehicle, Vending Business License and Vending Site Permit shall be returned to
   the licensee upon the proper display of current vehicle registration.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13055 (September 20, 2013).
