                                  DC REGULATIONS

HEALTH INSPECTION CERTIFICATE[COLON] APPLICATION (ยง 24-518)

518.1. A person shall apply for a health inspection certificate with the
   information and documents required by, and pursuant to the procedures of, the
   DOH Director. The DOH Director may require that a vendor submit to DOH the
   business address of the supplier of all prepared foods that the person intends
   to vend as required in Chapter 37, Subtitle A (Food and Food Operations) of
   Title 25 of the DCMR.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 7787 (December 2, 1994); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13055 (September 20, 2013).
