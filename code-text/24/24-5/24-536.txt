VENDING LOCATIONS[COLON] MOBILE ROADWAY VENDING LOCATIONS[COLON] APPLICATION FOR
NEW LOCATIONS (ยง 24-536)

536.1. A person may submit a request to the DCRA Director to create a new
   designated MRV location, provided that the person specifies a location that is
   not in conflict with the above standards.  All requests are subject to DCRA
   and DDOT review and approval.
