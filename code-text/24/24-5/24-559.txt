                                  DC REGULATIONS

INSPECTIONS[COLON] DESIGN STANDARDS (§ 24-559)

559.1. A vendor’s vending vehicle, vending stand, or vending cart shall be
   subject to inspection at any time by the DCRA Director to verify compliance
   with applicable design standards required by this chapter.

   559.2. Failure to comply with design standards for vending vehicles, vending
   stands, or vending carts may be cause for the issuance of a civil infraction
   or the summary suspension or revocation of a Vending Site Permit.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13055 (September 20, 2013).
