                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISPLAY OF SAMPLE ITEMS (ยง 24-566)

566.1. No vendor shall display any object (including signage) used as an
   example of merchandise or food for sale or to advertise merchandise, services,
   or food for sale unless the object conforms to the merchandise or food being
   sold by the vendor at that particular Vending Location.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13055 (September 20, 2013).
