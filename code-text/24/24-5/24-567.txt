                                  DC REGULATIONS

PURCHASE OR RECEIPT OF STOCK (ยง 24-567)

567.1. No vendor shall purchase any inventory stock from public space.

   567.2. No vendor shall take delivery of any inventory stock in public space,
   with the exception of perishable food items for consumption and ice.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13055 (September 20, 2013).
