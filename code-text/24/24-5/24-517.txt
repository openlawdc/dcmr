                                  DC REGULATIONS

HEALTH INSPECTION CERTIFICATE[COLON] GENERAL (ยง 24-517)

517.1. No person shall vend food in the District unless DOH has issued a valid
   health inspection certificate for the vending vehicle, cart, or stand, or
   public market from which the food is vended.

   517.2. A health inspection certificate shall be valid for six (6) months or
   until the time of the next inspection, whichever is earlier.

   517.3. A person shall not be issued a Class A or Class C Vending Business
   License, or a Vending Site Permit for a Class A or Class C Vending Business
   License, until the person receives a health inspection certificate from DOH.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 32 DCR 1347 (March 8, 1985); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13055 (September 20, 2013).
