                                  DC REGULATIONS

SPECIAL EVENTS (§ 24-571)

571.1. Persons who are authorized by the organizer of a licensed Special Event
   to vend within the boundaries of a licensed Special Event may vend without a
   Vending Business License or Vending Site Permit.

   571.2. A vendor authorized to vend within the boundaries of a licensed Special
   Event area shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding the
   registration of vendor’s business with the Office of Tax and Revenue and
   DCRA and shall comply with any standards imposed by DOH or other relevant
   agencies.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13055 (September 20, 2013).
