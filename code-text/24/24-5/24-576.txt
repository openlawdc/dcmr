                                  DC REGULATIONS

SEVERABILITY (ยง 24-576)

576.1. If any provision of this chapter, or the application of any provision
   of this chapter, is held invalid in any circumstance, the validity of the
   remainder of the provisions of this chapter, and the application of any
   provision in any other circumstance, shall not be affected; and to this end,
   the provisions of this chapter shall be severable.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13055 (September 20, 2013).
