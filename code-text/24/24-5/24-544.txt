                                  DC REGULATIONS

DESIGN STANDARDS[COLON] GENERAL (§ 24-544)

544.1. The DCRA Director may develop design standards for vending vehicles,
   vending stands, and vending carts in addition to those set forth in this
   chapter.

   544.2. The provisions of §§ 545, 546, 547, 548, 549, 553, 554, and 559 shall
   not apply to activities licensed and permitted under § 503.1(c).
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13055 (September 20, 2013).
