                                  DC REGULATIONS

PENALTIES (§ 24-618)

618.1. Each licensee shall be liable for all penalties provided for violation
   of any of the provisions of this chapter, whether the violations are committed
   by the licensee or the licensee’s agent or employee.

   618.2. Pursuant to D.C. Official Code § 47-2846, any person violating any
   provision of this chapter shall, upon conviction, be fined not more than three
   hundred dollars ($300) or imprisoned for not more than thirty (30) days, or
   both.

   618.3. Civil fines, penalties, and fees may be imposed as alternative
   sanctions for any infraction of this regulation pursuant to titles I-III of
   the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs Civil Infractions Act of
   1985. Adjudication of any infraction of this regulation shall be pursuant to
   titles I-III of the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs Civil
   Infractions Act of 1985.
