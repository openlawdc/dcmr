                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSFER AND MODIFICATION OF LICENSES (ยง 24-615)

615.1. Upon any change in the ownership, management, or mailing address of a
   licensed valet parking business, the licensee shall immediately notify the
   Director of that fact.

   615.2. The new owner, resident general agent, or attorney, as the case may be,
   shall be responsible for the conduct of the business after a change in
   ownership or management and shall, in a timely manner, file a new application
   with the Director.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 7303 (September 4, 2009).
