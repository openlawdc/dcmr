                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSFER AND MODIFICATION OF LICENSES (ยง 24-604)

604.1 	Upon any change in the ownership, management, or mailing address of the
licensed business, the parking facility licensee shall immediately notify the
Director of that fact.

604.2 	The new owner, resident general agent, or attorney, as the case may be,
shall be responsible for the conduct of the business after a change in ownership
or management and shall, in a timely manner, file a new application with the
Director.
