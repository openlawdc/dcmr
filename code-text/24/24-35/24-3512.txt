                                  DC REGULATIONS

REVIEW OF INFRACTIONS (§ 24-3512)

3512.1. Notices of infractions for violations of this section shall be issued,
   answered, and adjudicated pursuant to the Department of Consumer and
   Regulatory Affairs Civil Infraction Act of 1985, effective October 5, 1985
   (D.C. Law 6-42; D.C. Official Code §§ 2-1801.01, et seq.(2007 Repl.)) and
   the provisions of Chapter 31 of Title 16 of the District of Columbia Municipal
   Regulations.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 5323, 5336 (June 24,
   2011).
