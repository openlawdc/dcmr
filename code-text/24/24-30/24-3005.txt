                                  DC REGULATIONS

DECOMPRESSION (ยง 24-3005)

3005.1. Divers shall use the latest available U.S. Navy Standard Air
   Decompression Tables and other appropriate U.S. Navy diving tables for
   decompressions and repetitive dive decompressions.

   3005.2. A recompression chamber that meets the requirements of the most recent
   edition of the U.S. Navy Diving Manual shall be kept at the diving site
   whenever planned dives will exceed the &#8220;no decompression&#8221; limits
   as specified by the U.S. Navy Standard Air Decompression Tables.

   3005.3. Whenever a recompression chamber is required, a qualified person shall
   be immediately available to operate the chamber.

   3005.4. An adequate approved method of supplying air shall be readily
   available for the immediate operation of the chamber.
