                                  DC REGULATIONS

SALT AND SALT WATER (ยง 24-1004)

1004.1. No person shall dump, pour, or spill any salt or salt water or other
   deleterious matter upon any tree or tree space in any public place, street,
   avenue, roadway, or other highway.

   1004.2. No person shall keep or maintain upon any sidewalk within ten feet (10
   ft.) of any tree or tree space any receptacle from which salt water leaks or
   drips; and no person shall pour salt water into any parking or unconcreted
   gutter so as to injure any shade tree occupying public space.
