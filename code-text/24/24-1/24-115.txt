                                  DC REGULATIONS

SKATEBOARDS, ROLLERSKATES, AND ROLLERBLADES (§ 24-115)

115.1. Any person using skateboards, rollerskates, roller blades, or similar
   devices upon the parks and plazas located in the Pennsylvania Avenue
   Development area shall be subject to the rules under 18 DCMR §§1211.3
   through 1211.10.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Section 2 of the Pennsy lvania Avenue Development Area Parks and
   Plaza Public Safety Amendment Act of 1995, D.C. Law 11-19, 42 DCR 1843 (April
   21, 1995).
