                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRAINING (ยง 24-2709)

2709.1. MPD shall require all members assigned to the Intelligence Section,
   Intelligence Fusion Division, to attend training on the Police Investigations
   Concerning First Amendment Act of 2004 and the rules promulgated to implement
   the Act.

   2709.2. MPD shall require all members assigned to the Intelligence Section,
   Intelligence Fusion Division, to sign an acknowledgement that they have
   received, read, understood, will abide by, and will maintain a copy of this
   Act and the rules promulgated to implement it.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking Published at 55 DCR 1091 (February 1, 2008); as
   amended by Errata Published at 55 DCR 3271 (March 28, 2008).
