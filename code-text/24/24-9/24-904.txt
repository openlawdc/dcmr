                                  DC REGULATIONS

BEES (ยง 24-904)

904.1. No bees or hives of bees shall be permitted to be kept when there are
   human habitations within a radius of five hundred feet (500 ft.).

   904.2. The provisions of this section shall not apply to bees confined in
   hives, or to bees kept on property that is enclosed so that the bees cannot
   stray from the property.
