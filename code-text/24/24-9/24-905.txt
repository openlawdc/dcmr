WILD ANIMALS (ยง 24-905)

905.1. No person shall keep or display any captured wild animal (such as a
   bear, monkey, fox, or reptile) on any public space under the supervision of
   the Mayor, without first obtaining a permit to do so.

   905.2. Any person desiring to keep or display any wild animal shall make
   application to the Mayor or the Mayor&#8217;s designated agent on a form to be
   provided for that purpose, stating the place of the proposed display and the
   duration of the display.
