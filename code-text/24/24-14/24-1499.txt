                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 24-1499)

1499.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Demolition &#8211; the total removal of the existing structure.
   
   
   
   Discontinuance of an existing building &#8211; disallowance of further use of
   the building, and all services to the building are terminated without
   necessarily demolishing the building.
   
   
   
   Property associated with the street and alley closing &#8211; property that is
   or will be included in a development or developments requiring the closing of
   the street or alley.
   
   
   
   Retail tenant &#8211; a nonresidential tenant offering goods or
   nonprofessional services.
   
   
   
   Substantial rehabilitation &#8211; the alteration of a building wherein
   existing tenants would be required to move.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 2695, 2696a (April 24, 1987).
