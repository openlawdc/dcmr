                                  DC REGULATIONS

SPEECHES AND PUBLIC ADDRESSES (§ 24-700)

700.1. No person shall be permitted to make or deliver any address, speech, or
   sermon upon any subject whatever in or upon any street, avenue, alley,
   footway, highway, or other public space in the District of Columbia without
   first obtaining a permit in writing from the Chief of Police.

   700.2. The permit shall designate the time and precise location where the
   address, speech, or sermon may be given.
