                                  DC REGULATIONS

SCHEDULE OF FINES (ยง 24-3704)

3704.1. Any person or non-governmental entity that violates any provision of
   the Act, this chapter, or any condition of a Special Tree Removal Permit shall
   be subject to a civil infraction fine of one hundred dollars ($100) per inch
   of circumference of the tree or trees in question.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 52 DCR 546 (January 21, 2005).
