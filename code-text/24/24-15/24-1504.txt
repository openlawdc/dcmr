The President or his or her designee may, at any time before or after the
hearing, schedule a conference with the respondent and/or his or her
representatives, either in person or by phone, to attempt to reach an agreement
on conditions for the return of the animal to the owner.  The conference shall
not delay the scheduling and holding of the hearing.







	The President or his or her designee shall grant a formal hearing to any owner
or an animal seized under D.C. Official Code ยง 22-1004(b)(1) who complies with
the provisions of ยง 1502, unless:







The President dismisses or denies the request for a hearing pursuant to ยง 1503;







The President and the respondent agree on conditions for the return of the
animal to the owner prior to the formal hearing; or







The respondent withdraws the request for a formal hearing.
