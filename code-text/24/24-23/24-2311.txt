KNOWLEDGE OF FIREARMS AND TRAINING REQUIREMENTS (ยง 24-2311)

2311.1 	Knowledge of the laws of the District pertaining to firearms, and
knowledge of the safe and responsible use of firearms, shall be tested through a
written examination.

2311.2 	Under compelling circumstances, an oral test may be administered in
place of the written test.

2311.3 	The type of test and its content shall be at the sole discretion of the
Director.

2311.4 	[RESERVED].

2311.5 	[RESERVED].

2311.6 	Rifles and shotguns shall be considered the same type of firearm for the
purposes of testing.

2311.7 	If an applicant fails an examination, he or she shall be allowed one (1)
retest without charge.

2311.8 	A fee equal to that submitted with the original application may, at the
discretion of the Director, be assessed for the second retest and for each
subsequent retest.

2311.9 	An applicant shall complete a firearms training and safety class
provided by the Chief or submit evidence of compliance with ยง 203(a)(13)(B) of
the Act.
