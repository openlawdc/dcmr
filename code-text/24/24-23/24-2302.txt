SALE OR TRANSFER OF WEAPONS (ยง 24-2302)

2302.1. It shall unlawful for any person, firm, or corporation to sell, give,
   lend, rent, or otherwise transfer to any child under the age of eighteen (18)
   years any firearms, air rifle, air gun, air pistol, B-B gun, spring gun,
   blowgun, bowgun, or a similar type of gun; or ammunition, gunpowder, gun cap,
   cartridge primer, dynamite cap or detonator, B-B shot, pellet, projectile,
   dart, or other dangerous missile or explosive, in any quantity.

   2302.2. Nothing in this section shall be construed as prohibiting the sale or
   other transfer of toy paper caps containing not more the twenty-five
   hundredths (.25) of a grain of explosive composition per cap.

   2302.3. Nothing in this section shall be construed as prohibiting the transfer
   of any of the items listed in ยง2302.1 where the transfer is supervised by a
   person eighteen (18) or more years of age in connection with the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	A theatrical performance or athletic contest;
   
   
   
   (b)	A licensed shooting gallery; or
   
   
   
   (c)	Transfer at other locations where the use of the item is authorized by the
   Chief of Police.
