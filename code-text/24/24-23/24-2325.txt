                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRE-1985 PISTOLS (§ 24-2325)

2325.1 	Any pistol with a single action firing mechanism manufactured prior to
1985 shall be exempt from the application of § 504 of the Act (D.C. Official
Code § 7-2505.04 (2012 Repl.)).



2325.2 	Any pistol manufactured prior to 1985, not subject to § 2325.1, shall
be deemed included on the District Roster established pursuant to § 2323.
