                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY (ยง 24-1100)

1100.1. This chapter shall apply to any person engaged in redevelopment or
   substantial rehabilitation, including sidewalk rehabilitation, where more than
   fifty percent (50%) of the sidewalk area adjoining a project is planned for
   construction, within the Downtown Streetscape Area.
   
   
   
   	Source: Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 7117 (September 1, 2000).
