                                  DC REGULATIONS

MODIFICATION OF A PREVIOUSLY APPROVED STREETSCAPE PLAN (ยง 24-1104)

1104.1. The Director may approve a modification of a previously approved
   streetscape plan when the following conditions are fulfilled:
   
   
   
   (a)	Repair of a streetscape element is necessary or when construction
   conditions alter the situation that produced the original streetscape plan;
   
   
   
   (b)	Adhering to the approved streetscape plan will impose an undue hardship
   for the applicant; and
   
   
   
   (c)	A viable alternative and modified streetscape plan is proposed.

   1104.2. Modifications to approved streetscape plans shall be submitted
   pursuant to sections 1102 and 1103.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 7117 (September 1, 2000).
