LICENSURE AND RENEWAL FEES (ยง 17-8310)

8310.1. The fees related to pharmaceutical detailers are as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	Initial license fee $175.00
   
   
   
   (b)	Biennial renewal fee $165.00
   
   
   
   (c)	Late fee $85.00
   
   
   
   (d)	Duplicate certificate $34.00
   
   
   
   (e)	License verification $34.00
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 9317 (August 29, 2008).
