                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 17-7600)

7600.1. This chapter shall apply to any person who applies for or holds a
   license to practice respiratory therapy in the District of Columbia
   (&#8220;District&#8221;).

   7600.2. The provisions of this chapter shall be supplemented by the provisions
   of Chapter 35 of this title, entitled &#8220;Occupational and Professional
   License Fee Schedule&#8221;, Chapter 40 of this title, entitled &#8220;Health
   Occupations: General Rules&#8221;, and Chapter 41 of this title, entitled
   &#8220;Health Occupations: Administrative Procedures&#8221;.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 7282 (September
   17, 1999).
