                                  DC REGULATIONS

TERM OF LICENSE (ยง 17-7601)

7601.1. A license issued pursuant to this chapter shall be effective for not
   more than two years and shall expire at 12:00 midnight on January 31st of each
   odd-numbered year. The Director of the Department of Health may establish a
   different term and expiration date, by rule.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 7282 (September
   17, 1999).
