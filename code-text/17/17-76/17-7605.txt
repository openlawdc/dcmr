                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSURE BY ENDORSEMENT (ยง 17-7605)

7605.1. The Board shall issue a license to practice respiratory therapy, by
   endorsement, to an Applicant who:
   
   
   
   (a)	Is currently licensed or certified by another state;
   
   
   
   (b)	Is in good standing under the laws of another state with standards that
   the Board determines to be comparable to the requirements of the Act and this
   chapter; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Pays the applicable fee.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 7282 (September
   17, 1999).
