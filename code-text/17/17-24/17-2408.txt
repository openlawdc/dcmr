CERTIFICATION OF RECORDS (ยง 17-2408)

2408.1. The Mayor shall issue certifications of the signatures of District of
   Columbia governmental officials who are required to sign documents of public
   records.

   2408.2. The fee charged for each certification issued under this section shall
   be $15.00.
