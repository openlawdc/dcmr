TRANSCRIPTS OF HEARINGS (ยง 17-2417)

2417.1. In all hearings conducted by the Board, a complete record shall be
   made of all evidence presented during the course of a hearing.

   2417.2. Under the supervision of the presiding officer, each hearing shall be
   reported stenographically, or by other means, by an official reporter who may
   be a regular employee of the Board.

   2417.3. Copies of the transcript will be available to parties and to the
   public from the official reporter upon payment of the charges fixed for
   copies.
