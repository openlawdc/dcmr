                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECONSIDERATION AND REINSTATEMENT OF LICENSES (ยง 17-2421)

2421.1. A person whose application has been denied or whose commission has
   been revoked by the Board may, by filing a new application accompanied by the
   proper fee, request the Board to reconsider the matter.

   2421.2. Upon showing of cause satisfactory to it, the Board may direct the
   Secretary of the District of Columbia to issue a commission in the usual
   manner.
