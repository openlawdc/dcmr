                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMMISSION FEES (ยง 17-2409)

2409.1. Each notary public, before obtaining his or her commission, and for
   each renewal of his or her commission, shall pay to the District of Columbia
   Treasurer a license fee of seventy-five ($ 75).
