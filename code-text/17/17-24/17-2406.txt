                                  DC REGULATIONS

REAPPOINTMENTS (ยง 17-2406)

2406.1. When applying for reappointment, a notary public who is entitled under
   a commission and this chapter to charge fees shall submit a summary of his or
   her notary business for the three (3) months preceding the date of application
   for reappointment.

   2406.2. The summary of business shall be based on actual records, not
   estimates.
