                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADMINISTRATION OF MEDICATION IN EMERGENCY OR LIFE-THREATENING CIRCUMSTANCES (ยง
17-6111)

6111.1. An anaphylaxis emergency treatment kit, epipen, AnaKit or equivalent
   injection system of epinephrine may be administered by a trained medication
   employee as prescribed by a licensed practitioner pursuant to the
   program&#8217;s protocol or procedures.

   6111.2. The trained medication employee shall be trained by the supervisory
   registered nurse in the use of the program participant&#8217;s anaphylaxis
   emergency kit prior to administering any treatment.
