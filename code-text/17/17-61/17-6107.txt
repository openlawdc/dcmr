                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECERTIFICATION (ยง 17-6107)

6107.1. Recertification of a trained medication employee shall be required
   every two (2) years, and shall include verification of a Board-approved twelve
   (12) hours of in- service training in pharmacology or medication
   administration and the supervisory registered nurse&#8217;s verification of
   the trained medication employee&#8217;s continued adequacy of performance.

   6107.2. Recertification applications shall be submitted to the Board before
   the expiration of the current certification and shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Be signed by the trained medication employee;
   
   
   
   (b)	Include the supervisory registered nurse&#8217;s written verification of
   the trained medication employee&#8217;s continued adequacy of performance;
   
   
   
   (c)	Include documentation verifying successful completion of twelve (12) hours
   of board approved in-service training; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Be accompanied by the required registration fee.

   6107.3. The Board shall recertify an applicant upon receiving a complete
   recertification application, proof of the applicant&#8217;s continued
   competence, and the appropriate registration fee.
