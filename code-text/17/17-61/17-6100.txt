                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 17-6100)

6100.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a
   certificate as a trained medication employee, employed to work in a program,
   who administer medications to persons with mental retardation or other
   disabilities, and sets forth requirements for:
   
   
   
   (a)	The administration of medication to persons with mental retardation or
   other developmental disabilities;
   
   
   
   (b)	The certification of trained medication employees;
   
   
   
   (c)	The certification of medication administration trainers and master
   trainers; and
   
   
   
   (d)	The employment and supervision of trained medication employees.
