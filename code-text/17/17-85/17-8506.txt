                                  DC REGULATIONS

BOARD OF REVIEW (ยง 17-8506)

8506.1. Results of a criminal background check shall be forwarded to the Board
   regulating the health profession in which the applicant desires to be
   licensed, registered, or certified.  The Board shall utilize the information
   contained in the criminal background check, along with other information
   supplied in the application, to make a determination as to whether the
   applicant shall be licensed, registered, or certified.
   
   	Source: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 6025, 6028 (July 31, 2009).
