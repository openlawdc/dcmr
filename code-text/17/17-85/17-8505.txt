                                  DC REGULATIONS

ILLEGIBLE FINGERPRINT CARDS (§ 17-8505)

8505.1. If an applicant’s fingerprint card is rejected two (2) or more times
   by the Metropolitan Police Department because the prints are unreadable, an
   applicant shall come to the District of Columbia to have the Metropolitan
   Police Department conduct a fingerprint scan.  The applicant shall be
   responsible for paying all costs associated with the scan.
   
   	Source: Licensed Health Professional Criminal Background Check Amendment Act
   of 2006, effective March 6, 2007 (D.C. Law 16-222, D.C. Official Code §
   3-1205.22 et seq.); Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 6025, 6028 (July 31,
   2009).
