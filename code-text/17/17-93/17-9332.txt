                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESOURCES, FACILITIES, AND SERVICES (§ 17-9332)

9332.1. Each HHA training program shall maintain resources, facilities, and
   services which are adequate to accommodate the training program.  The
   resources, facilities, and services shall include, but are not limited to:
   
   
   
   (a)	Adequate temperature controls in each training facility;
   
   
   
   (b)	Clean and safe conditions of the facility’s premises;
   
   
   
   (c)	Adequate space to accommodate faculty;
   
   
   
   (d)	 Adequate lighting in each facility; and
   
   
   
   (e) 	Sufficient and adequate equipment for the program’s needs, including
   audio-visual equipment and equipment needed for simulating client care.

   9332.2. Each classroom, conference room, laboratory, and office shall be
   adequate to meet the needs of the HHA training program.

   9332.3. Each cooperative agreement between an HHA training program and an
   agency shall be in writing. The training program shall maintain a copy of the
   agreement in its records.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8359, 8384 (July 13, 2012).
