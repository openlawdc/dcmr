                                  DC REGULATIONS

RENEWAL OF CERTIFICATION (ยง 17-9307)

9307.1. An applicant for renewal shall have:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Completed at least twenty-four (24) hours of continuing education or
   in-service training in the area of health or nursing needs of an assigned
   client population during the certification period;
   
   
   
    (b)	Practiced a minimum of one hundred (100) hours during the prior
   twenty-four (24) months as an HHA under the supervision of a licensed nurse or
   other licensed health professional; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Completed a CBC as required.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8359, 8366 (July 13, 2012).
