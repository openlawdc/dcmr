                                  DC REGULATIONS

CERTIFICATION BY ENDORSEMENT (§ 17-9306)

9306.1 	An applicant for endorsement as an HHA shall provide proof of the
following:







Current certification as an HHA or similar title and duties; or







The applicant’s ability to perform skills listed in § 9327.2 (letter from
employer certifying ability).





SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8359, 8366 (July 13, 2012).
