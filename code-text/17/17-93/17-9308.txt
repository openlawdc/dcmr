INACTIVE STATUS; REACTIVATION OF CERTIFICATION (ยง 17-9308)

9308.1. An HHA with an active certification may request to be placed on
   inactive status.

   9308.2. While on inactive status, the individual shall not be subject to the
   renewal fee and shall not practice as an HHA in the District of Columbia.

   9308.3. To reactivate within five (5) years of an inactive status the
   individual shall submit evidence of having completed twenty-four (24) hours of
   continuing education or in-service training within the last twenty-four (24)
   months.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8359, 8366 (July 13, 2012).
