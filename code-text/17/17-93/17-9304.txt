CERTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS; APPLICATION FOR CERTIFICATION (ยง 17-9304)

9304.1. Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, a person applying for
   certification as a home health aide shall:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Be at least eighteen (18) years of age;
   
   
   
   (b)	Submit evidence of having successfully completed an approved home health
   aide program;
   
   
   
   (c)	Pass a Board approved certification examination;
   
   
   
   (d)	Complete a CBC; and
   
   
   
   		(e)	Submit an application and application fee.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A certified nursing assistant (CNA) applying for certification as an HHA
   shall:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Provide evidence of having successfully completed the training requirements
   specific to the role of an HHA as specified in ยง 9327;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Complete a CBC; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Submit an application and application fee.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8359, 8364 (July 13, 2012).
