                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISPLAY OF LICENSES (ยง 17-3007)

3007.1. A license as a funeral director or apprentice funeral director shall
   be conspicuously displayed in the funeral services establishment at which the
   licensee is employed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 2904, 2911 (April 22, 1988).
