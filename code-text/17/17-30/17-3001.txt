                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS FOR APPLICANTS (ยง 17-3001)

3000.1. An applicant for a license under this chapter, in addition to
   complying with chapter 33 of this title, shall prove to the satisfaction of
   the Board that the applicant is at least eighteen (18) years of age.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 2904 (April 22, 1988).
