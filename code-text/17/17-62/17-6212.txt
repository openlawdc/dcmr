                                  DC REGULATIONS

CODE OF PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT (§ 17-6212)

6212.1. A licensee licensed under this chapter, an Administrator-in-Training
   Program (A.I.T.), or a student or graduate authorized to practice nursing home
   administration pursuant to 17 DCMR § 6211 shall adhere to the standards set
   forth in the “Code of Ethics” as published by the American College of
   Health Care Administrators.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 10630 (November 19, 2004); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13385 (September 27, 2013).
