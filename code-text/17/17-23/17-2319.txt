                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPRAISER RECORDKEEPING (ยง 17-2319)

2319.1 	A licensee shall retain records of appraisal, analysis, and review
assignments, including oral testimony, in the following manner:

(a) 	For a period of at least five (5) years after preparation of the report
generated by the activity; or

(b) 	If a report was the subject of or used for litigation purposes, and
testimony was given, the report shall be kept for two (2) years after final
disposition of any judicial proceeding in which testimony was given.

2319.2 	A licensee shall, upon demand or request by the Board, promptly produce
any document, book, or record concerning any appraisal that the licensee or
certificate holder performed.

	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 8480 (December 10, 1993); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 5846 (July 21, 2006); as amended
by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 10590, 10599 (December 16,
2011).
