                                  DC REGULATIONS

REQUIRED NOTIFICATIONS (ยง 17-2315)

2315.1 	A licensee shall notify the Board in writing within thirty (30) days of
any name change or change of business address or residence address.

2315.2 	A licensee shall inform the Board in writing within thirty (30) days of
pleading guilty or nolo contendere, or being convicted or found guilty of any
felony or of any misdemeanor.

2315.3 	A licensee shall inform the Board in writing within thirty (30) days of
the suspension, revocation, or surrender of his or her appraiser license or
certificate held in any other jurisdiction.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 8480 (December 10, 1993); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 5846 (July 21, 2006); as amended
by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 10590, 10599 (December 16,
2011).
