                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPRAISAL EDUCATION FUND ASSESSMENT (ยง 17-2321)

2321.1. An applicant for a license shall pay, in addition to the applicable
   license fee, the sum of one hundred dollars ($100.00) into the Fund.

   2321.2. Upon renewal of a license the licensee shall pay, in addition to the
   applicable renewal fee, the sum of one hundred dollars ($100.00) into the
   Fund.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 8480 (December 10, 1993); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 5846 (July 21, 2006).
