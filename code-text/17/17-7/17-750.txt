                                  DC REGULATIONS

LIMITED COOPERATIVE ASSOCIATIONS[COLON] PAYMENT OF A FOR-PROFIT FILING FEE (ยง
17-750)

750.1   	If a limited cooperative association does not specify a nonprofit
purpose in the articles of organization filed with the Superintendent, the
limited cooperative association shall pay a for-profit filing fee.
