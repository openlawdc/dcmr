                                  DC REGULATIONS

FAILURE TO RENEW NAME REGISTRATION (ยง 17-707)

707.1. If a foreign filing entity or foreign limited liability partnership
   fails to renew the registration of its name within one (1) year of the date of
   registration:
   
   
   
   (a)  	The name registration shall expire; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The name shall be available for use by another entity on the next business day
   after the expiration date.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13139 (September 20, 2013).
