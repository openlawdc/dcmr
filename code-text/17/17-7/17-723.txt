TERMINATION OF REGISTRATION FOR DOMESTIC ENTITIES (ยง 17-723)

723.1. A domestic filing entity whose registration is terminated and seeks
   reinstatement in the District shall:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Comply with the same rules found in Chapter 1 (General Provisions) of the Act
   (D.C. Official Code ยง 29-101.01 et seq. (2011 Repl.)) governing a domestic
   filing entity that is dissolved administratively and seeking reinstatement;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   File with the Superintendent all reports which would have been due to the
   
   	Superintendent while the entity was dissolved administratively; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Pay all corporate filing fees which would have been due to the Superintendent
   while the entity was dissolved administratively.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13139 (September 20, 2013).
