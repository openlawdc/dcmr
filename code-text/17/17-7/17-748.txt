                                  DC REGULATIONS

LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES[COLON] AMENDMENT OR RESTATEMENT OF CERTIFICATE OF
ORGANIZATION (ยง 17-748)

748.1   	A limited liability company shall not use the restated or amended
certificate of organization to amend:







The registered agent on record;







The name of organizers; or







The address of organizers.
