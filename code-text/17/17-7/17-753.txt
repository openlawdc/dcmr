                                  DC REGULATIONS

STATUTORY TRUSTS[COLON] USE OF AMENDMENT, ARTICLES OF CONVERSION OR ARTICLES OF
MERGER (ยง 17-753)

753.1   	A statutory trust shall not use an amendment, articles of conversion,
or articles of merger to amend:







The registered agent on record;







The name of the initial trustee; or







The address of the initial trustee.









SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13139 (September 20, 2013).
