                                  DC REGULATIONS

DESIGNATION OF REGISTERED AGENT BY NONREGISTERED FOREIGN ENTITY OR NONFILING
DOMESTIC ENTITY (§ 17-715)

715.1. In addition to the requirements of Chapter 1 (General Provisions) of
   the Act (D.C. Official Code § 29-101.01 et seq. (2011 Repl.)), a
   nonregistered foreign entity or non-filing domestic entity filing a statement
   designating a registered agent shall include the entity’s principal address.
