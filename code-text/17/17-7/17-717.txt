                                  DC REGULATIONS

FOREIGN REGISTRATION STATEMENT (§ 17-717)

717.1 	In addition to the requirements of Chapter 1 (General Provisions) of the
Act (D.C. Official Code § 29-101.01 et seq. (2011 Repl.)), a foreign entity or
foreign limited liability partnership filing a statement of foreign registration
shall include in the statement:







The signature of the entity’s governor or other individual authorized to sign
on behalf of the governor; and







The signing individual’s name and capacity to sign.











SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13139 (September 20, 2013).
