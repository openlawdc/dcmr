                                  DC REGULATIONS

ENTITIES REQUIRED TO DESIGNATE AND MAINTAIN A REGISTERED AGENT (ยง 17-711)

711.1. The following types of entities shall designate and maintain a
   registered agent in the District:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A domestic filing entity;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A domestic limited liability partnership; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A registered foreign entity.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13139 (September 20, 2013).
