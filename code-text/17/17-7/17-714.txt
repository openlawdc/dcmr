CHANGE OF NAME, ADDRESS, TYPE OF ENTITY, OR JURISDICTION OF FORMATION BY
COMMERCIAL REGISTERED AGENT (ยง 17-714)

714.1. Upon filing a commercial registered agent statement of change, the
   Superintendent shall charge the commercial registered agent a fee to change
   the commercial registered agent information for each entity represented by the
   commercial registered agent.
