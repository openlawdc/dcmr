                                  DC REGULATIONS

NONPROFIT CORPORATIONS[COLON] AMENDING ARTICLES OF AMENDMENT (ยง 17-740)

740.1. A nonprofit corporation shall not use the articles of amendment to
   amend:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The registered agent on record;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The name of incorporators; or
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The address of incorporators.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13139 (September 20, 2013).
