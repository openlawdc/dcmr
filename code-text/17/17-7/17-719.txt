                                  DC REGULATIONS

WITHDRAWAL OF REGISTRATION OF REGISTERED FOREIGN ENTITY (§ 17-719)

719.1. In addition to the requirements of Chapter 1 (General Provisions) of
   the Act (D.C. Official Code § 29-101.01 et seq. (2011 Repl.)), the foreign
   entity shall:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Be in good standing in the District before filing its statement of withdrawal;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Include the date of its initial foreign registration in the District in its
   statement of withdrawal; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
    Include in the statement of withdrawal, the signature of the entity’s
   governor or other individual authorized to sign on behalf of the governor, and
   the signing individual’s name and capacity to sign.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13139 (September 20, 2013).
