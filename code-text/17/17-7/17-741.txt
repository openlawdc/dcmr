                                  DC REGULATIONS

NONPROFIT CORPORATIONS[COLON] AMENDING RESTATED ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION (§
17-741)

741.1   	A nonprofit corporation shall not use the restated articles of
   incorporation to amend:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The registered agent on record;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The name of incorporators; or
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The address of incorporators.

   741.2. A nonprofit corporation restating its articles of incorporation may
   identify the filing as a “restated articles of incorporation” or
   “restated and amended articles of incorporation” instead of an articles of
   amendment as stated in D.C. Official Code § 29-408.07(c) (2011 Repl.).
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 13139 (September 20, 2013).
