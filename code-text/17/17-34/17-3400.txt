                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY (ยง 17-3400)

3400.1 	This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a license to
practice architecture.



3400.2 	Chapter 33 of this Title shall supplement this chapter.



3400.3 	The provisions of this chapter and the Act shall prevail in the event of
a direct and irreconcilable conflict between this chapter or the Act and Chapter
33 of this Title.













SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 7266 (Dec. 29, 1995); Final
