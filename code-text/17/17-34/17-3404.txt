                                  DC REGULATIONS

WAIVER OF THE EDUCATION AND IDP REQUIREMENTS (ยง 17-3404)

3404.1 	The Board may exempt an applicant from the education and IDP
requirements when the applicant holds a current and valid certification issued
by NCARB, and the applicant submits to the Board satisfactory evidence of such
certification.
