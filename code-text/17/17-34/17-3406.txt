                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISPLAY OF LICENSE (§ 17-3406)

3406.1. A licensee shall display his or her license conspicuously in the
   licensee’s principal place of business or employment.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 7266, 7271 (Dec. 29,1995); Final
   Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 5454, 5459 (May 9, 2008).
