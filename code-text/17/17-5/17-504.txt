HOUSING[COLON] RESIDENTIAL ENDORSEMENT (ยง 17-504)

504.1. The Director shall charge fees for business license categories with a
   Housing: Residential endorsement as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	Apartment house:
   
   
   
   (1)	With three to five units: $167;
   
   
   
   (2)	With six to 25 units: $208;
   
   
   
   (3)	With 26 or more units: $208, plus $130 for each additional 25 units or
   fraction thereof;
   
   
   
   (b)	One-family rental: $35; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Two-family rental: $63.

   504.2. Each license in this section shall be charged a per unit Rental
   Accommodations Division fee, pursuant to D.C. Official Code ยง 42-3504.01.
