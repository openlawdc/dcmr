                                  DC REGULATIONS

EMPLOYMENT SERVICES ENDORSEMENT (ยง 17-513)

513.1. The Director shall charge fees for business license categories with an
   Employment Services endorsement as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	Employment agency: $1,300;
   
   
   
   (b)	Employment paid personnel service: $1,300; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Employment counseling: $1,300.

   published at 57 DCR 8792 	(September 24, 2010)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice
   of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 12191, 	12199 (December 24, 2010).
