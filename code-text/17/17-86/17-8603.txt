                                  DC REGULATIONS

INTERVIEW (ยง 17-8603)

8603.1. The Board may require an applicant for registration to practice as a
   psychology associate to be interviewed with respect to his or her past and
   present education and experience in relation to psychology to determine
   whether the applicant meets the requirements of the Act and this chapter.
