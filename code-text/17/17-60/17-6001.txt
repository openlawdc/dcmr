                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL REQUIREMENT (ยง 17-6001)

6001.1. Only a person currently licensed as a registered nurse under chapter
   54 of this title shall be eligible to apply for a certificate to practice as a
   clinical nurse specialist under this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 11771 (December 27, 2002).
