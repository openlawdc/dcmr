APPLICABILITY (ยง 17-5000)

5000.1. This chapter applies to applicants for and holders of a registration
   to practice naturopathy.

   5000.2. Sections 4001, 4104, 4005, 4006, 4012 and 4013 of Chapter 40
   supplement this chapter.
