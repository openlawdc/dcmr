HEARINGS AND APPEAL (ยง 17-3805)

3805.1. Any licensee on whom a notice has been served pursuant to ยง 3804 may
   file a written notice of appeal with the Office of Administrative Hearings
   (OAH).

   3805.2. All hearings and appeals shall be conducted pursuant to the
