                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTINUING EDUCATION CREDITS (ยง 17-8712)

8712.1 	The Board may grant continuing education credit for whole hours only,
with a minimum of fifty (50) minutes constituting one (1) credit hour. 

8712.2 	The Board may grant a maximum of five (5) hours of continuing education
credits per year to an applicant for attending in-service education programs.







	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 11937, 11949 (December
17, 2010).
