                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 17-8700)

8700.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a
   certification to practice as a certified addiction counselor I or II,
   positions established, respectively, by sections 851 and 852 of the Act (D.C.
   Official Code §§ 3-1208.51 and
   
   	3-1208.52).
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	Chapters 40 (Health Occupations: General Rules) and 41 (Health Occupations:
   				Administrative Procedures) of this title shall supplement this chapter.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 11937, 11938 (December
   17, 2010).
