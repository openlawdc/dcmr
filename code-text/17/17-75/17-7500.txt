                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 17-7500)

7500.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a license to
   practice massage therapy.

   7500.2. Chapters 40 (Health Occupations: General Rules) and 41 (Health
   Occupations: Administrative Procedures) of this title shall supplement this
   chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 7716 (October 30, 1998).
