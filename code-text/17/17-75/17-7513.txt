                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADVERTISEMENT (ยง 17-7513)

7513.1. A massage therapist licensed under the provisions of this chapter
   shall include the number of his or her license in any advertisement of massage
   services appearing in any newspaper, airwave transmission, telephone directory
   or other advertising medium.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 7716 (October 30, 1998); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 2048 (May 7, 2003).
