                                  DC REGULATIONS

CARDIAC PULMONARY RESUSCITATION AND FIRST AID REQUIREMENTS (ยง 17-7512)

7512.1. This section shall apply to renewal, reactivation, or reinstatement of
   a license for a term beginning February 1, 2009, and for subsequent terms
   thereafter.

   7512.2. An applicant for renewal, reactivation, or reinstatement of a license
   shall submit to the Board with the renewal, reactivation, or reinstatement
   application copies of certificates indicating CPR and first-aid certification
   valid at the date of renewal, reactivation, or reinstatement. Such
   certification shall not be used to satisfy continuing education requirements.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 7716 (October 30, 1998); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 8933 (September 14, 2007).
