                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSE REQUIREMENTS (ยง 17-1601)

1601.1. A separate license shall be required for each type of employment
   service that is provided.

   1601.2. Each license to operate an employment agency, employment counseling
   service, employer-paid personnel service, or a job listing service shall be
   issued for a period of one (1) year, beginning November 1st of each year and
   ending October 31st.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 3165 (May 15, 1987).
