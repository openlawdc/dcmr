                                  DC REGULATIONS

FAILURE TO REQUEST A HEARING (§ 17-1612)

1612.1. If a person served with a notice of proposed action fails to request a
   hearing within the time and in the manner specified in §§1611.1 and 1611.2,
   the Director may, without a hearing, take the action proposed in the notice.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 3172 (May 15, 1987).
