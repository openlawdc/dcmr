                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSE FEE (ยง 17-1607)

1607.1. The annual license fee for an employment agency, employment counseling
   service, employer-paid personnel service, or job listing service shall be five
   hundred dollars ($500).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 3165, 3168 (May 15, 1987).
