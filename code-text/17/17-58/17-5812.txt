                                  DC REGULATIONS

USE OF TITLE OR ABBREVIATIONS (ยง 17-5812)

5812.1. Only persons certified, as certified nurse-midwives by the Board shall
   be designated as such and have the right to use the title &#8220;Certified
   Nurse-Midwife&#8221; (CNM) and the title or abbreviations designated by the
   Board or an approved national certifying body.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 3497, 3504 (May 12, 1989); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 11751 (December 27, 2002).
