                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL REQUIREMENT (ยง 17-5801)

5801.1. Only a person currently licensed as a registered nurse under chapter
   54 of this title shall be eligible to apply for a certificate to practice
   nurse-midwifery under this chapter.
