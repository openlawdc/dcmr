                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRADE NAME FILING FEES (ยง 17-612)

612.1. The following fees shall be charged for trade name filings:
   
   
   
   (a) 	Trade name registration application: fifty-five dollars ($55);
   
   
   
   (b) 	Trade name renewal: fifty-five dollars ($55);
   
   
   
   (c) 	Trade name amendment application: fifty-five dollars ($55); and
   
   
   
   (d) 	Trade name cancellation: fifty-five dollars ($55).
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8165, 8183 (July 6, 2012).
