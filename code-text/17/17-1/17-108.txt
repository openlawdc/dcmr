                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSCRIPTS OF PROCEEDINGS (ยง 17-108)

108.1. In all hearings conducted under this chapter, a complete record shall
   be made of all evidence presented during the course of a hearing.

   108.2. Any party to the proceedings desiring it shall be furnished with a copy
   of the record, upon payment of the fee prescribed by the Mayor.
