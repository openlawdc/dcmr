                                  DC REGULATIONS

SEVERABILITY OF PROVISIONS (ยง 17-113)

113.1. If any part of this title is declared invalid, or the applicability of
   any provision of this chapter to any person or circumstance is held invalid,
   the validity of the remainder of the provisions of this title and their
   applicability to other persons and circumstances shall not be affected.
