RECONSIDERATION OR REINSTATEMENT (ยง 17-112)

112.1. A person whose application for a license or renewal of a license has
   been denied or whose license has been cancelled, suspended, or revoked may, by
   filing a new application accompanied by the proper fee, request the board or
   commission to reconsider the matter.

   112.2. Upon showing of cause satisfactory to it, a board or commission may
   issue the license or renewal of license.
