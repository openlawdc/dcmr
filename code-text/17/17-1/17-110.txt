                                  DC REGULATIONS

REOPENING PROCEEDINGS (ยง 17-110)

110.1. If, because of accident, sickness, or other good cause, a person fails
   to receive a hearing or fails to appear for a hearing which he or she has
   requested, the person may, within thirty (30) days from the date of the
   decision of the board or commission apply to the board or commission to reopen
   the proceedings.

   110.2. The board or commission, upon finding sufficient cause, shall fix
   immediately a time and place for a hearing and give the person, the
   Corporation Counsel, and the Director notice of the hearing (ยง103.2).

   110.3. The board or commission may also reopen a proceeding for any other
   cause sufficient to it, provided no appeal is pending before a court or has
   been decided by a court.
