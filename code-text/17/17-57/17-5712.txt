                                  DC REGULATIONS

USES OF TITLE OR ABBREVIATIONS (ยง 17-5712)

5712.1. Only persons certified as registered nurse-anesthetists by the Board
   shall be designated as such and have the right to use the title
   &#8220;Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetist&#8221; or &#8220;CRNA&#8221; or
   any other title or abbreviation designated by the Board or the approved
   national certifying body. No other person shall use any other title, words,
   letters, signs, or figures to indicate that the person using the name is
   recognized as a certified registered nurse-anesthetist.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 3491, 3498 (May 12, 1989); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 11751 (December 27, 2002).
