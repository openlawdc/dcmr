                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL REQUIREMENT (ยง 17-5701)

5701.1. Only a person currently licensed as a registered nurse under chapter
   54 of this title shall be eligible to apply for a license to practice
   nurse-anesthesia under this chapter.
