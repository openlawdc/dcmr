                                  DC REGULATIONS

OPPORTUNITY FOR A HEARING (ยง 17-2710)

2710.1. Every applicant for or holder of a license, or applicant for
   reinstatement after revocation or suspension, shall be afforded notice and an
   opportunity to be heard prior to the action of the Board, pursuant to D.C.
   Official Code ยง 47-2853.22(a), the effect of which would be as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	To deny a license for any cause other than failure to qualify;
   
   
   
   (b)	To suspend a license;
   
   
   
   (c)	To revoke a license;
   
   
   
   (d)	To restore a license;
   
   
   
   (e)	To refuse to issue renewals by annual registration for any cause other
   than failure to pay the prescribed fees;
   
   
   
   (f)	To impose a civil fine or other penalty;
   
   
   
   (g)	To require a course of remediation or period of probation; or
   
   
   
   (h)	To deny an application for reinstatement.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 52 DCR 6998 (July 29, 2005).
