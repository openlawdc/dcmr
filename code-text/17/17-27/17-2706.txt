                                  DC REGULATIONS

REAL ESTATE GUARANTY AND EDUCATION FUND RESTORATION OF LICENSE (ยง 17-2706)

2706.1. A licensee whose license has been suspended pending payment in full of
   an amount ordered paid from the Fund shall not be eligible to have his or her
   license reinstated until he or she has paid the full amount ordered paid from
   the Fund plus six percent (6%) interest per annum.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 52 DCR 6998 (July 29, 2005).
