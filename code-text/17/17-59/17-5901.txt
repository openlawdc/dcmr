                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL REQUIREMENT (ยง 17-5901)

5901.1. Only a person currently licensed, as a registered nurse under Chapter
   54 of this title shall be eligible to apply for a license to practice as a
   nurse-practitioner under this chapter.
