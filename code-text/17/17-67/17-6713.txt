                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESTRICTED LICENSES (§ 17-6713)

6713.1. Pursuant to § 514 of the Act (D.C. Official Code § 3-1205.14) the
   Board may restrict the license of any person as a condition of probation
   pursuant to § 514(c)(7) of the Act (D.C. Official Code § 3-1205.14(c)(7)).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 945, 953 (February 12, 1988); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 6451 (June 25, 2004).
