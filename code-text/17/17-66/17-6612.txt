                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTINUING COMPETENCY COURSES (ยง 17-6612)

6612.1. The Board may, in its discretion, approve courses that contribute to
   the competency of a professional counselor in one or more of the following
   areas:
   
   
   
   (a)	Ethics (standards of practice or laws governing behavioral science
   professions);
   
   
   
   (b)	Counseling theory;
   
   
   
   (c)	Human growth and development;
   
   
   
   (d)	Social and cultural foundations;
   
   
   
   (e)	The helping relationship;
   
   
   
   (f)	Group dynamics, processing and counseling;
   
   
   
   (g)	Lifestyle and career development;
   
   
   
   (h)	Appraisal of individuals;
   
   
   
   (i)	Research and evaluation;
   
   
   
   (j)	Professional orientation;
   
   
   
   (k)	Clinical supervision;
   
   
   
   (l)	Marriage and family therapy;
   
   
   
   (m)	Addictions; or
   
   
   
   (n)	Trauma.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 6145 (May 30, 2008).
