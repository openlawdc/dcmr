                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 17-7900)

7900.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a license to
   practice speech-language pathology.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Chapter 40 (Health Occupations: General Rules) and 41 (Health Occupations:
   Administrative Procedures) of this title shall supplement this chapter.
