                                  DC REGULATIONS

WAIVER OF CLINICAL FELLOWSHIP REQUIREMENTS (ยง 17-7911)

7911.1. The Board may waive the clinical fellowship requirements for
   applicants who:
   
   
   
   	(1) Provide proof of employment in the practice of speech language pathology
   for at least ten (10) of the last fifteen (15) years immediately preceding the
   effective date of these regulations;
   
   
   
   	(2) Meet all other requirements of these regulations; and
   
   
   
   	(3) Apply by December 31, 2009.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 7314 (September 4, 2009).
