                                  DC REGULATIONS

HEARING NOTICE PROCEDURES (ยง 17-2933)

2933.1. If a respondent requests a hearing, the Director shall, within twenty
   (20) days following receipt of the request, notify the respondent of the date,
   time, and place of the hearing.

   2933.2. The Director shall hold the hearing not less than fifteen (15) days
   following the date of service of the notice, unless the Director and all of
   the parties agree to the holding of the hearing at an earlier date.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 6630, 6648 (September 2, 1988).
