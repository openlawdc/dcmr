                                  DC REGULATIONS

TERM OF LICENSE (ยง 17-2905)

2905.1. The term of a license issued under this chapter shall begin on the
   date of issuance.

   2905.2. A license issued or renewed under this chapter shall be effective for
   one (1) year.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 6630, 6632 (September 2, 1988).
