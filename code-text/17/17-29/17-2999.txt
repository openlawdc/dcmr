                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (§ 17-2999)

2999.1. As used in this chapter, the following terms have the meanings
   ascribed:
   
   
   
   Act &#8211; the District of Columbia Board of Veterinary Practice Act of 1982,
   D.C. Law 4-171, D.C. Code §§2-2701 to 2-2737 (1988 Repl. Vol.).
   
   
   
   Animal facility &#8211; fixed or mobile establishment, veterinary hospital,
   animal hospital, or premises wherein the practice of veterinary medicine or
   any part thereof is performed, including a mobile clinic.
   
   
   
   Board &#8211; the District of Columbia Board of Veterinary Examiners for the
   District of Columbia, established by the Act.
   
   
   
   Director &#8211; the Director of the Department of Consumer and Regulatory
   Affairs, or the Director&#8217;s designee.
   
   
   
   License &#8211; a license to operate an animal facility under this chapter.
   
   
   
   Licensee &#8211; a person holding a license.
   
   
   
   Mobile clinic &#8211; an ambulatory facility used in the practice of
   veterinary medicine.
   
   
   
   Veterinarian &#8211; a person licensed to practice veterinary medicine under
   the Act and accordance with chapter 28 of Title 17 DCMR
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 6630, 6656 (September 2, 1988).
