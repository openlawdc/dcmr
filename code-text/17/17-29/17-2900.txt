APPLICABILITY (ยง 17-2900)

2900.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a license to
   operate an animal facility.

   2900.2. Chapter 28 (Veterinarians) of this title shall supplement this
   chapter.

   2900.3. All animal facilities in the District shall meet requirements set
   forth in this chapter by March 2, 1989.
