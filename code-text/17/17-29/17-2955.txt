                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPUTATION OF TIME (ยง 17-2955)

2955.1. In computing any period of time specified in this chapter, the day of
   the act, event, or default shall not be counted, and the last day of the
   period shall be counted unless it is not a business day, in which event the
   time period shall continue until the next day that is a business day.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 6630, 6656 (September 2, 1988).
