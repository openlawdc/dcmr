                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY (ยง 17-7100)

7100.1. This chapter applies to applicants for and holders of a registration
   to practice dance therapy.

   7100.2. Chapter 40 (General Rules) of this title supplements this chapter

   7100.3. Chapter 41 (Administrative Procedures) of this title shall also
   supplement this chapter, provided that the term &#8220;board&#8221; shall in
   all instances refer to the Director, and the director shall possess all powers
   and assume all functions, assigned to a &#8220;board&#8221; under these
   administrative procedures, as well as the powers and functions retained by the
   Director.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 6010 (September 14, 1990).
