                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSURE BY ENDORSEMENT (ยง 17-8205)

8205.1. The Board shall issue a license by endorsement to a physical therapist
   assistant who has a valid, unrestricted license in good standing from another
   jurisdiction of the United States and who meets all other requirements of this
   section.

   8205.2. An applicant for licensure by endorsement as a physical therapist
   assistant shall submit with a completed application the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Official, certified proof of licensure as a physical therapist assistant
   in good standing from another jurisdiction of the United States;
   
   
   
   (b)	Official, certified proof, from the body administering the examination,
   that the applicant has passed the Board-approved national licensure
   examination for a physical therapist assistant; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Proof pursuant to ยง 8202.1.

   8205.3. An applicant shall take and pass the District of Columbia
   jurisprudence examination for physical therapist assistants after obtaining
   Board approval.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 8802 (August 15, 2008).
