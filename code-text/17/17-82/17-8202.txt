                                  DC REGULATIONS

EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS (ยง 17-8202)

8202.1. Except as otherwise provided in this subtitle, an applicant applying
   for a license to practice as a physical therapist assistant shall establish to
   the satisfaction of the Board that the applicant has successfully completed an
   educational program in physical therapy appropriate for preparation as a
   physical therapist assistant, which is accredited by an agency recognized by
   the Secretary of the Department of Education or the Council of Postsecondary
   Accreditation.

   8202.2. An applicant shall submit with a completed application an official
   certified transcript of the applicant&#8217;s educational record and a
   certificate of graduation from the educational institution.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 8802 (August 15, 2008).
