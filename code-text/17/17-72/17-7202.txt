                                  DC REGULATIONS

EDUCATION, TRAINING, AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS FOR RECREATION THERAPISTS (ยง
17-7202)

7202.1. An applicant for registration to practice as a recreation therapist
   may meet the education, training, and experience requirements by furnishing to
   the Director satisfactory proof that the applicant has been certified by the
   National Council for Therapeutic Recreation Certification (NCTRC) at the
   professional level of Certified Therapeutic Recreation Specialist.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 5953 (August 19, 1994); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 8931 (September 14, 2007).
