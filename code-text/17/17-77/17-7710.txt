                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTINUING EDUCATION CREDITS (ยง 17-7710)

7710.1. Professional research and writing conducted pursuant to section 7708.2
   (e) or (f) shall account for a total of no more than ten (10) of the thirty
   (30) continuing education units required.
