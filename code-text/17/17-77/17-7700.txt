                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 17-7700)

7700.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a license to
   practice marriage and family therapy.

   7700.2. Chapters 40 (Health Occupations: General Rules) and 41 (Health
   Occupations: Administrative Procedures) of this title shall supplement this
   chapter.
