                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSURE BY ENDORSEMENT (ยง 17-7711)

7711.1. The Board shall issue a license to a marriage and family therapist who
   has a valid unrestricted license from another jurisdiction of the United
   States or Canada if:
   
   
   
   (a)	That person, when granted the license, met all requirements contained in
   section 7702.1 through section 7704.4 and any applicable Board rules; or
   
   
   
   (b)	The requirements of the other jurisdiction are, at the time of the
   application, substantially equivalent to the requirements of this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 7845 (September
   29, 2006); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 8247 (August 24,
   2007).
