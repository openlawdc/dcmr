5202.1. An applicant shall furnish proof satisfactory to the Board in
   accordance with ยง 504(e-1)(1) of the Act, (D.C. Official Code ยง
   3-1205.04(e-1)(1) (2001)), that the applicant has:
   
   
   
   (a)	Earned a degree of doctor of naturopathic medicine from a college or
   university which at the time of the awarding of the degree was accredited by
   or a candidate for accreditation with:
   
   
   
   (1)	The Council of Naturopathic Medicine Education (CNME), so long as the CNME
   maintains recognition from the United States Department of Education; or
   
   
   
   (2)	Any other accrediting agency recognized by the United States Department of
   Education.

   5202.2. The Board of Medicine shall not waive the educational requirements for
   licensure to practice naturopathic medicine for person registered to practice
   naturopathy or naturopathic healing.
