                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPUTATION OF TIME (ยง 17-4124)

4124.1. In computing any period of time specified in this chapter, the day of
   the act, event, or default shall not be counted, and the last day of the
   period shall be counted unless it is a Saturday, Sunday, legal holiday, or day
   on which the Department is officially closed, in which event the time period
   shall continue until the next day that is not a Saturday, Sunday, legal
   holiday, or day on which the Department is officially closed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 5872, 5897 (September 11, 1987).
