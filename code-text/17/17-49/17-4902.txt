                                  DC REGULATIONS

EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS (ยง 17-4902)

An applicant shall furnish proof satisfactory to the Board that the applicant
has successfully completed an educational program to practice as a physician
assistant accredited by the Committee on Allied Health Education and
Accreditation (CAHEA) or its successors by submitting to the Board, with a
completed application, a certified transcript and an official statement
verifying graduation from an educational program.



SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 8263, 8264 (December 8, 1989); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 2747, 2751 (May 4, 1990); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 4201 (March 22, 2013).
