4699.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Applicant &#8211; a person applying for a license to practice medicine under
   this chapter.

   Education (ACCME) or the American Osteopathic Association (AOA).
   
   
   
   Chronic pain &#8211; a pain state that is persistent.
   
   
   
   Licensed physician &#8211; a physician licensed under the Act.

   Physician &#8211; a person holding a degree in medicine or osteopathy.
   
   
   
   Postgraduate physician &#8211; a person who holds a degree in medicine or
   osteopathy who is enrolled in a postgraduate clinical training program prior
   to licensure in any jurisdiction in the United States.
   
   
   
   Substance abuse &#8211; the use of any substance for non-therapeutic purposes
   or use of medication for purposes other than those for which it is prescribed.
