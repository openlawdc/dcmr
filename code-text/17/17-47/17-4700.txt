                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 17-4700)

4700.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a license to
   practice acupuncture and to physicians collaborating with acupuncturists.

   4700.2. Chapters 40 (Health Occupations: General Rules), 41 (Health
   Occupations: Administrative Procedures), and 46 (Medicine) of this title shall
   supplement this chapter.

   4700.3. An applicant for a license under this chapter shall submit with a
   completed application three (3) letters of reference from physicians
   experienced in the practice of acupuncture or acupuncturists licensed in the
   United States, who have personal knowledge of the applicant&#8217;s abilities
   and qualifications to practice acupuncture.

   4700.4. The Board shall maintain a registry of licensed acupuncturists and
   shall make the registry available to the public for inspection.
