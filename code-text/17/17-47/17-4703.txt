                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICANTS EDUCATED IN FOREIGN COUNTRIES (ยง 17-4703)

4703.1. The Board may grant a license to practice acupuncture to an applicant
   who has completed an educational program in a foreign country, who
   demonstrates to the satisfaction of the Board the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	That the program is substantially equivalent to one of the programs
   required by this chapter and the Act; and
   
   
   
   (b)	That the applicant otherwise meets all the requirements of this chapter
   and the Act.

   4703.2. An applicant under this section who does not speak English as a native
   language shall be required &#8216;to receive a passing score on an English
   competency test approved by the Board.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 7159, 7162 (October 13, 1989).
