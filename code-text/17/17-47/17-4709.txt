                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSE BY RECIPROCITY (§ 17-4709)

4709.1. An applicant for a license by reciprocity under § 507 of the Act,
   D.C. Code § 2-3305.7 (1988), shall comply with the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	The requirements of § 4700.3 of this chapter; and
   
   
   
   (b)	The requirements of § 4014 of chapter 40 of this title.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 7159, 7163 (October 13, 1989).
