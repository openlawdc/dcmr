                                  DC REGULATIONS

NATIONAL EXAMINATION (ยง 17-4704)

4704.1. To qualify for a license, an applicant shall pass either the
   examination administered by the National Commission for the Certification of
   Acupuncturists (NCCA) (the national examination) or a practical examination
   administered by the Board (the District examination).

   4704.2. An applicant shall submit with a completed application the
   applicant&#8217;s national examination results, certified by the NCCA.

   4704.3. The passing score on the national examination shall be the passing
   score determined by NCCA.

   4704.4. An applicant who fails the national examination six (6) times shall
   not be eligible for a license in the District of Columbia.

   4704.5. An applicant who fails the District examination six (6) times shall
   not be eligible for a license in the District.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 7159, 7162 (October 13, 1989);
   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 2747, 2749 (May 4, 1990).
