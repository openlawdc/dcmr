                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISTRICT EXAMINATION (ยง 17-4705)

4705.1. The District examination shall cover the following areas:
   
   
   
   (a)	Acupuncture point location;
   
   
   
   (b)	Sterilization; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Needling and other therapeutic techniques.

   4705.2. The passing score on the District examination shall be the passing
   score determined by the Board.

   4705.3. An applicant who fails the District examination may take it two (2)
   additional times within a three (3) year period commencing from the date the
   applicant first took the District examination.

   4705.4. An applicant who fails to pass the District examination within the
   three (3) year period commencing from the date the applicant first took the
   District examination shall, in order to be eligible for reexamination,
   complete one hundred (100) hours of additional clinical instruction in the
   practice of acupuncture in a program accredited by an entity listed in ยง
   4702.2(a).

   4705.5. An applicant who fails the District examination six (6) times shall
   not be eligible for a license in the District.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 7159, 7163 (October 13, 1989).
