                                  DC REGULATIONS

REGISTRATION OF COLLABORATING PHYSICIANS AND ACUPUNCTURISTS (ยง 17-4715)

4715.1. An acupuncturist and a physician shall register their collaboration
   with the Board on the prescribed form.

   4715.2. An acupuncturist shall file a separate registration form for each
   physician with whom an acupuncturist collaborates.

   4715.3. The acupuncturist and collaborating physician shall provide the
   following information on the registration form:
   
   
   
   (a)	The name, address, and telephone number of the collaborating physician and
   the licensed acupuncturist;
   
   
   
   (b)	An acknowledgement by the physician that the physician understands and
   accepts the physician&#8217;s duties to the acupuncture patient and the
   acupuncturist as set forth in this chapter;
   
   
   
   (c)	An acknowledgement by the acupuncturist that the acupuncturist understands
   and accepts the acupuncturist&#8217;s duties to the patient and collaborating
   physician as set forth in this chapter; and
   
   
   
   (d)	The signatures of the physician and acupuncturists.

   4715.4. An acupuncturist shall not begin treating a patient until a
   registration form has been filed with the Board.

   4715.5. A collaborating physician and the acupuncturist shall give the Board
   written notice of the termination of an agreement to collaborate within
   fifteen (15) days following the termination of the agreement.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 7159, 7166 (October 13, 1989).
