                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSE FEES (§ 17-221)

221.1. The license fees specified in §3500 shall be separate from, or in
   addition to, another contractor&#8217;s or business license tax which may be
   fixed for this and similar occupations by law or by the Mayor.

   221.2. All fees shall be paid to the D.C. Treasurer before an examination and
   before a license is issued.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: 5J DCRR §§721 and 722.
