                                  DC REGULATIONS

EXPIRATION OF LICENSES (§ 17-205)

205.1. All licenses shall expire and be void after the date of expiration
   specified on the license, except that when the day of expiration of a license
   falls upon a Sunday or a holiday, the license shall be valid until the next
   business day.

   205.2. Business licenses shall run for twelve (12) months from the first of
   the month in which the license becomes effective.

   205.3. Occupational licenses shall run for two (2) years from the first of the
   month in which the license becomes effective.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: 5J DCRR §§405 and 406.
