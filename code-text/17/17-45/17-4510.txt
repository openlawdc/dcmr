                                  DC REGULATIONS

USE OF TITLE REGISTERED DIETITION (ยง 17-4510)

4510.1. An individual licensed under the Act to practice nutrition in the
   District of Columbia shall not use the title dietitian or registered dietitian
   unless the individual is licensed under the Act to practice dietetics in the
   District of Columbia.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 86 (January 6, 2006).
