                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISPLAY OF NAME (ยง 17-6415)

6415.1. An optometrist licensed under the Act shall include the designation
   &#8220;O.D.&#8221;; &#8220;Optometrist&#8221;; or &#8220;Doctor of
   Optometry&#8221; following his or her name in any advertisement, or written
   display of his or her name in the District of Columbia.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12043 (November 21, 2008).
