                                  DC REGULATIONS

EYEGLASS PRESCRIPTION EXPIRATION PERIOD (ยง 17-6416)

6416.1. An eyeglass prescription shall expire one (1) year after the issue
   date unless there is a medical reason that warrants a prescription for less
   than one (1) year. The medical reasons for issuing a prescription for less
   than one year shall be documented in the patient&#8217;s medical record.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12043 (November 21, 2008).
