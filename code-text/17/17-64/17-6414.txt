                                  DC REGULATIONS

STANDARDS OF CONDUCT (ยง 17-6414)

6414.1. Any holder of a license under this Chapter or any person authorized to
   practice optometry under this Chapter shall comply with the standards of
   ethical and professional conduct established by the American Optometric
   Association in its publication entitled &#8220;Code of Ethics,&#8221; as it
   may be amended or republished from time to time.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12043 (November 21, 2008).
