                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 17-8000)

8000.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a license to
   practice as a surgical assistant.

   8000.2. Chapter 40 (Health Occupations: General Rules) and 41 (Health
   Occupations: Administrative Procedures) shall supplement this chapter.
