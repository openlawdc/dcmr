                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSURE REQUIREMENTS (§ 17-8003)

8003.1. An applicant shall furnish proof satisfactory to the Board in
   accordance with § 504 of the Act, D.C. Official Code § 3-1205.04 that the
   applicant has met one of the following requirements:
   
   
   
   (a)	Earned a degree or certification from a surgical assistant program
   accredited by the Commission for the Accreditation of Allied Health
   Educational Programs, or by the commission&#8217;s successor; or
   
   
   
   (b)	Successfully completed a dedicated training program for surgical
   assistants in the armed forces; or
   
   
   
   (c)	Demonstrated to the satisfaction of the Board the completion of fulltime
   work experience performed in the United States under the direct supervision of
   a physician licensed in the United States and consisting of at least 1,300
   hours of performance as a surgical assistant within the 3 years preceding the
   date of the application

   8003.2. Additionally, an applicant shall submit proof satisfactory to the
   Board in accordance with § 504 of the Act, D.C. Official Code § 3-1205.04
   that the applicant was certified as a surgical assistant by at least one of
   the following:
   
   
   
   (1)	The National Surgical Assistant Association; or
   
   
   
   (2)	The American Board of Surgical Assistants.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 9549 (October 5, 2007).
