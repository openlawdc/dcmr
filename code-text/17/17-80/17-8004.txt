                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSITION TO LICENSURE (ยง 17-8004)

8004.1. All references to surgical assistants shall be deemed to refer to
   persons meeting the requirements for licensure in the District, regardless of
   whether they are licensed in fact, until March 6, 2009.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 9549 (October 5, 2007).
