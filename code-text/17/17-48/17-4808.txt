                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTINUING EDUCATION CREDITS (ยง 17-4808)

4808.1. The Board may grant continuing education credit for whole hours only,
   with a minimum of fifty (50) minutes constituting one (1) credit hour.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 6658, 6663 (September 2, 1988).
