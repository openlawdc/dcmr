                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESPONSIBILITY OF SUPERVISING DENTIST (ยง 17-9006)

9006.1. In all instances, the licensed dentist assumes ultimate responsibility
   for determining, on the basis of his or her diagnosis, the specific treatment
   the patient will receive and which aspects of treatment will be delegated to a
   dental assistant in accordance with this chapter and the Act.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 5852, 5856 (July 15,
   2011).
