                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 17-9000)

9000.1. This chapter applies to applicants for and holders of a registration
   to practice as a dental assistant.

   9000.2. Chapters 40 (Health Occupations: General Rules) and 41 (Health
   Occupations Administrative Procedures) of Title 17 of the District of Columbia
   Municipal Regulations supplement this chapter.
