                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (§ 17-9099)

9099.1. For the purposes of this chapter, the following terms shall have the
   meanings ascribed:
   
   Act &#8211; the District of Columbia Health Occupation Revision Act, effective
   March 15, 1986 (D.C. Law 6-99; D.C. Official Code §§ 3-1203.01, et seq.
   (2007 Repl.)).
   
   
   
   Board &#8211; the Board of Dentistry.
   
   
   
   Dental assistant &#8211; a person who is registered by the Board and is
   authorized to assist a licensed dentist in the performance of duties related
   to oral care under the direct supervision of a dentist.
   
   
   
   Direct supervision &#8211; the dentist is in the dental office or treatment
   facility, personally diagnoses the condition to be treated, personally
   authorizes the procedures, remains in the dental office or dental treatment
   facility while the procedures are being performed by the dental assistant, and
   personally evaluates the performance of the dental assistant before dismissal
   of the patient.
   
   
   
   Director – the Director of the Department of Health or the Director’s
   designee.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 5852, 5856 (July 15,
   2011); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 10055 (July 12,
   2013).
