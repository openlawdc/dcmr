                                  DC REGULATIONS

REGISTRATION REQUIRED (ยง 17-9001)

9001.1. No person shall perform or be permitted to perform any duties as a
   dental assistant without a registration issued by the Board.

   9001.2. Notwithstanding Subsection 9001.1, a person who is performing the
   duties of a dental assistant on the effective date of this chapter shall
   obtain a registration no later than September 17, 2012.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 5852, 5853 (July 15,
   2011); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 2401 (March 1,
   2013); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 10055 (July 12,
   2013).
