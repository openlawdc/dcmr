APPLICABILITY (ยง 17-2800)

2800.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a license to
   practice veterinary medicine.

   2800.2. Chapters 29 (Animal Facilities) and 33 (General Rules: Funeral
   Directors, Veterinarians, and Interior Designers) of this title shall
   supplement this chapter.

   2800.3. The provisions of this chapter prevail in the event of a direct and
   irreconcilable conflict between this chapter and chapter 33 of this title.
