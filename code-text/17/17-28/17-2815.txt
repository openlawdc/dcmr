                                  DC REGULATIONS

DIRECT SUPERVISION OF ANESTHESIA (ยง 17-2815)

2815.1. A veterinarian shall administer anesthesia to an animal until the
   animal is stabilized.

   2815.2. The veterinarian shall be present during the maintenance and
   monitoring of anesthesia to an animal by a nurse, veterinary assistant or
   other employee.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 2226, 2237 (March 25, 1988).
