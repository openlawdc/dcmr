                                  DC REGULATIONS

ISSUANCE OF RABIES VACCINATION CERTIFICATES (ยง 17-2812)

2812.1. Rabies vaccination certificates shall be signed personallv by a
   veterinarian licensed in the District of Columbia.

   2812.2. A veterinarian shall not issue a rabies vaccination certificate in
   blank or without being completed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 2226, 2234 (March 25, 1988).
