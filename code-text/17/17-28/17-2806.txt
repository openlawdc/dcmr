                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISPLAY OF LICENSE (ยง 17-2806)

2806.1. A veterinarian shall conspicuously display the license issued under
   this chapter in the clinic or animal facility at which the licensee is
   employed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 2226, 2230 (March 25, 1988).
