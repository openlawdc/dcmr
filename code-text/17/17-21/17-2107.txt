INVESTIGATION OF APPLICANTS (ยง 17-2107)

2107.1. As promptly as possible after an application is submitted, the Mayor
   shall submit fingerprints of the applicant for comparison and record checks to
   the Federal Bureau of Investigation and to other authorities that the Mayor
   may deem advisable.

   2107.2. The Mayor may make any other investigation of the applicant that the
   Mayor determines to be relevant.

   2107.3. The Mayor shall report the results of each investigation to the
   security agency or employer within one (1) week, excluding weekends and
   holidays, after the results have been received.
