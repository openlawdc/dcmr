   2127.6. A security agency that employs five (5) or more individuals as
   security officers shall maintain general liability insurance in an amount not
   less than $250,000 per occurrence and $600,000 in the aggregate, and submit
   proof of the required insurance to the Mayor.

   2127.7. No security agency license may be issued to an applicant, nor may a
   security agency license be renewed unless the applicant or security agency has
   submitted proof that the insurance requirements of this section have been met.
