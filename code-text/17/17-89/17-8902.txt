                                  DC REGULATIONS

TERM OF REGISTRATION (ยง 17-8902)

8902.1. If the trade name registration is related to a person registered with
   a master business license the trade name registration shall have the same
   expiration date as the master business license.

   8902.2. If the trade name registration is related to a person who is exempt
   from the master business license registration requirement, the trade name
   registration shall expire two years from the date of registration.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10075 (November 8,
   2002).
