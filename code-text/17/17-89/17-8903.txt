                                  DC REGULATIONS

RENEWAL (ยง 17-8903)

8903.1. The trade name associated with a master business license may be
   renewed upon renewal of the master business license, or,

   8903.2. The trade name if not associated with a master business license may be
   renewed upon expiration.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10075 (November 8,
   2002).
