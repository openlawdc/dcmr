8913.1. The Department shall provide all forms for registration of trade name,
   which shall be used to make the required filings or amendments and which will
   be available from the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Business License Center of the Department; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Other distribution points, including electronic medium, as the Director
   deems appropriate.
