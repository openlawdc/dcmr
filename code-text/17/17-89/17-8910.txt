                                  DC REGULATIONS

PUBLIC ACCESSIBILITY TO TRADE NAME RECORDS (ยง 17-8910)

8910.1. The Department shall maintain a comprehensive trade name database of
   all registered trade names that shall be available to the public.

   8910.2. The Department shall maintain an index of true and real names
   cross-referenced to trade names and an index of trade names cross-referenced
   to true and real names.

   8910.3. Upon request and the payment of fees as specified in section 8911 of
   this chapter, any person may request a search of the trade name database.

   8910.4. Each person that requests information contained within the trade name
   database shall be provided access to the database to determine any of the
   following information:
   
   
   
   (a)	Person or persons conducting business under a trade name;
   
   
   
   (b)	Registered agent or resident agent;
   
   
   
   (c)	Registered trade names;
   
   
   
   (d)	Firm names;
   
   
   
   (e)	Legal owner names;
   
   
   
   (f)	Verification of business existence;
   
   
   
   (g)	Phone number; and
   
   
   
   (h)	Mailing address

   8910.5. The Director shall provide for the preservation, storage, record
   management, and access to trade name records.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10075 (November 8,
   2002).
