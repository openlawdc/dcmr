                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY (ยง 17-8900)

8900.1. Each person who carries on, conducts or transacts business in the
   District of Columbia under any trade name shall register that trade name with
   the Department.

   8900.2. The Department shall provide to each person a form to register any
   trade name.

   8900.3. Upon receipt of a properly completed application for trade name
   registration and fee payment, the Department shall register the trade name.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10075 (November 8,
   2002).
