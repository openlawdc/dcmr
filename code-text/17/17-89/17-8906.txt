                                  DC REGULATIONS

REGISTRATION WITH OTHER AGENCIES (ยง 17-8906)

8906.1. Trade name registration with other agencies of the District of
   Columbia including the Alcoholic Beverage Regulation Administration or the
   Office of Tax and Revenue does not satisfy the requirement to file with the
   Department.

   8906.2. Trade name registration with the Department may not satisfy the legal
   requirements for registration with the Alcoholic Beverage Regulation
   Administration or any other District agency.

   8906.3. A trade name registration with other agencies of the District of
   Columbia does not preclude any person from filing the identical trade name
   with the Department.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10075 (November 8,
   2002).
