                                  DC REGULATIONS

CHANGES IN REGISTRATION, CANCELLATION, AND FILING REQUIREMENTS (ยง 17-8909)

8909.1. An amendment to a trade name registration shall be filed with the
   Department when a change occurs in any of the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	The true and real name of a person conducting a business with a trade name
   registered under this chapter; or
   
   
   
   (b)	The mailing address set forth on the registration or on any filed
   amendment; or
   
   
   
   (c)	A transfer occurs.

   8909.2. A notice of cancellation shall be filed with the Department when use
   of a trade name is discontinued.

   8909.3. A notice of cancellation, together with an application for
   registration of trade name shall be filed when:
   
   
   
   (a)	An addition, deletion or any change of person or persons conducting or
   transacting business under the trade name.
   
   
   
   (b)	There is a change in the wording or spelling of the trade name.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10075 (November 8,
   2002).
