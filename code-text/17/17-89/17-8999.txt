DEFINITIONS (ยง 17-8999)

8991.1. As used in this chapter, the following terms shall have the meanings
   ascribed:
   
   
   
   Application &#8211; the master business license application as prescribed in
   Chapter 80.
   
   
   
   Business &#8211; any trade, profession, or activity which provides, or holds
   itself out to provide, goods or services to the general public or to any
   portion of the general public, for hire or compensation, and which pays, or is
   subject to the payment of, taxes on earnings, or fees in lieu of taxes, to the
   District of Columbia (District), or which qualifies for tax-exempt status
   under District law.
   
   
   
   Department &#8211; the Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs.
   
   
   
   Director &#8211; the Director of the Department of Consumer and Regulatory
   Affairs.
   
   
   
   Executed &#8211; the signing of a document by a person under penalties of
   perjury and in an official and authorized capacity on behalf of the person
   submitting the document to the Department.
   
   
   
   Person &#8211; any individual, partnership, limited liability partnership,
   limited liability company, or corporation conducting or having an interest in
   a business in the District of Columbia.
   
   
   
   Style &#8211; the title, or appellation of a person.
   
   
   
   Trade name &#8211; a word or name, or any combination of a word or name, used
   by a person to identify the person&#8217;s business which:
   
   
   
   A.	Is not, or does not include, the true and real name of all persons
   conducting the business or with an interest therein; or
   
   
   
   B.	Include words which suggest additional parties of interest such as
   &#8220;company,&#8221; &#8220;and sons,&#8221; &#8220;and daughters,&#8221;
   &#8220;and associates.&#8221;; and
   
   
   
   C.	Which does not include words which are generally included with the
   registration of a true name such as &#8220;corporation&#8221;,
   &#8220;incorporated&#8221;, &#8220;partnership&#8221;, &#8220;limited&#8221;;
   and
   
   
   
   D.	Does not include words or terms that are sexually explicit; and
   
   
   
   E.	Does not include any word, phrase, or statement prohibited by D.C. Official
   Code ยง 29-101.08.
   
   
   
   Trade Name Application &#8211; the document incorporating pertinent data
   required for trade name registration.
   
   
   
   True and real name &#8211; one of the following:
   
   
   
   A.	The surname of an individual coupled with one or more of the
   individual&#8217;s other names, one or more of the individual&#8217;s
   initials, or any combination thereof;
   
   
   
   B.	The designation, style or appellation by which an individual is best known
   and called in the business community where that individual transacts business,
   if this is used as that individual&#8217;s legal signature;
   
   
   
   C.	The registered corporate name of a domestic corporation as filed with the
   Corporations Division of the Department;
   
   
   
   D.	The registered corporate name of a foreign corporation authorized to do
   business within the District of Columbia as filed with the Corporations
   Division of the Department;
   
   
   
   E.	The registered partnership name of a domestic limited partnership or
   domestic limited liability partnership filed with the Corporations Division of
   the Department;
   
   
   
   F.	The registered partnership name of a foreign limited partnership or foreign
   limited liability partnership as filed with the Corporations Division of the
   Department;
   
   
   
   G.	The name of a general partnership which includes in its name the true and
   real names, as defined in subparagraphs A through F of this paragraph, of each
   general partner as required in this chapter;
   
   
   
   H.	The registered limited liability company name of a domestic limited
   liability company as filed with the Corporations Division of the Department;
   or
   
   
   
   I.	The registered limited liability company name of a foreign limited
   liability company authorized to do business within the District of Columbia as
   filed with the Corporations Division of the Department.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10075 (November 8,
   2002).
