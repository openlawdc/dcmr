WAIVER OF EXAMINATION &#8211; SPECIALTY COSMETOLOGY OPERATOR (ยง 17-3708)

3708.1. The Board may waive the examination requirements of ยง3707 for an
   applicant who meets the training, experience, and qualifications requirements
   to practice braiding or electrology and who proves to the satisfaction of the
   Board that the applicant was practicing braiding or electrology on a
   substantially full-time basis within the District of Columbia for three (3)
   years during the five (5) years immediately preceding the effective date of
   this chapter.

   3708.2. The application for waiver of examination shall be filed within two
   years of the effective date of this chapter.

   3708.3. An applicant shall demonstrate to the Board that the applicant has
   been practicing braiding or electrology on a substantially full-time basis by
   submitting evidence satisfactory to the Board that include the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Affidavits from the applicant and three (3) licensed cosmetologists
   attesting to the nature of applicant&#8217;s practice during the applicable
   time period;
   
   
   
   (b)	An affidavit from a certified public accountant or bookkeeper of the
   applicant&#8217;s income tax or business record covering the applicable time
   period; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Any other information the Board considers relevant.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 7699 (September 12, 2003).
