                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADDITIONAL ADVERTISING RESTRICTIONS ON LICENSEES (ยง 17-3728)

3728.01. It shall be unlawful for any person licensed under this chapter to
   depict a barber pole in advertising or to place a barber pole in a location
   that would create or tend to create the impression to members of the public
   that a business located at or near the barber pole is a barbershop unless the
   licensee holds the licenses required to operate a barbershop or the licensee
   leases space or a booth to a licensed barber pursuant to Section 3720 of this
   chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 7559 (July 11, 2008).
