                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISPLAY OF LICENSE (ยง 17-3713)

3713.1. A licensee of a barber, cosmetology, or specialty cosmetology salon
   shall display the salon licenses unobstructed from the view of patrons.

   3713.2. All individual licensees shall wear their license on the front of
   their uniform or other acceptable work clothes in plain view of patrons.
   Individual licenses shall be placed where necessary at each station and in
   plain view of patrons.

   3713.3. Each person licensed, certified, or registered under this subchapter
   shall notify the Mayor of any change of address of the place of residence or
   place of business or employment within 30 days after the change of address.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 7699 (September 12, 2003).
