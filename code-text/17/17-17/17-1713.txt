                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADMINISTRATIVE PENALTIES (ยง 17-1713)

1713.1. The Director may issue a reprimand and/or assess a civil penalty
   against an athlete agent not to exceed twenty five thousand dollars ($25,000)
   per occurrence for violations of this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 50 DCR 9549 (November 14, 2003).
