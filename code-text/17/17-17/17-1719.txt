                                  DC REGULATIONS

HEARING NOTICE PROCEDURES (ยง 17-1719)

1719.1. If a respondent requests a hearing, the Director shall, within twenty
   (20) days following receipt of the request, notify the respondent of the date,
   time, and place of the hearing.

   1719.2. The Director shall hold the hearing not less than twenty (20) days
   following the date of service of the notice unless the Director and all of the
   parties agree to the holding of the hearing at an earlier date.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 50 DCR 9549 (November 14, 2003).
