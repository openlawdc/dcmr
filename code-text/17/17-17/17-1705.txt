                                  DC REGULATIONS

REGISTRATION AND RENEWAL FEES (ยง 17-1705)

1705.1. The appropriate fee as established by the Director must accompany an
   application for registration or renewal.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Athlete Agent Registration Fee Schedule (2003)
   
   
   
   (a)	Initial Registration &#8211; four hundred dollars ($400);
   
   
   
   (b)	Renewal of Registration &#8211; four hundred dollars ($400);
   
   
   
   (c)	Application based upon initial application of another State &#8211; four
   hundred dollars ($400);
   
   
   
   (d)	Application based upon renewal application of another State &#8211; four
   hundred dollars ($400); and
   
   
   
   (e)	Application fee to be included with the registration fees listed in (a)
   through (d) above &#8211; one hundred dollars ($100).

   1705.3. The Director may amend these fees via the fee schedule located in
   Chapter 35 of this title.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 50 DCR 9549 (November 14, 2003).
