                                  DC REGULATIONS

STUDENT-ATHLETE&#8217;S RIGHT TO CANCEL (ยง 17-1708)

1708.1. A student-athlete may cancel an agency contract by giving written
   notice of the cancellation to the athlete agent in a record within fourteen
   (14) days after the contract is signed.

   1708.2. A student-athlete may not waive the right to cancel an agency
   contract.

   1708.3. If a student-athlete cancels an agency contract, the student-athlete
   is not required to pay any consideration under the contract or to return any
   consideration received from the athlete-agent to induce the student-athlete to
   sign the contract.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 50 DCR 9549 (November 14, 2003).
