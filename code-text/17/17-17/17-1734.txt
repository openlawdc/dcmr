                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPUTATION OF TIME (ยง 17-1734)

1714 1	In computing any period of time specified in this chapter, the day of the
act, event, or default shall not be counted, and the last day of the period
shall be counted unless it is not a business day, in which event the time period
shall continue until the next business day.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 50 DCR 9549 (November 14, 2003).
