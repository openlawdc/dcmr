                                  DC REGULATIONS

TERM OF LICENSE (ยง 17-7801)

7801.1. Subject to subsection 7801.2, a license issued pursuant to this
   chapter shall expire at midnight of December 31 of each even-numbered year.

   7801.2. The Director may change the renewal system and the expiration dates of
   licenses issued pursuant to this chapter pursuant to subsection 4006.3.
