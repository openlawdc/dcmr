                                  DC REGULATIONS

WAIVER OF SUPERVISED EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS (ยง 17-7804)

7804.1. The Board may waive the supervised experience requirements for an
   applicant who:
   
   
   
   (a)	Provides proof that he or she has been employed in the practice of
   audiology for at least ten (10) of the fifteen (15) years immediately
   preceding the effective date of this chapter;
   
   
   
   (b)	Meets all other applicable requirements of this chapter; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Applies within six (6) months of the effective date of these
   					regulations.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 4336, 4340 (May 20,
   2011).
