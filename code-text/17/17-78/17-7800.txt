                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 17-7800)

7800.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a license to
   practice audiology.

   7800.2. Chapters 40 (Health Occupations: General Rules) and 41 (Health
   Occupations: Administrative Procedures) of this title shall supplement this
   chapter.
