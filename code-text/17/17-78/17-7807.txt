                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSURE BY ENDORSEMENT (ยง 17-7807)

7807.1. The Board may issue a license by endorsement to an audiologist who has
   a valid, unrestricted license in good standing from another jurisdiction of
   the United States with standards which are, as determined by the Board,
   substantially equivalent to the District of Columbia standards for licensure.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 4336, 4342 (May 20,
   2011).
