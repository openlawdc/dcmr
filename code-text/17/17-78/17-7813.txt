                                  DC REGULATIONS

STANDARDS OF CONDUCT (ยง 17-7813)

7813.1. A licensee or graduate student practicing audiology shall adhere to
   the standards set forth in either the then-current Code of Ethics of the
   American Speech-Language-Hearing Association or the then-current Code of
   Ethics for the American Academy of Audiology.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 4336, 4343 (May 20,
   2011).
