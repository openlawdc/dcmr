                                  DC REGULATIONS

HEARING NOTICE PROCEDURES (ยง 17-3317)

3317.1. If a respondent requests a hearing, a board shall, within twenty (20)
   days following receipt of the request, notify the respondent of the date,
   time, and place of the hearing.

   3317.2. The board shall hold the hearing not less than twenty (20) days
   following the date of service of the notice unless the board and all of the
   parties agree to the holding of the hearing at an earlier date.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3488, 3501 (May 13, 1988).
