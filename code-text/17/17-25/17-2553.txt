                                  DC REGULATIONS

ELECTRONIC SUBMISSION OF PEER REVIEW REPORTS TO BOARD (ยง 17-2553)

2553.1 	In lieu of submitting a hard copy of a peer review report and all
accompanying documents, a firm manager may notify the Board that the documents
requested can be viewed and downloaded at the website of the sponsoring
organization.  The firm manager may also email a copy of the peer review program
report and any supporting documents to the Board.



2553.2 	If the Board is not able to download a copy of the requested documents,
the firm manager shall submit the required hard copy to the Board.











	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 5186, 5190 (May 18,
2012).
