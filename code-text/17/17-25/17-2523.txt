                                  DC REGULATIONS

FAILURE TO APPEAR (ยง 17-2523)

2523.1. If a person who requested a hearing does not appear and no continuance
   has been requested or is granted, the Board may hear the evidence of the
   witnesses who appear.

   2523.2. The Board also may proceed to consider the matter and render a
   decision based on evidence before it, in the manner required by this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 4401 (April 30, 2004).
