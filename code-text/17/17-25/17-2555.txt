                                  DC REGULATIONS

TYPE OF REVIEW REQUIRED (§ 17-2555)

2555.1. If a firm’s sole practice is the preparation of compiled financial
   statements for management use only in accordance with SSARS No. 8, the firm
   shall be exempted from the peer review program requirement.
