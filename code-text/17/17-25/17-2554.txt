                                  DC REGULATIONS

EXTENSION OF TIME TO SUBMIT PEER REVIEW REPORT (ยง 17-2554)

2554.1 	Sponsoring organizations may grant an extension of time to a firm to
   complete the peer review program process.

   2554.2. If a firm is granted an extension of time, the firm shall immediately
   submit to the Board a copy of a letter from the sponsoring organization that
   granted the extension.
