                                  DC REGULATIONS

EXEMPTION FROM PEER REVIEW (ยง 17-2556)

2556.1. A peer reviewer shall be independent of the reviewed CPA firm and
   shall comply with the AICPA Standards for Performing and Reporting on Peer
   Reviews.

   2556.2. Any information obtained as a consequence of the peer review
   concerning a participating CPA firm, its clients or personnel, is confidential
   and shall not be disclosed to any person not involved in the peer review
   process.
