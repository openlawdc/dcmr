                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORD OF HEARING (ยง 17-2526)

2526.1. In all hearings conducted under this chapter, a complete record shall
   be made of all evidence presented during the course of the hearing.

   2526.2. Parties to the proceedings and the public may obtain a copy of the
   record after paying the fee established by the Mayor.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 4401 (April 30, 2004).
