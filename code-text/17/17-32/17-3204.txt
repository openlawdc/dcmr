                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECIPROCAL LICENSURE (ยง 17-3204)

3204.1. An applicant for a license by reciprocity shall furnish proof
   satisfactory to the Board that the following requirements are met:
   
   
   
   (a)	The applicant is licensed and in good standing as an interior designer in
   a jurisdiction of the United States with requirements that are substantially
   equivalent to the requirements of the Act and this chapter;
   
   
   
   (b)	The jurisdiction in which the applicant is licensed admits interior
   designers licensed by the District of Columbia in like manner; and
   
   
   
   (c)	The applicant has paid the required fees to the District.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 5441 (May 9, 2008).
