APPLICABILITY (ยง 17-3200)

3200.1. This chapter shall apply to applicants for and holders of a license to
   practice interior design.

   3200.2. Chapter 33 of this Title shall supplement this chapter.

   3200.3. The provisions of this chapter shall prevail in the event of a direct
   and irreconcilable conflict between this chapter and Chapter 33 of this Title.
