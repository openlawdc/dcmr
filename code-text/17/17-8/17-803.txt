                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISPOSAL OF WASTE TIRES (§ 17-803)

803.1. A licensee shall establish and maintain a contractual agreement with a
   waste tire hauler, or its equivalent, for the removal of waste tires, at a
   minimum of every three calendar (3) days, from the licensee’s property.

   803.2. A copy of the contractual agreement required by § 803.1 shall be
   available for inspection or review by the Department.
