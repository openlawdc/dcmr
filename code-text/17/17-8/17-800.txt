                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY (ยง 17-800)

800.1. This chapter shall apply to any person engaged in the storage,
   collection, or processing of waste tires in the District of Columbia,
   regardless of whether the waste tires are purchased or obtained free of
   charge.
