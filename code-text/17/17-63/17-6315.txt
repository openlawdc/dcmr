                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTINUING EDUCATION AUDIT (ยง 17-6315)

6315.1 	The Board may audit up to twenty percent (20%) of the number of
licensees to determine compliance with the continuing education contact hour
requirements.



6315.2 	Upon notification by the Board that a licensee has been selected for an
audit, the 			licensee shall submit the required documentation within thirty
(30) days of receipt 		of the notice.



	Source: A Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 3133, 3143 (April 20,
2012).
