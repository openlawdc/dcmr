                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPUTATION OF TIME (ยง 17-3149)

3149.1. In computing any period of time under this chapter, the day of the
   act, event, or default shall not be counted and the last day of the period
   shall be counted unless it is not a business day, in which event the time
   period shall continue until the next business day.
   
   Source: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7853, 7876 (October 23, 1992).
