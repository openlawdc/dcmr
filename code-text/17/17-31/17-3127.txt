                                  DC REGULATIONS

HEARING NOTICE PROCEDURES (ยง 17-3127)

3127.1 	If a respondent requests a hearing, the Director shall notify the
respondent of the date, time, and place of the hearing within twenty (20) days
following receipt of the request.

3127.2 	The Director shall hold the hearing not less than fifteen (15) days
following the date of the mailing or delivery of the notice, unless the Director
and all of the parties agree to the holding of the hearing at an earlier date.

Source: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7853, 7868 (October 23, 1992).
