                                  DC REGULATIONS

TERM OF LICENSE (ยง 17-3105)

3105.1 	A license issued under this chapter shall expire on September 30 of each
odd-numbered year.

3105.2 	A license issued under this chapter shall be effective until September
30 of the first odd-numbered year following issuance of the license or for two
(2) years, whichever is less.

	Source: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7853, 7856 (October 23, 1992).
