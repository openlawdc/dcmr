                                  DC REGULATIONS

RIGHT OF FENTRY AND INSPECTION (§ 17-3123)

3123.1 	The Director has the right, after presenting credentials, to enter a
funeral services establishment.

3123.2 	A licensee or applicant shall give the Director access to records,
policies and procedures, contracts, and any other information that the Director
deems necessary to determine the funeral services establishment’s compliance
with the act or this chapter.

Source: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7853, 7865 (October 23, 1992).
