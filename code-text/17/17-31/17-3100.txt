                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY (ยง 17-3100)

3100.1 	This chapter applies to applicants for licenses and licensees of funeral
services establishments.

3100.2 	Chapter 30 (Funeral Directors) of this title supplements this chapter.

3100.3 	Each funeral services establishment in the District shall meet the
requirements set forth in the Act and this chapter within six (6) months of the
effective date of this chapter.

	Source: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7853 (October 23, 1992).
