GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 17-2200)

2200.1. No person shall sell coal in the District of Columbia except a
   licensed dealer or the licensed agent of a licensed dealer.

   2200.2. Each dealer in coal and each person selling coal as an agent shall
   secure a license for each year or part of a year.

   2200.3. Violation of any of the provisions of this chapter shall subject the
   offender to the penalties prescribed in D.C. Code ยง47-2846 (1981).
