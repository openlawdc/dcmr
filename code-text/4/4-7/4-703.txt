                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTINUING VIOLATION (ยง 4-703)

703.1. The complainant may allege that his or her claim is a continuing
   violation if the claim involves a hostile work environment and at least one of
   the discriminatory acts occurred within the statutory period of one (1) year
   or three hundred sixty-five (365) days of the date of filing.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 007324 (September 4, 2009).
