                                  DC REGULATIONS

DIRECTORS DETERMINATION (ยง 4-715)

715.1. OGC shall send a draft LOD recommending probable cause or no probable
   cause to the Director. The Director shall review the LOD, make a substitute
   determination or approve the determination, and issue a final determination to
   the parties. The LOD shall state whether there is probable cause or no
   probable cause to credit the complaint, or whether the complaint should be
   dismissed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 007324 (September 4, 2009).
