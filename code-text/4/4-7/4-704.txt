                                  DC REGULATIONS

SUBMISSION OF INFORMATION REGARDING ALLEGED DISCRIMINATION (§ 4-704)

704.1. The Office shall accept information concerning alleged violations of
   the Act, Title VII of the federal Civil Rights Act of 1964, Title VIII of the
   Federal Civil Rights Act of 1968, and other applicable statutes from any
   person.

   704.2. Where the information discloses that a person is entitled to file a
   charge with the Office, the matter will be investigated.

   704.3. Any person or organization may request the issuance of a Director’s
   order for an inquiry into individual or systematic discrimination prohibited
   by any of the laws referred to in § 704.l.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 007324 (September 4, 2009).
