                                  DC REGULATIONS

ELECTION OF OFFICERS (ยง 4-301)

301.1. Members of the Commission shall elect officers other than the
   Chairperson. Officers may be reelected for any number of terms.

   301.2. Annual elections of officers shall be conducted at the regular January
   Commission meeting.

   301.3. Officers shall serve one (1) year terms to commence at the conclusion
   of the meeting at which annual elections are conducted. A special election to
   fill an unexpired term may be held at any regular monthly meeting; provided,
   that written notice has been given at least seven (7) calendar days prior to
   the election.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 6720, 6721 (October 7, 1994).
