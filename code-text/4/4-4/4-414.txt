                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTINUANCES (§ 4-414)

414.1. Any party may submit a motion for continuance to the hearing examiner,
   who shall grant it only for good cause shown.

   414.2. The party seeking a continuance shall seek the agreement of all other
   parties as to an alternate date and time, prior to submission of the motion
   for continuance, and shall cite the agreement in the motion, or state with
   particularity the reasons why a continuance should be granted in the absence
   of the parties’ agreement.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 1429, 1440 (March 24, 1995).
