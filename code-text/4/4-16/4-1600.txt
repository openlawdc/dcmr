                                  DC REGULATIONS

AUTHORITY AND PURPOSE (§ 4-1600)

1600.1. The provisions of this chapter are promulgated to implement the
   District of Columbia Family and Medical Leave Act of 1990, effective October
   3, 1990 (D.C. Law 8-181; D.C. Official Code §§ 32-501 et seq.) (Act or
   DCFMLA).
   
   
   
   1600.2 		The Director of the Office of Human Rights (OHR), under Mayor’s
   Order 2009-45, dated March 31, 2009, is delegated the authority to issue rules
   to implement the Act.

   1600.3. The purpose of these regulations is to provide guidance on, and
   procedures and standards for, the implementation of the DCFMLA and to notify
   the District government as an employer, other covered employers within the
   District, and eligible employees of their rights and responsibilities under
   the Act.
   
   
   
   1600.4 		An employer cannot create a rule or policy which diminishes the
   rights of employees or the obligations of employers.
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is § 18(a)
   of the Family and Medical Leave Act of 1990, D.C. Law 8-181, D.C. Official
   Code 32-501 (2001) et seq.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 4350 (July 12, 1991); as amended
   by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 10789 (November 19, 2010).
