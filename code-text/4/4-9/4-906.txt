                                  DC REGULATIONS

DIRECTORS DETERMINATION (ยง 4-906)

906.1. The Director shall make a thorough review of all information and
   documents submitted in response to a data request and shall determine whether
   there is probable cause to believe that the person or organization against
   whom allegations have been made has engaged or is engaging in conduct
   prohibited by the Act.

   906.2. If the Director determines that the conduct is not prohibited under the
   Act, the Director shall inform in writing the person or organization against
   whom the allegations have been made and the Director shall terminate the
   investigation.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 4791, 4792-93 (July 24, 1987).
