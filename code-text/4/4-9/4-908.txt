                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESOLUTION THROUGH VOLUNTARY COMPLIANCE (ยง 4-908)

908.1. If the person or organization consents to voluntary compliance, the
   Director may require the person or organization to enter a written compliance
   agreement. The Director may then terminate the investigation or may monitor
   the person or organization to ensure observance of the terms of a compliance
   agreement.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE:  Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 4791, 4793 (July 24, 1987).
