                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEALS (§ 4-121)

121.1. An appeal from the final decision of OHR may be taken to the Superior
   Court of the District of Columbia.

   121.2. Pursuant to § 109 and § 113, the final decisions of the Director
   include a summary determination or the final determination after the hearing
   examiner’s recommendation.

   121.3. The party must file a Petition for Review with the Clerk of the Civil
   Division within   thirty (30) days after service of notice of the final
   decision.
