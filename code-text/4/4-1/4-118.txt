                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORDS AND TRANSCRIPTS OF HEARINGS (ยง 4-118)

118.1. Each hearing shall be recorded and transcribed verbatim.

   118.2. The record shall consist of the complaint file, exhibits, transcripts,
   and all other documents submitted to and accepted by the Hearing Examiner
   related to the subject matter of the hearing and made a part of the record.

   118.3. The Director shall be responsible for the reproduction of records, at
   the expense of the respondent agency.

   118.4. A copy of the verbatim transcript, along with copies of each document
   made a part of the record by the Hearing Examiner, shall be furnished to the
   parties or their representatives, the agency involved, if not a party, the
   Hearing Examiner, and the Director.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 31 DCR 56, 76 (January 6, 1984); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 9162, 9181 (October 1, 2010).
