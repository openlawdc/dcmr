899.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   &#8220;Act&#8221; &#8211; the Human Rights Act of 1977, effective December 13,
   1977 (D.C. Law 2-38; D.C. Official code ยง 2-1401.01 et seq.).
   
   
   
   &#8220;Commission&#8221; &#8211; the Commission on Human Rights, established

   &#8220;Entities&#8221; &#8211; include all employers, housing providers,

   &#8220;Gender identity or expression&#8221; &#8211; a gender-related identity,

   individuals, and others whose appearance or characteristic are perceived to be
   gender-atypical.
