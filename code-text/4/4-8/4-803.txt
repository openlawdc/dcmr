                                  DC REGULATIONS

ACCOMMODATIONS FOR HEALTH CARE NEEDS (ยง 4-803)

803.1. When requested by the employee, an employer shall make reasonable
   accommodations (including medical leave) for transgender-related health care
   needs that are consistent with such accommodations that are provided for other
   medical needs. Such needs include but are not limited to medical or counseling
   appointments, surgery, recovery from surgery, and any other
   transgender-related procedures.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 8751, 8753 (October 27, 2006).
