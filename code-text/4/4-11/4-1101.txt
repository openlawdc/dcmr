                                  DC REGULATIONS

SCOPE (§ 4-1101)

1101.1. Except as hereinafter exempted, the provisions of this chapter shall
   apply to all District of Columbia government contracts subject to Mayor’s
   Order No. 85-85, and any rules, regulations, and procedures promulgated
   pursuant to that Mayor’s Order.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 4952 (August 15, 1986).
