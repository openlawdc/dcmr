                                  DC REGULATIONS

WAIVERS (ยง 4-1109)

1109.1. The Director may grant a waiver to a prospective contractor from the
   requirement to submit a set of minimum standards for the employment of
   minorities and women in a particular contract, if before the execution the
   contract and approval of the Affirmative Action Program, the contractor can
   document and otherwise prove it is unable to meet the standards in the
   performance of the contract.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 4952, 4961 (August 15, 1986).
