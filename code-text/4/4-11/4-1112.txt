                                  DC REGULATIONS

AFTER EXECUTION OF CONTRACT (§ 4-1112)

1112.1. Each contractor shall maintain throughout the term of the contract the
   minimum standards for the employment of minorities and women, as set forth in
   the approved Affirmative Action Program.

   1112.2. Each contractor shall require that each subcontractor, or vendor under
   the contract comply with the provisions of the contract and the Affirmative
   Action Program.

   1112.3. Each contractor shall furnish all information as required by this
   chapter, and permit access to all books and records pertaining to the
   contractor’s employment practices and worksites by the Director and the
   Contracting Agency for purposes of investigation to ascertain compliance with
   this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 4952, 4962-64 (August 15, 1986).
