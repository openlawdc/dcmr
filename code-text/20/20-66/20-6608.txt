                                  DC REGULATIONS

PENALTIES (§ 20-6608)

6608.1 		Penalties for failure to comply with a final compliance order, a final
cease and desist order, or a final suspension, revocation or denial order shall
be in accordance with D.C. Code §§ 8-113.09(d) through (g).



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 7835, 7921 (November 12, 1993); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 7699 (October 1, 1999); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 6678 (August 21, 2009).
