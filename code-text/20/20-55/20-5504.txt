                                  DC REGULATIONS

PARTIAL APPLICABILITY OF UST REGULATIONS TO UST SYSTEMS OF 110 GALLONS OR LESS,
HYDRAULIC LIFT TANKS AND ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT TANKS (§ 20-5504)

5504.1. The following UST systems are required to comply only with the
   provisions of this section:
   
   
   
   (a)	Equipment or machinery that contains regulated substances for operational
   purposes (such as hydraulic lift tanks and electrical equipment tanks); and
   
   
   
   (b)	Any UST system with a capacity of one-hundred-ten (110) gallons or less.

   5504.2. Where there is a suspected or confirmed release a responsible or
   remediating party shall comply with all of the provisions of Chapter 56 except
   §§ 5600.7(d) and 5601; and the provisions of Chapter 62.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 7699 (October 1, 1999).
