                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 20-3400)

3400.1. The D.C. Energy Extension Service (EES) Conservation Demonstration
   Awards Program (ECDAP) was established with federal funds by the D.C. Office
   of Energy to encourage local innovative energy conservation outreach
   activities.

   3400.2. Applicants seeking demonstration award grants shall submit proposals
   in areas that demonstrate energy savings for the commercial or residential
   sectors of the District of Columbia.

   3400.3. The Director of the D.C. Office of Energy (hereinafter,
   &#8220;Director&#8221;) shall be ultimately responsible for the EES program.

   3400.4. The Program Manager for the EES Program shall be responsible for the
   coordination, administration, and day-to-day operation of the program.
