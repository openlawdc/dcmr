                                  DC REGULATIONS

FUEL CHOICE (ยง 20-911)

911.1. The choice of clean-fuel vehicles and clean fuels shall be made by the
   covered fleet operators subject to the requirements of this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Section 3(a) of the Clean Fuel Fleet Vehicle Program and Alternative
   Fuels Incentives Amendment Act of 1994, D.C. Law 10-201, 41 DCR 7178, 7190
   (November 4, 1994).
