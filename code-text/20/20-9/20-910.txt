                                  DC REGULATIONS

FUEL AVAILABILITY REQUIREMENT (ยง 20-910)

910.1. Fuel providers shall make clean fuels available to covered fleet
   operators at locations at which covered fleet vehicles are fueled.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Section 3(a) of the Clean Fuel Fleet Vehicle Program and Alternative
   Fuels Incentives Amendment Act of 1994, D.C. Law 10-201, 41 DCR 7178, 7190
   (November 4, 1994).
