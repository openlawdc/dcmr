                                  DC REGULATIONS

VEHICULAR EXHAUST EMISSIONS (ยง 20-901)

901.1. The engine, power and exhaust mechanism of each motor vehicle shall be
   equipped, adjusted and operated to prevent the escape of a trail of visible
   fumes or smoke for more than ten (10) consecutive seconds.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Section 3 of the District of Columbia Air Pollution Control Act of
   1984, D.C. Law 5-165, 32 DCR 565, 647 (February 1, 1985).
