                                  DC REGULATIONS

LEAD CONTENT OF GASOLINE (ยง 20-902)

902.1. No gasoline containing more than one (1.0) gram of lead per gallon
   shall be sold.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Section 3 of the District of Columbia Air Pollution Control Act of
   1984, D.C. Law 5-165, 32 DCR 565, 647 (February 1, 1985).
