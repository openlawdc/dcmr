                                  DC REGULATIONS

ODOROUS OR OTHER NUISANCE AIR POLLUTANTS (ยง 20-903)

903.1. An emission into the atmosphere of odorous or other air pollutants from
   any source in any quantity and of any characteristic, and duration which is,
   or is likely to be injurious to the public health or welfare, or which
   interferes with the reasonable enjoyment of life and property is prohibited.

   903.2. [Repealed] D.C. Law 11-94, 42 DCR 7178 (December 29, 1995).
