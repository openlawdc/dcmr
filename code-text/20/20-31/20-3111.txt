                                  DC REGULATIONS

ABROGATION AND GREATER RESTRICTION (ยง 20-3111)

3111.1. This Chapter is not intended to repeal, abrogate, or impair any
   existing regulations, easements, covenants, or deed restrictions. However,
   where this chapter and another conflict or overlap, whichever imposes the more
   stringent restrictions shall prevail.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 32 DCR 6547, 6562 (November 15, 1985);

   19, 2010).
