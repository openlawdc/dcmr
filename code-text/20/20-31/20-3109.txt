                                  DC REGULATIONS

SEVERABILITY (ยง 20-3109)

3109.1. If any portion of this chapter is held invalid for any reason by a
   court of competent jurisdiction, that portion shall be deemed to be a
   separate, distinct, and independent provision, and that holding shall not
   affect the validity of any remaining provision.
   
   
   
   	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 10780 (November 19,
   2010).
