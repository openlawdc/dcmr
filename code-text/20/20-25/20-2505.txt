                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSE FEES AND TERMS (ยง 20-2505)

2505.1. Pesticide licenses shall be issued for a period of one (1) year
   beginning on April 1st and expiring on March 31st.

   2505.2. The following fee schedule shall apply to the following licenses:
   
   
   
   (a)	Pesticide Operator License:
   
   
   
   (1)	Type 1: $100
   
   
   
   (2)	Type 2: $100
   
   
   
   (3)	Type 3: No Charge
   
   
   
   (b)	Pesticide Applicator:
   
   
   
   (1)	Commercial: $50
   
   
   
   (2)	Public: No Charge
   
   
   
   (c)	Pesticide Dealer: $25
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by Final Rulemaking published at 25 DCR 3044 (October 6,
   1978), incorporating Proposed Rulemaking published at 25 DCR 2118 (August 25,
   1978).
