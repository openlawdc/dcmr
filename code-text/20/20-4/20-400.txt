                                  DC REGULATIONS

AIR POLLUTION REPORTING INDEX (§ 20-400)

400.1. The Mayor shall establish a simplified, daily, public reporting index
   of air pollution levels in the District of Columbia. This index shall indicate
   the levels of pollutant about which he or she determines the public should be
   informed. The Mayor, in adopting an index, shall coordinate as closely as
   possible with the Metropolitan Washington Council of Governments to ensure a
   uniform regional system of air quality levels reporting.
