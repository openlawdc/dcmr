                                  DC REGULATIONS

EMERGENCY AND RELEASE NOTIFICATION (ยง 20-4205)

4205.1. Notwithstanding any provision in the RCRA regulations, 40 CFR Parts
   124, 260 through 266, 268, 270, 273, and 279, to the contrary, whenever the
   RCRA regulations require that telephonic emergency or release notification be
   given to EPA, DOT, the National Response Center, or another federal agency,
   the person required to provide the notice shall, at the same time, provide
   telephonic notice to the District of Columbia Emergency Management Agency at
   (202) 727-6161 and the District Department of the Environment, Hazardous Waste
   Division at (202) 535-2270.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 52 DCR 9653 (October 28, 2005); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 5586 (June 8, 2007).
