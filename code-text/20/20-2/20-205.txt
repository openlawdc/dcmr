                                  DC REGULATIONS

NEW SOURCE PERFORMANCE STANDARDS (ยง 20-205)

205.1. A person shall not construct, modify, or operate or cause to be
   constructed, modified, or operated a New Source Performance Standard (NSPS)
   source which results or will result in violations of the provisions of 40 CFR
   Part 60, as in effect on September 30, 1997, with the terms used and defined
   in those provisions.
