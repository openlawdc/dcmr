CONSUMER PRODUCTS &#8211; ANTIPERSPIRANTS OR DEODORANTS (ยง 20-726)

726.1. No person shall sell, supply, offer for sale, or manufacture for sale
   in the District of Columbia any antiperspirant or deodorant which contains any
   compound that has been identified by CARB in Title 17, California Code of
   Regulations, Division 3, Chapter 1, Subchapter 7, ยง 93000, as a toxic air
   contaminant.
