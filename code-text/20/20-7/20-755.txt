                                  DC REGULATIONS

PORTABLE FUEL CONTAINERS AND SPOUTS – CERTIFICATION AND COMPLIANCE TEST
PROCEDURES (§ 20-755)

755.1. Testing to determine compliance with § 752 shall be performed by using
   test procedures specified in “CP-501, Certification Procedure for Portable
   Fuel Containers and Spill-Proof Spouts,” adopted by CARB on July 26, 2006,
   including any subsequent amendments, which are incorporated by reference
   herein.

   755.2. Alternative methods that are shown to be accurate, precise, and
   appropriate may be used upon written approval of the Department.

   755.3. Test procedures referred to in this section can be obtained from the
   Department and may be available at http://www.arb.ca.gov.
