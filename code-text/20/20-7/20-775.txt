                                  DC REGULATIONS

ARCHITECTURAL AND INDUSTRIAL MAINTENANCE COATING – EXEMPTIONS (§ 20-775)

775.1. Sections 773 through 778 do not apply to:
   
   
   
   (a)	Any architectural coating that is sold or manufactured for use outside of
   the District of Columbia or for shipment to other manufacturers for
   reformulation or repackaging;
   
   
   
   (b)	Any aerosol coating product; or
   
   
   
   (c)	Any architectural coating that is sold in a container with a volume of one
   liter (1 L) or one and fifty-seven hundredths quarts (1.057 q.) or less.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 11286, 11402 (December
   30, 2011).
