CONSUMER PRODUCTS &#8211; PRODUCTS REQUIRING DILUTION (ยง 20-723)

723.1. Consumer products wherein the label, packaging, or accompanying
   literature specifically states that the product should be diluted with water
   or non-VOC solvent before use shall comply with the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Limits specified in the Table of Standards in ยง 720.1 shall apply to the
   product only after the minimum recommended dilution has taken place; and
   
   
   
    (b)	Minimum recommended dilution shall not include recommendations for
   incidental use of a concentrated product to deal with limited special
   applications such as hardtoremove soils or stains.

   723.2. Consumer products wherein the label, packaging, or accompanying
   literature states that the product should be diluted with any VOC solvent
   prior to use, shall comply with the limits specified in the Table of Standards
   in ยง 720.1, only after the maximum recommended dilution has taken place.

   (December 30, 2011).
