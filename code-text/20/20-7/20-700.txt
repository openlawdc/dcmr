MISCELLANEOUS VOLATILE ORGANIC COMPOUNDS (VOCS) (ยง 20-700)

700.1. Unless otherwise specified, sources subject to subsequent sections of
   this chapter shall not be subject to ยง 700.

   700.2. No person shall discharge into the atmosphere more than fifteen (15)
   pounds of volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions in any one (1) day, nor
   more than three pounds (3 lb.) in any one (1) hour, from any combination of
   articles, machines, units, equipment, or other contrivances at a facility,
   unless the uncontrolled VOC emissions are reduced by at least ninety percent
   (90%) overall capture and control efficiency.
