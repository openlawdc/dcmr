                                  DC REGULATIONS

WASTE GAS DISPOSAL FROM ETHYLENE PRODUCING PLANT (§ 20-712)

712.1. The emission of a waste gas steam from an ethylene producing plant, or
   source utilizing ethylene as a raw material, into the atmosphere in excess of
   twenty (20) pounds per twenty-four (24) hour period shall be prohibited,
   unless the waste gas stream is properly burned at one thousand three hundred
   degrees Fahrenheit (1,300° F.) for three tenths (0.3) of a second or longer
   in a direct-flame after burner, or is removed by a method of comparable
   efficiency approved by the Mayor.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Section 3 of the District of Columbia Air Pollution Control Act of
   1984, D.C. Law 5-165, § 712, 32 DCR 565, 642 (February 1, 1985).
