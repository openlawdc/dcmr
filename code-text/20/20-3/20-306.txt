                                  DC REGULATIONS

ACID RAIN PROGRAM (ยง 20-306)

306.1. The requirements of 40 CFR parts 72 and 75, as in effect on September
   30, 1997, are hereby adopted by reference for purposes of implementing an acid
   rain program that meets the requirements of title IV of the Clean Air Act,
   with the terms used and defined, except that the term &#8220;permitting
   authority&#8221; shall mean &#8220;Director&#8221;.

   306.2. If the provisions or requirements of 40 CFR parts 72 or 75 conflict
   with or are not included in regulations in this chapter, the part 72 and 75
   provisions and requirements shall apply and take precedence.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 7039-7040 (October 2, 1998).
