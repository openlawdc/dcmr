                                  DC REGULATIONS

LIABILITY INSURANCE (ยง 20-2403)

2403.1. The Mayor shall not issue a pesticide operator&#8217;s license until
   the applicant has furnished evidence of financial responsibility in the form
   of liability insurance for the protection of persons who may suffer damages as
   a result of the operations of the applicant.

   2403.2. The amount of minimum financial responsibility shall be established by
   the Mayor and shall be maintained at not less than that sum as long as the
   pesticide operator engages in business in those categories for which his or
   her license is issued.

   2403.3. The insurer of a pesticide operator shall notify the Mayor in writing
   at least ten (10) days prior to the effective date of cancellation, if a
   licensee&#8217;s policy is to be canceled. It shall be the licensee&#8217;s
   responsibility to inform his insurer of this requirement.

   2403.4. Nothing in this section shall be construed to relieve any person from
   liability for any damages to the person or lands of another caused by the use
   of pesticides even though the use conforms to regulations prescribed by the
   Mayor.

   2403.5. A pesticide operator&#8217;s license shall not be issued until the
   applicant has filed with the Director of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs
   evidence of financial security in the form of liability insurance from an
   insurer authorized to do business in the District of Columbia.

   2403.6. The liability insurance shall be kept in full force and effect as long
   as the licensee is engaged in those categories for which the license is
   issued.

   2403.7. The requirements for minimum financial responsibility for bodily
   injury and property damage shall consist of liability insurance with limits
   not less than the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	For bodily injury $100,000 each person, $300,000 each occurrence; and
   
   
   
   (b)	For property damage $15,000 each occurrence, $30,000 annual aggregate
   provision.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Section 8 of the Pesticide Operations Act of 1977, D.C. Law 2-70, 25
   DCR 6867, 6885, and 6886 (February 17, 1978), as amended by Final Rulemaking
   published at 25 DCR 3044 (October 6, 1978), incorporating Proposed Rulemaking
   published at 25 DCR 2118 (August 25, 1978).
