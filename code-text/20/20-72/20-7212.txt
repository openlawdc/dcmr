                                  DC REGULATIONS

FEES (ยง 20-7212)

7212.1. The lead agency shall charge the applicant a fee for the review of the
   EISF and the EIS.

   7212.2. The EISF and EIS review fee is fifty-five dollars ($55) per hour.

   7212.3. For any EISF or EIS, the total review fee shall not exceed one percent
   (1.0%) of the total project cost of the proposed major action.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 2799 (May 9, 1997); as amended
   by Fiscal Year 2011 Budget Support Act of 2010, effective September 24, 2010
   (D.C. Law 18-223), published at 57 DCR 6242, 6368 (July 23, 2010).
