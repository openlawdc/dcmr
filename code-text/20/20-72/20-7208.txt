                                  DC REGULATIONS

PUBLIC REVIEW OF ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT STATEMENTS (ยง 20-7208)

7208.1. The lead agency shall transmit a copy of the completed EIS to the
   Council of the District of Columbia, any District agency that has
   responsibility for implementing the major action or that has special expertise
   with respect to any environmental impact involved, and any affected Advisory
   Neighborhood Commission.

   7208.2. The lead agency shall publish in the D.C. Register a notice of the
   availability of the EIS for a forty-five day (45) public comment period.

   7208.3. The lead agency shall make available to the public for inspection a
   copy of the EIS, by providing a copy of the EIS in its main office and in the
   M.L. King Public Library.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 2799 (May 9, 1997).
