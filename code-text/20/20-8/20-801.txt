                                  DC REGULATIONS

SULFUR CONTENT OF FUEL OILS (ยง 20-801)

801.1. The purchase, sale, offer for sale, storage, transport, or the use of,
   fuel oil which contains more than one percent (1%) sulfur by weight in the
   District is prohibited, if the fuel oil is to be burned in the District.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Section 3 of the District of Columbia Air Pollution Control Act of
   1984, D.C. Law 5-165, ยง 801, 32 DCR 565.644 (February 1, 1985).
