                                  DC REGULATIONS

NITROGEN OXIDE EMISSIONS (ยง 20-804)

804.1. No person shall discharge, or cause the discharge into the atmosphere
   of nitrogen oxides from fossil fuel-fired steam generating units of more than
   one hundred million (100,000,000) British Thermal Units (BTU) per hour heat
   input in excess of the emission limits set forth in Appendix 8-1.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Section 3 of the District of Columbia Air Pollution Control Act of
   1984, D.C. Law 5-165, ยง 804, 32D CR 565, 645 (February 1, 1985).
