                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORD KEEPING (ยง 20-1008)

1008.1. The owner or operator of each budget source shall retain records of
   all measurements, data, reports, and other information required by this
   Chapter for a period of at least five (5) years.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by final rulemaking published at 47 DCR 8646(October 10,
   2000); as amended by final rulemaking published at 47 DCR 9686 (December 8,
   2000).
