                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 20-1001)

1001.1. No later than December 31, 2000, and each December 31st thereafter,
   the owner or operator of each budget source shall hold a quantity of
   allowances in the budget source&#8217;s current year NOx Allowance Tracking
   System (NATS) account, as described in Section 1006, that is equal to or
   greater than the total tons of NOx emitted from that budget source during that
   year&#8217;s control period.

   1001.2. A budget source that begins operation after the initial allocation has
   been made shall obtain allowances to operate during the control period.
   Allowances may be obtained from existing budget sources.

   1001.3. Allowances shall not be used to meet or exceed limitations of any
   permit or regulation unrelated to this Chapter.

   1001.4. A budget source may achieve compliance by:
   
   
   
   (a)	Reducing NOx emissions from the budget source;
   
   
   
   (b)	Acquiring allowances from another budget source consistent with applicable
   federal requirements; or
   
   
   
   (c)	A combination of the options in paragraphs (a) and (b).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: As amended by final rulemaking published at 47 DCR 8646(October 10,
   2000); as amended by final rulemaking published at 47 DCR 9686 (December 8,
   2000).
