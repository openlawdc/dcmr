                                  DC REGULATIONS

CURRENT EDITIONS OF GREEN BUILDING STANDARDS (§ 20-3501)

3501.1. The current edition of the LEED® standard is LEED® 2009.

   3501.2. The current edition of the Green Communities standard is Green
   Communities 2008.

   3501.3. For both newly constructed and substantially improved public schools,
   the District shall aspire to meet LEED for Schools certification at the Gold
   level or higher.
