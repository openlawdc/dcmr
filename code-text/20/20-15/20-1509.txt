                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSPORTATION CONFORMITY – ENFORCEABILITY OF DESIGN CONCEPT AND SCOPE AND
PROJECT-LEVEL MITIGATION AND CONTROL MEASURES (§ 20-1509)

1509.1. Written commitments to mitigation measures must be obtained prior to a
   positive conformity determination and project sponsors must comply with such
   commitments.

   incorporating text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 3132, 3142
   (April 24, 2009).
