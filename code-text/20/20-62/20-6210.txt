                                  DC REGULATIONS

UPPER CONCENTRATION LIMITS FOR GROUNDWATER (ยง 20-6210)

6210.1. The Upper Concentration Limits (UCL) for benzene in ground water at a
   site contaminated by an underground storage tank shall be fifteen parts per
   million (15 ppm) or 15,000 parts per billion (15,000 ppb).

   6210.2. The Director shall establish UCLs for other chemicals of concern in
   ground water.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 7835, 7896 (November 12, 1993);
   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 7699 (October 1, 1999).
