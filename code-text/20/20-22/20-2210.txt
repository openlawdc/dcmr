GENERAL REQUIREMENTS FOR PESTICIDES (ยง 20-2210)

2210.1. Registered pesticides shall be used in strict accordance with the
   manufacturer&#8217;s labeling directions, and in compliance with District and
   federal laws and regulations.

   2210.2. Pesticide equipment or application apparatus shall be in sound
   mechanical condition and capable of satisfactory operation.

   2210.3. Pesticide containers shall not be used for any purposes other than
   containing the original labeled product.

   2210.4. The water supply for any equipment being filled shall be protected
   from contamination by means of an effective anti-siphon device.

   2210.5. Pesticide applications shall be made in a manner to prevent harmful
   effects to the environment.

   2210.6. No pesticide application shall be made by air or ground equipment
   whenever the wind velocity will cause the pesticide to drift beyond the target
   area.

   2210.7. Pesticides in leaking, broken, corroded, or otherwise damaged
   containers, or with damaged or obscure labels, shall not be displayed or
   offered for sale.

   2210.8. The inspection of premises to determine the presence of termites or
   other wood destroying organisms for the purpose of issuing wood infestation
   certificates shall only be performed by pesticide operators licensed in the
   subcategory of &#8220;Wood Destroying Organisms,&#8221; as described in ยง
   2301.1(d)(2) of Chapter 23 of this subtitle.
