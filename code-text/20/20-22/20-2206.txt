RESTRICTED USE PESTICIDES (ยง 20-2206)

2206.1. The official designation of restricted use pesticides as declared by
   the EPA under the provisions of ยง 3(d)(1)(c) of FIFRA shall be adopted by the
   District.

   2206.2. For the protection, health, and welfare of the people in the District,
   and after a public hearing, the Director may add to the official designation

   in the federal regulations.
