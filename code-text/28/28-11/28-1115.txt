                                  DC REGULATIONS

SUBGRANT APPLICATION APPEALS (ยง 28-1115)

1115.1. Each applicant for an action grant under the provision of the Omnibus
   Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968, as amended (currently referred to
   as the Crime Control Act of 1973), or under the Juvenile Justice Delinquency
   Prevention Act of 1974, who has been denied all or a discernable portion of
   the funds requested to conduct a project for which funds are available, shall
   be entitled to appeal to the Criminal Justice Supervisory Board or to an
   Appeals Committee of the Board, the decision on his or her application.

   1115.2. The Office of Criminal Justice Plans and Analysis shall notify each
   applicant in writing when an application has been denied, or denied and
   deferred, and the reasons for the action.

   1115.3. Notification shall be generally consistent with requirements set forth
   in ยง 303(9)(15) of the Crime Control Act of 1973.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 27 DCR 1121, 1132 (March 14, 1980).
