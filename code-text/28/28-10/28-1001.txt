                                  DC REGULATIONS

MEMBERS AND ALTERNATES (ยง 28-1001)

1001.1. Appointed members of the Board shall serve two (2) year terms.

   1001.2. Ex-officio members of the Board shall be members as long as they
   continue to hold the office or position which entitles them to Board
   membership.

   1001.3. Alternates may be designated for all members. Appointments of
   alternates shall be effective only upon prior written designation from the
   member to the Chairperson or to the Executive Director.

   1001.4. If an appointed member is absent from three (3) consecutive meetings
   of the Board, the position of that member shall be deemed to be vacant as of
   the third (3rd) meeting, and a successor shall be appointed to fill the
   unexpired term of that member.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 27 DCR 1111, 1112 (March 14, 1980).
