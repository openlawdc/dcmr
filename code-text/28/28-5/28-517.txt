                                  DC REGULATIONS

RIOT PROCEDURES (ยง 28-517)

517.1. In the event of a riot, the Administrator or higher ranking official
   within the chain-of-command may order immediate detention of any resident for
   a period of up to forty-eight (48) hours.

   517.2. Continued detention shall be authorized in writing by the Administrator
   or higher ranking official within the chain-of-command.

   517.3. Upon restoration of order, the normal disciplinary procedures shall be
   reinstated within a reasonable time. All residents who were detained under the
   riot provisions shall appear before the Adjustment Board or Hearing Officer in
   accordance with the procedures set forth in this chapter, or shall be returned
   to the area in the prison where they were assigned prior to detention.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 865, 888-89 (February 27, 1981).
