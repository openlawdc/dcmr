                                  DC REGULATIONS

SEGREGATION PENDING LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCY INVESTIGATIONS (ยง 28-530)

530.1. When a resident is confined in segregation pending investigation for
   possible criminal prosecution, the Administrator (or his or her designee)
   shall maintain contact with the law enforcement agency handling the
   investigation.

   530.2. The resident shall be notified every fifteen (15) days of the
   agency&#8217;s report of the progress of the investigation.

   530.3. Upon notification by the investigating law enforcement agency that no
   criminal prosecution will be initiated against the resident, the resident
   shall be released from segregation unless, within three (3) working days of
   receipt of that notification, the Board determines, after a housing hearing
   held in accordance with the provisions of this chapter, that the resident is
   in danger from a threat to the resident&#8217;s personal safety, poses a
   danger to the safety of others, or is a definite escape risk.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 865, 899 (February 27, 1981).
