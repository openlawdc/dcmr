                                  DC REGULATIONS

STANDING COMMITTEES (ยง 28-2803)

2803.1. The following standing committees shall be established by the
   Chairperson: Grant Review and Evaluation, Public Information and Community
   Affairs, Finance.

   2803.2. The Chairperson shall establish other standing committees as are
   deemed necessary and shall appoint one member of the Trust to chair each
   committee.

   2803.3. The Chairperson may reassign members of standing committees and may
   replace an assigned chairperson of a standing committee with another member.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 2751, 2752 (April 30, 1993).
