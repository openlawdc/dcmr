                                  DC REGULATIONS

AMENDMENT (ยง 28-2810)

2810.1. This chapter may be amended by a seven (7) or more member vote at any
   regular meeting. The proposed amendment shall have been submitted in writing
   to the Trust at a previous regular meeting and each member shall be given a
   fifteen (15) day written notice of the proposed amendment.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 2751, 2754 (April 30, 1993).
