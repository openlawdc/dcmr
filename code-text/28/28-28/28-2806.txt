                                  DC REGULATIONS

VACANCY IN THE OFFICE OF THE CHAIRPERSON (ยง 28-2806)

2806.1. If a vacancy occurs in the office of the Chairperson, the Vice
   Chairperson shall assume the duties of the Chairperson and the Treasurer shall
   assume the duties of the Vice Chairperson until such time as new officers are
   elected.

   2806.2. If vacancies shall occur in elected offices, the Trust shall elect the
   officers to fill the unexpired terms.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 2751, 2753 (April 30, 1993).
