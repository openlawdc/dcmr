                                  DC REGULATIONS

INTERVIEWS WITH INFORMED PERSONS (ยง 28-2041)

2041.1. The Commission shall interview the Chief Judge of the requesting
   judge&#8217;s court.

   2041.2. The Commission may seek pertinent information by interviews with
   others conducted by the full Commission, by one or more members, or by a
   special counsel or others of its staff.
