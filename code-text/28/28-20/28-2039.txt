                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECOMMENDATION STANDARDS (ยง 28-2039)

2039.1. A retired judge seeking a favorable recommendation for appointment or
   reappointment to a term as a senior judge shall be evaluated by the Commission
   through a review of the judge&#8217;s physical and mental fitness and his or
   her ability to perform judicial duties.

   2039.2. The recommendation standards are as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	Favorable &#8211; The judge is physically and mentally fit and able
   satisfactorily to perform judicial duties.
   
   
   
   (b)	Unfavorable &#8211; The judge is unfit for further judicial service.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 2190, 2194 (April 3, 1987); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 6032, 6047 (September 14,
   1990); and Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 9333, 9348 (December 11,
   1992); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 108 (January 6,
   2006); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 12322 (December 21,
   2007).
