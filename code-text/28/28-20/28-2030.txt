                                  DC REGULATIONS

EVALUATION OF CANDIDATES FOR RENOMINATION (ยง 28-2030)

2030.1. Not less than six (6) months prior to the expiration of his or her
   term of office, a judge seeking reappointment shall file with the Commission a
   declaration in writing of candidacy for reappointment.

   2030.2. Judges shall be urged to file the declaration well in advance of the
   six (6) month minimum, and shall, if possible, file the declaration nine (9)
   months prior to the expiration of his or her term.

   2030.3. Not less than six (6) months prior to expiration of his or her term,
   the candidate shall submit to the Commission a written statement, including
   illustrative materials, reviewing the significant aspects of his or her
   judicial activities that the judge believes may be helpful to the Commission
   in its evaluation of his or her candidacy.

   Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 12322 (December 21, 2007).
