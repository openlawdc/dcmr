                                  DC REGULATIONS

INTERVIEWS WITH INFORMED PERSONS (ยง 28-2033)

2033.1. Ordinarily the Commission shall interview the Chief Judge of the
   candidate&#8217;s court.

   2033.2. In addition, the Commission may seek pertinent information by
   interviews with others conducted by the full Commission, by one (1) or more
   members, or by a special counsel or others of its staff.

   Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 108 (January 6, 2006); as amended by
   Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 12322 (December 21, 2007).
