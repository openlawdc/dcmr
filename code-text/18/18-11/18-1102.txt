                                  DC REGULATIONS

OFFENSES RELATED TO VEHICLE IDENTIFICATION NUMBERS (ยง 18-1102)

1102.1. No person shall deface an identification number of a vehicle for which
   a certificate is outstanding on any engine, engine block, transmission, or
   other location on a motor vehicle.

   1102.2. No person shall knowingly acquire or dispose of a vehicle for which a
   certificate is outstanding the identification number of which is defaced on
   the engine, engine block, transmission, or other location on such vehicle, or
   knowingly acquire or dispose of any such vehicle which contains any such
   defaced number.

   1102.3. No person shall knowingly have in his or her possession or operate a
   vehicle for which a certificate is outstanding the identification number of
   which is defaced as described in ยง 1102.2.

   1102.4. Upon discovery by an owner that any identification number of vehicle
   has been defaced, the owner shall make application to the Director for a DMV
   number and a new certificate.
