                                  DC REGULATIONS

BICYCLE INFRACTIONS (§ 18-2602)

2602.1. The following civil infractions and their respective fines set forth
   in this section refer to bicycles and the operation of bicycles. The fine for
   any bicycle violation not listed in this section is twenty-five dollars
   ($25.00).
