                                  DC REGULATIONS

STARTING, STOPPING, AND BACKING (ยง 18-2206)

2206.1. No person shall start a vehicle which is stopped, standing, or parked
   unless and until the movement can be made with reasonable safety.

   2206.2. No person shall stop or suddenly decrease the speed of a vehicle
   without first giving an appropriate signal in the manner provided in this
   chapter to the driver of any vehicle immediately to the rear when there is
   opportunity to give such signal.

   2206.3. The driver of a vehicle shall not back the vehicle unless the backing
   movement can be made with reasonable safety and without interfering with other
   traffic.
