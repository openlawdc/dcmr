RIGHT-OF-WAY[COLON] SCHOOL BUSES AND MULTI-PURPOSE SCHOOL VEHICLES (ยง 18-2209)

2209.1. The driver of a vehicle approaching from any direction a school bus or
   a multi-purpose school vehicle on which a warning light is flashing or a stop
   signal arm is actuated, shall bring the vehicle to a complete stop not less
   than fifteen feet (15 ft.) from the school bus or multi-purpose school
   vehicle; provided, that this requirement shall not apply to a driver
   approaching a school bus or multi-purpose school vehicle from the opposite
   direction on a street with a median strip divider.

   2209.2. A driver who has stopped for a school bus or multi-purpose school
   vehicle in accordance with this section shall not proceed until the warning
   lights are no longer flashing and the stop signal arm is no longer actuated.
