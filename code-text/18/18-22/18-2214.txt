                                  DC REGULATIONS

ENTERING AND LEAVING A VEHICLE[COLON] VEHICLE DOORS (§ 18-2214)

2214.1. No person shall board or alight from a vehicle while the vehicle is in
   motion.

   2214.2. No driver shall permit any person to board or alight from the vehicle
   he or she is driving while the vehicle is in motion.

   2214.3. No motor vehicle shall be operated with any front door(s),
   sidedoor(s), or rear door(s) tied open or swinging.

   2214.4. No person shall open any door of a vehicle unless it is reasonably
   safe to do so and can be done without interfering with moving traffic or
   pedestrians and with safety to such person and passengers. No person shall
   leave a door open on a side of a vehicle available to moving traffic for a
   period of time longer than necessary to receive or discharge passengers.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Commissioners’ Order 274, 310/949 effective September 1, 1949, 17
   DCRR §§ 112, 113, 115 (October 19, 1970).
