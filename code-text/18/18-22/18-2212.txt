                                  DC REGULATIONS

COASTING (ยง 18-2212)

2212.1. The driver of any motor vehicle when traveling upon a down grade shall
   not coast, with the gears of the vehicle in neutral.

   2212.2. The driver of a commercial motor vehicle when traveling upon a
   downgrade shall not coast with the clutch disengaged.
