                                  DC REGULATIONS

LOW-SPEED VEHICLES (§ 18-757)

757.1. Low-speed vehicles shall comply with the safety standards set forth in
   Federal Motor Safety Standard No. 500 at 49 C.F.R. § 571.500.

   757.2. The manufacturer’s certificate of origin shall clearly identify the
   vehicle as a low-speed vehicle.

   757.3. Aftermarket conversion of manufactured non-compliant vehicles to
   low-speed vehicles not in compliance with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety
   Standards is prohibited.
