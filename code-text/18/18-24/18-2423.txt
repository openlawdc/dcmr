                                  DC REGULATIONS

STREET CLEANING PARKING PROVISIONS (ยง 18-2423)

2423.1. No person shall park any vehicle or permit any vehicle to remain
   parked during the times and days indicated on a Street Cleaning Route.

   2423.2. Parking prohibitions for street cleaning shall override any less
   restrictive parking privileges at designated locations during the times and
   days specified, except for parking restrictions imposed by the District during
   snow emergencies on designated snow emergency routes.

   2423.3. When the opposite blockface is restricted for street cleaning
   purposes, parking shall be allowed on designated streets that are otherwise
   restricted during the times and days specified.
