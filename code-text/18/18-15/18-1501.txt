                                  DC REGULATIONS

ROUTES (§ 18-1501)

1501.1. The following routes are established for the DC Circulator:
   
   
   
   (a)	Capitol Hill Loop &#8211; Union Station to points in the Capitol Hill
   neighborhood, which may include the Capitol South and Navy Yard Metro
   Stations;
   
   
   
   (b)	East-West Loop &#8211; Union Station, Massachusetts Avenue, N.W., the
   Washington Convention Center, and Georgetown;
   
   
   
   (c)	Georgetown Loop – which may include stops in Georgetown; Rosslyn,
   Virginia; Adams Morgan; Dupont Circle; and the U Street, N.W., corridor;
   
   
   
   (d)	Monuments Loop &#8211; Monuments and museums on the National Mall;
   
   
   
   (e)	North-South Loop &#8211; Mt. Vernon Square, 7th or 9th Street, NW, the
   National Mall, and Water Street, SW;
   
   
   
   (f)	Smithsonian Loop &#8211; Constitution Avenue, 1st Street, NE, 1st Street,
   SE, Independence Avenue, and 17th Street; and
   
   
   
   (g)	Woodley Park-Adams Morgan-Downtown loop – Woodley Park to Downtown,
   which may include points in Adams Morgan, Columbia Heights, and 14th Street,
   NW.

   1501.2. The routes established in § 1501.1 may operate seven (7) days a week
   between the hours of 5:00 a.m. and 3:30 a.m.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: D.C. Act 16-554 published at 53 DCR 10235 (December 29, 2006); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 7902 (August 27, 2010); as
   amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 11741 (December 10,
   2010).
