                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 18-1599)

1599.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms shall have the meaning
   ascribed:
   
   
   
   Department &#8211; District Department of Transportation.
   
   
   
   Metrobus &#8211; A bus operated by the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit
   Authority.
   
   
   
   Metrorail &#8211; A train operated by the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit
   Authority.
   
   
   
   Rechargeable fare media &#8211; a plastic, permanent farecard that may be
   
   issued by the Department or WMATA embedded with technology that
   
   keeps track of the fare value on the farecard, which may include tracking
   
   transfers, weekly passes, and monthly passes.  A WMATA SmartTrip
   
   Card is an example of a rechargeable fare media.
   
   
   
   WMATA or Metro &#8211; the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: D.C. Act 16-554 published at 53 DCR 10235 (December 29, 2006); as
   amended by Final rulemaking published at 57 DCR 7902, 7903 (August 27, 2010);
   as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 11741 (December
   10, 2010).
