                                  DC REGULATIONS

TAXICAB STANDS (§ 18-4026)

4026.1. Taxicab stands shall be established at the locations listed in this
   section.
   
   
   
   (a)	14th Street N.W.
   
   
   
   From Park Road to Irving Street, first layby south of Park Road on the west
   side, “No Parking Taxicab Stand.”.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: 17 DCRR § 40.116, Special Edition (September 28, 1970); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 3211 (May 5, 2000); as amended by
   Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 6376 (June 6, 2008).
