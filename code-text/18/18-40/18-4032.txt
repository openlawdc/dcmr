                                  DC REGULATIONS

CURB LOADING ZONES (ยง 18-4032)

4032.1. At the following location, the loading of vehicles shall be permitted
   only at curb space. All vehicles shall be prohibited from double parking to
   load or unload and from obstructing or impeding moving traffic.
   
   
   
   (a)	Eye Street, N.W.
   
   
   
   From 15th Street to 20th Street, on the north side, &#8220;Commercial Vehicles
   Only, Curb Lane Loading, 9:30 a.m. &#8211; 4:00 p.m., Monday &#8211;
   Friday&#8221;.
