SIGHTSEEING LIMOUSINE AND BUS STANDS (ยง 18-4027)

4027.1. Sightseeing limousine stands and sightseeing bus stands shall be
   established at the locations listed in this section.
   
   
   
   (a)	E Street, N.W.
