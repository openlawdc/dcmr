                                  DC REGULATIONS

ARTERIAL HIGHWAYS AND BOULEVARDS (§ 18-4003)

4003.1. The streets or parts of streets listed in this section are designated
   and established as arterial highways or boulevards.

   4003.2. The streets or parts of streets listed in this section shall also be
   known or referred to as “through streets.”
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: 17 DCRR § 40.101, Special Edition (September28, 1970).
