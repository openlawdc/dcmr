BICYCLE USE OF SIDEWALK AREAS (ยง 18-4029)

4029.1. The riding of bicycles is prohibited on sidewalks within the Central
   Business District, as defined in this title, except on the streets listed in
   this subsection.

   4029.2. The riding of bicycles on sidewalks is permitted outside of the
   Central Business District, except on those streets listed in this subsection.
