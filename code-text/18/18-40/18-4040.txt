                                  DC REGULATIONS

WORK ZONES (ยง 18-4040)

4040.1. The Director shall require the placement of WORK ZONE signs, which
   conform to the specifications established by the Chief Engineer of the
   District of Columbia, at the point of ingress of highway and roadway work zone
   locations.

   4040.2. Persons managing work zones are required to install WORK ZONE signs at
   all ingress points to a work zone.

   4040.3. Persons managing work zones are required to remove or cover WORK ZONE
   signs when workers are not actually performing work in their official
   capacity.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 3939 (May 15, 2009).
