   4023.4. Three (3) hour parking meter zones are established on the streets or
   portions of streets listed in this subsection.

   4023.5. Three (3) hour Motorcycle Parking Meter Zones are established on the
   streets or portions of street listed in this subsection and they are
   established for the days and hours specified for each location.	No person
   shall park any vehicle other than a motorcycle, motor-driven cycle, motor
   scooter, or motorized bicycle during the days and hours specified that the
   location is designated as a motorcycle parking meter zone.

   4023.6. The parking meter zones established under this section shall be
   designated by parking meters and marked parking spaces.

   4023.7. Restrictions on parking in zones established under this section are in
   addition to other restrictions which may be applicable under the provisions of
   this title.
   
   
   
   (a)	New York Avenue, N.W.
   
   
   
   From a point approximately 138 feet west of 5th Street to a point
   approximately 209 feet west of 5th Street, on the north side, &#8220;Twenty
   Minute Parking, at all other times.
