                                  DC REGULATIONS

BICYCLE LANES (ยง 18-4033)

4033.1. The lane(s) as indicated on the following streets shall be reserved
   for the use of bicycles and motorized bicycles; the operation of all other
   vehicles shall be prohibited within this lane.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 3894 (September 4, 1981),
   incorporating text of Proposed Rulemaking at 28 DCR 3042 (July 3, 1981).
