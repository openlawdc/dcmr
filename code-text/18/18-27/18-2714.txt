                                  DC REGULATIONS

DESIGNATION OF RESERVED SPACE (§ 18-2714)

2714.1. Upon approval of an application for a reserved space, the Director
   shall assign a space number and cause the posting of signs which shall denote
   the space. The sign shall contain the universal symbol for the disabled and
   the language, “Reserved, Parking by Special Permit No._, Only.” A reserved
   space shall not exceed twenty feet (22 ft.) in length.
