                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISABILITY PARKING REVIEW COMMITTEE (§ 18-2712)

2712.1. The Disability Parking Review Committee shall consist of the following
   members:
   
   
   
   (a)	A representative of the Mayor’s Committee on Persons with Disabilities;
   
   
   
   (b)	A representative of the Commission on Public Health;
   
   
   
   (c)	A representative of the Transportation Systems Administration; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Two (2) representatives of non-profit organizations representing
   individuals with disabilities.
