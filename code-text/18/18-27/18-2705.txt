LOSS OR THEFT OF SPECIAL LICENSE TAGS OR PERMIT (ยง 18-2705)

2705.1. Loss or theft of the permit or tags shall be reported to the Director
   and to the police precinct in which the loss or theft occurred. The person
   with a disability shall give similar notice if the permit is recovered.

   2705.2. After each report of a lost or stolen tag or permit as provided in ยง
   2705.1, a person may apply for a duplicate tag or permit, up to two (2) times
   in a one (1) year period.
