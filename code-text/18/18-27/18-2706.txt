                                  DC REGULATIONS

SURRENDER OF SPECIAL LICENSE TAGS OR PERMIT (§ 18-2706)

2706.1. Whenever the special license tags or special parking permit issued
   pursuant to this chapter is no longer needed by the applicant, or the
   applicant is no longer eligible for the privileges under this chapter, the
   applicant or the applicant’s representative shall, within ten (10) days,
   notify and surrender the tags or permit to the Director.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 5692, 5698 (August 31, 1990).
