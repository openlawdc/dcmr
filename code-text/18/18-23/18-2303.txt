                                  DC REGULATIONS

RIGHT-OF-WAY IN CROSSWALKS (ยง 18-2303)

2303.1. [Repealed]

   2303.2. No pedestrian shall suddenly leave a curb, safety platform, safety
   zone, loading platform, or other designated place of safety and walk or turn
   into the path of a vehicle which is so close that it is impossible for the
   driver to yield.

   2303.3. No pedestrian shall cross a roadway intersection diagonally unless
   authorized by official traffic control devices.

   2303.4. When authorized to cross diagonally, pedestrians shall cross only in
   accordance with the official traffic control devices pertaining to such
   crossing movements.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: D.C. Act 7-62 published at 34 DCR 5316 (August 14, 1987).
