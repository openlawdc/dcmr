                                  DC REGULATIONS

WITNESSES (ยง 18-1031)

1031.1. Witnesses at a hearing shall testify under oath or affirmation.

   1031.2. The examiner may examine the witnesses and shall allow the parties or
   their representatives to do so.

   1031.3. If the examiner conducts the examination of a witness, he or she may
   allow the parties to suggest matters as to which they desire the witness to be
   questioned, and the examiner shall do so if such matters are relevant and
   material to any issue pending for decision before him or her.
