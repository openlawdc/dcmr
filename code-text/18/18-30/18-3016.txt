                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEALS WITHOUT TRANSCRIPTS (ยง 18-3016)

3016.1. Except as provided for in this section, an appellant shall obtain a
   transcript pursuant to ยง 3017.

   3016.2. An appellant may file an appeal without a transcript in an appeal of a
   final determination of a notice of infraction issued for a parking infraction.

   3016.3. An appellant may file an appeal without a transcript in an appeal
   where the only issue being appealed is the appropriateness of the fines and
   penalties assessed.
