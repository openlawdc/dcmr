                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEAL FEE (ยง 18-3015)

3015.1. A fee of ten dollars ($ 10) must be paid at the time an appeal is
   filed, unless waived by the Director or his or her authorized agent in cases
   of extreme hardship. This fee shall be refunded to any appellant who is
   successful in an appeal.

   3015.2. No notice of appeal will be accepted for filing by the Appeals Board
   unless accompanied by the appeal fee, except in those cases where the fee has
   been waived.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: D.C. Act 15-295 published at 51 DCR 1340 (February 6, 2004).
