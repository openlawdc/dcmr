                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICATION FOR A COMMERCIAL DRIVERS LICENSE (§ 18-1301)

1301.1. The application or renewal application for a commercial driver&#8217;s
   license or commercial driver&#8217;s instruction license shall include the
   following:
   
   
   
   (a) 	Applicant&#8217;s full name, and correct mailing and residential
   addresses;
   
   
   
   (b) 	Applicant&#8217;s date of birth, height, weight and sex;
   
   
   
   (c) 	Applicant&#8217;s social security number;
   
   (d) 	Applicant&#8217;s certification that he or she is not subject to any
   disqualification, or any license suspension, revocation, or cancellation under
   State or District law, and that he or she does not have a driver&#8217;s
   license from more than one State or jurisdiction;
   
   
   
   (e) 	Applicant&#8217;s consent to release driving record information and
   consent to authorize the District of Columbia to make information on the
   applicant&#8217;s traffic record available for inquiries from the Commercial
   Driver&#8217;s License Information System, National Driver Register or similar
   systems;
   
   
   
   (f)	All jurisdictions in which the applicant has previously been licensed to
   operate any type of motor vehicle;
   
   
   
   (g) 	Applicant&#8217;s implied consent to take chemical tests to determine use
   of alcohol or controlled substances;
   
   
   
   (h)	Applicant’s certification that he or she either meets, or, in the case
   of an applicant who does not operate in interstate commerce, that he or she is
   exempt from the applicable requirements of title 49 of the Code of Federal
   Regulations, Part 391; and
   
   
   
   (i)	Applicant’s certification that the motor vehicle in which the applicant
   is tested is representative of the type of commercial motor vehicle that the
   applicant operates or expects to operate.

   1301.2. All applicants for a commercial driver’s license or commercial
   driver’s instruction license shall complete and sign the application
   provided for in § 1301.1.

   1301.3. Any person who knowingly falsifies information or certifications
   required in this chapter shall be subject to suspension, revocation or
   cancellation of his or her commercial driver’s license for a period of not
   less than sixty (60) calendar days.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 10913 (November 26, 2004); Final
   Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 2206 (March 24, 2006); as amended by Notice of
   Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 10912 (November 19, 2010).
