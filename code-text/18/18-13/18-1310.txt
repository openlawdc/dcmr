                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMMERCIAL DRIVERS LICENSE CONTENT (§ 18-1310)

1310.1. The commercial driver’s license shall be marked “CDL” and shall
   include, in addition to the information included on a regular driver’s
   license, the group(s) of commercial motor vehicles that the licensee is
   authorized to operate, as specified by class in § 1312 and by endorsement in
   § 1313.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 10913 (November 26, 2004).
