                                  DC REGULATIONS

REGISTRATION CARD INCORRECTLY DRAWN (ยง 18-420)

420.1. Whenever a registration card is incorrectly drawn, the registrant
   shall, within five (5) business days after such error becomes apparent, return
   the registration card to the Director.

   420.2. The Director, when satisfied such registration card is incorrectly
   drawn, shall issue a new registration card.
