                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 18-2500)

2500.1. It shall be unlawful for any person to drive, move, or for the owner
   to cause or knowingly permit to be driven or moved, on any highway or street,
   any vehicle or vehicles of a size or weight exceeding the limitations stated
   in this chapter or otherwise in violation of the provisions of this chapter.

   2500.2. The provisions of this chapter governing size, weight, and load shall
   not apply to fire apparatus or to a vehicle operated under the terms of a
   special permit issued as provided in this chapter.
