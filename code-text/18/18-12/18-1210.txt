                                  DC REGULATIONS

REMOVAL OF BICYCLES FROM PUBLIC SPACE (§ 18-1210)

1210.1. Any bicycle left unused in public space for more than thirty (30) days
   shall be considered abandoned. The Director may remove an abandoned bicycle
   after placing notice on the bicycle for a period of at least ten (10) days.
   The Director shall attempt to identify and contact the owner of a registered
   bicycle prior to removing it from public space.

   1210.2. Bicycles removed from public space that are in working order may be
   auctioned off to the highest bidder or given free-of-charge to minors as part
   of a bicycle recreation, safety, or responsibility program. Bicycles that are
   not in working order may be disposed of as solid waste.
