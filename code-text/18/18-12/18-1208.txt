                                  DC REGULATIONS

BICYCLE RACKS (§ 18-1208)

1208.1. No bicycle rack shall be placed on public space unless a permit has
   been obtained from the Mayor.

   1208.2. [REPEALED]

   1208.3. No permit shall be issued for any rack which would unduly obstruct
   pedestrian movement.

   1208.4. There shall be no fee for rental of public space for racks.

   1208.5. Any rack placed on public space by the permittee shall be removable;
   shall be maintained in a good, clean condition; and shall not be allowed to
   deteriorate, become unsightly, or dangerous to the public.
