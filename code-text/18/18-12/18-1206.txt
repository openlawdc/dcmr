                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSPORTING BICYCLES (§ 18-1206)

1206.1. A mountable rack may be attached to a vehicle for the purpose of
   transporting a bicycle; Provided, that the number of bicycles transported in
   the rack shall not exceed the number which the rack is designed to carry.

   1206.2. No mountable rack shall extend beyond the bumper design margins of a
   vehicle in any manner which is hazardous or dangerous, nor shall any such
   mountable rack obstruct the vehicle’s stop or turn signals.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Regulation No. 72-13 effective June 30, 1972, 32 DCRR § 11.501 (a),
   Special Edition.
