                                  DC REGULATIONS

FAILURE TO APPEAR FOR A HEARING (ยง 30-406)

406.1. If a party to any proceeding under this chapter shall waive a hearing
   or, without sufficient reason, fail to appear at the time and place set for
   the hearing, the hearing examiner may proceed to hear the matter on the
   record.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 8665, 8682 (December 29, 1989).
   
   
   
   	HISTORICAL NOTE: Prior to December 29, 1989, the D.C. Charitable Games
   Control Board published Final Rulemaking at 29 DCR 2202, 2210 (May 28, 1992).
