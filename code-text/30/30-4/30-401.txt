HEARING EXAMINER (ยง 30-401)

401.1. The Executive Director shall designate a hearing examiner to conduct
   the hearing and make proposed findings of fact and conclusions of law.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 8665, 8680 (December 29, 1989).

   Control Board published Final Rulemaking at 29 DCR 2202, 2210 (May 28, 1992).
