                                  DC REGULATIONS

GAME INSTRUCTIONS (ยง 30-701)

701	Before the commencement of a particular instant game, or any lottery game,
   or variation of a game, the Executive Director shall issue game instructions
   to agents and the public governing and implementing its operation. The
   instructions shall include the price of the ticket.

   701.2. Game instructions shall be made available to the public at agent
   locations and the Agency&#8217;s principal offices.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7439, 7449 (October 2, 1992).
   
   
   
   	HISTORICAL NOTE: Prior to October 2, 1992, the D.C. Charitable Games Control
   Board published Final Rulemakings at 29 DCR 2202, 2212 (May 28, 1982).
