                                  DC REGULATIONS

MEMBER RESPONSIBLE FOR GROSS RECEIPTS (ยง 30-1302)

1302.1. Each licensed organization shall designate a member of the licensed
   organization who shall be in charge of and primarily responsible for the
   proper use and disposition of all gross receipts.

   1302.2. The member responsible for gross receipts shall be bonded.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3788, 3802 (May 20, 1988).
