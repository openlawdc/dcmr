LIST REQUIRED TO BE MAINTAINED (ยง 30-1300)

1300.1. Each licensed organization shall maintain and keep a current list of
   membership available for inspection at all reasonable times.

   1300.2. Upon request of the Board, a licensed organization shall file lists of
   its current members, officers, and directors and supplementary lists of
   additions, deletions, and other changes required to keep the original list
   up-to-date.
