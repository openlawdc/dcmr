                                  DC REGULATIONS

NONASSIGNMENT AND NONACCELERATION OF THE PRIZE (§ 30-607)

607.1. No rights of any person to a prize or a portion of a prize shall be
   assignable.

   607.2. No payment of an annuitized prize shall be accelerated beyond the
   normal dates of payment, except as provided for in the POWERBALL® game in
   §908.4.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7439, 7447 (October 2, 1992); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 6959, 6960 (October 1, 1993).
   
   
   
   	HISTORICAL NOTE: Prior to October 2, 1992, the D.C. Lottery and Charitable
   Games Control Board published Final Rulemaking at 29 DCR 2202, 2211 (May 28,
   1982).
