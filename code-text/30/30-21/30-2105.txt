                                  DC REGULATIONS

DETERMINATIONS AND FINDINGS (§ 30-2105)

2105.1. The following provisions of Chapter 12 of Title 27 DCMR relating to
   determinations and findings shall apply to procurements by the Agency:
   
   
   
   (a)	Title 27 DCMR, Chapter 12, §§ 1206.1 through 1206.7;
   
   
   
   (b)	Title 27 DCMR, Chapter 12, § 1207; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Title 27 DCMR, Chapter 12, § 1208.

   2105.2. The provisions of § 1206.8 of Title 27 DCMR shall not apply to
   procurement by the Agency. Wherever certification of a determination and
   findings (&#8220;D&#038;F&#8221;) is required under this title or Title 27
   DCMR, the D&#038;F shall be certified by the Executive Director.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6711 (September 22, 1989).
