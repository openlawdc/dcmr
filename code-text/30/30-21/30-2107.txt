                                  DC REGULATIONS

POSTAWARD ORIENTATION (§ 30-2107)

2107.1. The provisions of § 1211 of Title 27 DCMR shall govern the use of
   post award orientation by contracting officers of the Agency.

   2107.2. The provisions of §§ 1212 and 1213 of Title 27 DCMR shall govern the
   use and conduct of post award conferences, including post award conferences
   with subcontractors, by contracting officers of the Agency.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6712 (September 22, 1989).
