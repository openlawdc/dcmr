                                  DC REGULATIONS

GAME SELL OUT PROHIBITION (§ 30-805)

805.1. Game sell outs shall be prohibited and no person shall directly and
   knowingly sell a ticket or combination of tickets to any person or entity
   which would guarantee the purchaser a prize, including a POWER BALL® Grand
   Prize.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 6959, 6961 (October 1, 1993).
