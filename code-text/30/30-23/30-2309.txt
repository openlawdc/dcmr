                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICE TO OFFERORS OF LATE ACTIONS (ยง 30-2309)

2309.1. If a proposal, or modification or withdrawal of proposal, is received
   late, and it is clear from available information that it cannot be considered,
   the contracting officer shall promptly notify the offeror.

   2309.2. If a late proposal, or late modification or withdrawal of proposal, is
   transmitted by registered or certified mail and is received before award, but
   it is not clear from available information whether it can be considered, the
   offeror shall be promptly notified.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6745 (September 22, 1989).
