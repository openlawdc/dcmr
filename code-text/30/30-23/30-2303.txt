                                  DC REGULATIONS

SOLICITATIONS FOR INFORMATION AND PLANNING PURPOSES (ยง 30-2303)

2303.1. When information necessary for planning purposes cannot be obtained
   from prospective offerors by more economical and less formal means, the
   contracting officer may determine in writing that a solicitation for
   information and planning purposes is justified. If this determination is
   approved by the Director of Procurement, the contracting officer shall then
   issue the solicitation.

   2303.2. A contracting officer shall not use a formal RFP as a solicitation for
   information or planning purposes.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6741 (September 22, 1989).
