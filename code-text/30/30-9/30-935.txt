                                  DC REGULATIONS

RACE2RICHES® VALIDATION AND PRIZE CLAIMS (§ 30-935)

935.1. If a player validates a winning RACE2RICHES® ticket and a portion of
   the RACE2RICHES®  ticket has draw(s) remaining, a RACE2RICHES®  Exchange
   Ticket will be reissued to the player at no additional cost. A RACE2RICHES® 
   Exchange Ticket shall contain the exact RACE2RICHES®  Game Play and future
   drawing date(s) appearing on the validated RACE2RICHES®  ticket it is
   replacing and shall have all other characteristics of a RACE2RICHES®  ticket
   except as otherwise stated in these rules. An Exchange Ticket shall not
   contain a Ticket price.

   935.2. RACE2RICHES® Exchange Tickets are valid beginning with the next draw
   through the last draw on the original RACE2RICHES®  Ticket.
   
                 Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 10832,
   10910 (November 19, 2010).
