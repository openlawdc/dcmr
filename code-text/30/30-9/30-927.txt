                                  DC REGULATIONS

KENO VALIDATION AND PRIZE CLAIMS (ยง 30-927)

927.1. If a player validates a winning KENO Ticket and a portion of the KENO
   Ticket has draw(s) remaining, a KENO Exchange Ticket will be reissued to the
   player at no additional cost. A KENO Exchange Ticket shall contain the exact
   KENO Game Play and future drawing date(s) appearing on the validated KENO
   Ticket it is replacing and shall have all other characteristics of a KENO
   Ticket except as otherwise stated in these rules. An Exchange Ticket shall not
   contain a Ticket price.

   927.2. KENO Exchange Tickets are valid beginning with the next draw through
   the last draw on the original KENO Ticket.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 1520 (February 14, 2003).
