DC JACKPOT PRIZE POOL AND PRIZE STRUCTURE (ยง 30-952)

952.1. DC JACKPOT tickets will be drawn from a pool of two hundred forty
   thousand (240,000) tickets for each of the three price points: two dollars
   ($2); five dollars ($5); or ten dollars ($10) per ticket.
