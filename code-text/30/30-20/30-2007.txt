                                  DC REGULATIONS

CANCELLATION OF A SHELTERED MARKET SOLICITATION (ยง 30-2007)

2007.1. If the contracting officer cancels a sheltered market solicitation for
   any reason, the contracting officer shall provide a copy of the determination
   to the Executive Director and the Board.

   2007.2. If the service or supply is still required after the contracting
   officer cancels a sheltered market solicitation, the contracting officer may
   resolicit the requirement in the open market.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6687 (September 22, 1989).
