                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROCUREMENT TRAINING (§ 30-2038)

2038.1. The provisions of Chapter 45 of Title 27 DCMR shall apply to
   procurement by the Agency, except as provided otherwise in this section.

   2038.2. The Director of Procurement may utilize programs established by the
   Director, under Chapter 45 of Title 27 DCMR to meet the requirements of this
   section.

   2038.3. The provisions of §§4500.5 and 4501 of Title 27 DCMR shall not apply
   to procurement by the Agency.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6700 (September 22, 1989).
