                                  DC REGULATIONS

THE EQUAL OPPORTUNITY FOR LOCAL, SMALL AND DISADVANTAGED BUSINESS ENTERPRISES
ACT (§ 30-2010)

2010.1. Agency solicitations and contracts which are issued or awarded under
   this title shall be consistent with the requirements of D.C. Law 9-217 (D.C.
   Code §1-1152 et seq. (1992 Repl. Vol.)) and the rules or regulations which
   are promulgated pursuant thereto including, but not limited to, provisions for
   set-asides, preferences, joint-ventures, subcontracting, and certification.

   2010.2. The provisions of §2010.1 shall apply to Mayor&#8217;s orders and
   issuances of any agency or officer of the government of the District of
   Columbia made pursuant to the Act or its implementation.

   2010.3. Notwithstanding the provisions of §§2000.1 and 2000.7 of this
   chapter, where any statute, rule or regulation is referred to in this section,
   the reference shall be to its most recent amendment or version.

   2010.4. For the purpose of this section, the &#8220;Act&#8221; shall mean the
   Equal Opportunity for Local, Small and Disadvantaged Business Enterprises Act
   of 1922, D.C. Law 9-217 (D.C. Code §1-1152 et seq. (1992 Repl. Vol.).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 3235 (June 3, 1994).
