                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTRACTING FOR SERVICES (§ 30-2011)

2011.1. The provisions of §§ 1900 and 1901 of Title 27 DCMR shall apply to
   all contracts for the procurement of services, except as provided otherwise in
   this section.

   2011.2. The provisions of § 1900.2 shall not apply to procurement by the
   Agency. Requirements for competition and competitive procedures shall be
   governed by the provisions of this title.

   2011.3. The provisions of § 1901.1 shall not apply to procurement by the
   Agency. The determination whether to use expert and consultant services shall
   be based on the best interests of the Agency.

   2011.4. In accordance with the provisions of D.C. Code §1-1110(4) (1992 Repl.
   Vol.). the Executive Director may waive the requirements for formal
   advertising under this title when the services under a contract are required
   to be performed by the contractor in person and are of a professional nature.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6688 (September 22, 1989).
