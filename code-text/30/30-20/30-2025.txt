                                  DC REGULATIONS

PATENTS, COPYRIGHTS, AND PROPRIETARY INFORMATION (ยง 30-2025)

2025.1. Information which has been designated as confidential or proprietary
   by a business, in accordance with Chapter 31 of Title 27 DCMR, and which has
   been submitted by that business as part of its response to a procurement
   solicitation by the Agency, shall be treated in a confidential manner by the
   Director of Procurement and all other employees of the Agency.

   2025.2. Information which has been designated as confidential or proprietary
   by a business and submitted to the Agency under ยง2025.1 shall be disclosed
   only to employees of the Agency or District government for use in the
   procurement process and shall not be disclosed to any other person or party
   without the prior written consent of that business.

   2025.3. The provisions of Chapter 31 of Title 27 DCMR shall apply to
   procurement by the. Agency, except as provided otherwise in this section.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6696 (September 22, 1989).
