                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFECTIVE TICKETS (ยง 30-504)

504.1. A defective instant ticket is a ticket which is partially or wholly
   blank, distorted, misregistered, miscut, printed or produced in error, or a
   ticket from which the protective covering is removed or is not intact.

   504.2. The sole and exclusive remedy for a defective ticket shall be
   replacement with a ticket for the same game or a refund.

   504.3. No defective on-line ticket(s) shall be replaced after the draw break
   for the draw date printed on the ticket.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7459, 7442 (October 2, 1992).
   
   
   
   	HISTORICAL NOTE: Prior to October 2, 1992, the D.C. Lottery and Charitable
   Games Control Board published Final Rulemaking published at 29 DCR 2202, 2211
   (May 28, 1982).
