                                  DC REGULATIONS

MODIFICATION OF PURCHASE ORDERS (ยง 30-2512)

2512.1. A purchase order may be modified by using the modification form
   approved by the Director. Each purchase order modification shall identify the
   order it modifies and shall contain an appropriate modification number.
   
   
   
   2512.2.	The contracting officer shall obtain a contractor&#8217;s written
   acceptance of a purchase order modification if the written acceptance is
   determined by the contracting officer to be necessary to ensure the
   contractor&#8217;s compliance with the purchase order as revised.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6778 (September 22, 1989).
