                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRIZES (ยง 30-1605)

1605.1. The total aggregate value of all prizes to be awarded at a Monte Carlo
   night party shall not exceed one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) per
   event.

   1605.2. There shall be no cash prizes awarded at a Monte Carlo night party.

   1605.3. All prizes shall be awarded. Prizes may be distributed by either an
   auction sale or by a raffle.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3788, 3820 (May 20, 1988).
   
   
   
   	HISTORICAL NOTE: Prior to May 20, 1988, the D.C. Lottery and Charitable Games
   Control Board published Final Rulemaking at 29 DCR 5016, 5039 (November 12,
   1982).
