                                  DC REGULATIONS

FINANCIAL REPORT (ยง 30-1610)

1610.1. A financial report on a form provided by a Board shall be completed
   and filed within ten (10) days of the Monte Carlo Night party.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3788, 3822 (May 20, 1988).
   
   
   
   	HISTORICAL NOTE: Prior to May 20, 1988, the D.C. Lottery and Charitable Games
   Control Board published Final Rulemaking at 29 DCR 5016, 5039 (November 12,
   1982).
