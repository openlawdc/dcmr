                                  DC REGULATIONS

AD HOC COMMITTEE MEETING (ยง 30-106)

106.1. The Chairperson of any ad hoc committee or any two (2) members of an ad
   hoc committee may call a meeting of the committee by informing the Executive
   Director of the purpose of the meeting not less than forty-eight (48) hours
   before the meeting.

   106.2. An ad hoc committee shall be composed of at least three (3) Board
   members.

   106.3. Two (2) Board members shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of
   business.

   106.4. The affirmative vote of two (2) Board members present at the time of a
   vote shall constitute the action by an ad hoc committee.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 3704, 3708 (June 20, 1986).
