                                  DC REGULATIONS

CANCELLATIO OF INVITATIONS FOR BIDS BEFORE OPENING (ยง 30-2217)

2217.1. An IFB shall not be cancelled unless the Director determines in
   writing that cancellation is in the best interests of the Agency.

   2217.2. If an IFB is cancelled, all bids that have been received shall be
   returned unopened to the bidders.

   2217.3. The contracting officer shall send a notice of cancellation to all
   prospective bidders to which IFB&#8217;s were issued.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6723 (September 22, 1989).
