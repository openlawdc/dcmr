                                  DC REGULATIONS

TWO-STEP SEALED BIDDING (ยง 30-2250)

2250.1. The two-step sealed bidding method may be used for procurements
   requiring technical proposals, particularly those for complex items.

   2250.2. The two-step sealed bidding method shall be conducted as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	The first step consists of solicitation of technical proposals, evaluation
   of proposals, and (if necessary) discussion of the proposals; and
   
   
   
   (b)	The second step involves the submission of sealed priced bids by those who
   submitted acceptable technical proposals in step one.

   2250.3. The contracting officer shall not request, and the bidder shall not
   submit, price or price information in step one.

   2250.4. The contracting officer shall determine the acceptability of the
   supplies or services offered through clarification and discussion, if
   necessary, relating to technical proposals.

   2250.5. Bids submitted shall be evaluated and awards made in accordance with
   the provisions of this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6735 (September 22, 1989).
