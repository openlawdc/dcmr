                                  DC REGULATIONS

REINSTATEMENT ON SOLICITATION MAILING LISTS (ยง 30-2212)

2212.1. Prospective bidders that have been removed from solicitation mailing
   lists may be reinstated by the Director upon request or by filing new
   application.

   2212.2. No debarred or suspended bidder shall be reinstated on a solicitation
   mailing list during the period of a debarment or suspension.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6721 (September 22, 1989).
