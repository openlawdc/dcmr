                                  DC REGULATIONS

BID REQUIREMENTS (ยง 30-2204)

2204.1. Each bid shall be based upon specifications contained in the IFB.

   2204.2. Each bid shall be typewritten or written legibly in ink.

   2204.3. Each erasure or alteration shall be initialed by the signer in ink.

   2204.4. Each bid, except a telegraphic bid, shall be signed in ink.

   2204.5. Each bid, except a telegraphic bid, shall be submitted in an envelope
   that clearly indicates that it contains a bid and identifies the IFB.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6718 (September 22, 1989).
