                                  DC REGULATIONS

SUBMISSION OF BIDS[COLON] GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 30-2220)

2220.1. To be considered for award, a bid shall be required to comply in all
   material respects with the IFB.

   2220.2. Bids shall be filled out, executed, and submitted in accordance with
   the instructions in the IFB.

   2220.3. Bids shall be submitted so that they will be received in the office
   designated in the IFB not later than the exact time set for opening of bids.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 6681, 6723 (September 22, 1989).
