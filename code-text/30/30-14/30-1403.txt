                                  DC REGULATIONS

BINGO EQUIPMENT AND SUPPLIES (ยง 30-1403)

1403.1. A licensed organization shall purchase or receive bingo supplies and
   equipment specifically designed or adapted for use in the bingo operation only
   from a licensed supplier or another licensed organization, if the licensed
   organization selling the used equipment is currently licensed or had been
   licensed not less than twelve (12) months preceding the sale.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3788, 3804 (May 20, 1988).
   
   
   
   	HISTORICAL NOTE: Prior to May 20, 1988, the D.C. Lottery and Charitable Games
   Control Board published Final Rulemaking at 29 DCR 5016, 5028 (November 12,
   1982).
