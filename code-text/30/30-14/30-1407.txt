                                  DC REGULATIONS

PAYMENT OF WINNERS (ยง 30-1407)

1407.1. Bingo prizes, charity game prizes, and any cash door prizes shall be
   paid by funds provided by a cashier from a separate bank used only for that
   purpose, except that the foregoing shall not apply to multihall bingo prizes
   permitted by ยง1408.6.

   1407.2. In no event shall bingo prizes be paid from the bank of a checker
   collecting money from the sale of bingo cards, supplies, or charity game
   tickets.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3788, 3807 (May 20, 1988); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 2048, 2052 (April 5, 1991).
   
   
   
   	HISTORICAL NOTE: Prior to May 20, 1988, the D.C. Lottery and Charitable Games
   Control Board published Final Rulemaking at 29 DCR 5016, 5028 (November 12,
   1982).
