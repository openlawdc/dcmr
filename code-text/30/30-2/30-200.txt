                                  DC REGULATIONS

BOARD APPROVAL (§ 30-200)

200.1. For the purposes of Chapters 2 through 4, the formal title
   &#8220;Lottery and Charitable Games Control Board&#8221; and any abbreviated
   reference to the &#8220;Board&#8221; shall mean the board, appointed under
   D.C. Code §2-2501 (1994 Repl. Vol.). The term &#8220;the Agency,&#8221; shall
   mean the independent agency of the government of the District of Columbia
   which operates under the authority and direction of the Board and under the
   direct administrative and management supervision of the Executive Director
   appointed in accordance with D.C. Code §2-2503 (1994 Repl. Vol.).

   200.2. No license shall be issued, suspended, or revoked by the Agency, except
   upon the approval of the Board as provided in D.C. Official Code §3-1302 and
   in accordance with this chapter.
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is §4 of
   the Law to Legalize Lotteries, Daily Numbers Games, and Bingo and Raffles for
   Charitable Purposes in the District of Columbia, D.C. Law 3-172, D.C. Code
   §2-2501 et seq. (1994 Rept. Vol.).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 8665 (December 29, 1989).
   
   
   
   	HISTORICAL NOTE: Prior to December 29, 1989, the D.C. Lottery and Charitable
   Games Control Board published Final Rulemaking published at 29 DCR 2202 (May
   28, 1982); and at 30 DCR 1597, 1609 (April 8, 1983).
