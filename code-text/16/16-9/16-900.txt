                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 16-900)

900.1. The provisions of this chapter are adopted pursuant to the &#8220;Act
   to regulate and license pawnbrokers in the District of Columbia,&#8221;
   approved August 6, 1956, (70 Stat. 1036; D.C. Official Code §§ 47-2884.01 et
   seq.), which is also referred to in this chapter as the &#8220;Act.&#8221;

   900.2. Except as otherwise provided, the Director of the D.C. Department of
   Consumer and Regulatory Affairs or the Director&#8217;s designee (also
   referred to in this chapter as the &#8220;Director&#8221;) shall perform the
   functions vested in the Mayor by the Act with respect to licensing,
   inspection, investigation, and control of operations of pawnbroking
   operations, as set forth in this chapter and the Act.
