                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONDUCT OF HEARINGS (ยง 16-1518)

1518.1. Hearings shall be conducted as adjudicatory proceedings.

   1518.2. Parties have the right to present their cases or defenses in person or
   by counsel, by oral and documentary evidence, to submit rebuttal evidence, and
   to conduct such cross-examination as may be required for full and true
   disclosure of facts.

   1518.3. Procedurally, a complainant&#8217;s case shall be presented first, and
   the respondent&#8217;s case shall follow.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 6176, 6186 (October 10, 1986).
