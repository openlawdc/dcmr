                                  DC REGULATIONS

AUTHORIZED AGENTS (ยง 16-1206)

1206.1. No person shall act as an agent for a proprietary school located in
   the District or elsewhere, without having first obtained an agent&#8217;s
   permit from the Department.

   1206.2. Each agent representing a proprietary school shall make application to
   the Department for an agent&#8217;s permit, upon forms provided by the
   Department. An agent shall be required to obtain a separate permit for each
   proprietary school he or she represents.

   1206.3. Each application shall be accompanied by a fee to be determined by the
   Department.
