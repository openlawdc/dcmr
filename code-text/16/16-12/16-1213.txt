                                  DC REGULATIONS

ENFORCEMENT AND PENALTIES (ยง 16-1213)

1213.1. Any person violating any provision of this chapter shall, upon
   conviction, be fined not more than three hundred dollars ($ 300) or imprisoned
   for not more than ninety (90) days.

   1213.2. If any provision of this chapter is adjudged unconstitutional, or the
   applicability thereof to any person or circumstances is held invalid, the
   validity of the remainder of the chapter and the applicability of such
   provision to other persons and circumstances shall not be affected.
