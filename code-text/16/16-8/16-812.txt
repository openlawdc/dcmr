                                  DC REGULATIONS

PERMITS (ยง 16-812)

812.1. Each home improvement contractor entering into a contract for the
   performance of any home improvement work for which a permit is required by
   applicable law of the District of Columbia shall be responsible for taking
   such action as may be necessary to ensure that the work is performed only
   under the authority of the required permit and in accordance with all of its
   terms.
