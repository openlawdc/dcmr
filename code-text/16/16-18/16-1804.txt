                                  DC REGULATIONS

TERM AND RENEWAL OF LICENSES (ยง 16-1804)

1804.1. An alarm dealer license and an alarm agent license shall expire at
   12:00 midnight on July 31st of every other year.

   1804.2. A licensee shall submit a renewal application for an alarm dealer or
   alarm agent license to the Director at least thirty (30) days before the
   expiration date of the current license.

   1804.3. The Director shall approve renewal applications if the following
   conditions are met:
   
   
   
   (a)	There are no outstanding violations of the Act or this chapter;
   
   
   
   (b)	All fines or collaterals with respect to violations of the Act or this
   chapter that have become due have been paid;
   
   
   
   (c)	The applicant is in compliance with the Act, this chapter and all other
   laws and regulations of the District; and
   
   
   
   (d)	The license renewal fee has been paid.

   1804.4. If the Director fails to act on a timely renewal request before the
   expiration of the existing license, the applicant shall be considered still
   licensed until the Director acts on the renewal application.

   1804.5. The Director may for &#8220;good cause shown&#8221; grant an extension
   of time for filing renewal application.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8448, 8449 (December 2, 1988).
