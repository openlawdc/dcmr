                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSE REQUIREMENTS (ยง 16-201)

201.1. It shall be unlawful for any person to engage in the District of
   Columbia in the business of loaning money without first obtaining a money
   lender&#8217;s license.

   201.2. A Money Lender&#8217;s License Class A shall authorize the holder of
   the license to engage in the District of Columbia in the business of loaning
   money upon which a rate of interest in excess of six percent (6%) per annum is
   charged or received. The license fee for this license shall be eight hundred
   dollars ($ 800) per year.

   201.3. A Money Lender&#8217;s License Class B shall authorize the holder of
   the license to engage in the District of Columbia in the business of loaning
   money upon which a rate of interest of six percent (6%) per annum or less is
   charged or received. The license fee for this license shall be three hundred
   nineteen dollars ($ 319) per year.
