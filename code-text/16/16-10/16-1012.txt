                                  DC REGULATIONS

EXEMPT TRANSACTIONS (ยง 16-1012)

1012.1. The chapter shall not apply to transactions involving the sale or
   purchase of any of the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Coins that change substantially in relation to the precious metal content
   of those coins;
   
   
   
   (b)	Medallion form bullion; or
   
   
   
   (c)	Bullion with a recognizable hallmark, fineness stamp, and weight
   delineation.
