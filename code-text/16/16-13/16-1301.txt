                                  DC REGULATIONS

EXEMPTION OF SMALL SOLICITATIONS (§ 16-1301)

1301.1. Under the authority of § 4(d) of the Act [D.C. Official Code §
   44-1703(d)] (1981 Ed.), any person or individual who, in connection with a
   solicitation, does not actually receive contributions in excess of fifteen
   hundred dollars ($ 1,500) during a calendar year and who complies with the
   provisions of this section, shall be exempt from the provisions of D.C.
   Official Code §§ 44-1703(a), 44-1705, and 44-1706 (2001 Ed.).

   1301.2. All functions, including fund raising functions, of a person or
   individual exempt under this section shall be carried on by individuals who
   are unpaid for their services.

   1301.3. No part of the contributions collected by an individual or person
   exempt under this section shall inure to the benefit of or be paid to any
   officer, member, or solicitor.
