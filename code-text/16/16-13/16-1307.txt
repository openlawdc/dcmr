                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISPLAY OF SOLICITOR INFORMATION CARDS (ยง 16-1307)

1307.1. A solicitor information card may be displayed in any manner that the
   registrant or the individual solicitor desires, but the card shall be produced
   or displayed whenever any solicitation is made, and the person solicited shall
   be given an opportunity to examine the information contained on the card.

   1307.2. If solicitations are made at a fixed location, the registrant or
   solicitor may post an enlargement of the solicitor information card, or a sign
   in the English language containing the same information as the card.

   1307.3. The enlarged card or sign shall be prominently displayed in a manner
   that is readily legible to any prospective contributor.
