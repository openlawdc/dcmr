                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADJUDICATION BY MAIL (ยง 16-3106)

3106.1. Adjudication by mail shall be available only to a respondent who
   answers an initial NOI in a timely manner and admits the infraction with
   explanation.

   3106.2. A respondent who admits an infraction with explanation shall submit
   with the NOI all evidence of mitigating circumstances or other evidence
   relevant to the respondent&#8217;s explanation.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 5718, 5725 (September 4, 1987).
