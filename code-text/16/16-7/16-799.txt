DEFINITIONS (ยง 16-799)

799.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Director &#8211; the Director of the Department of Consumer and Regulatory
   Affairs or the Director&#8217;s representative, agent, or designee.
   
   
   
   Household goods &#8211; furniture, appliances, personal effects, and other
   property used or to be used in a dwelling.
   
   
   
   Household goods contractor or contractor &#8211; any person (except the
   federal government, any political subdivision of the federal government, or
   the government of the District of Columbia) who owns a business which
   transports household goods for a consideration.
   
   
   
   Moving contract &#8211; an agreement for the performance of transportation of
   household goods.
   
   
   
   Owner &#8211; any person, or that person&#8217;s agent, who enters into a
   moving contract for the transportation of household goods owned by that
   person.
   
   
   
   Person &#8211; an individual, partnership, corporation, or other legal entity.
   Registrant &#8211; a person registered with the Department pursuant to this
   chapter.
   
   
   
   Transportation of household goods &#8211; the act of moving the location of
   household goods from a dwelling or a place of storage within the District to a
   dwelling or to a place of storage within the District or within the
   Washington, D.C. Commercial Zone.
   
   
   
   Washington, D.C. Commercial Zone &#8211; that area adjacent to and
   commercially a part of Washington, D.C., as defined and limited in Title 49,
   ยง 1048.10 of the Code of Federal Regulations.
