                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSING FEES (ยง 16-412)

412.1. (a)	The fee for a towing business license shall be $ 775.00 per annum,
   payable biennially.
   
   
   
   (b)	The fee for a towing service storage lot license shall be $ 150.00 per
   annum, payable biennially.
   
   
   
   (c)	The fee for a towing vehicle license shall be $ 163.00 per annum, payable
   biennially.
   
   
   
   (d)	The fee for replacing a lost or damaged towing related license shall be $
   25.00.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 52 DCR 522 (January 21, 2005).
