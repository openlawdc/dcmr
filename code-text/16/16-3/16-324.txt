                                  DC REGULATIONS

DOWN PAYMENTS (ยง 16-324)

324.1. No dealer shall demand or receive, either directly or indirectly, as a
   cash down payment any payment from any buyer in excess of the amount shown on
   the retail installment contract opposite the item captioned &#8220;CASH DOWN
   PAYMENT.&#8221;

   324.2. No promissory note accepted by a dealer as all or part of the
   &#8220;CASH DOWN PAYMENT&#8221; in connection with a retail installment
   transaction shall be secured by the motor vehicle purchased in the
   transaction. Any such promissory note shall be deemed to have been accepted as
   the equivalent of cash and shall be so recorded on the retail installment
   contract.
