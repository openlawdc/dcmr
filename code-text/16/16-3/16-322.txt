                                  DC REGULATIONS

DELIVERY OF CONTRACT TO BUYER (ยง 16-322)

322.1. The original invoice shall be delivered to the purchaser at the time of
   the sale.

   322.2. The duplicate copy of the retail installment contract shall be
   delivered to the purchaser at the time the purchaser signs the instrument of
   security.
