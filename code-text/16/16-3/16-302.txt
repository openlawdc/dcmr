                                  DC REGULATIONS

LICENSE APPLICATION (ยง 16-302)

302.1. Application for a license to act as a dealer, as a salesperson, as an
   automobile repossessor, or as a sales finance company shall be filed with the
   Department of Consumer and Regulatory Affairs (also referred to as the
   &#8220;Department&#8221;) on a form prescribed by the Director of the
   Department (The Director or the Director&#8217;s designee are also referred to
   in this chapter as the &#8220;Director&#8221;).

   302.2. Each person applying for a license shall submit to the Director under
   oath any information that the Director may require to determine whether the
   applicant has the qualifications required for a license.

   302.3. Each application for a salesperson&#8217;s or an automobile
   repossessor&#8217;s license shall be accompanied by three (3) copies of a
   full-faced photograph of the applicant. Each photograph shall be one inch by
   one and one-half inches (1 in. x 1 1/2 in.) in size and shall have been taken
   not more than three (3) months prior to the date of filing the application.

   302.4. Each application for a salesperson&#8217;s license shall include a
   certification by each licensed dealer for whom the applicant proposes to act
   as a salesperson that the applicant will, if licensed, be employed by that
   dealer.
