                                  DC REGULATIONS

WARRANTIES AND GUARANTEES (ยง 16-334)

334.1. No dealer shall demand or receive any payment for a warranty or
   guarantee respecting a motor vehicle sold under a retail installment contract.

   334.2. Nothing in this section shall prohibit a dealer from including a charge
   for a warranty or guarantee in the &#8220;CASH SALE PRICE.&#8221;

   334.3. No warranty or guarantee by the dealer shall be made in any other form
   than in writing signed by the dealer.

   334.4. The dealer shall deliver a written warranty or guarantee to the buyer
   simultaneously with the delivery to the buyer of the buyer&#8217;s copy of the
   retail installment contract.
