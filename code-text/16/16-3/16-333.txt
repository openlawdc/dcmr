                                  DC REGULATIONS

HANDLING AND DELIVERY CHARGES (ยง 16-333)

333.1. No dealer shall demand or receive any payment for conditioning,
   preparation, handling, or delivery charges respecting a used or second-hand
   motor vehicle sold under a retail installment contract.

   333.2. Nothing in this section shall prohibit a dealer from including any
   charge for conditioning, preparation, handling, or delivery in the &#8220;CASH
   SALE PRICE.&#8221;
