                                  DC REGULATIONS

CIVIL PENALTIES (§ 16-1603)

1603.1. Civil fines, penalties, and fees may be imposed as additional
   sanctions for any infraction of this chapter pursuant to D.C. Official Code
   §§ 2-1801.01 et seq.

   1603.2. Adjudication of any infraction of this chapter shall be pursuant to
   D.C. Official Code §§ 2-1801.01 et seq.
