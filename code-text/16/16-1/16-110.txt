                                  DC REGULATIONS

ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF DELIVERY OF CONTRACT (ยง 16-110)

110.1. Any acknowledgment by the buyer of delivery of a copy of the contract
   shall be a rebuttable presumption of that delivery.

   110.2. Acknowledgments may contain statements to the effect that &#8220;buyer
   acknowledges that before buyer signed the contract, seller submitted the
   contract to buyer with all blank spaces filled in; that buyer had a reasonable
   opportunity to examine it; and that thereafter a legible, executed, and
   completed copy was delivered to the buyer.&#8221;
