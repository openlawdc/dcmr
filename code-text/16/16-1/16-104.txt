                                  DC REGULATIONS

EXAMINATIONS AND INVESTIGATIONS (ยง 16-104)

104.1. For the purpose of discovering violations of this chapter, the
   Department may at any time investigate the transactions, business, and records
   of any person subject to this chapter relating to matters covered by this
   chapter.

   104.2. If any records subject to this section are located outside the
   District, the registrant shall at his or her option, either make them
   available to the Department at a convenient location within the District, or
   pay the reasonable and necessary expenses for the Director of the Department
   or his or her representative to examine them at the place where they are
   maintained.

   104.3. The Director of the Department may designate representatives, including
   comparable officials of the State in which the records are located, to inspect
   them on his or her behalf.
