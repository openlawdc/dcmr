                                  DC REGULATIONS

ANTI-SCAVENGING (ยง 21-2038)

2038.1. Nothing in this chapter shall limit the right of an individual to
   donate, sell, or otherwise recycle his or her materials.

   2038.2. After materials are placed out for collection by a recycling hauler,
   the materials shall not be removed by anyone other than the hauler.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7470, 7479 (October 2, 1992); as
   amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 11962, 11965
   (December 17, 2010).
