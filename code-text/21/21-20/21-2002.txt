                                  DC REGULATIONS

PREPARING PAPER, PAPERBOARD, AND CARDBOARD FOR COLLECTION (ยง 21-2002)

2002.1 	Owners and occupants shall separate paper, paperboard, and cardboard
   from their general household solid waste and set it out for recycling.
   
   
   
   2002.2 	Paper, paperboard, and cardboard shall be placed in recycling bins
   approved by the Director or in bins of a similar size and shape and with
   similar handles.  These materials may be placed in the same recycling bin as
   metal food and beverage containers, glass food and beverage containers, and
   plastic containers placed out for recycling.

   2002.3. Contaminated or soiled paper, paperboard, or cardboard shall not be
   separated from general household waste and shall not be placed out for
   recycling purposes.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 7470, 7472 (October 2, 1992); as
   amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 11962, 11963
   (December 17, 2010).
