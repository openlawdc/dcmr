DEFINITIONS (ยง 21-199)

199.1. When used in this chapter, the following words and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   		Department &#8211; the District of Columbia Water and Sewer Authority.
   
   
   
   		Director &#8211; the General Manager of the District of Columbia Water and
   			Sewer Authority or his or her lawful agent, representative, or designee.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 1300 (February 12, 1993); as
   amended by the Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 6941, 6944
   (August 12, 2011).
