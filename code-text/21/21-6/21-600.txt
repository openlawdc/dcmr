                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONSTRUCTION, REPAIR, AND DREDGING IN D.C. WATERS (§ 21-600)

600.1. No structure shall be constructed, reconstructed, or repaired in any
   waters shoreward of the pierhead line unless the Mayor, pursuant to this
   chapter, issues a permit for the construction, reconstruction, or repair of
   the structure.

   600.2. All structures between the bulkhead line and the pierhead line shall be
   of open pile construction.

   600.3. No construction of wharves or piers or any other structures shall be
   authorized or permitted riverward beyond the pierhead line.

   600.4. No private dredging shall be done in any waters shoreward of the
   pierhead line unless the Mayor, pursuant to this chapter, issues a permit for
   the dredging.

   600.5. Private dredging shall be done in accordance with the terms set forth
   in the permit, and shall be completed within the time period prescribed in the
   permit.

   600.6. Permits are not required for repairs which do not alter any structural
   parts. These facts shall be determined in each case by the Department.
