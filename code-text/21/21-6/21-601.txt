                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICATION FOR A PERMIT (ยง 21-601)

601.1. Application for a permit to dredge, construct, reconstruct, or repair
   any structure covered by this chapter shall be made to the Department of
   Consumer and Regulatory Affairs (also referred to in this chapter as the
   &#8220;Department&#8221;).

   601.2. Each application shall be accompanied by two (2) blueprints, showing
   the proposed dredging construction, reconstruction, or repair, and a statement
   from the District Engineer, Washington District, U.S. Army Corps of Engineers,
   with respect to compliance with Department of the Army regulations.

   601.3. The Department shall refer the application and plans to the Engineer of
   Bridges for approval or suggestions for structural changes.

   601.4. All work under permits shall be inspected for compliance by the
   Department.
