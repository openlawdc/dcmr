                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 21-300)

300.1. All water meters which measure the supply of water to premises shall be
   installed by the Authority and shall be owned by the Authority. Authority
   owned water meters are all meters installed at the expense of the consumer by
   the Authority. The Authority may require the replacement of any existing meter
   &#8220;within a reasonable time frame after written notification is given to
   the owner or owner&#8217;s agent&#8221;.
