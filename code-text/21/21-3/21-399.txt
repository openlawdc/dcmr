                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 21-399)

399.1. When used in this chapter, the following words and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Authority &#8211; The District of Columbia Water and Sewer Authority.
   
   
   
   Dwelling unit &#8211; A habitable room with kitchen and bathroom facilities
   exclusively under the control of and for the use of the occupant.
   
   
   
   General Manager &#8211; The General Manager of the Authority, or his or her
   designee.
   
   
   
   Meter Ready &#8211; The circumstance that pertains after the property owner
   has created, built and installed the vault, meter risers and appurtenances so
   that the premises is ready for the installation of the meter.
   
   
   
   Meters &#8211; Includes any device which is used to measure the amount of
   water utilized by consumers.
   
   
   
   Consumer &#8211; Individuals or Businesses which use water provided by the
   Water and Sewer Authority.
   
   
   
   Commercial Water Meter &#8211; For purposes of this chapter, a
   &#8220;commercial water meter&#8221; is a meter which serves a premises used
   as other than a single family dwelling or a two-family flat, whether the
   premises is served directly from a District-owned water main or from an
   un-metered privately owned water main.
   
   
   
   Residential Meters &#8211; In general, are meters on water services that
   supply residential users that range in size from five-eighths inch (5/8 in.)
   through two inches (2 in.).
   
   
   
   Preventive Maintenance &#8211; For purposes of this chapter, the term
   &#8220;preventive maintenance&#8221; means maintenance, including testing and
   repair (if necessary and when possible) performed in accordance with the
   current American Water Works Association (AWWA) schedule based on meter size
   and recorded consumption, or as otherwise determined by the General Manager.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 1300, 1306 (February 12, 1993);
   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 782(January 24, 2003).
