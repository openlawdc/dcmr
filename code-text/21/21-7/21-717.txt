                                  DC REGULATIONS

DENIAL, SUSPENSION, OR REVOCATION OF LICENSE (§ 21-717)

717.1. Failure to permit the inspections required by this chapter shall be
   grounds for denial of an application for a solid waste collector&#8217;s
   license.

   717.2. Continued or repeated failure to comply with any of the provisions of
   this chapter shall be grounds for suspension or revocation of a solid waste
   collector&#8217;s license.

   717.3. A licensee shall be given an opportunity to answer and be heard by the
   Director upon charges against the licensee.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Regulation No. 71.21, approved June 29, 1971, 18 DCR 16 (July 12,
   1971); 8 DCRR §§8-3:611(a) and (b).
