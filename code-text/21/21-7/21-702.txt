REMOVAL OF REFUSE FROM PUBLIC SPACE ADJACENT TO PRIVATE PROPERTY (ยง 21-702)

702.1. Except as provided in ยง702.2, each owner, tenant, or lessee (or the
   agent of that person) who has control of or occupies any building, lot, or
   land within the District shall maintain in clean condition the public space
   from the property line of that building, lot, or land and extending eighteen
   inches (18 in.) from the curb line (or the lateral lines of the roadway) into
   the abutting roadway.

   702.2. Each owner, tenant, or lessee (or the agent of that person) who has
   control of or occupies any building that contains three (3) or fewer dwelling
   units within the District shall maintain in clean condition the public space
   between the curb line (or lateral lines of the roadway) and the property line
   of that building.

   702.3. Construction and demolition wastes and material shall not be collected
   by District collection services.
