CUSTOMER CLASSIFICATIONS FOR WATER AND SEWER RATES (§ 21-4104)

4104.1 	The customer classifications for water and sewer rates shall consist of
a residential class, multi-family, and a non-residential class:



(a) 	Residential – a single-family dwelling used for domestic purposes; a
condominium or apartment unit where each unit is served by a separate service
line and is individually metered and the unit is used for domestic purposes; or
a multifamily structure of less than four apartment units where all the units
are served by a single service line that is master metered.



(b) 	Multi-Family – a multifamily structure (such as a condominium or
apartment dwelling) used for domestic purposes, with four or more units.



(c) 	Non-residential – all customers not within either the residential or
multi-family class.



SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 2730 (April 10, 2009); as amended
