DISCOUNT PROGRAM[COLON] ADMINISTRATIVE APPEALS AND JUDICIAL REVIEW (ยง 21-4109)

4109.1. Any appeals related to a DDOE action regarding BMP application review
   or approval, eligibility, and stormwater retention volume calculations shall
   be taken in accordance with Section 563 of this title.

   4109.2. Appeals of DC Water actions regarding the IAC Incentive Discount shall
   be taken in accordance with Chapter 4 of this title.
