                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (§ 21-4199)

4199.1 	When used in this chapter, the following words and phrases shall have
the meanings ascribed:



Best Management Practice (BMP) &#8211; Structural or nonstructural practice that
minimizes the impact of stormwater runoff on receiving waterbodies and other
environmental resources, especially by reducing runoff volume and the pollutant
loads carried in that runoff.



Cooling Water – water used as a heat conductor in cooling towers.



DC Water &#8211; the District of Columbia Water and Sewer Authority.



DDOE &#8211; the District Department of the Environment.



Non-Potable Water Source – water from a source or sources including, but not
limited to, surface water or trucked water, but excluding water from the
District’s water distribution supply system, groundwater, or cooling water.





SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8820, 8823 (July 27, 2012); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11244 (August 2, 2013).
