                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA CLEAN RIVERS IMPERVIOUS SURFACE AREA CHARGE INCENTIVE
DISCOUNT PROGRAM[COLON] DISCOUNT REDUCTION (§ 21-4108)

4108.1 	If DDOE determines that a customer’s stormwater fee discount shall be
reduced, DC Water shall reduce the IAC Incentive Discount proportionately upon
receipt of DDOE’s decision to reduce the stormwater retention value expressed
in ERUs for the property.





SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11244 (August 2, 2013).
