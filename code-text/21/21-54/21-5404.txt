DUTY OF OWNERS TO IDENTIFY CROSS CONNECTIONS (ยง 21-5404)

5404.1. An Owner shall, upon written request made by the General Manager,
   inspect all fixtures and appurtenant Interior Plumbing and inform the General
   Manager, in writing, of all cross connections on all properties that are the
   subject of General Manager&#8217;s request.
