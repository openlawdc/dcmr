DISCIPLINARY PROCESS (ยง 21-5209)

5209.1. Employees will comply with the Authority&#8217;s regulations, policies
   and procedures, and performance standards and requirements. Employees may be
   subject to disciplinary action up to and including termination for any failure
   to comply with such regulations, policies and procedures, and performance
   standards and requirements.
