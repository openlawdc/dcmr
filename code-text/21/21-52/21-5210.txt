                                  DC REGULATIONS

NON-UNION APPEALS (ยง 21-5210)

5210.1. This regulation applies only to non-union full-time employees in
   regular positions who have completed their probationary period.

   5210.2. A non-union full-time employee in a regular position who has completed
   his or her probationary period has the right to appeal a final disciplinary
   action resulting in a suspension for a period greater than 30 days or
   termination.

   5210.3. All employees may appeal their annual performance rating in accordance
   with the Authority&#8217;s personnel policies and procedures.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 6868 (June 20, 2008).
