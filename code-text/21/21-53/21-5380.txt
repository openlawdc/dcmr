                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROTECTING THE ENVIRONMENT (ยง 21-5380)

5380.1. It is a fundamental principle of the Authority that it will respect
   and manage our finite natural resources. Accordingly, the Authority will plan
   and conduct its procurement as an environmental steward. The Authority
   recognizes that how it carries out its environmental stewardship will have
   effects on a regional and global scale.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 4484 (June 12, 2009).
