                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL STANDARDS OF ETHICAL CONDUCT (ยง 21-5304)

5304.1. Authority employees involved in the procurement process shall conduct
   business impartially and in a manner above reproach, with preferential
   treatment for none. Authority employees shall strictly avoid any conflict of
   interest or the appearance of a conflict of interest in the procurement
   process.

   5304.2. Any attempt by an employee, officer or agent to realize personal gain
   through employment with the Authority by conduct inconsistent with proper
   discharge of duties is a breach of ethical standards.

   5304.3. Any attempt by a non-employee to influence any Authority employee to
   breach the standards of ethical conduct set forth in this Section is a breach
   of ethical standards.

   5304.4. Any attempt by a Board member or alternate member to realize personal
   gain through the exercise of the duties or responsibilities of Board members
   or to influence any employee to violate the standards of ethical conduct set
   forth in this Section is a breach of ethical standards.
