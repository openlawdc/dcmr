                                  DC REGULATIONS

DIVIDING PROCUREMENTS PROHIBITED (ยง 21-5335)

5335.1. Procurements shall not be divided as a means to circumvent the
   competitive process, or to avoid the procedures applicable to procurements of
   greater value.

   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 4484 (June 12, 2009).
