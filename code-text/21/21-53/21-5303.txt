                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY (ยง 21-5303)

5303.1. The Regulations apply to all procurements made by the Authority. In
   the case of a procurement funded with federal, state or local funds, the
   Regulations shall be applied to conform to the requirements of the funding
   source, except to the extent that doing so would prevent compliance with the
   terms and conditions of other applicable federal, state, or local laws.
