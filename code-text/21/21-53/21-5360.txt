                                  DC REGULATIONS

MANDATORY CONTRACT CLAUSES (§ 21-5360)

5360.1. All Authority purchase contracts above the level of small purchases
   shall include clauses for “Changes”, “Termination for Convenience” and
   “Termination for Default”, in addition to clauses and provisions
   applicable to the type of solicitation or contract.
