                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 21-5301)

5301.1. The Regulations shall govern the procurement of goods and services,
   including construction.

   5301.2. In conformity with its statutory mandate, the Authority reserves the
   right, at any time and in its sole discretion, to modify, rescind, delete, or
   otherwise change the provisions of the Regulations.

   5301.3. The General Manager shall issue a District of Columbia Water and Sewer
   Authority (“DC WASA”) Procurement Manual setting forth guidelines and
   procedures to be followed consistent with these Regulations.

   5301.4. The Board delegates to the General Manager the authority to develop,
   implement and enforce such policies and procedures, consistent with these
   Regulations, as deemed necessary or useful.

   5301.5. The General Manager is responsible for recommending to the Board new
   procurement regulations or modifications to existing procurement regulations.
   
   
   
   5301.6 	If any provision in this chapter is deemed invalid, void or
   unenforceable by a court of competent jurisdiction, the chapter shall be
   construed as though the provision does not appear. Any such finding by a court
   of competent jurisdiction shall not affect the validity of any other
   provision, section, paragraph, or sentence of this chapter.
