                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTINUATION (ยง 21-5353)

5353.1. During resolution of a protest, all procurement activities and, where
   applicable, contractor performance, shall continue unless the Contracting
   Officer determines there is a reason to suspend or delay all or part of the
   procurement activities and/or contractor performance.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Emergency Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 613 (January 22, 1999)
   [EXPIRED]; as Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 7349 (September 17, 1999);
   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 4484 (June 12, 2009).
