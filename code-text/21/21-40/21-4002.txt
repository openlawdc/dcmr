                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADOPTION OF RETAIL RATE (ยง 21-4002)

4002.1. Following the completion of the hearing and the comment period
   specified in the Board&#8217;s notice of proposed ratemaking, a rate may be
   established or adjusted by a majority vote of all of the District members at a
   Board meeting.

   4002.2. An approved retail rate shall take effect upon its publication in the
   D.C. Register.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 2633, 2635 (May 2, 1997).
