                                  DC REGULATIONS

GROUND WATER CLASSIFICATION (ยง 21-1154)

1154.1. All ground waters shall be classified as Class G1 until enough
   information that warrants a different classification is provided to or
   obtained by the Director and evaluated.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 4203, 4205 (July 2, 1993);
   renumbered by Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 1075 (March 4, 1994).
