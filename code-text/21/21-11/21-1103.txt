                                  DC REGULATIONS

WETLANDS (§ 21-1103)

1103.1. In a wetland, the numerical and the narrative criteria shall be
   applied to the column of water above the wetland in accordance with the
   designated use.

   1103.2. Wetlands with rooted vascular aquatic vegetation, except those
   specifically constructed or created as waste water treatment devices and
   except as provided in D.C. Official Code §§ 8-103.03(d) and 8-103.06(a)(3),
   shall be protected from significant adverse hydrologic modifications,
   excessive sedimentation, deposition of toxic substances in toxic amounts,
   nutrient imbalances, and other adverse anthropogenic impacts.
