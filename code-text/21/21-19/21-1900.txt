PURPOSE AND SCOPE (ยง 21-1900)

1900.1. The purpose of this chapter is to provide for accurate, consistent and
   reproducible water quality monitoring data for decisionmaking purposes. This
   chapter shall apply to ambient surface and ground water quality monitoring,
   special monitoring studies, compliance monitoring, monitoring required as a
   part of a permit, or to modify a permit, and self-monitoring of discharges.

   1900.2. If any provision of this chapter, or the application of any provision
   of this chapter to any person or circumstance, is held invalid in a court of
   law, the remainder of the rules of this chapter and its application to any
   other persons or circumstances shall not be affected.
