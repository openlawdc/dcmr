                                  DC REGULATIONS

OTHER MONITORING (ยง 21-1904)

1904.1. The Director may waive part or all of the requirements for written
   quality assurance information for monitoring needed for oil spills, hazardous
   material spills, ground water contamination investigations and for other
   emergency situations; however, the waiver shall not be construed to allow the
   collection and use of poor quality data.

   1904.2. The Director may require additional information for special water
   quality monitoring studies which are unique, attempt to define particular
   water quality parameter relationships, or which are for the purpose of
   investigating point source effects.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 654, 656 (February 3, 1995).
