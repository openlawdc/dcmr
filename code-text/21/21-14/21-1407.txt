                                  DC REGULATIONS

HERBICIDES (ยง 21-1407)

1407.1. The use of any herbicide or chemical to control submerged aquatic
   vegetation shall be prohibited unless approved by the Director against aquatic
   life which is a nuisance.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 1971, 1976 (April 15, 1994).
