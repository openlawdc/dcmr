                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 21-299)

299.1. When used in this chapter, the following words and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Bureau &#8211; the Bureau of Water Measurement and Billing or its lawful
   agent, representative, designee or successor.
   
   
   
   Chief &#8211; the Chief of the Bureau of Water Measurement and Billing or his
   or her lawful agent, representative, designee or successor.
   
   
   
   General Manager &#8211; the chief administrative official of WASA or his or
   her designee.
   
   
   
   Groundwater &#8211; water existing beneath the earth in confined or unconfined
   aquifers.
   
   
   
   Improved real property &#8211; any real property with an existing structure or
   paved surface.
   
   
   
   Sewer allowance &#8211; the discounted rate granted to account holders for the
   quantity of water supplied from the District&#8217;s water distribution system
   during a normal billing cycle and not discharged in District sanitary or
   combined sewer system.
   
   
   
   Sewer system &#8211; pipes, structures, catch basins and other appurtenances,
   which are used to collect and transport wastewater to a treatment plant.
   
   
   
   WASA &#8211; The District of Columbia Water and Sewer Authority.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 1300, 1304 (February 12, 1993);
   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 5358 (June 18, 1999).
