                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROOF OF SERVICE (ยง 21-418)

418.1. A party filing a document with the hearing officer shall furnish
   written proof that a copy also has been sent to every other party.

   418.2. The proof shall show the date and manner of service and may be as
   follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	Written acknowledgement of service;
   
   
   
   (b)	Affidavit of the person making service;
   
   
   
   (c)	Certificate of an attorney of record; or
   
   
   
   (d)	By other proof satisfactory to the hearing officer.

   418.3. Acknowledgement of service may be made by any person at the address
   shown on the document who is sixteen (16) years of age or older.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 1300, 1315 (February 12, 1993);
   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 5358 (June 18, 1999).
