INCONCLUSIVE FINDINGS (ยง 21-408)

408.1. In cases in which all checks and tests result in inconclusive findings
   that provide no reasonable explanation for excessive consumption, no
   adjustment shall be made to the bill for any portion of the excessive
   consumption, except as may be approved by the General Manager, based upon a
   demonstration by the owner or occupant that such an adjustment will further a
   significant public interest.
