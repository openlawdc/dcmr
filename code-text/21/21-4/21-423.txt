                                  DC REGULATIONS

PAYMENT (ยง 21-423)

423.1. Checks or money orders shall be made payable to the &#8220;District of
   Columbia Water and Sewer Authority&#8221;.

   423.2. Certified checks or cash shall be required for payments exceeding two
   hundred dollars ($ 200).

   423.3. If any check or other instrument offered to make any payment due is
   dishonored, the owner or occupant shall be responsible for any fees
   established by WASA.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 1300, 1319 (February 12, 1993);
   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 5358 (June 18, 1999).
