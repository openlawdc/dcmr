REPORT AND ADJUSTMENT (ยง 21-404)

404.1. Upon completion of the investigation, WASA shall issue a written
   decision containing a brief description of the investigation and findings.

   404.2. On the basis of the investigation and findings, WASA shall make
   appropriate adjustments to the bill for water or sewer charges in accordance
   with the provisions of this chapter.
