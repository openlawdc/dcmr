                                  DC REGULATIONS

INVESTIGATION OF CHALLENGED BILL (ยง 21-403)

403.1. Upon receipt of a challenge to a water, sewer or groundwater sewer
   bill, WASA shall suspend the obligation of the owner and occupant to pay the
   contested charges contained in the bill pending investigation.

   403.2. As necessary to investigate the challenge, WASA may:
   
   
   
   (a)	Verify the computations made in the formulation of the water and sewer
   charges;
   
   
   
   (b)	Verify the meter reading for possible meter overread or doubtful
   registration;
   
   
   
   (c)	If feasible, check the premises for leaking fixtures, underground
   invisible leaks, and house-side connection leaks;
   
   
   
   (d)	Check the meter for malfunction;
   
   
   
   (e)	Check the water-cooled air conditioning system, if any, for malfunction;
   and
   
   
   
   (f)	Make a reasonable investigation of any facts asserted by the owner or
   occupant which are material to the determination of a correct bill.

   as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 5358 (June 18, 1999).
