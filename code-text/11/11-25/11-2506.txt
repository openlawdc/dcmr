                                  DC REGULATIONS

SIGNS (ยง 11-2506)

2506.1. Any outdoor sign or other form of exterior advertising erected in any
   district shall comply with the sign regulations in the D.C. Construction Code,
   12 DCMR art. 14, that govern the erection, hanging, placing, painting,
   display, and maintenance of outdoor display signs and other forms of exterior
   advertising within the District of Columbia.
