                                  DC REGULATIONS

REFERRALS TO OTHER AGENCIES (ยง 11-2509)

2509.1. Where the provisions of this title provide for the referral of an
   application to another public agency for review and report, a period of forty
   (40) days from the date of the submission shall be allowed, unless a different
   period is stated specifically in this title.

   2509.2. The period of time may be extended for an additional forty (40) days
   upon the mutual agreement of all agencies involved.
