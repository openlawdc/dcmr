                                  DC REGULATIONS

MAXIMUM FLOOR AREA RATIO (FAR) – SINGLE BUILDING (§ 11-2907)

2907.1. The maximum FAR for any single building in the USN District shall be
   six and one half (6.5), which shall be computed in accordance with § 2909.
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 4788, 4798 (June 3,
   2011).
