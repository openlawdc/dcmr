TIMEFRAME FOR APPROVAL (ยง 11-2929)

2929.1 	The Zoning Commission may approve timeframes within which time an
application must be filed for a building permit or a Stage 2 application must be
filed.

	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 4788, 4810 (June 3,
2011).
