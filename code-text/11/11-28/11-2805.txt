PROHIBITED USES (HE) (ยง 11-2805)

2805.1. The following uses are prohibited within the HE District as both
   principal and accessory uses, unless otherwise noted:
   
   
   
   (a)	Any establishment that has as its principal use the administration of
   massages;
   
   
   
   (b)	Any industrial use first permitted in an M District;
   
   
   
   (c)	At-grade surface parking lots, except as provided in ยง 2803.11;
   
   
   
   (d)	Drive-through establishment (any establishment where goods are sold/rented
   or services rendered, directly to occupants of motor vehicles while in the
   vehicles).
   
   
   
   (e)	Self-service storage establishment that provides separate storage areas
   for individual or business uses;
   
   
   
   (f)	Sexually-oriented business establishment;
   
   
   
   (g)	Vehicle repair and servicing, including full-serve and mini-serve gas
   stations, unattended key card stations, car washes, quick lubrication
   services, and vehicle emission test sites; and
   
   
   
   (h)	Vehicle sales.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking and Order No. 04-05 published at 56 DCR 6181 (August
   7, 2009); as corrected by Errata Notice published at 58 DCR 4314, 4316 (May
   20, 2011).
