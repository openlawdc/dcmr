                                  DC REGULATIONS

ROOF STRUCTURES (HE) (§ 11-2809)

2809.1. The provisions of §§ 411 and 400.7 shall apply to roof structures in
   the HE District.

   2809.2. The gross floor area of roof structures permitted under this section
   shall not be counted in determining the amount of off-street parking that is
   required by Chapter 21.
