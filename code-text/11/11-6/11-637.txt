SIDE YARDS (CR) (ยง 11-637)

637.1. No side yard shall be required for any structure located in a CR
   district, except as may be otherwise established for ground mounted antennas.

   637.2. If a side yard is provided, its minimum width shall be three inches per
   foot (3 in./ft.) of building height, but not less than eight feet (8 ft.).
