   841.2. The bulk of a structure erected, altered, converted, or reconstructed
   for use as a mechanical parking garage, as determined by the floor area ratio
   for the district in which it is located, may be based upon the assumption that
   the finished-floor to finished-floor distance between floors is ten feet (10
   ft.).
