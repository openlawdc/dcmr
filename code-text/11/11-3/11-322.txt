                                  DC REGULATIONS

SPECIAL EXCEPTIONS[COLON] GENERAL (R-3) (§ 11-322)

322.1. Any use or structure permitted in R-2 Districts under §§ 302.1 and
   303 through 306 shall be permitted as a special exception in an R-3 District
   if approved by the Board of Zoning Adjustment under § 3104.

   322.2. A college or university use permitted in R-2 Districts under § 302.2
   shall be permitted as a special exception in an R-3 District if approved by
   the Zoning Commission under § 3104.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: § 3103.4 of the Zoning Regulations effective May 12, 1958; as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 9725, 9730 (December 8, 2000).
