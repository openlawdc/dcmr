                                  DC REGULATIONS

SPECIAL EXCEPTIONS[COLON] GENERAL (R-2) (§ 11-302)

302.1. Any use or structure permitted in R-1 Districts under §§ 205 through
   209, 211 through 221, and 223 shall be permitted as a special exception in an
   R-2 District if approved by the Board of Zoning Adjustment under § 3104.

   302.2. A college or university use permitted in R-1 Districts under § 210
   shall be permitted as a special exception in an R-2 District if approved by
   the Zoning Commission under § 3104.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: § 3102.4 of the Zoning Regulations, effective May 12, 1958; as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 9725, 9729-9730 (December 8,
   2000).
