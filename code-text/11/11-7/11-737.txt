                                  DC REGULATIONS

PET SHOP (C-2) (ยง 11-737)

737.1. A pet shop may be permitted as a special exception if approved by the
   Board of Zoning Adjustment under ยง 3104, subject to the requirements of this
   section.

   737.2. The pet shop shall be located and designed to create no objectionable
   condition to adjacent properties resulting from animal noise, odor, or waste.

   737.3. The pet shop shall not abut an existing residential use or a Residence
   District.

   737.4. External yards or other external facilities for the keeping of animals
   shall not be permitted.

   737.5. The Board may impose additional requirements as it deems necessary to
   protect adjacent or nearby properties.
