                                  DC REGULATIONS

SIDE YARDS (C) (ยง 11-775)

775.1. Side yards shall be provided for structures in a Commercial District as
   specified in this section.

   775.2. A one-family detached dwelling shall be subject to the side yard
   requirements of an R-1 District.

   775.3. A one-family semi-detached dwelling shall be subject to the side yard
   requirements of an R-2 District.

   775.4. No side yard shall be required for a hotel; but if a side yard is
   provided, the width of the yard shall be as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	In a C-1, C-2, or C-3-A District: three inches (3 in.) wide for each foot
   of height of building, but not less than eight feet (8 ft.); and
   
   
   
   (b)	In a C-3-B, C-3-C, C-4, or C-5 (PAD) District: two inches (2 in.) wide for
   each foot of height of building, but not less than six feet (6 ft.).

   775.5. No side yard shall be required for any other building or structure; but
   if a side yard is provided, it shall be at least two inches (2 in.) wide for
   each foot of height of building, but not less than six feet (6 ft.).
