11-2104)

2104.1. The number of parking spaces required for a nonresidential building or
   structure shall be seventy-five percent (75%) of the amount otherwise
   ordinarily required under ยง 2101.1 if the building is located within a radius
   of eight hundred feet (800 ft.) of a Metrorail station entrance and:
   
   (a)	The building or structure is located in a nonresidential district and is
   at least eight hundred feet (800 ft.) from any R-1, R-2, R-3, or R-4 District;
   and
   
   
   
   (b)	The Metrorail station is currently in operation or is one for which a
   construction contract has been awarded.
