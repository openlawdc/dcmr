                                  DC REGULATIONS

EXCEPTIONS TO THE SCHEDULE OF REQUIREMENTS[COLON] COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES (ยง
11-2106)

2106.1. For a college or university use, if a campus plan has been approved by
   the Zoning Commission or the Board of Zoning Adjustment for the college or
   university, parking shall be provided as set forth in the approved campus
   plan.
