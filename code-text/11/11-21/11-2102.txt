EXCEPTIONS TO THE SCHEDULE OF REQUIREMENTS[COLON] C-4 DISTRICT (ยง 11-2102)

2102.1. In a C-4 District, the provisions in this section shall apply.

   2102.2. The parking requirements applicable to a Disposition Lot as defined in
   the Urban Renewal Plan for the Downtown Urban Renewal Area shall be as
   specified in that Plan.

   2102.3. The parking spaces required pursuant to ยง 2101.1 for office and
   retail or service uses need not be limited to use by employees, occupants,
   guests, visitors, or customers of such uses and may be used for general public
   parking.
