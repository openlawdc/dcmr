                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEARANCE IN PARKING LOT APPLICATION CASES (ยง 11-3107)

3107.1. At the public hearing on an application to establish or continue a
   parking lot, the owner of the real property, the lessee (if any), and a person
   who has personal knowledge of and can testify to the day-to-day operation of
   the parking lot shall appear before the Board. If the owner is not an
   individual, then the owner may be represented by an authorized agent who can
   advise the Board of the owner&#8217;s future plans for the property.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 7853, 7898 (October 1, 1999).
