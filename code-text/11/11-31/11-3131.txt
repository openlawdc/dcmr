                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMMENCEMENT OF SPECIAL EXCEPTION TERMS (ยง 11-3131)

3131.1. When the Board limits its approval of a special exception to a term of
   years, the length of that term begins on the date upon which the order became
   final.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 9492, 9494 (October 8, 2010).
