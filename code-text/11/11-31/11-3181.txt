ADMINISTRATION OF FEES (ยง 11-3181)

3181.1. All fees shall be paid by check or money order made payable to the
   District of Columbia Treasurer.

   3181.2. The Director shall be responsible for administering, interpreting, and
   applying the terms of the fee schedule in ยง 3180.

   3181.3. Any decision of the Director regarding the application of the fee
   schedule in ยง 3180 may be appealed to the Board by the appellant or
   applicant. The fee appeal shall be in writing and set forth specifically the
   error allegedly committed by the Director, the grounds for the appeal, and the
   relief requested. The Board shall decide the appeal at a meeting or hearing as
   a preliminary matter to considering the fee appeal or application.

   3181.4. The Board may authorize the refund of all or a portion of the filing
   fee if it finds that the application was incorrectly filed at the direction of
   the Zoning Administrator.
