                                  DC REGULATIONS

HS OVERLAY HOUSING SUB-DISTRICT (HS-H) (§ 11-1321)

1321.1. The purpose of the housing sub-district is to encourage housing.

   1321.2. The floor area ratio for new construction in the HS-H Sub-district may
   not exceed 0.5 FAR for non-residential uses, except as provided in § 1321.3.

   1321.3. New construction that preserves an existing façade constructed before
   1958 is permitted to use an additional 1.0 FAR, for up to a maximum
   non-residential density of 1.5 FAR, for office uses or neighborhood-serving
   retail uses as permitted in §§ 701.1 and 701.4.

   1321.4. In the event that a grocery store is constructed on Square 776, a
   maximum non-residential density of 1.5 FAR shall be permitted on that Square.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 1708, 1714 (March 10, 2006).
