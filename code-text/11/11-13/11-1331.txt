                                  DC REGULATIONS

PLANNED UNIT DEVELOPMENT PROVISIONS (GA) (ยง 11-1331)

1331.1. A planned unit development (PUD) in the GA Overlay District shall be
   subject to the following provisions in addition to those of Chapter 24 of this
   Title:
   
   
   
   (a)	Any additional height and floor area above that permitted as a
   matter-of-right in the underlying zone shall be for residential use only; and
   
   
   
   (b)	The minimum area included within the proposed PUD, including the area of
   public streets or alleys proposed to be closed, shall be a total of ten
   thousand square feet (10,000 ft.2).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 0747, 0755 (January 25, 2008).
