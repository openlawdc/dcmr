                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (NC) (ยง 11-1301)

1301.1. The NC Overlay District is mapped in combination with the underlying
   Commercial District and not instead of the underlying district.

   1301.2. Except as provided in ยง 1301.3 and in other provisions of this
   chapter, all uses, buildings, and structures permitted in accordance with this
   chapter and the appropriate regulations of the underlying district with which
   the mapped NC Overlay District is combined shall be permitted in the combined
   district.

   1301.3. Where there is a conflict between this chapter and the underlying
   zoning, the more restrictive provisions of this title shall govern.
