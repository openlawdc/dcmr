                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY DATE (§ 11-2608)

2608.1. The provisions of §§ 2600 through 2607 of this Chapter as adopted by
   Zoning Commission Orders No. 04-33, 04-33A, and 04-33B and all amendments made
   by Orders No. 04-33A and 04-33B to 11 DCMR Chapters 1, 11 through 14, 15, 16,
   and 19 shall become effective upon the publication of the first
   purchase/rental schedule in the D.C. Register.

   2608.2. The provisions of this Chapter shall not apply to any building
   approved by the Zoning Commission pursuant to Chapter 24 if the approved
   application was set down for hearing prior to March 14, 2008.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 7013 (August 25, 2006); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 6943 (July 20, 2007); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 2604 (March 14, 2008).
