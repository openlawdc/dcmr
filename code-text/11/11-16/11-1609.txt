                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRIVATE DRIVEWAYS ON P STREET S.W. (CG) (ยง 11-1609)

1609.1. No private driveway may be constructed or used from P Street S.W. to
   any parking or loading berth areas in or adjacent to a building or structure
   subject to the provisions of this chapter that is constructed after February
   16, 2007.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 1587-1592 (February 16, 2007);
   as corrected by Errata Notice published at 58 DCR 4314, 4315 (May 20, 2011).
