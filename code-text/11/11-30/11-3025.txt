POST-HEARING PROCEDURES[COLON] GENERAL (ยง 11-3025)

3025.1. Prior to filing the final decision, the Commission may on its own
   motion re-open the record and require further hearing on designated issues
   before the Commission.

   3025.2. Notice of a further hearing along with a designation of issues shall
   be forwarded to any party who participated in the earlier proceedings, or the
   party&#8217;s authorized representative. Notice shall be given at least
   fourteen (14) days prior to the date set for further hearing.

   3025.3. A proposed action to amend the text of the Zoning Regulations or the
   Zoning Map, or to approve a planned unit development or air space development
   shall be referred to the National Capital Planning Commission for comment and
   review pursuant to ยง 492(b)(2) of the District of Columbia Self-Government
   and Governmental Reorganization Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 774,
   810; D.C. Official Code ยง 6-641.05 (formerly codified at D.C. Code ยง 5-417
   (1994 Repl.))).

   3025.4. The report of the National Capital Planning Commission shall be in
   writing. It also may be presented orally by the chairperson or a duly
   authorized representative.
