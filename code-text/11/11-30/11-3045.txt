                                  DC REGULATIONS

MISCELLANEOUS FEES (§ 11-3045)

3045.1. Fees for miscellaneous zoning services and documents provided by the
   Office of Zoning are as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	The fee for providing a zoning certification is fifty dollars ($50.00);
   
   
   
   (b)	The fee for photocopying is twenty cents (20¢) per page;
   
   
   
   (c)	Repealed;
   
   
   
   (d)	Repealed; and
   
   
   
   (e)	The fee for retrieving Office of Zoning records located off-site is
   fifteen dollars ($15.00) per record. This fee will be waived when the records
   are sought for noncommercial use and the request is made by an educational or
   scientific institution for scholarly or scientific research or by a
   representative of the news media. The Office of Zoning may not require advance
   payment of the fee unless the requester has previously failed to pay fees in a
   timely fashion.

   3045.2. All fees shall be paid by check or money order made payable to the
   D.C. Treasurer.
