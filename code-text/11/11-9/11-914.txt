                                  DC REGULATIONS

ANTENNA TOWER AND MONOPOLE (W-1, W-2, AND W-3) (ยง 11-914)

914.1. If the Board considers that it is appropriate in furthering the
   objectives of the Waterfront Districts, an antenna tower or monopole shall be
   permitted as a special exception in the W-1, W-2, and W-3 Districts, if
   approved by the Board of Zoning Adjustment subject to the standards and
   procedures as specified in chapter 27 of this title.
