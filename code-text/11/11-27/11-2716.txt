                                  DC REGULATIONS

REMOVAL OF ANTENNAS, ANTENNA TOWERS MONOPOLES AND RELATED EQUIPMENT (ยง 11-2716)

2716.1. Antennas, antenna towers, monopoles, equipment cabinets, or equipment
   shelters shall be removed at the expense of the property owner if they have
   not been used for a period of one year. A one-year extension may be granted by
   the Board of Zoning Adjustment to this requirement for good cause shown.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 34 (January 4, 2008).
