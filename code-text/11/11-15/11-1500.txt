                                  DC REGULATIONS

PREAMBLE (§ 11-1500)

1500.1. Miscellaneous Overlay Districts are created to implement the
   Comprehensive Plan and other public policy goals and objectives.

   1500.2. The Miscellaneous Overlay Districts include, but are not limited to,
   those overlay districts that are applied to a specific small area or
   neighborhood that requires special treatment in controlling land uses.

   1500.3. The Miscellaneous Overlay Districts may provide development
   incentives, restrictions, or a combination thereof.
