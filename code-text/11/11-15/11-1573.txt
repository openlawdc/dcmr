                                  DC REGULATIONS

PLANNED UNIT DEVELOPMENT STANDARDS (CHC) (§ 11-1573)

1573.1. In the CHC Overlay District, the development standards in §§ 1572.3
   through 1572.4 shall serve as the maximum permitted floor area ratio for a
   planned unit development.
