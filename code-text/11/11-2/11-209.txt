COMMUNITY CENTERS (R-1) (ยง 11-209)

209.1. Use as a community center building, park, playground, swimming pool, or
   athletic field operated by a local community organization or association shall
   be permitted as a special exception in an R-1 District if approved by the
   Board of Zoning Adjustment under ยง 3104, subject to the provisions of this
   section.

   209.2. A community center shall not be organized for profit, but shall be
   organized exclusively for the promotion of the social welfare of the
   neighborhood in which it is proposed to be located.

   209.3. A community center shall offer no articles of commerce for sale in the
   center.

   209.4. A community center shall not likely become objectionable in a Residence
   District because of noise or traffic.

   209.5. The use of a community center shall be reasonably necessary or
   convenient to the neighborhood in which it is proposed to be located.
