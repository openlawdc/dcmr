CLERICAL AND RELIGIOUS GROUP RESIDENCES (SP) (ยง 11-516)

516.1. Use as residences for clerical groups and religious denominations in
   excess of fifteen (15) persons shall be permitted in an SP District if
   approved by the Board of Zoning Adjustment as a special exception under ยง
   3104, subject to the provisions of this section.

   516.2. Use as residences for clerical groups and religious denominations shall
   not adversely affect the use of neighboring property.

   516.3. The amount and arrangement of parking spaces shall be adequate.
