508.1. Construction of a new office building or construction of an addition to
   a building for office use, or conversion of an existing building to office

   508.3. The use, height, bulk, and design shall be in harmony with existing
   uses and structures on neighboring property.

   508.4. The use shall not create dangerous or other objectionable traffic
   conditions.

   508.5. The Board may require special treatment in the way of design, screening
   of buildings, accessory uses, signs, and other facilities as it deems
   necessary to protect the value of neighboring property.
