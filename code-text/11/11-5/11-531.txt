   531.2. A public recreation and community center shall not exceed a 1.8 floor
   area ratio.

   531.3. The maximum permitted floor area ratio may be increased for specific
   applications approved by the Zoning Commission under the planned unit
   development process specified in chapter 24 of this title.

   531.4. In the computation of gross floor area for a hotel, guest room areas
   and service areas shall be charged against the floor area ratio for
   &#8220;Apartment house or other residential use,&#8221; as specified in §
   531.1. Function rooms, exhibit space, and commercial adjuncts shall be charged
   against the floor area ratio for &#8220;other permitted use,&#8221; as
   specified in § 531.1.

   531.5. For the purposes of this section, an inn or community-based residential
   facility shall be charged against the floor area ratio for &#8220;Apartment
   house or other residential use,&#8221; as specified in § 531.1.
