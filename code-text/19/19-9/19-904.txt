                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPOINTMENT AND REMOVAL OF THE DIRECTOR (ยง 19-904)

904.1. The Board of Library Trustees shall appoint by secret ballot a Director
   for the District of Columbia Public Library at a regular meeting or at a
   special meeting called for the purpose.

   904.2. The Board may, at any time, remove the Director for cause.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 7488, 7490 (October 14, 1988).
