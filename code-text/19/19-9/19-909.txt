                                  DC REGULATIONS

ORDER AND CONDUCT OF BUSINESS (§ 19-909)

909.1. The order of business at regular meetings of the Board shall be as
   follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	 Call of the roll;
   
   
   
   (b)	 Approval of the minutes;
   
   
   
   (c)	Reports of officers;
   
   
   
   (d)	Reports of standing committees;
   
   
   
   (e)	 Reports of special committees;
   
   
   
   (f)	Report of the Director;
   
   
   
   (g)	 Unfinished business;
   
   
   
   (h)	New business; and
   
   
   
   (i)	Expressions from the audience.

   909.2. In conducting its business the Board shall follow the latest edition of
   Robert’s Rules of Order in all matters not covered by these rules.

   909.3. The President of the Board may appoint a Parliamentarian on the basis
   of pertinent experience and other qualifications. The Parliamentarian shall
   serve at the President’s pleasure.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 7488, 7492 (October 14, 1988).
