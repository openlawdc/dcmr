                                  DC REGULATIONS

DUTIES OF THE OFFICERS (ยง 19-903)

903.1. It shall be the duty of the President to preside at all meetings of the
   Board, to appoint all committees not otherwise provided for, and to sign all
   papers, communications, and instruments which may require the
   President&#8217;s official signature. The President shall exercise a general
   supervision over the affairs of the Library Board of Trustees and shall be an
   ex officio member of all committees. The President may vote in the election of
   officers and in the appointment or removal of the Director. The President may
   also vote on other issues if the vote would change the result or if the vote
   is taken by secret ballot.

   903.2. In the absence of the President, the Vice-President shall preside at
   all meetings of the Board, and shall generally discharge all duties of the
   President in case of the latter&#8217;s absence from the District of Columbia
   or inability to act.

   903.3. The duties of the Director as Secretary of the Board shall be to keep a
   permanent record of the meetings of the Board, to have custody of the records
   and general papers of the Board, and to perform the other functions usual to
   this office. The duties of the Director as Treasurer of the Board shall be to
   keep the financial accounts of the Library.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 7488, 7489-90 (October 14,
   1988).
