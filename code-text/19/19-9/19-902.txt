VACANCY IN THE OFFICES OF PRESIDENT OR VICE-PRESIDENT (ยง 19-902)

902.1. A vacancy in the office of President shall be filled by the Vice
   President for the remainder of the term.

   902.2. A vacancy in the office of Vice President shall be filled at a regular
   meeting, or at a special meeting called for the purpose, by election, pursuant
   to the requirements of ยง 906.3.
