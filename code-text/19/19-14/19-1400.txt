                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL LICENSE PROVISIONS (§ 19-1400)

1400.1. Any individual, partnership, or corporation owning or operating a
   video arcade or an establishment in which mechanical amusement machines are
   offered for public use shall be required to obtain an annual license for the
   operation of the video arcade or mechanical amusement machine, and to pay the
   required license fee.
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is the
   “Police Regulations of the District of Columbia/Video Arcades and Mechanical
   Amusement Machines Regulations Amendment Act of 1984,” D.C. Law 5-88,
   effective June 29, 1984.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: D.C. Act 5-129, § 2(a) published at 31 DCR 2331 (May 18, 1984).
