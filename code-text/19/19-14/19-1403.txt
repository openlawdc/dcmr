                                  DC REGULATIONS

DENIAL OF APPLICATION (ยง 19-1403)

1403.1. In the event that an application for a license to operate a video
   arcade is denied, the Mayor shall provide the applicant with a written
   statement setting forth the reasons for the denial within thirty (30) days of
   the date of the denial, as evidenced by any written or verbal notification to
   the applicant that his or her applicant has been denied.

   1403.2. Any applicant may appeal an adverse decision of the Mayor to the Board
   of Appeals and Review of the District of Columbia.
