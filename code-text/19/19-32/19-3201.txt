                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESPONSIBILITY (ยง 19-3201)

3201.1. The ultimate responsibility for responding to requests for records of
   the Commission is vested in the executive director.

   3201.2. The Commission executive director may designate an individual as the
   information officer of the Commission and may delegate to that individual the
   authority to grant and deny requests.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 5232, 5236 (September 15, 1995).
