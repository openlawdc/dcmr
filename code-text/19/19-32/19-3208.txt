                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORDS OF REQUESTS (ยง 19-3208)

3208.1. The executive director shall maintain records pertaining to each
   request, including copies of all requests, correspondence, fees received or
   waived, and denials. The records shall be maintained by individual request.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 5232, 5241 (September 15, 1995).
