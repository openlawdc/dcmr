                                  DC REGULATIONS

PAYMENTS FOR VALUE RECEIVED (ยง 19-312)

312.1. The CCO shall take all reasonable action to prevent employees from
   making any unauthorized obligation to procure goods, services or construction.

   312.2. The CCO may make a payment for value received under an unauthorized
   obligation if the CCO makes a written determination that:
   
   
   
   (a)	the Authority has obtained a benefit from another party&#8217;s
   performance pursuant to the unauthorized obligation;
   
   
   
   (b)	the obligation is within the authority conferred on the CCO by these
   regulations;
   
   
   
   (c)	the price to be obligated for performance is fair and reasonable; and
   
   
   
   (d)	sufficient funds were available at the time of the unauthorized obligation
   and remain available.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 4907, 4918 (August 22, 1997);
   and as amended by Final Rulemaking at 49 DCR 10814 (November 29, 2002).
