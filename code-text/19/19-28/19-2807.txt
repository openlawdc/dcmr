                                  DC REGULATIONS

LOCAL, SMALL, DISADVANTAGED BUSINESS ENTERPRISES (ยง 19-2807)

2807.1. It is the policy of the Commission to encourage the purchase of goods
   and services which maximize the efficient utilization of local, small,
   disadvantaged business enterprises and promote significant participation by
   such enterprises.

   2807.2. The Commission shall investigate, evaluate and consider each bid or
   proposal in accordance with applicable law and regulations.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 7420, 7428 (October 9, 1998).
