                                  DC REGULATIONS

FORTUNE TELLERS, MEDIUMS, CLAIRVOYANTS, SOOTHSAYERS, AND PALM READERS [DELETED]
(§ 19-1307)

EDITOR&#8217;S NOTE: Subsection 2003(pp)(36) of D.C. Law 12-261, the “Second
Omnibus Regulatory Reform Amendment Act of 1998” repealed paragraph 43 of
section 7 of An Act Making appropriations to provide for the government of the
District of Columbia for the fiscal year ending June thirtieth, nineteen hundred
and three, and for other purpose, approved July 1, 1902, which set forth the
general licensing law provisions governing fortune tellers, mediums,
clairvoyants, soothsayers and palm readers.
