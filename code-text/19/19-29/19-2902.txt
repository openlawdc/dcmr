EXCLUSIONS (ยง 19-2902)

2902.1. This chapter shall not apply to paid commercial advertisements or
   signs and banners containing governmental directives displayed in accordance
   with other policies, procedures and agreements as shall be adopted or approved
   by the D.C. Armory Board.
