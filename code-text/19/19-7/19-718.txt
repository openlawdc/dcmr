                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESPONSIBILITY FOR REPAIRS TO PROPERTIES (ยง 19-718)

718.1. Equipment or properties under the control of the Department damaged by
   school activities shall be repaired or replaced by methods or means acceptable
   to the officers of the two agencies.

   718.2. Any organization using buildings, grounds, and equipment shall exercise
   utmost care of the property and make good any damage arising from the use.

   718.3. In granting permits, the Director of the Department of Parks and
   Recreation may require a money guarantee as a protection against property loss
   or damage.
