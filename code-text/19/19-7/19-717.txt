                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRECEDENCE FOR USE OF RECREATION PROPERTIES (ยง 19-717)

717.1. The recreational use of properties under the direct control of the
   Department shall have direct precedence over use for other purposes, except
   where recreation properties in the immediate vicinity of a public school or
   charter school are the only recreation facilities available for school use.

   717.2. Department facilities are the only playgrounds available for school
   use, it shall be the mutual responsibility of the unit director and the school
   principal to work out a satisfactory schedule of use.
