                                  DC REGULATIONS

SMOKING, DRINKING, AND GAMBLING (§ 19-702)

702.1. The use of tobacco in a recreation building or on a small children’s
   playground where posted is prohibited.

   702.2. The use of alcoholic beverages in any form on the premises of a
   recreation center or playground is prohibited.

   702.3. Gambling in any form is prohibited at all recreation units.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: 15 DCRR § 1.5 (August 10, 1970 and December 23, 1970).
