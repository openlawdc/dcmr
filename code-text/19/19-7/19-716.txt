                                  DC REGULATIONS

ESTABLISHMENT OF FEES (ยง 19-716)

716.1. Admission fees, club dues, and donations to cover expenses incidental
   to the promotion of the recreation program, and not met through public
   appropriations, may be authorized by the Director of the Department of Parks
   and Recreation.

   716.2. The Director of the Department of Parks and Recreation shall establish
   a uniform method to govern the control, collection, and distribution of fees,
   club dues, and donations.

   716.3. All funds collected from activities supervised or authorized by the
   Department shall be deposited in the miscellaneous trust fund under the
   control of the Director of the Department of Parks and Recreation, and shall
   be expended and audited in accordance with Public Law 534, 77th Congress, and
   rules and regulations governing trust funds.

   716.4. [Reserved]

   716.5. The following shall be fees for providing and maintaining recreational
   facilities.
