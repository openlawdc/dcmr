                                  DC REGULATIONS

REIMBURSEMENT FOR HEAT, LIGHT, AND JANITORIAL SUPPLIES (ยง 19-714)

714.1. The Department shall reimburse the Board of Education for the cost of
   heat, light, and janitorial supplies.

   714.2. Equipment or properties damaged by Department activities shall be
   repaired or replaced by methods or means mutually acceptable to the officers
   of both agencies.
