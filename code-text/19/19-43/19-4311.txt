                                  DC REGULATIONS

MODIFICATION OF PURCHASE ODERS (ยง 19-4311)

4311.1. A purchase order may be modified by using a form approved by the CCO.
   Each purchase order modification shall identify the order it modifies and
   shall contain an appropriate modification number.

   4311.2. The CCO may obtain a contractor&#8217;s written acceptance of a
   purchase order modification if the written acceptance is determined by the CCO
   to be necessary to ensure the contractor&#8217;s compliance and acceptance of
   the purchase order as modified.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 493 (January 18, 2008).
