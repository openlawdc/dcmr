                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRE-CONSTRUCTION CONFERENCE (ยง 19-4344)

4344.1. The CCO may conduct a pre-construction conference to inform the
   contractor about the labor standards requirements, permits, subcontracting and
   other relevant pre-construction matters deemed appropriate by the CCO.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 493 (January 18, 2008).
