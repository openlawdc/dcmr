                                  DC REGULATIONS

REDESIGN RESPONSIBILITY FOR DESIGN ERRORS OR DEFICIENCIES (ยง 19-4354)

4354.1. The architect-engineer firm shall make necessary corrections at no
   cost to the Library if the designs, drawings, specifications, or other items
   or services furnished by the firm contain any errors, deficiencies, or
   inadequacies.

   4354.2. If the CCO does not require a firm to correct errors, the CCO shall
   include a written statement of the reasons for that decision in the contract
   file.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 493 (January 18, 2008).
