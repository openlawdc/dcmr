                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONSTRUCTION CONTRACTS WITH ARCHITECT-ENGINEER FIRMS (ยง 19-4339)

4339.1. Except as provided in Section 4339.2, the CCO shall not award a
   contract for the construction of a project to the firm that designed the
   project or its subsidiaries or affiliates without the prior written approval
   of the Procurement Executive.

   4339.2. If a proposed construction contract will use a design build method of
   construction, the CCO may award the construction contract to an affiliate of
   the architect- engineer firm that designed the project without prior approval
   of the Procurement executive.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 493 (January 18, 2008).
