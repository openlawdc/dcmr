                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITE QUANTITY CONTRACTS (ยง 19-4330)

4330.1. The CCO may use a definite quantity contract that provides for
   delivery of a specified quantity of supplies or services either at specified
   times or when ordered. Quantities ordered under a definite quantity contract
   shall be limited to the quantity stated in the contract, unless the contract
   contains an increased quantity option.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 493 (January 18, 2008).
