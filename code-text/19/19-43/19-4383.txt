                                  DC REGULATIONS

STANDARD CLAUSES, PROVISIONS AND PROCUREMENT MANUAL (ยง 19-4383)

4383.1. The CCO shall develop standard provisions and clauses for all Library
   solicitations and contracts as required by these Regulations. These standard
   provisions and clauses define the responsibilities and rights of the parties.
   The CCO shall develop a procurement manual that provides the details and
   instructions for implementing these Regulations within 120 after promulgation
   of these Regulations.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 493 (January 18, 2008).
