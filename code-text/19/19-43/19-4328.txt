LABOR-HOUR CONTRACTS (ยง 19-4328)

4328.1. The CCO may use a labor-hour contract when materials are not required.

   4328.2. A labor-hour contract is a variation of a time and materials contract
   and may be used when a contractor&#8217;s hourly rates are fixed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 493 (January 18, 2008).
