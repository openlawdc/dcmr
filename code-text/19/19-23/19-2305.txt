                                  DC REGULATIONS

ARRIVAL; FAILURE TO PERFORM CONTRACT (§ 19-2305)

2305.1. A contestant shall report to the inspector in the dressing room at
   least one (1) hour before the contestant is scheduled to enter the ring.

   2305.2. A contestant who fails to perform in a contest for which the
   contestant or the manager has signed a contract and who does not furnish an
   acceptable reason, or doctor’s certificate in case of physical disability,
   to the Commission is subject to disciplinary action.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 35 DCR 3759, 3760 (May 20, 1988).
