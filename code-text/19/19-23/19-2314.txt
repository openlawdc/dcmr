                                  DC REGULATIONS

REFEREES INSPECTION (ยง 19-2314)

2314.1. The referee shall inspect the bandages and the gloves of each
   contestant to ensure that no grease or other foreign substance has been
   illegally applied thereto.

   2314.2. The referee shall ensure that each contestant is wearing a protective
   cup and mouthpiece.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 35 DCR 3759, 3764 (May 20, 1988).
