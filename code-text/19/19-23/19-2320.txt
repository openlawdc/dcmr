                                  DC REGULATIONS

INTENTIONAL LOSSES (§ 19-2320)

2320.1. If the referee determines that one or both of the contestants are
   attempting to lose the contest, the referee shall stop the contest and notify
   each contestant involved that the contestant’s purse will be withheld
   pending a final decision as to whether the purse is forfeited.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 35 DCR 3759, 3767 (May 20, 1988).
