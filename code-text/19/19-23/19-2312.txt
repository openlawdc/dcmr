                                  DC REGULATIONS

LENGTH AND NUMBER OF ROUNDS (ยง 19-2312)

2312.1. The length of a round in a contest is two (2) minutes, and there is a
   one (1) minute rest between rounds.

   2312.2. A professional contest shall be scheduled for a minimum of five (5)
   rounds.

   2312.3. An amateur contest may not exceed five (5) rounds.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 35 DCR 3759, 3763 (May 20, 1988).
