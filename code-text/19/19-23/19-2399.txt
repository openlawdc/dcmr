                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (§ 19-2399)

2399.1. As used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases have the
   meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Contest &#8211; single kickboxing match.
   
   
   
   Contestant &#8211; a kickboxer taking part in a specific contest.
   
   
   
   Event – a kickboxing contest, match, exhibition, or showing.

   2399.2. The definitions in § 2099 of Chapter 20 of this title are
   incorporated by reference into and are applicable to this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 35 DCR 3759, 3773 (May 20, 1988).
