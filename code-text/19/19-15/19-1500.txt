                                  DC REGULATIONS

PURPOSE AND SCOPE (§ 19-1500)

1500.1. The rules in §§ 1501 through 1508.4 provide the minimum guidelines
   and procedures for the implementation of section 4 of D.C. Law 5-188, the
   Water Pollution Control Act of 1984, D.C. Code § 6-923 (1995 Repl.) which
   mandates the protection of aquatic animals and plants and the restoration and
   preservation of aquatic life in the District’s waters for aesthetic
   enjoyment, recreation, and industry. The rules in § 1560 implement § 5 of an
   Act to Revise and Modernize the Fish and Wildlife Laws of the District of
   Columbia, Pub. L. No. 85-730, 72 Stat. 815, D.C. Code § 22-1632 (1996 Repl.).

   1500.2. The purpose of these rules is to ensure that the District’s
   fisheries and wildlife resources are properly managed and protected.

   1500.3. The rules in §§ 1501 through 1507.3 shall apply to all District
   waters except bodies of water on private property.
