                                  DC REGULATIONS

OFFICE AND REGISTERED AGENT (ยง 19-101)

101.1 	The Authority shall continuously maintain in the District of Columbia a
registered office at such place as may be designated by the Board of Directors
(the Board). The principal office of the Authority shall be in the District of
Columbia, at such address as may from time to time be designated by the Board.
The Authority may also have offices at such other places as the Board may from
time to time designate.

101.2 	The Authority shall continuously maintain within the District of Columbia
a registered agent, which shall be designated by the Board.
