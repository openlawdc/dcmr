                                  DC REGULATIONS

AMENDMENTS (ยง 19-112)

112.1 	These bylaws may be amended from time to time, in any manner not
inconsistent with the Act, by the affirmative vote of a majority of the entire
membership of the Board at any meeting of the Board, if notice of the substance
of the proposed Amendment be contained in the notice of the meeting, or if such
notice be waived as herein provided.

2013).
