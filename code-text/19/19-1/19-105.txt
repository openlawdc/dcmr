                                  DC REGULATIONS

OPEN MEETINGS (ยง 19-105)

105.1 	All meetings of the Board at which action of any kind is taken shall be
open to the public, and no official action shall be effective unless taken at
such meeting.



105.2 	A written transcript shall be kept for all such meetings and shall be
made available to the public during normal business hours of the Authority.
Copies of such written transcripts or copies of such transcriptions shall be
available, upon request, to the public at a reasonable cost.
