                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRESIDENT AND CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER (ยง 19-108)

108.1 	The President and Chief Executive Officer shall have the duties described
in the Act and such other duties as may be authorized by the Board for the
effective and efficient management of the Authority.
