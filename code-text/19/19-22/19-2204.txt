                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROVISION OF NAMES OF CONTESTANTS (ยง 19-2204)

2204.1. The promoter of a wrestling event shall furnish the Commission the
   names of each contestant at least four (4) days before the event.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 35 DCR 3753, 3754 (May 20, 1988).
