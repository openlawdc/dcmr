                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEARANCE OF CONTESTANTS (ยง 19-2207)

2207.1. Contestants shall be clean and neat in appearance.

   2207.2. The skin of each contestant shall be free from grease, lotions, or
   other foreign substances.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 35 DCR 3753, 3754 (May 20, 1988).
