                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTRACTS (§ 19-2203)

2203.1. No person shall promote a professional event in the District without
   having first entered into a valid written contract, conforming to the
   requirements of this chapter with each contestant or manager.

   2203.2. A contract required by this section shall include the following
   information;
   
   
   
   (a)	The contestant’s name;
   
   
   
   (b)	The amount of compensation to be paid to the contestant;
   
   
   
   (c)	A certification by the contestant or the contestant’s manager that the
   contestant is in excellent physical condition and that the contestant is not
   concealing any illness or injury; and
   
   
   
   (d)	A statement by the contestant that the contestant will report immediately
   to the Commission if the contestant is approached by any person regarding a
   bribe or a fake or sham event.

   2203.3. The promoter shall provide the Commission with the originals or
   certified copies of all contracts between the promoter and all contestants at
   least five (5) days before the event.

   2203.4. No contract shall provide for, contemplate, or be conditioned upon the
   performance by a contestant of services other than participating in one
   contest.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking at 35 DCR 3753, 3754 (May 20, 1988).
