                                  DC REGULATIONS

VOTING (ยง 19-3504)

3504.1. Each member of the Council shall be entitled to one (1) vote.

   3504.2. Each member shall attend fifty percent (50%) of all meetings held
   between elections and funding of programs to be entitled to vote for the
   funding of programs.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 2521, 2522 (April 10, 1992).
