                                  DC REGULATIONS

COUNCIL OF CHAIRPERSONS[COLON] CONFLICT OF INTEREST (ยง 19-3523)

3523.1. It shall be a breach of ethical standards for any Council of
   Chairpersons officer to participate, indirectly or directly, in activities
   where the officer knows that the activity would lead to financial gain for
   himself or herself or any member of his or her family.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 2521, 2535 (April 10, 1992).
