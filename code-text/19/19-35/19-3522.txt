                                  DC REGULATIONS

COUNCIL OF CHAIRPERSONS[COLON] CONDUCT OF MEETINGS (§ 19-3522)

3522.1. Robert’s Rules of Order, Revised, shall be the basic guide for the
   conduct of all meetings of the Council.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 2521, 2534 (April 10, 1992).
