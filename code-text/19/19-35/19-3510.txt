                                  DC REGULATIONS

ORDER OF BUSINESS (ยง 19-3510)

3510.1. The regular order of business at any meeting of the Board shall be as
   follows:
   
   
   
   a.	Meeting Called to Order by Chairperson or Presiding Officer;
   
   
   
   b.	Reading of Minutes of the Last Meeting;
   
   
   
   c.	Reports of Officers;
   
   
   
   d.	Reports of Committees;
   
   
   
   e.	Unfinished Business;
   
   
   
   f.	New Business;
   
   
   
   g.	Adjournment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 2521, 2527-28 (April 10, 1992).
