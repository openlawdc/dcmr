                                  DC REGULATIONS

PUBLIC ACCESS (§ 19-3509)

3509.1. All meetings of the NPC, including special meetings of Boards, shall
   be open to the public.

   3509.2. Notice of the date, time, and place of every meeting of the Board and,
   in the case of a special meeting, of the purpose or purposes for which the
   meeting is called, shall be given to Board Members at least five (5) days
   prior to the meeting and the notice shall be posted in NPC headquarters. 
   Whenever possible, mass media shall be used.

   3509.3. A quorum at any meeting of the Board shall consist of not less than
   one-third (1/3) of the Board’s members.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 2521, 2527 (April 10, 1992).
