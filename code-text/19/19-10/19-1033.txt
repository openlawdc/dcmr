                                  DC REGULATIONS

PREVENTION OF WATER POLLUTION (ยง 19-1033)

1033.1. Discharging, depositing, throwing, or spilling any crude petroleum,
   gasoline, or other similar volatile liquids, or any waste, whether solid or
   liquid, in District of Columbia waters from a vessel is prohibited.

   1033.2. No person shall pump or empty the bilge water or any bilge residue
   from the bilges of any steamship, tugboat, tanker, oil barge, or other vessel
   that burns or conveys oil or distillates while the vessel is in District of
   Columbia waters.

   Columbia (January 1983).
