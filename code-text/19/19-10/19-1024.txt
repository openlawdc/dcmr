                                  DC REGULATIONS

CIVIL LIABILITY (ยง 19-1024)

1024.1. Whenever any vessel is operated in District of Columbia waters by any
   person other than the owner, with the expressed or implied consent of the
   owner, the operator of the vessel shall be deemed to be the agent of the owner
   of the vessel in the case of a collision, accident, or other casualty.

   1024.2. The proof of ownership of the vessel shall be prima facie evidence
   that the person operated the vessel with the consent of the owner.
