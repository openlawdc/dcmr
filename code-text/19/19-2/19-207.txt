                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSFERS AND PROMOTIONS (ยง 19-207)

207.1 	The President and Chief Executive Officer shall authorize and maintain
procedures for promotions.



207.2 	A promotion or transfer shall be contingent upon the employee
satisfactorily completing an initial review period.



SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 6236, 6239 (July 30, 1999); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8191, 8195 (July 6, 2012).
