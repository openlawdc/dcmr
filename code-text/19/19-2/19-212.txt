                                  DC REGULATIONS

EMPLOYEE DISABILITY BENEFITS (ยง 19-212)

212.1 	Regular full-time employees shall be enrolled in a short and long-term
disability insurance program selected by the Authority.



212.2 	The Authority shall pay the cost of such disability insurance programs.
Benefits for these insurance programs shall be payable in accordance with the
respective plan provisions.



SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 6236, 6242 (July 30, 1999); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8191, 8198 (July 6, 2012).
