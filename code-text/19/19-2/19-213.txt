                                  DC REGULATIONS

EMPLOYEE DEATH BENEFITS (§ 19-213)

213.1 	The designated beneficiary of a deceased employee shall be eligible to
receive unpaid compensation and a refund of the employee’s contribution to the
Employees’ Retirement Plan if the employee is not vested in accordance with
the provisions of the Retirement Plan.



SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 6236, 6242 (July 30, 1999); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8191, 8198 (July 6, 2012).
