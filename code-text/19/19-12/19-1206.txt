                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPLAINT AND CONTACT INFORMATION (ยง 19-1206)

1206.1. All sightseeing tour companies or sightseeing tour guides shall
   furnish each person on a sightseeing tour with a card or ticket containing the
   following:
   
   
   
   (a) 	The name, address, and telephone number of a person or office authorized
   to receive complaints relative to the conduct or any part of a sightseeing
   tour; and
   
   
   
   (b) 	The name, address, and telephone number of the person, firm, or
   corporation responsible for the conduct and management of the tour.
   
   
   
   1206.2 	The authorized person or office specified under ยง 1206.1(a) shall be
   available to receive complaints during the regular business hours of each day
   that sightseeing tours are conducted by the sightseeing tour company.
   
   
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 6116, 6120 (July 16, 2010).
