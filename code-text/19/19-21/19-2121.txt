                                  DC REGULATIONS

FINAL DECISION (ยง 19-2121)

2121.1. Judges designated by the Commission shall make the final decision in
   each event, unless the contest is first stopped by the referee for reason of a
   knockout, technical knockout, foul, inability of a contestant to continue, or
   other reason for which the referee is authorized to stop a contest.

   2121.2. The referee shall officially designate the winner of each event by
   raising the right hand of the winner.
   
   
   
   The Commission may overrule the referee or the judges concerning any decision
   in a contest.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3731, 3743 (May 20, 1988).
