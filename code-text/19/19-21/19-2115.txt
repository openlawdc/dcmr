                                  DC REGULATIONS

LENGTH ROUNDS AND REST PERIODS (ยง 19-2115)

2115.1. The length of a round in a professional boxing contest shall be three
   (3) minutes and there shall be a one (1) minute rest between rounds.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3731, 3738 (May 20, 1988).
