                                  DC REGULATIONS

JUDGES (ยง 19-2133)

2133.1. The Commission shall designate three (3) judges for each contest, who
   shall score the contestants and make final decisions.

   2133.2. The Commission may designate substitute judges for a contest in the
   absence of the judges originally designated for the contest.  Such substitutes
   may be licensed referees, members of the Commission or other officials of the
   Commission.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3731, 3750 (May 20, 1988).
