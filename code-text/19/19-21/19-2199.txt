DEFINITIONS (ยง 19-2199)

2199.1. As used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases have the
   meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Contest &#8211; single boxing match.
   
   
   
   Contestant &#8211; a boxer taking part in a specific contest.
   
   
   
   Boxing event &#8211; a boxing contest, match, exhibition, or showing.
   
   
   
   Program &#8211; one or more contests held at the same place on the same day or
   for which a single admission fee is charged.
   
   
   
   Take down &#8211; clinching or wrestling an opponent to the mat.
   
   
   
   Technical knockout &#8211; a termination of a contest by the referee in order
   to protect an injured, defenseless contestant, and such that the decision is
   awarded to the non-injured contestant unless that contestant has caused injury
   by fouling.

   2199.2. The definitions in ยง 2099 of Chapter 20 of this title are
   incorporated by reference into and are applicable to this chapter.
