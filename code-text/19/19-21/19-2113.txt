                                  DC REGULATIONS

REFEREES INSPECTION (ยง 19-2113)

2113.1. The referee shall inspect the bandages and the gloves of each
   contestant to ensure that no grease or other foreign substance has been
   applied thereto.

   2113.2. The referee shall ensure that each contestant is wearing a protective
   cup and mouthpiece.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3731, 3737-38 (May 20, 1988).
