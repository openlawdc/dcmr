                                  DC REGULATIONS

HAZARDOUS WEATHER CONDITIONS (ยง 19-3424)

When necessary, the Commission may close due to hazardous weather conditions.
The Executive Director may close the Commission for operations when weather
conditions impede safe travel to work.  The Commission Personnel Procedures
Manual shall describe the process for notifying employees of work schedules
under hazardous conditions.



	SOURCE:  Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 4938, 4951 (August 29, 1997).
