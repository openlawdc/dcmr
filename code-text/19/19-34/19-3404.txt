                                  DC REGULATIONS

EMPLOYMENT AGENCIES AND TEMPORARY HELP SERVICE (ยง 19-3404)

3404.1. The Commission shall not pay fees for services of private agencies in
   recruiting for permanent staff positions, other than advertising fees.

   3404.2. The Commission may use temporary help services to assist during
   periods of heavy workload, to substitute for absent employees, or to work on
   projects of limited duration.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 4938, 4940 (August 29, 1997).
