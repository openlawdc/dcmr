                                  DC REGULATIONS

WORKERS COMPENSATION (§ 19-3414)

3414.1. Each employee on the payroll of the Commission shall be covered by a
   Workers’ Compensation policy.  Benefits shall be payable in accordance with
   District of Columbia laws.

   3414.2. Each employee injured on the job shall report the incident to their
   supervisor and Personnel Office as soon as possible.

   3414.3. Permanent employees on workers’ compensation leave shall be entitled
   to every benefit and across-the-board adjustment available to other staff
   members.

   3414.4. The Commission shall require a doctor’s certification for the
   employee to resume work following a workers’ compensation injury.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 4938, 4945 (August 29, 1997).
