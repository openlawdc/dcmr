                                  DC REGULATIONS

CHANGE IN EMPLOYEE STATUS (ยง 19-3410)

3410.1. An employee shall be eligible for health and life insurance benefits
   when she or he changes from temporary to permanent status.  In addition, the
   employee shall have eligibility for:  service awards, annual and sick leave
   accrual, and retirement.

   3410.2. When an employee changes from permanent part-time to permanent
   full-time status while in the same position, the probationary period shall
   begin retroactively to the date she or he was first placed into the position.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 4938, 4943 (August 29, 1997).
