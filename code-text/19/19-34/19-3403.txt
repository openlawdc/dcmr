                                  DC REGULATIONS

RELATIVES AND VETERANS (§ 19-3403)

3403.1. An officer/supervisor of the Commission may not appoint, employ,
   promote or advance nor advocate for the appointment, employment, promotion or
   advancement of a relative in or to a position over which the
   officer/supervisor has supervisory authority.

   3403.2. For the purpose of this section “relatives” are defined as spouse,
   parent, parent- in-law, child, step-child, sister, brother, brother-in-law,
   sister-in-law, step-parent, niece, nephew, grandparent, or grandchild.

   3403.3. A permanent full-time employee who leaves a position with the
   Commission to enter military service shall retain re-employment rights
   applicable under District or federal law in effect at the time of his/her
   separation.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 4938, 4940 (August 29, 1997).
