                                  DC REGULATIONS

SANCTIONS (§ 19-507)

507.1. Any violation of the provisions of this chapter shall be grounds for
   corrective action pursuant to the personnel regulations set forth in 19 DCMR
   chapter 2.

   507.2. The sanctions imposed by this section are in addition to, not in lieu
   of, the sanctions imposed by 5 U.S.C. § 7326, 18 U.S.C. §§ 201, 205, 208
   and 209, D.C. Code §§ 1-1461 and 1-1462, or any other applicable law or
   regulation of the United States or the District of Columbia.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 7140, 7143 (November 21, 1997).
