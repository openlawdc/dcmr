                                  DC REGULATIONS

MANAGERS LICENSE (§ 19-2008)

2008.1. Subject to § 2008.2, no person shall manage a professional contestant
   without a valid manager’s license issued by the Commission.

   2008.2. A contestant who manages his or her own affairs is not required to
   obtain a manager’s license.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3710, 3713-14 (May 20, 1988).
