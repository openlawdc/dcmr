                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPUTATION OF TIME (ยง 19-2052)

2052.1. In computing any period of time specified in this chapter, the day of
   the act, event, or default is not be counted, and the last day of the period
   is counted unless it is a Saturday, Sunday, legal holiday, or day on which the
   Department is officially closed, in which event the time period continues
   until the next day that is not a Saturday, Sunday, legal holiday, or day on
   which the Department is officially closed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3710, 3728-29 (May 20, 1988).
