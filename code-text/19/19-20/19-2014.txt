                                  DC REGULATIONS

INSPECTORS LICENSE (§ 19-2014)

2014.1. No person shall act as an inspector in an event without an
   inspector’s license issued pursuant to this chapter.

   2014.2. An applicant for a license as an inspector shall pass a written or
   oral examination administered by the Commission which tests an applicant’s
   knowledge of the rules governing contests of the type for which the applicant
   is qualified to act as an inspector.

   2014.3. An applicant for a license as an inspector shall prove to the
   satisfaction of the
   
   Commission that the inspector:
   
   
   
   (a)	Does not maintain, directly or indirectly, a financial or business
   interest in the management of a contestant;
   
   
   
   (b)	Is not an individual promoter; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Is not a stockholder or an employee of, or otherwise has a financial or
   business interest in a corporation, unincorporated club, partnership, or
   association that promotes contests.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3710, 3715-16 (May 20, 1988).
