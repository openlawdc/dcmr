                                  DC REGULATIONS

JUDICIAL REVIEW (ยง 19-2050)

2050.1. A person aggrieved by an adverse decision of the Commission, issued
   after a hearing, may seek a review of the decision by the District of Columbia
   Court of Appeals.

   2050.2. Filing notice of appeal does not act as a stay of the order of the
   Commission.

   2050.3. Failure to seek judicial review in the manner and within the time as
   the Court of Appeals by rule may prescribe results in the decision of the
   Commission becoming final.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3710, 3728 (May 20, 1988).
