                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL LICENSING PROVISIONS (ยง 19-2002)

2002.1. The Commission shall issue licenses for the following participants in
   an event:
   
   
   
   (a)	Professional boxer;
   
   
   
   (b)	Professional wrestler;
   
   
   
   (c)	Professional kickboxer;
   
   
   
   (d)	Amateur boxer;
   
   
   
   (e)	Manager;
   
   
   
   (f)	Promoter;
   
   
   
   (g)	Second;
   
   
   
   (h)	Matchmaker;
   
   
   
   (i)	Referee;
   
   
   
   (j)	Timekeeper;
   
   
   
   (k)	 Inspector;
   
   
   
   (l)	Physician;
   
   
   
   (m)	Judge; and
   
   
   
   (n)	Announcer.

   2002.2. A license issued by the Commission is valid for one (1) year from the
   date it is issued.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3710, 3711 (May 20, 1988).
