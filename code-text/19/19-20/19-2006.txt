                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROFESSIONAL KICKBOXERS LICENSE (§ 19-2006)

2006.1. No person shall kickbox in a professional kickboxing event without a
   professional kickboxer’s license issued pursuant to this chapter.

   2006.2. An applicant for a professional kickboxer’s license shall prove to
   the satisfaction of the Commission that the applicant is at least eighteen
   (18) years of age.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3710, 3713 (May 20, 1988).
