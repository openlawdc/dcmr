                                  DC REGULATIONS

REFEREES LICENSE (§ 19-2012)

2012.1. No person shall act as a referee for an event without a referee’s
   license issued pursuant to this chapter.

   2012.2. An applicant for a license as a referee shall pass a written or oral
   examination administered by the Commission which tests an applicant’s
   knowledge of the rules governing events of the type which the applicant is
   qualified to referee.

   2012.3. An applicant for a license as a referee shall pass a medical
   examination conducted by a licensed physician approved by the Commission.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3710, 3715 (May 20, 1988).
