                                  DC REGULATIONS

SECONDS LICENSE (§ 19-2010)

2010.1. Except as provided in § 2010.2, no person shall act as a second in an
   event between professional contestants without a second’s license issued
   pursuant to this chapter.

   2010.2. The manager of a contestant may act as a second for the contestant if
   the manager is licensed pursuant to this chapter.

   2010.3. An applicant for a license as a second shall include with the
   applicant the name of the applicant’s employer.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 3710, 3714-15 (May 20, 1988).
