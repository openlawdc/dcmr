                                  DC REGULATIONS

WARNINGS (ยง 19-2414)

2414.1. The referee shall issue a single warning for the following
   infractions. After the initial warning, if the prohibited conduct persists, a
   penalty will be issued. The penalty may result in a deduction of points or
   disqualification:
   
   
   
   (a)	Holding or grabbing the fence;
   
   
   
   (b)	Holding opponent&#8217;s shorts or gloves; or
   
   
   
   (c)	The presence of more than one second on the fighting area perimeter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 7226 (July 27, 2007).
