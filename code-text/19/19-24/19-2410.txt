                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEARANCE (ยง 19-2410)

2410.1. All contestants shall be cleanly shaven immediately prior to
   competition, except that a contestant may wear a closely cropped mustache.

   2410.2. Hair shall be trimmed or tied back in such a manner as not to
   interfere with the vision of either contestant or cover any part of a
   contestant&#8217;s face.

   2410.3. Jewelry or piercing accessories are prohibited during competition.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 7224 (July 27, 2007).
