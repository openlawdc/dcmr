                                  DC REGULATIONS

ROUND LENGTH (ยง 19-2411)

2411.1. Each non-championship mixed martial arts contest shall be three
   rounds, of five minutes duration, with a one-minute rest period between each
   round.

   2411.2. Each championship mixed martial arts contest shall be five rounds, of
   five minutes duration, with a one-minute rest period between each round.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 7224 (July 27, 2007).
