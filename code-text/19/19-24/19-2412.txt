                                  DC REGULATIONS

STOPPING A CONTEST (ยง 19-2412)

2412.1. The referee and ringside physician are the sole arbiters of a bout and
   are the only individuals authorized to enter the fighting area at any time
   during competition and authorized to stop a contest.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 7224 (July 27, 2007).
