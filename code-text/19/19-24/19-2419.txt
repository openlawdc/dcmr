LICENSING (ยง 19-2419)

2419.1. All mixed martial arts events shall be subject to the licensing
   requirements of 19 DCMR Chapter 20.

   2419.2. The fee for a mixed martial artist license shall be as set forth in 17
   DCMR Chapter 35.
