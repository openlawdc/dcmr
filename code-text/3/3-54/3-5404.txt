                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEALS (§ 3-5404)

5404.1. Appeals of any final order issued or fine levied by the Board in
   accordance with the Act or regulations promulgated pursuant to the Act shall
   be made to the Superior Court of the District of Columbia within twenty (20)
   days of the date the Board or Director’s final order or fine is served upon
   a person subject to the final order or fine.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 747 (January 25, 2013).
