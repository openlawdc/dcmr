GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 3-1900)

1900.1. In accordance with the provisions of the D.C. Self-Government Act, the
   Council of the District of Columbia may, by Resolution, call a special
   election to present for an advisory referendum vote of the people any
   proposition upon which the Council desires to take action.

   1900.2. The District of Columbia Board of Elections and Ethics shall be
   authorized to promulgate rules and regulations for the implementation and
   conduct of elections for advisory referendum votes, pursuant to D.C. Code ยง
   1-1001.05(a)(14) (2006 Repl.).

   1900.3. Any political committee organized in support of, or in opposition to,
   any advisory referendum presented to voters shall file a verified statement of
   contributions with the Office of Campaign Finance.
