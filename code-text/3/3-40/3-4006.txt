DURATION OF TRANSITION COMMITTEES (ยง 3-4006)

4006.1. A transition committee shall terminate no later than forty-five (45)
   days from the beginning of the term of the new Mayor or Council Chairman,
   except that the transition committee may continue to accept contributions
   necessary to retire the debts of the committee.

   4006.2. When terminating, transition committees shall adhere to the applicable
   provisions of ยง 3016 of Chapter 30 of this title.
