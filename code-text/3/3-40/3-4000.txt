                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSITION COMMITTEES, GENERALLY (ยง 3-4000)

4000.1. A transition committee is a person, or group of persons, organized for
   the purpose of soliciting, accepting, or expending funds for office and
   personnel transition on behalf of the Chairman of the Council or the Mayor.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 1402 (February 8, 2013); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11864 (August 16, 2013).
