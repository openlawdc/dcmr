LIMITATIONS ON THE USE OF TRANSITION COMMITTEE FUNDS (ยง 3-4005)

4005.1. Transition committee funds shall be used solely for the purpose of
   facilitating the office and personnel transition on behalf of either the
   Chairman of the Council or the Mayor.

   4005.2. The provisions of ยง 3013 of Chapter 30 of this title, concerning
   impermissible uses of campaign funds, shall apply to transition committees,
   unless the expenditures stated therein are solely related to activities
   necessary to facilitate the office and personnel transition on behalf of the
   newly elected official.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 1402 (February 8, 2013); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11864 (August 16, 2013).
