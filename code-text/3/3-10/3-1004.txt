SIGNATURE REQUIREMENTS FOR INITIATIVE AND REFERENDUM PETITIONS (ยง 3-1004)

1004.1. An initiative or referendum petition shall be signed by registered
   voters equal in number to five percent (5%) of the registered qualified
   electors of the District of Columbia; Provided, that the total signatures
   submitted include five percent (5%) of the registered qualified electors in
   each of five (5) or more of the eight (8) election wards.

   1004.2. The number of registered qualified electors used for computing the
   signature requirements in ยง 1004.1 shall be based upon the latest official
   count of registered qualified electors made by the Board which was issued
   thirty (30) or more days prior to submission of the signatures for the
   particular initiative or referendum petition.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 43 D.C. Reg. 103, 109 (January 12,
   1996).
