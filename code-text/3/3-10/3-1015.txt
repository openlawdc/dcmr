                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROPOSER SUBSTITUTION (ยง 3-1015)

1015.1. In the event that any proposer of an initiative or referendum measure
   becomes unable or unwilling to act prior to submission of the petition for
   filing, the Board may, based on the evidence presented and for good cause
   shown, consider another register qualified elector as a substitute or
   additional proposer for the measure.

   1015.2. In order to be considered as a substitute or additional proposer for a
   measure, a registered qualified elector shall also submit to the Board the
   following:
   
   
   
   (a)	A statement indicating which measure he or she wishes to be considered
   for;
   
   
   
   (b)	An affidavit under oath containing the name, telephone number, and
   residence address of the proposer; and
   
   
   
   (c)	A statement that the proposer is a registered qualified elector of the
   District of Columbia.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 43 D.C. Reg. 1928 (April 12, 1996).
