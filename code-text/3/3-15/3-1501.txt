                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOMINATION OF CANDIDATES BY PETITION (ยง 3-1501)

1501.1. The front of each page of the nominating petition shall contain the
   following information:
   
   
   
   (a)	The names of the candidates for President and Vice President;
   
   
   
   (b)	The name of the political party with which they are affiliated;
   
   
   
   (c)	The names, addresses, and registration number of the three (3) candidates
   for presidential electors; and
   
   
   
   (d)	A statement indicating that any registered voter, regardless of party
   affiliation, may sign the petition; and
   
   
   
   (e)	A statement that only the names of the candidates for President and Vice
   President will be listed on the ballot.

   1501.2. The back of each page of the petition shall contain the name, address,
   and telephone number of the person circulating the petition.

   1501.3. No nominating petition form shall be issued by the Board until the
   information required by ยง 1501.1 has been placed in all appropriate spaces on
   the petition form.

   1501.4. If a candidate is not affiliated with any political party, each
   nominating petition form issued by the Board on his or her behalf shall
   indicate that he or she is &#8220;Independent.&#8221;
