                                  DC REGULATIONS

OVERSIGHT (ยง 3-2015)

2015.1. On or before the 30th day of June of each calendar year, the Secretary
   shall compile and submit to the Council of the District of Columbia on behalf
   of the Mayor, a report covering the disclosure activities of each agency and
   of the Executive as a whole during the preceding year.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 46 D.C. Reg. 1191, 1198 (February 12,
   1999).
