                                  DC REGULATIONS

WITHDRAWAL OF CANDIDATES (ยง 3-1311)

1311.1. A person who has submitted a petition for appointment to the office of
   Advisory Neighborhood Commissioner may withdraw his or her petition by
   executing and filing with the Board a notarized affidavit stating that he or
   she irrevocably withdraws his or her candidacy for appointment to the office.

   1311.2. In the event that the Board has already forwarded the list of names to
   the respective Advisory Neighborhood Commission, the Board shall notify the
   Chairperson of that Advisory Neighborhood Commission of the withdrawal.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 43 D.C. Reg. 1078, 1102 (March 1,
   1996).
