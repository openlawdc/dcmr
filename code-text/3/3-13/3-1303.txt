                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTIFICATION OF PROSPECTIVE RESIGNATION (§ 3-1303)

1303.1. A Commissioner may resign prospectively, by submitting an irrevocable
   letter of prospective resignation to the Board of Elections.

   1303.2. The letter of prospective resignation shall be sworn, shall state that
   it is irrevocable, and shall give the date that the resignation will become
   effective.

   1303.3. The Commissioner shall also submit copies of the letter of prospective
   resignation to the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	The Council of the District of Columbia;
   
   
   
   (b)	The Mayor; and
   
   
   
   (c)	The Chairperson of the member’s Advisory Neighborhood Commission.

   1303.4. Upon receipt of an irrevocable letter of prospective resignation, the
   Board shall process the prospective vacancy in the same manner outlined for
   filling an existing vacancy.

   1303.5. The resignation shall become effective not more than sixty (60) days
   following receipt of the letter by the Board of Elections.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 43 D.C. Reg. 1078, 1095-96 (March 1,
   1996).
