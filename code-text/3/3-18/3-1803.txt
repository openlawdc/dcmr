                                  DC REGULATIONS

ELECTOR REVIEW (ยง 3-1803)

1803.1. Within ten (10) days of publication in the D.C. Register, any
   registered qualified elector who objects to the proposed formulations may
   request a hearing before the Board, pursuant to chapter 4 of this title, to
   raise any objections and/or correct any alleged inaccuracies or indicators of
   prejudice for or against the proposed Charter amendment(s).

   1803.2. If no hearing is requested, the proposed formulations, as published by
   the Board in the D.C. Register, shall be considered to be accepted at the
   expiration of the ten (10) day review period.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 D.C. Reg. 7083, 7086 (October 8,
   1993); as amended by Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at
   59 DCR 627 (January 27, 2012)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final
   Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 4780, 4783 (May 11, 2012).
   
   
   
   	HISTORICAL NOTE: Prior to October 8, 1993, the Board of Elections and Ethics
   published Final Rulemaking notice published at 35 D.C. Reg. 5454, 5460 (July
   15, 1988).
