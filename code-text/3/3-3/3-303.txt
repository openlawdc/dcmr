                                  DC REGULATIONS

ISSUANCE OF ADVISORY OPINIONS (ยง 3-303)

303.1. The Board shall issue an advisory opinion in response to each request
   within a reasonable period of time following the elapsed period of time noted
   for receipt of public comments.

   303.2. Within five (5) working days after issuance of the opinion, the Board
   shall notify the requestor by telephone and shall forward a copy of the
   advisory opinion by certified mail to the requestor.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 D.C. Reg. 2153, 2154 (May 15, 1981).
