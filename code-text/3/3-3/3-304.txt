                                  DC REGULATIONS

PUBLICATION OF ADVISORY OPINIONS (ยง 3-304)

304.1. The Board shall publish each advisory opinion in the D.C. Register
   within thirty (30) calendar days of the issuance of the opinion.

   304.2. The published advisory opinion shall not disclose the identity of the
   requestor unless the disclosure has been previously consented to in writing by
   the requestor.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 D.C. Reg. 2153, 2155 (May 15, 1981).
