                                  DC REGULATIONS

REQUESTS FOR ADVISORY OPINIONS (ยง 3-301)

301.1. A request for an advisory opinion shall be in writing, signed by the
   requestor and filed with the General Counsel to the Board of Elections and
   Ethics.

   301.2. Upon receipt of a request for an advisory opinion relative to the
   Campaign Finance Act, the General Counsel shall transmit a copy of the request
   for an advisory opinion to the Director of Campaign Finance.

   301.3. A request for an advisory opinion shall contain the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	The full name, residence address, and telephone number of the requestor;
   and
   
   
   
   (b)	A clear and concise statement of the facts relating to the specific
   transaction or activity which constitute a violation of the law.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 D.C. Reg. 2153-54 (May 15, 1981).
