                                  DC REGULATIONS

LEGAL DEFENSE COMMITTEES, GENERALLY (§ 3-3800)

3800.1. A legal defense committee is a person, or group of persons, organized
   for the purpose of soliciting, accepting, or expending funds to defray the
   professional fees and costs for a public official’s legal defense to one or
   more civil, criminal, or administrative proceedings.

   3800.2. One legal defense committee and one legal defense checking account may
   be established and maintained for the purpose set forth in § 3800.1.

   3800.3. No committee, fund, entity, or trust may be established to defray
   professional fees and costs except pursuant to this chapter.
