                                  DC REGULATIONS

POST GENERAL ELECTION SUMMARY REPORT (§ 3-817)

817.1. Within ninety (90) days following every general election, the Board
   shall publish on its website a report (“post general election summary
   report”) containing the following information:
   
   
   
   (a)  	The total number of ballots cast and counted, with subtotals for each
   type of ballot;
   
   (b)  	The total number of spoiled and special ballots not counted;
   
   (c)  	The total number of persons registered to vote more than thirty (30)
   days preceding the election, broken down by party, ward, and precinct;
   
   (d)  	The number of persons who registered to vote between thirty (30) days
   preceding the election and the date of the election;
   
   (e)  	The number of persons who registered to vote at an early voting
   		center;
   
   (f)  	The number of persons who registered to vote on Election Day;
   
   (g)  	The number of polling place officials at each precinct, broken down by
   position title;
   
   (h)  	Copies of any unofficial summary reports generated by the Board on
   election night;
   
   (i) 	A summary of issues identified in Precinct Captain or Area Representative
   reports;
   
   (j)  	Performance measurement data of polling place officials;
   
   (k)  	A description of any irregularities experienced on Election Day; 		and
   
   (l) 	Any other relevant information.
