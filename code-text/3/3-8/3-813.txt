                                  DC REGULATIONS

CERTIFICATION OF ELECTION RESULTS (§ 3-813)

813.1. The Board shall certify the results of each election.

   813.2. The Board shall publish the results of each election and the nominees
   or winners in the D.C. Register and on the Board’s website.
