                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISCRETIONARY MANUAL TABULATION (ยง 3-810)

810.1. Notwithstanding instances when manual tabulation is required by law or
   this chapter, the Board may order that ballots be manually inspected and
   tabulated under the following circumstances:
   
   
   
   (a)  	Upon the filing of a recount petition, when it appears that a
   disproportionate number of potential undervotes or overvotes have occurred in
   a particular precinct, or to determine whether write-in votes have been cast
   that affect vote totals for candidates whose names are pre-printed on the
   ballot;
   
   
   
   (b)	When there is evidence of a machine miscount or malfunction; 			or
   
   (c)	When it is determined by the Board that manual tabulation is necessary to
   ascertain correct vote totals.

   810.2. When manual tabulation is ordered pursuant to this section:
   
   
   
   (a) 	Validity of ballots and votes and tabulation procedures shall conform to
   the rules specified in this chapter;
   
   
   
   (b) 	Only the ballots for those precincts and contests designated by the Board
   shall be manually tabulated; and
   
   
   
   (c) 	The Board shall direct that the tabulation be conducted at a time that is
   practicable.
