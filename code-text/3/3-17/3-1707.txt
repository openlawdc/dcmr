                                  DC REGULATIONS

WITHDRAWAL OF CANDIDATES (§ 3-1707)

1707.1. A person who has been nominated as a candidate for any office under
   this section may withdraw his or her nomination by executing and filing with
   the Board a notarized affidavit stating that he or she irrevocably withdraws
   his or her candidacy for the office to which he or she has been nominated.

   1707.2. In the event the affidavit is received less than forty-five (45) days
   before the next election, the name of the person may appear on the official
   ballot, notwithstanding withdrawal, if the Board determines that it is
   impracticable to exclude that person’s name.

   1707.3. Notice shall be given to the electorate in the affected precincts
   informing the voter of the withdrawal of nomination for office by candidates
   whose names shall appear on the ballot.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 D.C. Reg. 2006, 2027 (March 11,
