                                  DC REGULATIONS

ORDERS OF THE BOARD (ยง 3-104)

104.1. The Chairperson shall approve or disapprove Board orders in writing
   with his or her signature, as may be appropriate.

   104.2. The Chairperson shall be legally bound to administer a Board order
   notwithstanding the fact that he or she may have disapproved the order.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 D.C. Reg. 1726 (April 17, 1981),
   incorporating by reference the text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 28
   D.C. Reg.684, 688 (February 13, 1981).
