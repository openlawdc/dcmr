                                  DC REGULATIONS

WRITTEN COMMUNICATIONS (§ 3-105)

105.1. For the purposes of this chapter, the term &#8220;written
   communications&#8221; means letters, memoranda, or other documents.

   105.2. All requests for documents shall be handled in accordance with
   procedures set forth in the District of Columbia Freedom of Information Act.

   105.3. Where a majority of the Board votes to issue a communication, the
   Chairperson or the Chairperson’s designee may sign the document and issue it
   on behalf of the Board.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 D.C. Reg. 1726 (April 17, 1981),
   incorporating by reference the text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 28
   D.C. Reg.684, 688 (February 13, 1981).
   
   
   
   	EDITOR’S NOTE: The District of Columbia Freedom of Information Act, which
   is referenced in subsection 105.2 of this section is codified at D.C. Code
   §§ 2-531 et seq. (2006 Repl.).
