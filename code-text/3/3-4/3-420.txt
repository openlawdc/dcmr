                                  DC REGULATIONS

SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS (ยง 3-420)

420.1. The Fifth Amendment privilege against self-incrimination is applicable
   to hearings before the Board.

   420.2. The Board shall on the record inform witnesses of their right to assert
   this privilege.

   420.3. The Board shall warn witnesses that failure to assert this protection
   waives it.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 D.C. Reg. 432, 447 (January 23,
   1998).
