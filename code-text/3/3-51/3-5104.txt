                                  DC REGULATIONS

ORDERS OF THE BOARD (§ 3-5104)

5104.1. The Board shall issue all final orders in writing, signed manually or
   electronically by each member of the Board participating in the final order.

   5104.2. Any Board member participating in a final order may issue a concurring
   or dissenting opinion to the final order, and such opinion shall be attached
   to the Board’s final order.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 739 (January 25, 2013).
