                                  DC REGULATIONS

REQUEST FOR EXEMPTION (ยง 3-3500)

3500.1. Any member, contributor, or recipient of expenditures involving a
   minor party may request an exemption from the disclosure provisions of this
   title.

   3500.2. The requestor shall apply, in writing, to the Director.
