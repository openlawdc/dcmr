DURATION OF INAUGURAL COMMITTEES (ยง 3-3906)

3906.1. An inaugural committee shall terminate no later than forty-five (45)
   days from the beginning of the term of the new Mayor, except that the
   inaugural committee may continue to accept contributions necessary to retire
   the debts of the committee.

   3906.2. When terminating, inaugural committees shall adhere to the applicable
   provisions of ยง 3016 of Chapter 30 of this title.
