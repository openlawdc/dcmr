                                  DC REGULATIONS

INAUGURAL COMMITTEES, GENERALLY (ยง 3-3900)

3900.1. An inaugural committee is a person, or group of persons, organized for
   the purpose of soliciting, accepting, and spending funds and coordinating
   activities to celebrate the election of a new Mayor.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 1402 (February 8, 2013); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11864 (August 16, 2013).
