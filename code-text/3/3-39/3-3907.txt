                                  DC REGULATIONS

USE OF SURPLUS FUNDS (ยง 3-3907)

3907.1. Any remaining funds of an inaugural committee shall be transferred
   only to either:
   
   
   
   (a)	A non-profit organization within the meaning of Section 501(c) of the
   Internal Revenue Code operating in good standing in the District of Columbia
   for a minimum of one calendar year prior to the date of any transfer; or
   
   
   
   (b)	A constituent-service program.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 1402 (February 8, 2013); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11864 (August 16, 2013).
