LIMITATIONS ON THE USE OF INAUGURAL COMMITTEE FUNDS (ยง 3-3905)

3905.1. Inaugural committee funds shall be used solely for the purpose of
   financing activities to celebrate the election of a new Mayor.

   3905.2. The provisions of ยง 3013 of Chapter 30 of this title, concerning
   impermissible uses of campaign funds, shall apply to inaugural committees
   unless the expenditures stated therein are solely related to activities to
   celebrate the election of a new Mayor.
