                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRIMARY ELECTION[COLON] VOTING AND CERTIFICATION OF NOMINEES IN INITIAL ELECTION
FOR U.S. SENATOR (ยง 3-1611)

1611.1. Each person eligible to vote in the initial primary election for the
   office of U.S. Senator may cast a ballot for no more than two (2) candidates
   in the contest.

   1611.2. At the time of certification of the initial primary election results
   for the office U.S. Senator, the Board shall certify the two (2) individuals
   receiving the highest number of votes in each party primary as the nominees of
   such parties.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 D.C. Reg. 8364, 8370 (December 15,
   1989).
