                                  DC REGULATIONS

PETITION FORM FOR CANDIDATES AFFILIATED WITH A PARTY NOT ELIGIBLE TO CONDUCT A
PRIMARY ELECTION (ยง 3-1603)

1603.1. The front of each page of the petition shall contain the name, address
   and registration number of the candidate; the office to which the candidate
   seeks election; the ward, school district or single-member district (where
   applicable); and the political party with which the candidate is affiliated.

   1603.2. The back of each page of the petition shall contain the name, address
   (telephone number optional) of the person circulating the petition and the
   affidavit of circulator required by this chapter.

   1603.3. The back of the petition page shall inform the circulator of the
   penalties for conviction of providing false information in the affidavit of
   the circulator required by this chapter.

   1603.4. No nominating petition form shall be issued by the Board until the
   information required pursuant to ยง 1603.1 has been placed in all appropriate
   spaces on the petition form.
