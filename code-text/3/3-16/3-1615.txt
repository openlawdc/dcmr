                                  DC REGULATIONS

WITHDRAWAL OF CANDIDATES (ยง 3-1615)

1615.1. A person who has been nominated may withdraw his or her nomination by
   executing and filing with the Board a notarized affidavit stating that he or
   she irrevocably withdraws the candidacy for the office to which he or she has
   been nominated.

   1615.2. The affidavit of withdrawal shall be filed with the Board before the
   forty-fifth (45th) day prior to election day. If the affidavit of withdrawal
   is filed after the deadline for withdrawals, the name of the candidate may
   appear on the official ballot.

   1615.3. Notice shall be given in the affected precincts informing the voters
   of the withdrawal of candidates whose names appear on the ballot.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 D.C. Reg. 8364, 8371 (December 15,
   1989).
