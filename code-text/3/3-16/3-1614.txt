                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL ELECTION[COLON] U.S. REPRESENTATIVE (ยง 3-1614)

1614.1. Each person eligible to vote in the general election for U.S.
   Representative may cast a ballot for no more than one (1) candidate in the
   contest.

   1614.2. At the time of certification of the general election results for the
   office of U.S. Representative, the Board shall certify the individual
   receiving the highest number of votes as the winner.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 D.C. Reg. 8364, 8371 (December 15,
   1989).
