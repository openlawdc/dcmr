                                  DC REGULATIONS

FILING AND PROCESSING OF THE DECLARATION OF CANDIDACY (§ 3-602)

602.1. Except as provided in § 602.2, the deadline for filing the Declaration
   of Candidacy and Affidavit of Qualifications shall be the same date as the
   deadline for filing nominating petitions for the particular office sought.

   602.2. In the event the nomination of candidates for election to the office of
   presidential elector is made by message to the Board pursuant to D.C. Code §
   1-1001.08(d) (2006 Repl.), the deadline for filing the Declaration shall be
   the same date as the deadline for making nominations by message.

   602.3. The Board shall reject the nominating petition of any individual who
   fails to timely file a Declaration of Candidacy and Affidavit of
   Qualifications.

   602.4. Within three (3) working days after the deadline for filing nomination
   petitions for any office, the Executive Director or his or her designee shall
   issue a preliminary determination as to the eligibility of the declarant to be
   candidate for the particular office sought.

   602.5. Notice of the Executive Director’s determination shall be served
   immediately by certified mail upon each declarant and upon the chairperson of
   any political committee(s) registered as supporting that individual’s
   candidacy.

   602.6. The preliminary determination of eligibility shall be based solely upon
   information contained in the Declaration of Candidacy and Affidavit of
   Qualifications and upon information contained in other public records and
   documents as may be maintained by the Board.

   602.7. The preliminary determination of eligibility shall in no way be deemed
   to preclude further inquiry into or challenge to the eligibility of an
   individual for candidacy or office made prior to the certification of election
   results by the Board and based upon evidence which was not known to the Board
   at the time of the preliminary determination or upon evidence of changed
   circumstances.

   602.8. The criteria used for determining eligibility to be a candidate shall
   be limited to the appropriate statutory qualifications for the particular
   office sought.

   602.9. In the event it is determined that an individual is ineligible to be a
   candidate for the particular office sought, the individual’s nominating
   petition shall nevertheless be posted for the challenge period specified in
   D.C. Code § 1-1001.08(o) (2006 Repl.), along with the Executive Director’s
   determination.
