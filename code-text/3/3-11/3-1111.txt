                                  DC REGULATIONS

RETENTION OF RECORDS (ยง 3-1111)

1111.1. The Board shall preserve recall petitions for one (1) year after the
   date of the election for which the petition qualified or attempted to qualify
   for placement on the ballot.

   1111.2. Recall petitions shall be destroyed following the lapse of the one (1)
   year period unless legal action relating to the petitions is pending.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 D.C. Reg. 4419, 4434 (August 18,
   1995).
