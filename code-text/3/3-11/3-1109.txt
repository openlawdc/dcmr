                                  DC REGULATIONS

CERTIFICATION OF INSUFFICIENCY (ยง 3-1109)

1109.1. Upon completion of the counting and validation process, if a recall
   petition is found to be numerically insufficient, the Board shall certify the
   insufficiency of the petition and that the recall measure did not qualify for
   placement on the ballot.

   1109.2. Valid signatures appearing on an insufficient petition shall not be
   resubmitted for filing in support of that recall measure or submitted in
   support of another.

   1109.3. The proposer(s) of a recall measure which failed to qualify for the
   ballot due to the numerical insufficiency of the petition shall commence the
   respective process anew.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 D.C. Reg. 4419, 4432-33 (August 18,
   1995).
