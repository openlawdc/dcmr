                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROPOSED FINDINGS (ยง 3-5520)

5520.1. The Board may request parties to submit proposed findings of fact and
   conclusions of law for the consideration of the Board within the time as the
   Chairperson may direct.

   5520.2. Copies of proposed findings and conclusions shall be served by each
   party upon all other parties.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 747 (January 25, 2013).
