                                  DC REGULATIONS

STIPULATIONS (ยง 3-5512)

5512.1. Apart from stipulations reached during or as a result of the
   pre-hearing conference, the parties may stipulate in writing at any stage in
   the proceeding or orally during the hearing any relevant fact or the contents
   or authenticity of any document.

   5512.2. Post-conference stipulations may be received as evidence.

   5512.3. Parties may also stipulate the procedure to be followed in the
   proceeding and such stipulation may, on motion of all parties, be approved by
   the Board and govern the conduct of the proceeding.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 747 (January 25, 2013).
