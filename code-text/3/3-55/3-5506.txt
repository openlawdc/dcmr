                                  DC REGULATIONS

MEETINGS AND HEARINGS (ยง 3-5506)

5506.1. Hearings shall be scheduled as needed for the purpose of receiving
   evidence and testimony on specific matters.

   5506.2. Meetings and hearings shall be held at the time and place the Board or
   the Chairperson designates.

   5506.3. A member absent at the decision meeting on any matter may cast an
   absentee vote only if the member attended all other of the meetings or
   hearings on a matter before Board.

   5506.4. A member attending the decision meeting and having read the transcript
   and reviewed the complete record may vote even though that member may not have
   attended any or all of the prior meetings or hearings on a matter before the
   Board.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 747 (January 25, 2013).
