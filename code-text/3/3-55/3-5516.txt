                                  DC REGULATIONS

INTERPRETERS (ยง 3-5516)

5516.1. The Board shall ascertain before the hearing whether an interpreter
   will be required and make appropriate arrangements.

   5516.2. An oath or affirmation shall be administered to the interpreter orally
   or in writing.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 747 (January 25, 2013).
