                                  DC REGULATIONS

NONAPPEARANCE OF PARTIES AND DEFAULTS (ยง 3-5514)

5514.1. The Board may wait a reasonable length of time for a party to appear
   before beginning a proceeding. After a reasonable time, however, if a party
   who has received notice has not appeared, the Chairperson may proceed as
   follows:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The Chairperson may proceed with the hearing, obtain the testimony of those
   persons present, and, on the basis of the testimony and the record, the Board
   may issue a decision in the case; or
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The Chairman, for good and sufficient cause, may postpone the hearing without
   taking testimony.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 747 (January 25, 2013).
