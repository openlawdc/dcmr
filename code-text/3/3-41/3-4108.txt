                                  DC REGULATIONS

USE OF SURPLUS FUNDS (ยง 3-4108)

4108.1. Any remaining funds of an exploratory committee shall be transferred
   only to either:
   
   
   
   (a)	An established principal campaign or political committee; or
   
   
   
   (b)	A charitable organization that meets the requirements of tax laws of the
   District of Columbia.

   4108.2. All contributions and fund balances of any exploratory committee shall
   not be deemed the personal funds of any individual, including the prospective
   candidate of the exploratory committee.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 1402 (February 8, 2013); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11864 (August 16, 2013).
