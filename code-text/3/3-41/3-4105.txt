EXPLORATORY COMMITTEE CONTRIBUTION LIMITATIONS (ยง 3-4105)

4105.1. Contributions in support of an exploratory committee shall be received
   or made in accordance with ยง 3009 of Chapter 30 of this title, except that
   individual and aggregate contributions shall be limited for the following
   exploratory committees to the amounts specified:
   
   
   
   (a)	Mayor &#8211; $2,000 individual, and $200,000 aggregate;
   
   
   
   (b)	Chairman of the Council &#8211; $1,500 individual, and $150,000 aggregate;
   
   
   
   (c)	At-large member of the Council &#8211; $1,000 individual, and $100,000
   aggregate;
   
   
   
   (d)	Ward Councilmember or President of the State Board of Education &#8211;
   $500 individual, and $50,000 aggregate; and
   
   
   
   (e)	Member of the State Board of Education &#8211; $200 individual, and
   $20,000 aggregate.
