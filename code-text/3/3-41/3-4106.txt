LIMITATIONS ON THE USE OF EXPLORATORY COMMITTEE FUNDS (ยง 3-4106)

4106.1. Exploratory committee funds shall be used solely for the purpose of
   financing, directly or indirectly, an examination of the feasibility of
   becoming a candidate for an elective office in the District of Columbia.

   4106.2. The provisions of ยง 3013 of Chapter 30 of this title, concerning
   impermissible uses of campaign funds, shall apply to exploratory committees
   unless the expenditures stated therein are solely related to exploratory
   activities.
