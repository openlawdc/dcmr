                                  DC REGULATIONS

SUMMARY ADJUDICATION (ยง 1-2819)

2819.1. A party may request that an Administrative Law Judge decide a case
   summarily, without an evidentiary hearing.  Such a motion must include
   sufficient evidence of undisputed facts and citation of controlling legal
   authority.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 6399 (June 25, 2004); as amended
   by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 12541, 12555 (December 31,
   2010).
