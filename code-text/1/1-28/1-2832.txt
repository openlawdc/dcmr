                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECUSAL; ETHICS COMPLIANCE (ยง 1-2832)

2832.1. An Administrative Law Judge shall recuse himself or herself in
   accordance with the standards applicable to judges of the Superior Court of
   the District of Columbia.

   2832.2. Administrative Law Judges shall at all times be in compliance with the
   OAH Code of Judicial Ethics, which shall be available to the public.
