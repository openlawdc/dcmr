                                  DC REGULATIONS

OUTSTANDING ADVANCES (ยง 1-808)

808.1. There shall be no more than one (1) outstanding advance or portion
   thereof, due the government from any traveler.

   808.2. Requests for additional advances or requests for travel shall not be
   processed until such time that the previous advance or portion thereof that is
   due has been repaid.

   808.3. Outstanding advances which have not been fully recovered by deductions
   from reimbursement vouchers or voluntary refunds by the traveler shall be
   recovered from the person to whom the advance was made, or his or her estate,
   in accordance with law and regulation.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 32 DCR 1337, 1343 (March 8, 1985).
