                                  DC REGULATIONS

PURPOSE AND SCOPE (§ 1-800)

800.1. The purpose of this chapter is to establish the procedure for the
   request and authorization of official travel and the reimbursement of official
   business expenses, pursuant to Reorganization Plan No. 5 of 1983 and Mayor’s
   Order 84-52.

   800.2. The provisions of this chapter shall apply to all employees of the
   District of Columbia Government.
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is section
   448 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act of 1973, as amended, Pub. L. No.
   93-198, 87 Stat. 801 (codified at D.C. Code § 47-310, as amended, (1997 Repl.
   And 1999 Supp.)) and Reorganization Plan No. 5 of 1983, effective March 1,
   1984.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 32 DCR 1337 (March 8, 1985).
