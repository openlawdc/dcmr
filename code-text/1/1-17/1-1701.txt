                                  DC REGULATIONS

DESIGNATED PAYMENT OFFICERS (§ 1-1701)

1701.1. The primary certifying officer for each agency shall serve as the
   agency’s designated payment officer.

   1701.2. The designated payment officer shall be responsible for the following
   duties:
   
   
   
   (a)	Designate the central receiving point for all invoices from business
   concerns;
   
   
   
   (b)	Establish a system for recording the receipt of all invoices;
   
   
   
   (c)	Determine whether invoices meet the criteria for proper invoices under §
   1704;
   
   
   
   (d)	Notify a business concern in writing, as provided in § 1705, of any
   defects or impropriety in invoices which would prevent payment of the invoice;
   
   
   
   (e)	Determine the required payment date for all invoices that meet the
   requirements for payments; and
   
   
   
   (f)	Calculate the penalties due on payments which are not made by the required
   payment date.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8131-32 (November 18, 1988).
