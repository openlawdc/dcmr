                                  DC REGULATIONS

TIME DISCOUNTS (ยง 1-1708)

1708.1. An agency shall not take a time discount, by expediting a payment in
   exchange for a discount of the amount due, unless it is more economical to
   expedite payments than to pay on the required date.

   1708.2. An agency shall not pay the discounted price if the payment is not
   made within the specified discount period.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8131, 8135 (November 18, 1988).
