                                  DC REGULATIONS

PAYMENT OF CIVIL SANCTIONS (ยง 1-2215)

2215.1. An administrative law judge may impose monetary fines and penalties.

   2215.2. All checks must be certified and made payable to &#8220;D.C.
   Treasurer.&#8221;

   2215.3. Payment of the fine does not relieve the respondent of the obligation
   to abate the infraction cited in the NOI.
