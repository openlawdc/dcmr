                                  DC REGULATIONS

ORDER OF PROCEDURE (§ 1-2207)

2207.1. The order of procedure for presenting evidence at the hearing shall be
   as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	Introductory statement by the Presiding Officer;
   
   
   
   (b)	Applicant’s case;
   
   
   
   (c)	Testimony from parties in support of the application;
   
   
   
   (d)	Testimony from parties in opposition to the application; and
   
   
   
   (e)	Rebuttal by applicant.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 5101, 5106 (July 1, 1988).
