                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONDUCT OF MEETINGS (ยง 1-602)

602.1 	The Chairperson of the Board shall conduct each meeting.



602.2 	If the Chairperson is unable to serve, the Vice Chairperson shall do so.



602.3 	The Chairperson may, at his or her discretion, allow members of the
public to make statements at any meeting where the statements address:



(a)	Any Construction Codes provisions under consideration by the Board; or



(b)	Any procedures employed by the Board in considering amendments to the
Construction Codes.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 31 DCR 6111 (December 7, 1984); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 124, 125 (January 1, 2010).
