                                  DC REGULATIONS

PURPOSE (§ 1-600)

600.1 	This chapter establishes the procedures by which the Construction Codes
Coordinating Board (“Board”) and the Department of Consumer and Regulatory
Affairs (“Department”) shall comply with the requirements of Mayor’s Order
2009-22, dated February 25, 2009, in implementing the promulgation of revisions
to the District of Columbia Construction Codes (D.C. Official Code §§ 6-1401
et seq. and Title 12 of the District of Columbia Municipal Regulations)
(“Construction Codes”).





	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 31 DCR 6111 (December 7, 1984); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 124 (January 1, 2010).



	EDITOR’S NOTE: The Building Code Advisory Committee, established by Mayor’s
Order 81-65, dated March 9, 1981, was amended by Mayor’s Order 89-257, dated
November 7, 1989.
