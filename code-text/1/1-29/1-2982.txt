                                  DC REGULATIONS

UNEMPLOYMENT INSURANCE CASES &#8211; REPRESENTATIVES (ยง 1-2982)

2982.1. An authorized agent employed by a firm whose usual business includes
   providing representation in unemployment compensation cases may represent any
   party.

   2982.2. The practice of lawyers or other party representatives is governed by
   Sections 2833 and 2835.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 12541, 12591 (December
   31, 2010).
