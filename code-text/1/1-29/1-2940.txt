                                  DC REGULATIONS

RENTAL HOUSING CASES &#8211; ATTORNEY&#8217;S FEES (§ 1-2940)

2940.1 	All motions for an award of attorney’s fees shall be filed within
thirty (30) calendar days of service of the final order.

amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 12541, 12583 (December
31, 2010).
