                                  DC REGULATIONS

DCPS HEARINGS &#8211; SCOPE (ยง 1-2900)

2900.1. Sections 2900 through 2909 govern procedures in cases referred to OAH
   by the District of Columbia Public Schools (DCPS).

   2900.2. For procedural issues not covered in Sections 2900 through 2909, the
   rules in Chapter 28 apply.

   2900.3. OAH is not required to follow any other procedural rules adopted by
   DCPS in cases referred to OAH by DCPS.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 11122
   (December 23, 2011)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking
   published at 59 DCR 3128 (April 20, 2012).
