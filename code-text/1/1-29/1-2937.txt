                                  DC REGULATIONS

RENTAL HOUSING CASES &#8211; FINAL ORDERS (ยง 1-2937)

2937.1 	OAH shall serve all final orders on the parties by first-class mail. 
OAH also shall serve all final orders on the Rent Administrator and the
Commission.
