                                  DC REGULATIONS

REPRODUCTION OF RECORDS IN THE DISTRICT RECORDS CENTER (ยง 1-1519)

1519.1. The Records Manager shall establish procedures for the reproduction of
   records in the Records Center.

   1519.2. The Records Manager shall prohibit the copying of a record by any
   method that might result in damage to the document.

   1519.3. The Administrator may charge a fee for reproduction of records by
   Records Center personnel or with Records Center equipment.

   1519.4. The Administrator shall publish a fee schedule for the copying of
   records in the Records Center.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 7001, 7017-18 (October 30,
   1987).
