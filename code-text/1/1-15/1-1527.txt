                                  DC REGULATIONS

REPRODUCTION OF LIBRARY MATERIALS (ยง 1-1527)

1527.1. The Chief of the Library shall establish procedures for the
   reproduction of Library material.

   1527.2. The Chief of the Library shall prohibit the copying of Library
   material by any method that might result in damage to an item.

   1527.3. The Administrator may charge a fee for the reproduction of Library
   material by Library personnel or with Library equipment.

   1527.4. The Administrator shall publish a fee schedule for the copying of
   Library materials.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 7001, 7021 (October 30, 1987).
