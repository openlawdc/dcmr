                                  DC REGULATIONS

INSPECTION OF RECORDS (ยง 1-1506)

1506.1. In accordance with section 11(a) of the Act, the Administrator may
   inspect any public record of the District of Columbia. Officers and employees
   of the government shall cooperate fully in such inspections and no document or
   record may be withheld on the grounds that it is privileged or confidential.

   1506.2. Neither the Administrator nor his designee shall disclose information
   obtained from the examination of privileged or confidential records.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 7001, 7007 (October 30, 1987).
