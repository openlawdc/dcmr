                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISTURBANCES PROHIBITED (ยง 1-1415)

1415.1. No person shall engage in conduct which creates a disturbance,
   including the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Loitering;
   
   
   
   (b)	Loud and unusual noise;
   
   
   
   (c)	Obstruction of the use of entrances, foyers, lobbies, corridors,
   elevators, stairways, or parking lots;
   
   
   
   (d)	Any conduct which disrupts the performance of official duties by District
   government employees; and
   
   
   
   (e)	Any conduct which prevents the general public from obtaining the services
   normally provided by District government employees.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 1316, 1323 (February 17, 1989).
