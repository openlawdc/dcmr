                                  DC REGULATIONS

CLOSING OF PROPERTY (ยง 1-1414)

1414.1. Property shall be closed to the public during other than normal
   working hours, except in specific instances in which OPM has issued a permit
   for use after normal working hours.

   1414.2. The decisions to close a property on an emergency basis shall be made
   by the Director, OPM. This action by the Director may be coordinated with
   affected District agency officials.

   1414.3. When property or a portion thereof is closed, admission shall be
   restricted to authorized persons who shall do the following when requested:
   
   
   
   (a)	Sign a register upon entry; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Display government or other identifying credentials to Protective Services
   officials or other authorized individuals.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 1316, 1322-23 (February 17,
   1989).
