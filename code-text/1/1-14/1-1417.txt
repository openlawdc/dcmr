                                  DC REGULATIONS

PHOTOGRAPHS FOR NEWS PURPOSES (ยง 1-1417)

1417.1. Photographs for news purposes may be taken in entrances, lobbies,
   foyers, corridors or auditoriums when used for public meetings.

   1417.2. Photographs for news purposes may be taken in spaces occupied by a
   tenant agency only with the consent of the occupying agency.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 1316, 1324 (February 17, 1989).
