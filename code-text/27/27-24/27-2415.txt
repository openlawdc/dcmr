                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITE-QUANTITY CONTRACTS (ยง 27-2415)

2415.1. The contracting officer may use a definite-quantity contract when it
   can be determined in advance that a specific quantity of goods or services
   will be required during the contract period and the goods or services are
   regularly available or will be available after a short lead time.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1564 (February 26, 1988).
