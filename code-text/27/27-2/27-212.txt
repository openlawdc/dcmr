                                  DC REGULATIONS

POSTHEARING BRIEFS (ยง 27-212)

212.1. Unless filed electronically, an original and two (2) copies of paper
   posthearing briefs shall be submitted as directed by the Board at the
   conclusion of the hearing.

   212.2. Briefs and any memoranda of law shall be filed electronically or be
   typewritten on white bond 8 1/2 x 11 inch paper and shall be double spaced
   except for quotations.

   212.3. Briefs shall contain, in the following order, a short procedural
   history of the case, a table of contents, a table of authorities cited, a
   concise summary of argument, proposed findings of fact with citations to those
   places in the record where supporting evidence can be found, proposed
   conclusions of law with citations to supporting legal authorities, and the
   relief desired by the party.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
