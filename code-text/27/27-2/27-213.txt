                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORD (ยง 27-213)

213.1. The record of the appeal shall include the complaint, answer, appeal
   file, all motions and other submissions filed by the parties with the Board
   pursuant to these rules; all correspondence exchanged between the Board and
   the parties or their attorney; transcripts made of hearings before the Board;
   all exhibits and other evidence admitted to the record; and all findings,
   decisions, opinions, and orders of the Board.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
