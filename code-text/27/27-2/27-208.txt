                                  DC REGULATIONS

ELECTION OF PROCEDURE (ยง 27-208)

208.1. After the complaint, answer, appeal file and any supplemental pleadings
   have been filed, each party shall inform the Board whether it desires a
   hearing, as prescribed by Rule 211, or whether it desires to submit its case
   on the record without a hearing, as prescribed by Rule 209.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
