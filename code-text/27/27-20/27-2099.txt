                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 27-2099)

2099.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   City-wide Telecommunications Systems &#8211; voice and data networks
   consisting of not fewer than 25,000 station terminations.
   
   
   
   Director &#8211; the Director of the Office of Contracting and Procurement or
   the District of Columbia Chief Procurement Officer.
   
   
   
   Multiyear contract &#8211; a contract for a period covering more than one (1)
   fiscal year but not more than five (5) fiscal years.
   
   
   
   Nonrecurring costs &#8211; those production costs which are generally incurred
   on a one-time basis and include costs such as plant or equipment relocation,
   plant rearrangement, pre-production engineering, initial spoilage and rework,
   and specialized work force training.
   
   
   
   Option &#8211; a unilateral right in a contract under which, for a specified
   time, the District may elect to purchase additional quantities or services
   called for by the contract, or may elect to extend the term of the contract.
   
   
   
   Recurring costs &#8211; the production costs that vary with the quantity being
   produced, such as labor and materials.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1515 (February 26, 1988); as
   Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 398a (January 28, 1994).
