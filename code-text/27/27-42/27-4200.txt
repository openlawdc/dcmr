FREEDOM OF INFORMATION REQUESTS (ยง 27-4200)

4200.1. The contracting officer shall direct any request for records which
   specifically cites the D.C. Freedom of Information Act (the
   &#8220;FOIA&#8221;), D.C. Code ยง1-1551 et seq. (1981), to the agency head or
   to the information officer designated by the agency head under ยง401 of
   chapter 4 of Title 1 of the D.C. Municipal Regulations (DCMR).

   4200.2. If a request for records contains no reference to the FOIA, and if the
   contracting officer is unsure whether the requested records can be released,
   the contracting officer shall forward the request to the agency information
   officer or agency head.

   4200.3. The contracting officer shall comply with requests from the
   information officer to provide documents for review.
