                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROPRIETARY AND CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION (ยง 27-4201)

4201.1. No District employee shall disclose proprietary or confidential
   information which has been properly designated as such in accordance with the
   provisions of chapter 31 of this title to any person other than District
   employees who need access to the information as part of their duties in
   connection with the procurement process.

   4201.2. The restriction in ยง4201.1 shall apply even if the proprietary or
   confidential information has been incorporated into a contract with the
   District.
