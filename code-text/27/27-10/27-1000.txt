                                  DC REGULATIONS

IMPLEMENTATION OF PROCUREMENT CODE (§ 27-1000)

These rules implement the provisions in the Procurement Practices Reform Act
   of 2010, effective April 8, 2011, (D.C. Law 18-371; D.C. Official Code §§
   2-351.01, et seq.)(2011 Repl.) (Act).

   1000.2. Where any statute or regulation is referred to in this title, the
   reference shall be to the most recent version, and any amendments or revisions
   to the statute or regulation.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1385 (February 26, 1988); as
   amended by Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 58 DCR
   9071 (September 27, 2011)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking
   published at 58 DCR 11071 (December 23, 2011).
