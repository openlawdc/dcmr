                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPLIANCE WITH PROCUREMENT ACT AND REGULATIONS (§ 27-1001)

1001.1. Except as otherwise provided in the Act or this section, a contract
   which is 	entered into in violation of the Act and this title is void.

   1001.2. Notwithstanding the provisions of § 1001.1, a contract shall not be
   void if 	a determination is made that good faith has been shown by all parties
   and 	the violation of the provisions of the Act and this title are de minimis.
    The 	determination of good faith may be made by any of the following:
   
   
   
   (a)  	The Director;
   
   
   
   (b)  	The Contract Appeals Board; or
   
   
   
   (c)  	A court of competent jurisdiction.

   1001.3. The Director’s determination of good faith and that a violation of
   the provisions of the Act and this title are de minimis made pursuant to §
   1001.2(a) shall be in writing based upon a written request for review by the
   contractor or contracting officer.  The request shall fully describe the
   contract, the status of performance, the reason why the contract is void, and
   the grounds for the determination.
