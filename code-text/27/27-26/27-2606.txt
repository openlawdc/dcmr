                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONCURRENT PERFORMANCE OF CONSTRUCTION CONTRACTS (ยง 27-2606)

2606.1. Because of potential labor and administrative problems,
   cost-plus-fixed-fee, price-incentive, or other types of contracts with cost
   variation or cost adjustment features shall not be permitted concurrently at
   the same work site with firm-fixed-price, lump-sum, or unit-price contracts
   without the prior written approval of the Director.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1582 (February 26, 1988).
