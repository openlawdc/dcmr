                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRE-CONSTRUCTION CONFERENCE (ยง 27-2613)

2613.1. The contracting officer may conduct a pre-construction conference to
   inform the contractor about the labor standards provisions of the contract and
   other pre-construction matters deemed appropriate by the contracting officer.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1586 (February 26, 1988).
