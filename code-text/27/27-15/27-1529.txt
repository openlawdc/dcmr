                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORDING OF BIDS (ยง 27-1529)

1529.1. The contracting officer shall complete and certify the accuracy of the
   bid abstract prior to contract award.

   1529.2. The contacting officer shall promptly publish the bid abstract on the
   Internet.
