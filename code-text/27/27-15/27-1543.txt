                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESOLVING TIE BIDS (ยง 27-1543)

1543.1. When two (2) or more low bids are equal in all respects, priority
   shall be 	given in the following order of priority to:
   
   
   
   (a)  	A certified small business enterprise certified by the DSLBD; and
   
   
   
   (b)  	A certified business enterprise other than a small business enterprise
   described in ยง 1543.1(a).

   1543.2. If two (2) or more bidders remain equally eligible for contract award,
   	award shall be made by a drawing by lot limited to those bidders.

   1543.3. The drawing shall be witnessed by at least three (3) persons, and the
   	contract file shall contain the names and addresses of the witnesses and 	the
   person supervising the drawing.
