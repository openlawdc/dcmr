                                  DC REGULATIONS

STEP TWO PROCEDURES (ยง 27-1554)

1554.1. Competitive sealed bidding procedures shall be followed except as
   follows:
   
   
   
   (a)  	An IFB shall be issued only to those bidders which submitted acceptable
   technical proposals in step one;
   
   
   
   (b)  	The IFB shall prominently state that a bidder shall comply with the
   specifications and the bidder&#8217;s technical proposal; and
   
   
   
   (c)  	The IFB shall not be publically advertised.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1444 (February 26, 1988); as
   amended by Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 59 DCR
   3714 (March 6, 2012)[EXPIRED]; as amended Notice of Final Rulemaking published
   at 59 DCR 6290, 6313 (June 1, 2012).
