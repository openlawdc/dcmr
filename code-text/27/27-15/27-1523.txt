                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRE-OPENING MODIFICATION OR WITHDRAWAL OF BIDS (ยง 27-1523)

1523.1  	Bids may be modified or withdrawn by written notice received at the
	location designated in the IFB prior to the time and date set for bid 	opening.
 An electronic modification or withdrawal received from the 	bidder prior to the
time and date set for bid opening will be effective; 	provided that, there is
objective evidence in 	electronic form confirming 	that the modification or
withdrawal was received prior to the time and date 	set for bid opening.



1523.2  	If a bid is withdrawn in accordance with this subsection, the bid
security, 			if any, shall be returned to the bidder.
