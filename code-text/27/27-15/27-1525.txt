                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICE TO BIDDERS OF LATE ACTIONS (ยง 27-1525)

1525.1. The contracting officer shall promptly notify the bidder if a bid,
   modification of bid, or withdrawal of bid is received late, and it is clear
   from available information that it cannot be considered.
