                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONDITIONS FOR USE OF TWO-STEP SEALED BIDDING (ยง 27-1551)

1551.1. The two-step sealed bidding method may be used when all of the
   	following conditions are present:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Available specifications or purchase descriptions are not definite or
   complete, or may be too restrictive without technical evaluation, and
   discussion of the technical aspects of the requirement is needed to ensure
   mutual understanding between each prospective bidder and the District;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Definite criteria exist for evaluating technical proposals;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   More than one (1) technically qualified source is expected to be available;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Sufficient time will be available for use of the two-step method; and
   
   
   
   (e)  	A firm-fixed-price contract or a fixed-price contract with economic
   price adjustment will be used.

   1551.2. None of the following issues shall preclude the use of two-step sealed
   	bidding:
   
   
   
   (a)  	Multiyear contracting;
   
   
   
   (b)  	District-owned facilities to be made available to the successful bidder;
   or
   
   
   
   (c)  	A set-aside procurement.
