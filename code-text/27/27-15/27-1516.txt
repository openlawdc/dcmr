                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRE-BID CONFERENCES (ยง 27-1516)

1516.1. The contracting officer may conduct pre-bid conferences to explain the
   procurement requirements.

   1516.2. The contracting officer shall announce pre-bid conferences in the IFB
   to all prospective bidders.

   1516.3. The contracting officer shall hold a pre-bid conference as early as
   possible after an IFB has been issued and before bids are due.

   1516.4. Nothing stated at the pre-bid conference shall change an IFB unless a
   change is made by the contracting officer by written amendment.
