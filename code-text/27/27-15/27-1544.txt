                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICE OF CONTRACT AWARD (ยง 27-1544)

1544.1. The contracting officer shall send written notice of award to the
   successful 	bidder.

   1544.2. The contracting officer shall publish notice of contract awards in
   	accordance with the requirements of chapter 13 of this title.

   1544.3. The contracting officer shall notify unsuccessful bidders promptly in
   			writing that their bids were not accepted, and shall return any bid
   security 			to the unsuccessful bidders.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1440 (February 26, 1988); as
   amended by Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 59 DCR
   3714 (March 6, 2012)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking
   published at 59 DCR 6290, 6308 (June 1, 2012).
