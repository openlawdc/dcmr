                                  DC REGULATIONS

CANCELLATION OF INVITATIONS FOR BIDS BEFORE OPENING (ยง 27-1518)

1518.1. The contracting officer shall not cancel an IFB unless the Director
   determines in writing that cancellation is in the best interest of the
   District.

   1518.2. If an IFB is canceled before bid opening, the contracting officer
   shall:
   
   
   
   (a)  	Return to the bidders unopened bids that have been received via mail or
   hand-delivery; or
   
   
   
   (b)  	Purge all bids that have been received electronically from primary and
   backup data storage systems.

   1518.3. The contracting officer shall promptly publish on the Internet a
   notice of cancellation.
