SELECTION OF HUMAN CARE SERVICES PROVIDERS (ยง 27-1906)

1906.1. Prior to conducting discussions with a service provider who has
   submitted a statement of qualifications in accordance with ยง 1905.3, the
   contracting officer shall make a written determination that the service
   provider is qualified, based on the criteria in ยง1905.6.

   1906.2. Following pre-qualification of service providers, the contracting
   officer may:
   
   
   
   (a)	Conduct discussions with all qualified service providers, and negotiate a
   price on a unit rate or fee for service basis using benchmarks and
   quantifiable measurements that are uniformly applied, including but not
   limited to each service provider&#8217;s cost data attributable to provision
   of the services and consideration of each service provider&#8217;s maximum
   customer capacity; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Award a human care agreement to one or more service providers to satisfy
   all or part of the District&#8217;s anticipated requirements based on the
   contracting officer&#8217;s determination that the contract is in the best
   interest of the District, considering the service provider&#8217;s
   qualifications, its capability of providing the service, and a judgment that
   the price is reasonable.

   1906.3. The contracting officer shall retain statements of qualifications for
   approved service providers, and consider those providers for award of human
   care agreements, for a period of three years following pre- qualification of
   the providers.
