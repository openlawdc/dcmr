                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTRACT EXECUTION BY THE DISTRICT (ยง 27-1200)

1200.1. Only a contracting officer is authorized to sign and enter into a
   contract on behalf of the District.

   1200.2. The contracting officer&#8217;s name and official title shall be typed
   or printed on the contract.

   1200.3. The contracting officer shall:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Ensure that the person signing on behalf of the contractor has the authority
   to bind the contractor; and
   
   
   
    (b)  	Sign the contract after it has been properly signed by the contractor.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1402 (February 26, 1988); as
   amended by Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 58 DCR
   9071 (September 27, 2011)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking
   published at 58 DCR 11077 (December 23, 2011).
