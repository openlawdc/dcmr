                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 27-3899)

3899.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the 	meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Claim &#8211; a written demand or written assertion by the District or a
   contractor seeking, as a matter of right, the payment of money in a sum
   certain, the adjustment or interpretation of contract terms, or other relief
   arising under or relating to the contract.
   
   
   
   Person &#8211; any business entity, individual, union, committee, club, or
   other organization or group of individuals.
