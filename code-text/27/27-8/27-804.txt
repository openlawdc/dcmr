                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESIDENT-OWNED BUSINESS ENTERPRISE; ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS (§ 27-804)

804.1 	An applicant requesting certification as a resident-owned business
enterprise must provide proof of residency, including but not limited to,
personal income tax returns, a deed, mortgage, or lease for his or her primary
residence, a District of Columbia driver’s license or identification card, and
utility bills for the residence.
