                                  DC REGULATIONS

LIST OF CERTIFIED BUSINESS ENTERPRISES (§ 27-829)

829.1 		The Department shall establish and maintain a centralized list of CBEs.



829.2 		The Department’s centralized list of CBEs shall set forth the name of
each CBE, contact information for each CBE, the CBE’s certification
categories, the procurement categories along with the names of the CBEs
certified in each procurement category, and the expiration date of the CBE’s
registration.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 9052 (December 4, 1992); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 8439 (October 23, 2009),
incorporating by reference text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 5622,
5643 (July 10, 2009).
