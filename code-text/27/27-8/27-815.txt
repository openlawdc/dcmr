                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPLIANCE REVIEW (ยง 27-815)

815.1 	The Commission or the Department may conduct periodic compliance reviews
of CBEs to assure their continued compliance with the eligibility requirements
of the Act and these regulations.



815.2 		In conducting a compliance review, the Commission or the Department may
perform site visits, review documents, and interview witnesses to determine the
continued eligibility of a CBE.



815.3 		Where a CBE is found to be non-compliant, a notice shall be issued to
the CBE within 30 days which shall specify:

		(a) 	The nature of the non-compliance;

		(b) 	The corrective action that must be taken; and

		(c) 	The date by which the deficiencies must be corrected.



815.4 		If the CBE fails to take corrective action within 30 days of the notice,
the Department may institute revocation proceedings.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 9052 (December 4, 1992); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 8439 (October 23, 2009),
