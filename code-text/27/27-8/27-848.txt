EQUITY AND DEVELOPMENT REPORTS (ยง 27-848)

848.1. Developers must submit quarterly reports to the Department regarding
   the fulfillment of the EDP Program requirements on such forms as determined by
   the Department.  The reports shall include information regarding:
   
   
   
   	(a)	 Changes in ownership interest of the owners/partners;
   
   
   
   	(b)	 Additions or deletions of an owner/partner;
   
   
   
   	(c) 	Changes in the legal status of an existing owner/partner;
   
   
   
   	(d) 	Changes in the percentage of revenue distribution to an owner/partner;
   
   
   
   	(e) 	A description of team member activities; and
   
   
   
   (f) 	The amount of development fees paid to each team member, participant,
   partner, or owner.

   848.2. Information provided under ยง 848.1 shall be considered commercial or
   financial information which could result in substantial harm if disclosed to
   the competitive position of the provider of the information and shall be
   exempt from disclosure under D.C. Official Code ยง 2-534(a)(1).
