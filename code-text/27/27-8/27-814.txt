                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTINUING ELIGIBILITY AND REPORTING REQUIREMENTS (ยง 27-814)

814.1 		During the term of a certificate, a CBE shall report to the Department
any change of address, change of ownership, or other change that may affect the
continued eligibility of the CBE within thirty (30) days of the date of the
change.



814.2 		Upon learning of any changes, the Department may request documentation
regarding compliance with the Act and these regulations and conduct onsite
inspections.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 39 DCR 9052 (December 4, 1992); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 8439 (October 23, 2009),
incorporating by reference text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 5622,
5634 (July 10, 2009).
