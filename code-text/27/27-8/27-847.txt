                                  DC REGULATIONS

EQUITY OR DEVELOPMENT PARTICIPANT LOSS OF CERTIFICATION (ยง 27-847)

847.1. If an Equity Participant or Development Participant loses its LSDBE
   certification during the course of a Covered Project resulting in less than
   the minimum Equity Participation or Development Participation requirement
   under Section 2349a of the Act, the Department shall evaluate on a
   case-by-case basis whether:
   
   
   
   (a)	Another Equity Participant or Development Participant can participate in
   the Covered Project without causing any detriment to the overall project or
   the Master Developer, so that the 20% Equity Participation and 20% Development
   Participation requirements are met;
   
   
   
   (b)	To modify or waive some or all of the EDP Program requirements for the
   Covered Project; or
   
   
   
   (c)	After assessing the circumstances, some other appropriate action is
   warranted.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 8439 (October 23, 2009),
   incorporating by reference text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 56 DCR
   5622, 5653 (July 10, 2009).
