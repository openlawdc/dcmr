                                  DC REGULATIONS

REQUESTS FOR PROPOSALS IN THE SET-ASIDE MARKET (ยง 27-1605)

1605.1. Before the contracting officer restricts a CSP procurement to the
   small business set-aside market, the contracting officer shall determine that:
   
   
   
   (a)	There is a reasonable expectation that proposals will be obtained from at
   least two (2) responsible small business enterprises certified by the
   Department of Small and Local Business Development (DSLBD); and
   
   
   
   	(b)	An award will be made at reasonable prices.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1448 (February 26, 1988); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 1136 (February 1, 2013).
