                                  DC REGULATIONS

USE OF COMPETITIVE SEALED PROPOSALS (ยง 27-1600)

1600.1. If the contracting officer determines competitive sealed bidding is
   not practicable or advantageous to the District, a procurement may be
   conducted using the competitive sealed proposal (CSP) method.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	Proposals shall be solicited from the maximum number of qualified sources,
   and in a manner consistent with the nature of, and the need for, the goods or
   services being acquired.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1446 (February 26, 1988); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 1136 (February 1, 2013).
