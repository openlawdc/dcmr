                                  DC REGULATIONS

SEALED DOCUMENTS (ยง 27-409)

409.1. A motion to file documents under seal may be filed and served
   electronically. Redacted copies of documents filed under seal may be filed and
   served electronically; however, documents filed under seal containing
   privileged information shall be filed in paper form.
