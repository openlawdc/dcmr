                                  DC REGULATIONS

ELECTRONIC FILING OF PLEADINGS AND OTHER DOCUMENTS (§ 27-400)

400.1. All pleadings, motions, memoranda of law, orders, or other documents
   may be filed electronically through the Board&#8217;s designated vendor,
   CourtLink Corporation (&#8220;Vendor&#8221;), which can be contacted online at
   www.courtlink.com. Documents may be filed as either E Documents or E Images.
   
   
   
   	Authority: District of Columbia Procurement Practices Act of 1985, as
   amended, D.C. Code §§ 2-309.01 through 2-309.8 (2001).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002); as amended
   by Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 52 DCR 4782 (May 20, 2005)
   [EXPIRED].
