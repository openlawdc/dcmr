                                  DC REGULATIONS

MAINTENANCE OF ORIGINAL DOCUMENT (ยง 27-402)

402.1. Unless otherwise ordered by the Board, an original of all documents
   filed electronically, including original signatures, shall be maintained by
   the party filing the document and shall be made available, upon reasonable
   notice, for inspection by other counsel or the Board. From time to time, it
   may be necessary to provide the Board with a hard copy of an electronically
   filed document.
