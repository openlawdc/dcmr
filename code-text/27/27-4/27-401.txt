                                  DC REGULATIONS

ASSIGNMENT BY THE VENDOR OF PERSONAL IDENTIFICATION NUMBERS (ยง 27-401)

401.1. Upon receipt by the Vendor of a properly executed E File Subscriber
   Agreement at their website, the Vendor shall assign a confidential password to
   the attorney or other designated representative of a party before the Board
   which must be used to file, serve, receive, review, and retrieve
   electronically filed pleadings, orders, and other documents. (A password
   received from the Vendor by attorneys for filing in the Superior Court of the
   District of Columbia may also be used to make filings to the Board.) An
   attorney or other authorized user shall be responsible for any use of his or
   her password.
