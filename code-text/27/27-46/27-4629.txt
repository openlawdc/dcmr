                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSFER OF CONTRACTS (§ 27-4629)

4629.1. Contracts or pending procurements related to the Hospital may be
   transferred by the Hospital to any agency or instrumentality of the District
   in accordance with the provisions of this section.

   4629.2. The CCO shall review the proposed procurement or contract and
   determine whether it is in the best interest of the Hospital to transfer the
   procurement or contract.

   4629.3. If the CCO determines it is in the Hospital’s best interest to
   transfer a contract or procurement, the Contracting Officer shall have the
   authority to transfer the contract.

   4629.4. Transfer of a contract or procurement shall not operate to transfer
   funds to support the assigned contract or procurement.  Funds shall be
   transferred pursuant to an intra-District memorandum of understanding,
   reprogramming, or other appropriate process.
   
   
   
   Source: Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 8507
   (October 8, 2010)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Emergency and Proposed
   Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 3086 (April 18, 2011)[EXPIRED]; as amended by
   Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 4565, 4595 (May 27, 2011).
