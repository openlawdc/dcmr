NOTICE OF INTENT TO AWARD A SOLE SOURCE CONTRACT (ยง 27-1304)

1304.1     	A notice of intent to award a sole source contract shall be
   published on the OCP Internet for at least ten (10) days prior to contract
   award if the estimated price is over five thousand dollars ($5,000).

   1304.2. A notice of intent to award a sole source contract shall include:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A copy of the proposed determination and findings required under ยง 404(b) of
   the Act;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A clear description of the item to be procured;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The procuring agency;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A response due date;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A point of contact; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The intended sole source contractor.
   
   
   
   1304.3 	Sole source awards, regardless of dollar value, shall be published in
   accordance with the provisions in ยง 1305 of this chapter.
