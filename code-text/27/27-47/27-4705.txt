                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTRACTOR QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS (ยง 27-4705)

4705.1. To be eligible to receive a contract from the Department, a
   prospective contractor must satisfy two (2) basic qualification requirements. 
   Specifically, the prospective contractor must:
   
   
   
   (a)	Be responsible; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Not be disqualified on the basis of conflicts of interest either personal
   or organizational, or related ethical concerns.
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 8805
   (October 14, 2011)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking
   published at 59 DCR 985, 989 (February 10, 2012).
