                                  DC REGULATIONS

D.C. SUPPLY SCHEDULE; GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION SCHEDULE (ยง 27-4724)

4724.1. The Department may purchase goods and services but not construction or
   any other type of contract awarded on a cost reimbursement basis from the D.C.
   Supply Schedules or the General Services Administration (GSA) Schedule.
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 58 DC 8805
   (October 14, 2011)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking
   published at 59 DCR 985, 1010 (February 10, 2012).
