                                  DC REGULATIONS

SOVEREIGN IMMUNITY; PUNITIVE DAMAGES; INTEREST (ยง 27-4742)

4742.1. Unless otherwise specifically provided by law of the District, the
   District government and every officer, department, agency, or other unit of
   the District government shall not raise the defense of sovereign immunity in
   the courts of the District in an action based upon a written procurement
   contract executed on behalf of the District government.

   4742.2. In an action in contract based upon a written contract executed on
   behalf of the District government, or by an official or employee acting within
   the scope of the official&#8217;s or the employee&#8217;s authority, the
   District government, and its officers, departments, agencies, or other units
   of government, shall not be liable for punitive damages.
