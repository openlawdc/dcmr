                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISPUTES AND PROTESTS (ยง 27-4734)

4734.1. The District of Columbia Contract Appeals Board shall be the exclusive
   hearing tribunal for bid protests and disputes in connection with decisions by
   the contracting officer under sections 4732 and 4733.  Claims shall be made in
   accordance with title X of the PPRA.
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 58 DC 8805
   (October 14, 2011)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking
   published at 59 DCR 985, 1016 (February 10, 2012).
