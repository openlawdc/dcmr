                                  DC REGULATIONS

CHIEF CONTRACTING OFFICER (§ 27-4702)

4702.1. The Director shall serve as the Department’s Chief Contracting
   Officer (CCO).

   4702.2. The CCO shall have overall responsibility for supervising the
   Department’s procurement activities and to award any contracts pursuant to
   this chapter.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 8805
   (October 14, 2011)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking
   published at 59 DCR 985,  988 (February 10, 2012).
