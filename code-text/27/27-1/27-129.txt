SEAL OF THE BOARD (ยง 27-129)

129.1. The seal of the Board shall be a circular boss, the center portion of
   which shall depict the flag of the District of Columbia and flag of the United
   States. The outer margin of the seal shall bear the legend, &#8220;CONTRACT
   APPEALS BOARD, D.C.&#8221;
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
