                                  DC REGULATIONS

BURDEN OF PROOF (ยง 27-120)

120.1. Except as otherwise provided by law, the burden of persuasion by a
   party to establish a fact or facts in dispute shall be met by a preponderance
   of the evidence.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
