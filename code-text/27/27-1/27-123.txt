                                  DC REGULATIONS

ENLARGEMENT OF TIME (ยง 27-123)

123.1. The Board, for good cause shown, may enlarge the time prescribed by the
   Board rules, or by its order, for doing any act, or may permit an act to be
   done after the expiration of the prescribed time. The Board, may not, however,
   enlarge the time for filing a protest or an appeal.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
