                                  DC REGULATIONS

STIPULATIONS (ยง 27-111)

111.1. The parties may stipulate to facts, issues, admission of relevant
   documents, testimony, discovery procedures, and other matters which may aid in
   expediting the proceedings in a case, subject to acceptance by the Board. A
   stipulation, however, may not extend a time limit established by a rule or
   order of the Board without its approval.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
