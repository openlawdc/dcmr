                                  DC REGULATIONS

UNEXCUSED ABSENCE OF A PARTY (ยง 27-125)

125.1. The unexcused absence of a party at the time set for a motion or merits
   hearing will not be occasion for delay. In the event of such absence, the
   hearing will proceed and the motion or case will be regarded as submitted on
   the record by the absent party.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
