                                  DC REGULATIONS

SUSPENSION OF RULES (ยง 27-116)

116.1. In the interest of expediting a decision in a case or for other good
   cause shown, the Board may, except for the time requirements for filing a
   protest or a notice of appeal, suspend or dispense with the filing
   requirements and procedural provisions of these rules on the motion of a party
   or on its own initiative and may order proceedings in accordance with its
   direction.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
