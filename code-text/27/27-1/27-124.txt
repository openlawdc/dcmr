                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTINUANCES (ยง 27-124)

124.1. Any party may move in writing to request a continuance of any scheduled
   hearing, or to extend the time to file a pleading, or for leave to amend a
   pleading if the motion is served on opposing parties and the Board at least
   five (5) business days before the hearing or the time limit

   124.2. Continuance shall be approved only for good cause shown.

   124.3. Conflicting engagements of counsel, absence of counsel, or the
   employment of new counsel shall not be regarded as good cause for continuance
   unless set forth promptly.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
