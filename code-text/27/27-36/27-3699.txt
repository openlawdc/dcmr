DEFINITIONS (ยง 27-3699)

3699.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Amendment &#8211; any change to a solicitation issued by the contracting
   officer.
   
   
   
   Bilateral contract modification &#8211; a contract modification that is signed
   by the contractor and the contracting officer.
   
   
   
   Change order &#8211; a written order signed by the contracting officer
   directing the contractor to make a change that the contracting officer is
   authorized to order without the contractor&#8217;s consent pursuant to the
   contract.
   
   
   
   Contract modification &#8211; any written change in the terms of a contract.
   
   
   
   Supplemental agreement &#8211; a bilateral contract modification.
   
   
   
   Unilateral modification &#8211; a contract modification that is signed only by
   the contracting officer.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1688 (February 26, 1988); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 14956 (December 21, 2012).
