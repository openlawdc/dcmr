                                  DC REGULATIONS

PENALTY FOR PROVIDING FALSE INFORMATION (ยง 27-506)

506.1. An applicant for employment or a volunteer position with a private
   entity who provides false information in the course of applying for the
   position shall be subject to prosecution pursuant to section 404 of the
   District of Columbia Theft and White Collar Crimes Act of 1982 (D.C. Law
   4-164; D.C. Official Code ยง 22-2405), and, upon conviction, shall be fined
   not more than $1,000 or imprisoned for not more than 180 days, or both.
