                                  DC REGULATIONS

RIGHT TO APPEAL CONTRACTING OFFICER&#8217;S TECHNICAL REPRESENTATIVE&#8217;S
DECISION (ยง 27-507)

507.1. If a COTR denies an application, the COTR shall notify the contracting
   officer who shall advise the private entity to notify the applicant of such
   determination. The private entity shall inform the applicant in writing that
   she or he may appeal the denial to the Commission on Human Rights within
   thirty (30) days of the determination.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 56 DCR 3490 (May 1, 2009).
