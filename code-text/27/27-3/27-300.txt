                                  DC REGULATIONS

PARTIES ENTITLED TO PROTEST (§ 27-300)

300.1. An aggrieved person, as defined in Rule 100.2(a), may protest to the
   Board a solicitation issued by or for a District contracting agency for the
   procurement of property or services, or a proposed award, or the award of such
   a contract.
   
   
   
   	Authority: District of Columbia Procurement Practices Act of 1985, as
   amended, D.C. Code §§ 1-1189.1 through 1-1189.8 (Supp. 1997).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
