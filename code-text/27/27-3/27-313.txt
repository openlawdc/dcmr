                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECONSIDERATION (ยง 27-313)

313.1. The protester, the contracting agency, or an interested party who filed
   comments on the Agency Report may by motion request the Board to reconsider a
   decision.

   313.2. Motions for reconsideration shall be conducted in accordance with Rule
   117 of these rules, except that the time periods contained in Rules 117.2 and
   117.4 shall be shortened to fifteen (15) days and seven (7) days respectively.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2078 (March 8, 2002).
