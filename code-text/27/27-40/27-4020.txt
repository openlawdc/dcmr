                                  DC REGULATIONS

PLACE OF ACCEPTANCE (ยง 27-4020)

4020.1. Each contract shall specify the place of acceptance.

   4020.2. Contracts that provide for District contract quality assurance at the
   source shall provide for acceptance at the source.

   4020.3. Contracts that provide for District contract quality assurance at
   destination shall provide for acceptance at destination.

   4020.4. Goods accepted at a place other than destination shall not be
   reinspected at destination for acceptance purposes, but shall be examined at
   destination for quantity, damage in transit, and possible substitution or
   fraud.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1723 (February 26, 1988).
