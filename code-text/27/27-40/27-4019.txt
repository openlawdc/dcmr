                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESPONSIBILITY FOR ACCEPTANCE (ยง 27-4019)

4019.1. Acceptance of goods, services, or construction shall be the
   responsibility of the contracting officer.

   4019.2. When the contracting officer assigns the responsibility for acceptance
   to another District employee, acceptance by that employee shall be binding on
   the District.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1723 (February 26, 1988).
