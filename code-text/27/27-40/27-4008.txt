                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTRACT QUALITY ASSURANCE AT DESTINATION (ยง 27-4008)

4008.1. District contract quality assurance that can be performed at
   destination shall be limited to inspection of the goods or services.

   4008.2. Inspection shall be performed at destination under the following
   circumstances:
   
   
   
   (a)	Goods are purchased off-the-shelf and require no technical inspection;
   
   
   
   (b)	Necessary testing equipment is located only at destination;
   
   
   
   (c)	The goods are perishable;
   
   
   
   (d)	The contract is for services performed at the destination; or
   
   
   
   (e)	The contracting officer determines that inspection at destination is in
   the best interests of the District.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1719 (February 26, 1988).
