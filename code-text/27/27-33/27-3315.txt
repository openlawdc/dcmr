                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRE-CONTRACT COSTS (ยง 27-3315)

3315.1. Pre-contract costs shall include costs incurred in anticipation of,
   and prior to, the effective date of the contract.

   3315.2. Pre-contract costs necessary to comply with the proposed contract
   delivery schedule shall be allowable to the extent that they would have been
   allowable if incurred after the date of the contract, provided that the
   contract shall set forth the period of time and maximum amount that will be
   covered as allowable pre-contract costs.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1656 (February 26, 1988).
