                                  DC REGULATIONS

CREDITS (ยง 27-3310)

3310.1. The contracting officer shall credit to the District as a cost
   reduction, or by cash refund, the applicable portion of any income, rebate,
   allowance, or other credit relating to any allowable cost which is received by
   or accrued to the contractor.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1654 (February 26, 1988).
