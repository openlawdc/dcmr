                                  DC REGULATIONS

BAD DEBTS (ยง 27-3330)

3330.1. Bad debts shall not be allowable, including losses arising from
   uncollectable accounts receivable due from customers and other claims and any
   directly associated costs such as collection and legal costs.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1666 (February 26, 1988).
