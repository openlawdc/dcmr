                                  DC REGULATIONS

LOSSES ON OTHER CONTRACTS (ยง 27-3336)

3336.1. An excess of costs over income under any other contract, including the
   contractor&#8217;s contributed portion under cost-sharing contracts, shall not
   be allowable.
