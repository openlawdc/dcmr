                                  DC REGULATIONS

LABOR RELATIONS COSTS (ยง 27-3343)

3343.1. Costs incurred in maintaining satisfactory relations between the
   contractor and its employees, including costs of shop stewards, labor
   management committees, employee publications, and other related activities,
   shall be allowable.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1673 (February 26, 1988).
