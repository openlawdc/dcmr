                                  DC REGULATIONS

ELECTRONIC SOLICITATIONS (§ 27-1401)

1401.1. Each electronic solicitation shall comply with the requirements of
   this title and the District of Columbia Procurement Practices Reform Act of
   2010, effective April 8, 2011 (D.C. Law 18-371; D.C. Official Code §§
   2-351.01, et seq. (2011 Supp.)), for the procurement method being utilized.
