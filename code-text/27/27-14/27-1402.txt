                                  DC REGULATIONS

FAILURE OF THE ELECTRONIC SYSTEM (§ 27-1402)

1402.1. If a failure of the District’s electronic system interferes with the
   ability of bidders or offerors to participate in an electronic transaction,
   the District shall amend the notice or solicitation when the electronic system
   becomes available to extend the date and time for receipt of electronic bids
   or offers.
