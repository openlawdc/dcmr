                                  DC REGULATIONS

SUSPENSION OF WORK (ยง 27-2307)

2307.1. Suspension of work under a construction or architect-engineer contract
   may be ordered by the contracting officer for a reasonable period of time.

   2307.2. If the contractor believes that the suspension is unreasonable, the
   contractor may submit a written claim for increases in the cost of performance
   due to the suspension, excluding profit, in accordance with the provisions of
   Chapter 38 of this title.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1551 (February 26, 1988).
