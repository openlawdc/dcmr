                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISTRICT DELAY OF WORK (ยง 27-2309)

2309.1. If the District unreasonably delays the work on a contract, the
   contractor may submit a written claim for equitable adjustment of the contract
   price based on increases in the cost of performance due to the work delay, in
   accordance with the procedures set forth in Chapter 38 of this title.

   2309.2. The contracting officer shall retain in the file a record of all
   negotiations leading to any equitable adjustment, along with related cost or
   pricing data.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1553 (February 26, 1988).
