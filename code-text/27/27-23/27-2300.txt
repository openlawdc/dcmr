                                  DC REGULATIONS

DELIVERY AND PERFORMANCE[COLON] GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 27-2300)

2300.1. The time of delivery or performance is an essential contract element
   and shall be clearly stated in each solicitation.

   2300.2. A contracting officer shall ensure that a delivery or performance
   schedule is realistic and meets the requirements of the procurement.

   2300.3. Except when clearly unnecessary, a solicitation shall inform bidders
   or offerors of the basis on which their bids or proposals will be evaluated
   with respect to time of delivery or performance.
