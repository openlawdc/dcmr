                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 27-2399)

2399.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Date of contract &#8211; the date on which the contract is signed by the
   contracting officer.
   
   
   
   Director &#8211; the Director of the Office of Contracting and Procurement or
   the District of Columbia Chief Procurement Officer.
   
   
   
   Stop-work order &#8211; a written document issued by the contracting officer
   advising a contractor to cease work.
   
   
   
   Subsistence items &#8211; perishable food items.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 1553 (February 26, 1988).
