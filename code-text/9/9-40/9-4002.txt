                                  DC REGULATIONS

TAX PERIODS COVERED (ยง 9-4002)

4002.1. The amnesty program shall apply to tax liabilities for taxable periods
   which returns or reports were required to be filed prior to November 1, 1986.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 4091, 4094 (June 26, 1987).
