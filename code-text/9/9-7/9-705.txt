                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEPRECIATION (ยง 9-705)

705.1. Depreciation rates, in general, shall be published in the instructions
   for filing a personal property tax return.

   705.2. The depreciation rate on a specific type of tangible personal property
   that is not shown in the instructions for filing a personal property tax
   return may be obtained from the Office.

   705.3. Accelerated depreciation methods shall not be used to compute the
   current value of the tangible personal property on the return.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 6014, 6020 (August 5, 1988).

   (September 18, 1981).
