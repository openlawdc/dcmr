                                  DC REGULATIONS

CERTIFICATION BY A QUALIFIED HIGH TECHNOLOGY COMPANY (QHTC) (ยง 9-1101)

1101.1. To claim a credit or other benefit, a QHTC shall attach to its
   applicable tax return or claim for refund an original affidavit certifying in
   good faith that it is a QHTC.

   1101.2. The affidavit must be on the form prescribed by the Office of Tax and
   Revenue.

   1101.3. A business which certifies that it is a QHTC shall be subject to audit
   to the same extent as any other taxpayer in determining District tax
   liability, applicable credits, exemptions and to verify that it is or was a
   QHTC.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 2142 (March 8, 2002).
