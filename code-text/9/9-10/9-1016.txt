                                  DC REGULATIONS

SALES OF CIGARETTES OTHER THAN IN ORIGINAL PACKAGES (ยง 9-1016)

1016.1. Cigarettes may not be sold in numbers less than the number contained
   in the original package.

   1016.2. The sale of loose cigarettes from opened or original packages is
   prohibited.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 29 DCR 2804, 2811 (July 2, 1982).
