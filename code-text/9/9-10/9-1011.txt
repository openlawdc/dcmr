                                  DC REGULATIONS

RETAILERS LICENSE (ยง 9-1011)

1011.1. A separate license shall be required for each location where
   cigarettes are sold.

   1011.2. If the only cigarettes sold or offered for sale in a retail
   establishment are from a licensed cigarette vending machine, the retail
   establishment is not required to have a retailer&#8217;s license.

   1011.3. A retailer&#8217;s license shall not authorize the licensee to sell to
   other licensees for resale.

   1011.4. The licensee shall be displayed in the retail establishment at or near
   the place where cigarettes are sold.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 29 DCR 2804, 2808 (July 2, 1982).
