                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEEDS ISSUED PURSUANT TO FORECLOSURE PROCEEDINGS (§ 9-511)

511.1. Any deed which is issued pursuant to a foreclosure sale and submitted
   for recordation shall be subject to the tax imposed pursuant to § 303 of the
   Act (D.C. Code § 45-923) regardless of the identity of the grantee named in
   the deed, including a grantee who is the original secured party or mortgagee
   under the deed of trust or mortgage which secured the debt or other obligation
   on the property in question.

   511.2. Any deed which is issued in place of a foreclosure sale and submitted
   for recordation shall be subject to the tax imposed pursuant to § 303 of the
   Act (D.C. Code § 45-923) regardless of the identity of the grantee named in
   the deed, including a grantee who is the original secured party or mortgagee
   under the deed of trust or mortgage which secured the debt or other obligation
   on the property in question.

   511.3. For purposes of this section, the measure of the tax shall be the
   consideration for the deed as provided in § 303 of the Act.

   511.4. The provisions of § 502 of this chapter shall apply to this section.
