                                  DC REGULATIONS

LEASES (ยง 9-526)

526.1. A lease for more than ninety-nine (99) years shall be treated as a fee
   for purpose of this chapter.

   526.2. For the purpose of determining the length of a lease term, options to
   renew shall be included.

   526.3. Consideration for a lease in excess of ninety-nine (99) years shall be
   the amount of the assessed value of the property at the date of the
   transaction.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 8653, 8663 (December 29, 1989).
