                                  DC REGULATIONS

FILING REQUIREMENTS (§ 9-525)

525.1. A deed, in the form prescribed by the Deputy Chief Financial Officer,
   shall be filed within thirty (30) days of a transfer of an economic interest
   by the entity in which the transfer has occurred.

   525.2. The form of deed to record transfers of economic interests and
   accompanying returns shall be available at the Office of Recorder of Deeds.

   525.3. Deeds and accompanying returns evidencing transfers of economic
   interests shall be subject to rules for filing with the Recorder of Deeds as
   found in §§ 502 through 508 of this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 8653, 8662 (December 29, 1989).
