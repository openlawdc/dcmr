                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEALS OF COMMISSION DECISIONS (§ 9-2021)

2021. 1	Except as provided in D.C. Official Code § 47-830 (2012 Supp.), the
petitioner may appeal a decision of the Commission to the Tax Division of the
Superior Court of the District of Columbia in the same manner and to the same
extent as provided in D.C. Official Code §§ 47-3303 and 47-3304 (2005 Repl.),
by September 30 of the tax year except as otherwise provided by law.



SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 24, 37 (January 2, 1998); as
amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 9888 (July 5, 2013).
