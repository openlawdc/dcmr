                                  DC REGULATIONS

SUPPLEMENTAL PETITION (ยง 9-2008)

2008.1. An original petition may be augmented by a supplemental filing with
   OTR and the Commission if the supplemental filing is filed no later than
   twenty (20) days after the filing of the appeal, and if it is based on new
   information which was not available prior to the filing deadline for the
   original petition.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 24, 31 (January 2, 1998); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 9888 (July 5, 2013).
