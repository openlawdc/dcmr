                                  DC REGULATIONS

BURDEN OF PROOF (ยง 9-2013)

2013.1. In an appeal, the proposed assessed value or classification of the
   subject property shall be presumed to be correct, and the petitioner has the
   burden of demonstrating that the assessment does not represent the estimated
   market value of the property or that the challenged classification of the
   property is erroneous.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 24, 33 (January 2, 1998); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 9888 (July 5, 2013).
