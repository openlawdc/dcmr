                                  DC REGULATIONS

TAX RETURNS OF PARTNERSHIPS (ยง 9-108)

108.1. Returns shall be filed by every partnership engaging in any trade or
   business or receiving income from sources within the District.

   108.2. If a partnership is not required to file an unincorporated business
   return under certain conditions, it is not relieved from filing a partnership
   return as required by Title 5 of the Act; except that a partnership return, as
   such, shall not be required to be filed if the taxpayer files an
   unincorporated business tax return.
