                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESIDENTIAL PROPERTIES (§ 9-612)

612	The phrase “residential properties” as referenced in D.C. Official Code
§ 47-903(a-4) shall have the same meaning as Class 1 Property, as defined by
D.C. Official Code § 47-813(c-8)(2), or such other section under D.C. Official
Code § 47-813 as may supersede D.C. Official Code § 47-813(c-8)(2).



SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 3634 (March 15, 2013).
