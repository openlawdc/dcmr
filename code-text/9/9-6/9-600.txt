                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 9-600)

600.1. The provisions of this chapter are adopted under authority set forth in
   § 416 of the &#8220;District of Columbia Revenue Act of 1980&#8243; (also
   referred to in this chapter as the &#8220;Act&#8221;); D.C. Law 3-92
   (effective Sept. 13, 1980); D.C. Code § 47-916 (1981 Ed.).

   600.2. For the purposes of this chapter, the words, terms, and phrases defined
   in the Act shall have the same meanings when used in this chapter, unless
   otherwise required by the context of this chapter.

   600.3. When used in this chapter, the term &#8220;Deputy Chief Financial
   Officer&#8221; means the Deputy Chief Financial Officer of the Office of Tax
   and Revenue (&#8220;Office&#8221;), or the Deputy Chief Financial
   Officer&#8217;s lawfully appointed agent, representative, or designee.

   600.4. When used in this chapter, the term &#8220;Recorder of Deeds&#8221;
   shall mean the Recorder of Deeds of the District of Columbia, or his or her
   lawfully appointed agent, representative, or designee.

   600.5. When used in the Act, the term &#8220;parent&#8221; means a person who
   is the natural parent of a child or a person who has legally adopted a child.
   The term &#8220;child&#8221; includes both minors and adults.

   600.6. When used in the Act and this chapter the term &#8220;fair market
   value&#8221; means the price at which a willing seller and a willing buyer
   will trade, or the price which would in all probability have been arrived at
   between a willing seller and a willing buyer.
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is § 416
   of the District of Columbia Revenue Act of 1980, D.C. Law 3-92, D.C. Code §
   47-916 (1981 Ed.).
