GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 9-3700)

3700.1. The provisions of this chapter are adopted under authority of ยง 21 of
   the Inheritance and Estate Tax Revision Act of 1986, as amended, effective
   February 24, 1987 (D.C. Law 6-168; 22 DCR 7008).

   3700.2. In accordance with Mayor&#8217;s Order 87-90, the Deputy Chief
   Financial Officer of the Office of Tax and Revenue (&#8220;Office&#8221;) is
   constituted the representative of the Mayor for the supervision and
   enforcement of the Inheritance and Estate Tax Revision Act of 1986, as the
   same may be amended from time to time.

   3700.3. References in this chapter to sections of the &#8220;Act&#8221; are to
   sections of the Inheritance and Estate Tax Revision Act of 1986, as amended,
   effective 24, 1987 (D.C. Law 6-168; 33 DCR 7008).

   3700.4. This chapter shall apply only to estates of persons dying after March
   31, 1987, and subject to the provisions of the Act.

   3700.5. All estates of persons dying on or before March 31, 1987, shall be
   governed by the rules contained in Chapter 2 of this title.
