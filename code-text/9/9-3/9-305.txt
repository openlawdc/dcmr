                                  DC REGULATIONS

ASSESSMENTS (§ 9-305)

305.1. All real property shall be assessed on an annual basis.

   305.2. For the purposes of this chapter, the term &#8220;real property&#8221;
   means real estate identified by plat on the records of the District of
   Columbia Surveyor according to lot and square, together with improvements on
   that real estate.

   305.3. For the purposes of this chapter, except where specifically provided
   otherwise, the word &#8220;assess&#8221; means to value real property for tax
   purposes.

   305.4. For the purposes of this chapter, except where specifically provided
   otherwise, the word &#8220;assessment&#8221; means a real property valuation
   established by the Deputy Chief Financial Officer for tax purposes against
   which the rate of tax is applied to arrive at the tax liability.

   305.5. For the purposes of this section, the terms &#8220;assess&#8221; and
   &#8220;assessed&#8221; do not include changes in assessed value resulting from
   new construction, additions to existing structures, damages to or destruction
   of property, and any other similar changes specified in D.C. Code §§ 47-829
   and 47-830 (1981 Ed.).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Regulation No. 74-35 effective December 12, 1974, 21 DCR 1643
   (January 20, 1975), 16 DCRR §§ 101, 104.
