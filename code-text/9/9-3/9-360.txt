                                  DC REGULATIONS

SUPPLEMENTAL ASSESSMENTS[COLON] GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 9-360)

360.1. Except as otherwise provided, §§ 360 through 368 shall apply to
   supplemental assessments.

   360.2. The rules pertaining to supplemental assessments shall apply to
   supplemental assessments conducted on or after January 1, 1991.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 8171 (November 19, 1993).
