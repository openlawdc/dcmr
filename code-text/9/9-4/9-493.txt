SALES PRICE[COLON] COMMUNICATION SERVICES (ยง 9-493)

For purposes of D.C. Official Code ยง 47-2001(p)(2), the term &#8220;sales
price&#8221; shall not include a charge for a nontaxable service that is made in
connection with a sale of a taxable communication service, even if the
nontaxable charges are aggregated with and not separately stated from the
taxable charges for communication services, if the vendor can reasonably
identify charges not subject to tax from its books and records that are kept in
the regular course of business.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 6029 (June 11, 2004).
