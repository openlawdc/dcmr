                                  DC REGULATIONS

PERSONS FURNISHING NON-TAXABLE SERVICES (ยง 9-401)

401.1. Persons who furnish a nontaxable service are considered consumers of
   all materials and supplies purchased to operate that business.

   401.2. Persons who furnish non-taxable services must reimburse the vendors
   from whom they purchase materials and supplies for the sales tax.
