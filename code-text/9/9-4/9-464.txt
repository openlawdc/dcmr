                                  DC REGULATIONS

MAINTENANCE AND SERVICE CONTRACTS (ยง 9-464)

464.1. A contract for the maintenance of tangible personal property is
   considered a repair contract and shall be subject to tax, whether paid in one
   payment or periodic payments.

   464.2. The tax shall apply to the total amount charged, whether or not any
   materials are furnished.

   464.3. Some examples of taxable maintenance contracts are the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Office machines;
   
   
   
   (b)	Electrical signs;
   
   
   
   (c)	Manufacturing machinery; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Home appliances, such as televisions, refrigerators, washers, and dryers.
