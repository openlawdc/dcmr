                                  DC REGULATIONS

AUCTIONEERS (ยง 9-433)

433.1. Persons engaged in the business and making retail sales at auction of
   tangible personal property owned by them or other persons, shall be considered
   to be retailers for the purposes of the Act, and are required to obtain a
   certificate of registration and file returns.

   433.2. Receipts from the sale of items at auction shall be subject to the tax.
