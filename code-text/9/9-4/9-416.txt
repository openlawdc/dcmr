                                  DC REGULATIONS

UNLAWFUL ADVERTISING (ยง 9-416)

416.1. It shall be unlawful for any vendor to advertise, hold out, or state to
   the public or to any customer, directly or indirectly, that the reimbursement
   for the tax or any part of the tax to be collected by the vendor under this
   chapter will be assumed or absorbed by the vendor.

   416.2. It shall be unlawful for any vendor to advertise, hold out, or state to
   the public or to any customer, directly or indirectly, that the reimbursement
   for the tax or any part of the tax to be collected by the vendor under this
   chapter will not be added to the selling price of the property sold or the
   taxable services rendered; or if added to the sales price, that the tax (or
   any part of it) will be refunded.

   416.3. Any person violating any provision of this section shall, upon
   conviction, be fined not more than five hundred dollars ($ 500) or imprisoned
   for not more than six (6) months, or both, for each offense.
