                                  DC REGULATIONS

STORAGE WAREHOUSEMEN AND FURNITURE MOVERS (ยง 9-469)

469.1. For the purposes of this chapter, storage warehousemen, furniture
   movers, and persons engaged in similar businesses, are engaged primarily in
   the rendition of a service, and the receipts from such services are not
   subject to tax.

   469.2. Storage warehousemen, furniture movers and other persons covered under
   this section must reimburse their vendors for the tax on all commodities
   purchased for use in carrying on their businesses.
