                                  DC REGULATIONS

PARKING FEES[COLON] GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 9-454)

454.1. The exemption from the sales tax on parking shall apply only to parking
   facilities which are used for residential parking and not to parking
   facilities which are used solely for nonresidential purposes, even though
   these facilities are within one half (1/2) mile of an individual&#8217;s place
   of residence.

   454.2. In no event shall the exemption from sales tax on parking be applicable
   to commercial tenants, parking for commercial purposes, or to vehicles used
   for commercial purposes.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Commissioners&#8217; Order 54-1415, 1 DCR 4 (July 19, 1954); as
   amended by the Third Amendment to the Revenue Act of 1975 Act, D.C. Law 1-61,
   22 DCR 4383, 4384 (February 12, 1976).
