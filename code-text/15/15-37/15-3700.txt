                                  DC REGULATIONS

PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY (§ 15-3700)

3700.1. The purpose of this chapter is to establish standards and requirements
   for ensuring that a natural gas utility and natural gas service providers
   operating in the District of Columbia meet an adequate level of quality,
   reliability, and safety in the natural gas service provided to District of
   Columbia customers.

   3700.2. This chapter shall apply to all natural gas utility and natural gas
   service providers operating in the District of Columbia, subject to the
   authority of the Public Service Commission of the District of Columbia
   (Commission).

   3700.3. All written Natural Gas Quality of Service Standards (“NGQSS”)
   quarterly and annual reports, studies, surveys, or filings required to be
   submitted to the Commission shall be docketed under Formal Case No. 977
   (“F.C. 977”).
