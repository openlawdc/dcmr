                                  DC REGULATIONS

OFFICIAL RECORD CONTENTS (ยง 15-2004)

2004.1. The official record compiled by the Office shall include the Case
   Manager&#8217;s written statement of need, the Agency Administrator&#8217;s
   recommended determination as submitted to the affected utility, written
   oppositions or comments submitted by any affected utility in response to the
   recommended determination, the determination by People&#8217;s Counsel, the
   proposed order, and any other documents submitted to the Commission relating
   to the Notice of Agency Fund Requirements.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 32 DCR 5620 (October 4, 1985).
