                                  DC REGULATIONS

VOLUNTARY TERMINATION OF ARBITRATION PROCEEDINGS (ยง 15-2616)

If after the initiation of an arbitration proceeding, the arbitrating parties
reach a negotiated agreement that resolves all of the issues submitted for
arbitration, the arbitrating parties shall file a joint request to dismiss the
arbitration petition. The Commission may review and grant this request.
