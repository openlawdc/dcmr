                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONSOLIDATION OF PROCEEDINGS (ยง 15-2614)

2614.1. In order to reduce administrative burdens on telecommunications
   carriers and/or the Commission, the Commission may, sua sponte, or upon the
   motion of a party in any arbitration, interpretation, or enforcement
   proceedings, consolidate arbitration, interpretation, or enforcement
   proceedings, in whole or in part, pursuant to 47 U.S.C. ยง 252(g).

   2614.2. In a consolidated arbitration proceeding, all petitioning and
   responding carriers participating in the separate arbitration, interpretation,
   or enforcement proceedings shall participate as parties in the consolidated
   proceeding.
