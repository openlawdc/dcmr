                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMMISSION PROCEEDINGS FOR REVIEW OF NEGOTIATED AGREEMENTS (ยง 15-2604)

Within ninety (90) calendar days of the date that a negotiated agreement is
filed with the Commission, the Commission shall either approve or reject the
negotiated agreement, or any portion thereof, in accordance with the standards
set forth in 47 U.S.C. ยง 252(e)(2). The Commission shall make written findings
as to any deficiencies in the negotiated agreement if the negotiated agreement
is rejected.
