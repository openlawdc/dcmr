                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPOINTMENT OF A MEDIATOR PURSUANT TO 47 U.S.C. ยง 252(A)(2) (ยง 15-2606)

The Commission shall appoint a mediator(s). The mediator may be a Commission
staff member and/or any competent, impartial, and disinterested person. The
requesting carrier and all participants in the negotiation for which mediation
has been requested shall be notified by the Commission Secretary of the
appointment of the mediator within five (5) business days of the date on which
the request for mediation is filed.
