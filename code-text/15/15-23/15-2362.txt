LEAK TESTS (ยง 15-2362)

2362.1. The meter shall not leak when subjected to pressure and shall be
   checked for leaks in the following manner:
   
   (a)	Immersion;
   
   (b)	Soap test; or
   
   (c)	A pressure drop test of a type acceptable to the Commission&#8217;s Office
   of Engineering.

   2362.2. Tinned steel case meters shall be subjected to an internal pressure of
   at least two pounds (2 lbs.) per square inch gauge (psig) when testing for
   leaks.

   2362.3. Iron or aluminum case meters shall be tested at a pressure at least
   fifty percent (50%) above operating pressure. The meters may be sample tested
   in accordance with the plan described in ยง 2360.11. If one (1) or more meters
   in the sample is found to leak, additional testing shall be done as described
   in ยง 2360.11.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 7938, 7946 (November 4, 1988); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 7 (January 4, 2013).
