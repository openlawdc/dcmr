                                  DC REGULATIONS

FILING REQUIREMENT FOR NEW SERVICES (ยง 15-297)

297.1. All tariff filings for new services provided by the Chesapeake and
   Potomac Telephone Company shall contain a complete cost study, including all
   workpapers and related data, which supports the proposed rates for the new
   services.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 1299 (February 12, 1993).
