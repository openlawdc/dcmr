                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISCRIMINATION EXPENSES (ยง 15-207)

207.1. Discrimination expenses shall be accounted for and reported in the
   manner as prescribed by the Commission.

   207.2. Any rate change application shall contain a statement of discrimination
   expenses for the test year separately identified by account.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 2941 (May 8, 1987).
