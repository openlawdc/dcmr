                                  DC REGULATIONS

TARIFF COMPLIANCE FILING REQUIREMENTS (ยง 15-296)

296.1. Compliance filings affecting existing rates shall be filed with the
   Secretary of the Commission, the Office of the People&#8217;s Counsel, and
   each intervenor.

   296.2. Service on the Office of the People&#8217;s Counsel and intervenors
   shall be on the same day the compliance filing is made with the Commission,
   where possible.

   296.3. There shall be a period of five (5) days after the compliance filing is
   submitted to the Commission within which the Office of the People&#8217;s
   Counsel, staff, and intervenors may comment on the compliance filing.

   296.4. The Commission shall not entertain motions for enlargement of time
   within which to comment on tariff compliance filings except upon a clear
   showing of extraordinary circumstances.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 1155, 1178 (February 13, 1987).
