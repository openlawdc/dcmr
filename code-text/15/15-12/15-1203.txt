                                  DC REGULATIONS

PURCHASE PROVISIONS (ยง 15-1203)

1203.1. The number of subsidized bus tickets authorized shall correspond to a
   single round trip for each day of the program or course to be taken during the
   restricted travel period.

   1203.2. Student tickets shall be purchased in multiples of ten from sales
   outlets of the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority (WMATA) as shall
   be designated by the Director of Public Works.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 2443 (May 29, 1981),
   incorporating text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 1834, 1837
   (April 24, 1981).
