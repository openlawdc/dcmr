                                  DC REGULATIONS

VALIDATION OF APPLICATIONS (ยง 15-1202)

1202.1. Applications shall be reviewed and validated by the Office of Mass
   Transportation, D.C. Department of Public Works.

   1202.2. Approved applications shall be returned to the school of origin
   together with ticket purchase blank forms that allow the student the number of
   subsidized bus tickets authorized for travel to the student&#8217;s program or
   course.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 2443 (May 29, 1981),
   incorporating text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 1834, 1836
   (April 24, 1981).
