                                  DC REGULATIONS

WAIVER (ยง 15-146)

146.1. The Commission may, in its discretion, waive any of the provisions of
   Chapters 1 and 2 of this title in any proceeding after duly advising the
   parties of its intention to do so.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 2984, 3036 (July 3, 1981).
