                                  DC REGULATIONS

LIMITED APPEARANCE (ยง 15-107)

107.1. At the discretion of the Commission, any person may make a limited
   appearance in any proceeding by presenting a statement orally or in writing at
   any time prior to the close of the record.

   107.2. A person entering a limited appearance shall not be a party to the
   proceeding and shall not have the right to present testimony or cross-examine
   witnesses.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 2984, 2991 (July 3, 1981).
