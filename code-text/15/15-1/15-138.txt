                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROPOSED FINDINGS AND CONCLUSIONS (ยง 15-138)

138.1. Each party to the proceeding, after the close of the record within the
   time prescribed for post-hearing briefs, or as otherwise directed by the
   Commission shall serve on all parties and file with the Commission proposed
   findings of fact and conclusions of law for consideration by the Commission.

   138.2. The proposed findings of fact and conclusions of law shall be
   referenced to the record of the proceedings.

   138.3. The proposed findings of fact and conclusions of law may be included in
   the post hearing briefs required by this section.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 2984, 3013 (July 3, 1981).
