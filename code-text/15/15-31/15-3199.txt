                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 15-3199)

When used in this chapter, the terms and phrases defined in this section shall
have the meaning ascribed:



Cable Operator &#8211; any person or group (A) who provides cable service over a
cable system and directly or through one or more affiliates owns a significant
interest in such cable system, or (B) who otherwise controls or is responsible
for, through any arrangement, the management or operation of such a cable
system.



Customer Service Representative &#8211; a person employed or contracted by a
cable operator to assist, or provide service to, subscribers, whether by
answering telephone calls, managing repair or installation orders, answering
subscribers&#8217; questions, receiving and processing payments, or performing
other customer service-related tasks.



OCT &#8211; District of Columbia Government Office of Cable Television.



Standard Installation &#8211; any installation to a residence from the cable
operator&#8217;s existing network that does not exceed two hundred fifty (250)
feet or as defined by an applicable franchise agreement.



Subscriber &#8211; a person who lawfully receives or requests to receive cable
service of any sort from a cable operator. The term shall have the same meaning
as the term &#8220;customer&#8221;.



Unusual Circumstance &#8211; a special occasion when the cable operator
voluntarily suspends its normal operations, such as a staff meeting. The term
does not include suspensions of operations caused by emergency circumstances
outside of the control of the cable operator, such as a natural disaster or any
identified force majeure.



Website &#8211; an Internet website dedicated to local subscribers of a District
of Columbia cable operator. The website may be a part of a cable
operator&#8217;s larger website.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 6797 (July 19, 2002).
