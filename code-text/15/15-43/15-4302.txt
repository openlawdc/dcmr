                                  DC REGULATIONS

INFORMATION TO BE FILED WITH THE COMMISSION (ยง 15-4302)

4302.1. On or before May 1 of each year, the electric utility shall file a
   report entitled &#8220;Distribution Transformer Report&#8221; which
   demonstrates whether the utility complied with the Energy Standards Act of
   2007. The report shall include:
   
   
   
   (a)	The number of models of transformers in each capacity class or type that
   were evaluated during the reporting period;
   
   
   
   (b)	The avoided cost of generation and transmission energy and capacity used
   in the life-cycle cost analysis; and
   
   
   
   (c)	The estimated savings in energy and capacity resulting from the purchase
   of transformers in the year, compared with minimally efficient distribution
   transformers of the same capacity, class or type.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 11319 (October 31, 2008),
   incorporating by reference the text of Proposed Rulemaking published at 55 DCR
   9740-9743 (September 12, 2008);
