603.1. A PSP may request that its certificate be canceled.

   603.2. A request for cancellation of the certificate shall be in writing and
   shall include:
   
   
   
   (a)	A list of the locations of each PSP payphone(s), if any;
   
   
   
   (b)	The PSP&#8217;s specific plans for terminating service and removing the
   pay telephone(s), or its plans for transferring the PSP&#8217;s registered pay
   telephones to another certified PSP in accordance with ยง 610; and
   
   
   
   (c)	The date on which the PSP wishes the cancellation to become effective.

   603.3. If a request for cancellation of a certificate meets the requirements
   under this section, the Commission shall within ten (10) days send written
   notice to the PSP that its certificate has been canceled.
