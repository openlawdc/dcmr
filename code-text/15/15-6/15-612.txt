                                  DC REGULATIONS

INFORMAL CONFERENCE (ยง 15-612)

612.1. Within fifteen (15) days of receiving a complaint, the
   Commission&#8217;s staff shall attempt to resolve the dispute informally. The
   time period for resolving the dispute informally may be extended by consent of
   the parties.

   612.2. Meetings to discuss informal resolution shall be held in a manner
   (including telephone conferences), and at times (including evening hours) and
   locations that are convenient to the public and the PSP.

   612.3. If the complaint is resolved informally, the Commission shall dismiss
   the matter upon the submission of a signed settlement agreement that sets
   forth the terms of the settlement and states that the complainant wishes to
   withdraw the complaint.

   612.4. Notice of informal proceedings shall be sent by certified mail to the
   Complainant(s),the PSP, the ANC Chairperson and Single Member District ANC
   Commissioner of the community in which the instrument is located, the Office
   of the People&#8217;s Counsel, and the MPD District in which the instrument(s)
   is located or proposed to be located. The notice shall state that OPC is
   available to assist and/or represent the complainant.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 1151 (February 9, 2001).
