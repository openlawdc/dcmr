                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 15-699)

Pay Telephone Service Provider &#8211; Any corporation, company, association,
partnership or person engaged in the business of providing pay telephone
service.



Pay Telephone &#8211; A telecommunications device connected to a local exchange
carrier that is designed to allow a customer to make telephone calls with a coin
and/or credit card.



Day &#8211; Unless the particular rule states otherwise, day shall mean calendar
day.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 1151 (February 9, 2001).
