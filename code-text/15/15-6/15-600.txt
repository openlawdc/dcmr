                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY (§ 15-600)

600.1. The provisions in this Chapter shall apply exclusively to outdoor pay
   telephones unless otherwise specified.

   600.2. The provisions in this Chapter do not apply to outdoor pay telephones
   installed and operated on federal military installations and federal military
   hospitals where access by the public is limited thereon.
