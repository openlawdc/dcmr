                                  DC REGULATIONS

FINES (§ 15-617)

617.1. Pursuant to D.C. Code §§ 43-306 and 310, a PSP that fails to comply
   with any lawful requirement or order of the Commission shall be subject to a
   fine of $ 300 for each day that the PSP willfully fails to comply with the
   Commission&#8217;s order or directive.

   617.2. Operation of a pay telephone without first registering the instrument
   with the Commission shall subject the PSP to a fine of $ 3,000.
