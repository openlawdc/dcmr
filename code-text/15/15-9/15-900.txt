                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 15-900)

900.1. The purpose of this chapter is to set forth the policies and procedures
   for implementation of the net energy metering provisions of the “Retail
   Electric Competition and Consumer Protection Act of 1999,” as amended, and
   the Clean and Affordable Energy Act of 2008 (“CAEA”).

   900.2. This chapter establishes the Public Service Commission of the District
   of Columbia Rules and Regulations Governing Net Energy Metering, including
   eligibility for participating in net energy metering, a bill crediting
   mechanism, net energy billing requirements for participants, net
   metering-related equipment requirements, a standard contract requirement, and
   safety and performance standards. This chapter shall be cited as the
   “District of Columbia Net Energy Metering Rules.”

   900.3. The provisions of this chapter are promulgated pursuant to the
   authority set forth in Section 34-1518 of the D.C. Official Code.
