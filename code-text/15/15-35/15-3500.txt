                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY (§ 15-3500)

Pursuant to D.C. Code §§ 43-527, 43-609, 43-701 et seq., and 43-1452, this
Chapter creates an expedited review procedure that shall apply to applications
by entities regulated by the Commission for authority to issue or amend tariffs
and issue stock or evidences of indebtedness that are payable in more than one
year.



	Source: Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 8202 (October 13, 2000).
