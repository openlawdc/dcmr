                                  DC REGULATIONS

PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY (§ 15-4000)

4000.1. This Chapter establishes the District of Columbia Small Generator
   Interconnection Rules (&#8220;DSGIR&#8221;) which apply to facilities
   satisfying the following criteria:
   
   
   
   (a)	The total nameplate capacity of the small generator facility is equal to
   or less than 10 megawatts (&#8220;MW&#8221;).
   
   
   
   (b)	The small generator facility is not subject to the interconnection
   requirements of PJM Interconnection.
   
   
   
   (c)	The small generator facility is designed to operate in parallel with the
   electric distribution system.
