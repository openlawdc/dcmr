                                  DC REGULATIONS

WAIVER (ยง 15-2821)

2821.1. Whenever compliance with any requirement of this chapter would result
   in unreasonable hardship upon or excessive expense to a party or parties
   subject to the rules of this chapter, the Commission may, upon application or
   sua sponte, and for good cause shown, waive or modify the requirements of this
   chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 8198 (October 3,
   2003); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 8585, 8595
   (October 7, 2011).
