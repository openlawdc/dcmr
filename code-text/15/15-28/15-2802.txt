                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA UNIVERSAL SERVICES (ยง 15-2802)

2802.1  	District of Columbia Universal Services shall consist of the following
services:







Voice grade access to the public switched network, with the ability to place and
receive calls or its functional equivalent;







Minutes of use for local service provided at no additional cost to end users;







Access to emergency services, including, access to 911 and enhanced 911 (E911)
services;







Telecommunications Relay Service (TRS); and







Toll limitation services for qualifying low-income consumers at no charge to the
customer.



2802.2  	The Commission may expand universal services to be supported by the
District of Columbia Universal Service Trust Fund (DC USTF or Fund) after notice
and comment.
