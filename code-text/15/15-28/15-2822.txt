                                  DC REGULATIONS

VIOLATIONS (ยง 15-2822)

2822.1. Failure to pay an assessed contribution to the DC USTF shall be deemed
   a violation of this chapter.

   2822.2. If the Commission determines after notice and hearing that an ETC has
   acted in violation of this chapter, the Commission may file an action on
   behalf of the DC USTF to recover any unpaid fees and charges the Commission
   has determined are due and payable, including interest, administrative and
   adjudicative costs, and attorney fees.  Upon collection of the above charges
   and costs, the Fund Administrator shall pay the costs of the actions and
   deposit the remaining funds in the DC USTF as appropriate.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 8198 (October 3,
   2003); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 8585, 8595
   (October 7, 2011).
