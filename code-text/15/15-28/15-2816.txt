                                  DC REGULATIONS

REQUEST FOR DC USTF FUNDING (§ 15-2816)

2816.1. Any ETC may request funding from the DC USTF for the provision of
   	Lifeline service to an eligible customer.

   2816.2. An ETC requesting funds from the DC USTF for the provision of Lifeline
   	service to eligible customers shall submit a Request for DC USTF 	Funding
   Application (Application) with supporting documentation to the 	DC USTF
   Administrator and the Commission Secretary’s Office.

   2816.3. Concurrent with the filing of the Application, the ETC requesting
   funding 	shall serve each District ETC and OPC with a copy of the Application

   2816.4. Any entity responsible for certifying Lifeline customers may request
   					funding from the DC USTF for reimbursement of costs incurred as the
   					manager of the certification and recertification processes.

   2816.5. Any TRS provider may request funding from the DC USTF consistent
   					with its contract with the Commission to provide TRS services.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 8198 (October 3,
   2003); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 8585, 8591
   (October 7, 2011).
