                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRIORITY OF DISTRIBUTION IN THE EVENT OF A SHORTFALL (ยง 15-2818)

2818.1. If the DC USTF experiences a shortfall, the available funds will be
   distributed in the following order:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   To support the services designated as universal services; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Any residual funds will be used to cover the costs associated with
   administering the fund.

   2818.2. The remaining costs associated with administering the fund will be
   paid as additional funds become available.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 8198 (October 3,
   2003); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 8585, 8592
   (October 7, 2011).
