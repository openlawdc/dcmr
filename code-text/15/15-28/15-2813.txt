REPORTING REQUIREMENTS FOR THE DC USTF ADMINISTRATOR (ยง 15-2813)

2813.1. On a quarterly basis, the Fund Administrator shall submit to the
   Commission a report including:
   
   (a)		A statement of collections and distributions from the universal service
   fund for each local exchange carrier and VoIP service provider;
   
   
   
   (b)		A statement detailing the purpose for which the universal service funds
   were used (to support an enumerated service listed in ยง 2802.1 or for
   verification of lifeline eligibility); and
   
   
   
   (c)		A record of total cost of universal service fund administration.

   2813.2. On September 30 every year after the establishment of the DC USTF, the
   Fund Administrator shall submit to the Commission a report that includes a
   proposed budget for the upcoming year.
