                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESOLUTION OF OTHER DISPUTES (ยง 15-2811)

2811.1. Any adversely impacted party may dispute the actions of a local
   exchange carrier or VoIP service provider related to the provisioning of
   Universal Services.  The adversely affected party may contact the Fund
   Administrator for resolution of the dispute.

   2811.2. If resolution is not achieved, the local exchange carrier, VoIP
   service provider, other adversely affected party, or Fund Administrator may
   file an application requesting the Commission to resolve the dispute.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 8198 (October 3,
   2003); as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 10343,
   10344 (December 9, 2011).
