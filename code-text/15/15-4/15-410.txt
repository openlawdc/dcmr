                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICE OF UTILITY CHARGE PAID BY TENANT (ยง 15-410)

410.1. The utility company shall submit to the owner a monthly listing of the
   utility charges made by those tenants who have elected to receive service in
   their own name. The tenant shall be authorized to reduce his or her rent
   payment to the owner by an amount equal to the utility charge. The owner shall
   have no recourse for collecting the amount of rent so reduced from the tenant.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 3329, 3337 (July 24, 1981).
