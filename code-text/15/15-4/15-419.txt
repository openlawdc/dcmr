VIOLATIONS (ยง 15-419)

419.1. Any willful or malicious violation of this chapter by any utility or
   owner shall be punishable by a fine of not more than five hundred dollars
   ($500.00) or imprisonment of not more than thirty (30) days or both.

   419.2. Any owner who collects or attempts to collect any rent from any tenant
   or an apartment house subject to an order appointing a receiver pursuant to
   ยง408.2 shall be found, after due notice and a hearing, to be in contempt of
   court.
