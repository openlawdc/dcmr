PRESERVATION OF UTILITIES RIGHT OF ACTION (ยง 15-416)

416.1. Nothing in this chapter shall be interpreted as preventing the utility
   from pursuing any other action or remedy at law or equity that it might have
   against the owner.

   416.2. No provision in this chapter shall be construed to relieve the owner
   from liability under a contract for the provision of utility services with a
   utility company until such time as the Public Service Commission makes a
   finding to terminate service pursuant to ยง401.
