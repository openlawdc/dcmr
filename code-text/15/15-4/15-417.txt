                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRESERVATION OF TENANT&#8217;S RIGHT OF ACTION (ยง 15-417)

417.1. Nothing in this chapter shall be interpreted as preventing the tenant
   of the apartment house from pursuing any other action or remedy at law or
   equity that he or she may have against the owner or company.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 3329, 3339 (July 24, 1981).
