                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 15-1699)

1699.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the meaning ascribed:
   
   
   
   Commission &#8211; the Public Service Commission of the District of Columbia.
   
   
   
   Complainant &#8211; a cable television system operator or a utility who files
   a complaint.
   
   
   
   Complaint &#8211; a filing by either a cable television system operator or a
   utility alleging that a rate, term or condition for a pole attachment is not
   just and reasonable.
   
   
   
   Pole Attachment &#8211; any attachment by a cable television system to a pole,
   duct, conduit, or right-of-way owned or controlled by a utility company.
   
   
   
   Respondent &#8211; a cable television system operator or a utility against
   whom a complaint is filed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 31 DCR 6496, 6501 (December 21, 1984).
