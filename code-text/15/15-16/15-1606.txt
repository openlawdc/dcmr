                                  DC REGULATIONS

REMEDIES (ยง 15-1606)

1606.1. If the Commission determines that the rate, term, or condition
   complained of is not just and reasonable, it may prescribe a just and
   reasonable rate, term, or condition and may take any of the following actions:
   
   
   
   (a)	Terminate the unjust and unreasonable rate, term or condition; or
   
   
   
   (b)	Substitute in the pole attachment agreement the just and reasonable rate,
   term, or condition established by the Commission.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 31 DCR 6496, 6500 (December 21, 1984).
