                                  DC REGULATIONS

ENFORCEMENT (ยง 15-1607)

1607.1. If the respondent fails to obey an order imposed under this subpart,
   the Commission on its own motion or upon the motion of the complainant may
   order the respondent to show cause why it should not cease and desist from
   violating the Commission&#8217;s order.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 31 DCR 6496, 6500 (December 21, 1984).
