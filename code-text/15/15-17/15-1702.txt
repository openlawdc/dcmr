                                  DC REGULATIONS

JURISDICTION OF THE PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION (ยง 15-1702)

1702.1. The Public Service Commission shall retain jurisdiction over
   operations conducted under this chapter and may under its statutory authority,
   take the action it deems necessary to protect the public, or for any other
   reason consistent with its statutory mandate.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 32 DCR 3455, 3456 (June 21, 1985).
