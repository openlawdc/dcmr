                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESTRICTIONS ON USE OF EMPLOYEES AND EQUIPMENT (ยง 15-3907)

3907.1. An electric company is prohibited from sharing employees with an
   affiliate.

   3907.2. An energy utility and an affiliate may share the same
   telecommunications system or computer system, so long as adequate security and
   system protections are in place to prevent the accessing of information or
   data of the energy utility by the affiliate that would be in violation of
   other provisions of this Chapter.

   3907.3. An electric company shall not temporarily assign any employee of the
   electric company to an affiliate.
