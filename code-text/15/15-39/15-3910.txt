                                  DC REGULATIONS

WAIVER (ยง 15-3910)

3910.1. An energy utility may petition for a waiver from any section of the
   Affiliate Transactions Code of Conduct, which may be granted by the Commission
   upon a showing of good cause.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   		Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 1109, 1115 (February
   4, 2011).
