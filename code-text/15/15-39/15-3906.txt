                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSFER OR SALE OF ASSETS (§ 15-3906)

3906.1. Transfers of assets from an energy utility to an affiliate must be
   recorded on the utility’s books at the greater of net book cost or market
   value.  Transfers of assets from an affiliate to the energy utility shall be
   at the lesser of net book cost or market value.  Such asymmetric pricing shall
   not apply to any transaction resulting from a competitive bidding process.
