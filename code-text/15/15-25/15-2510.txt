TARIFFS MUST BE FILED AND MAINTAINED WITH THE PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION (ยง
15-2510)

Prior to commencing service, all certificated local exchange carriers shall file
tariffs with the Commission for each service offered within the District. The
tariffs shall describe the service being offered and all terms and conditions,
and specify the rate or rates charged for the service pursuant to D.C. Code ยง
43-1452(f). Tariffs shall be maintained and updated as necessary.
