                                  DC REGULATIONS

AUTOMATIC CERTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS (ยง 15-2504)

2504.1. An applicant shall be exempt from any other certification requirements
   if the applicant demonstrates in its Application that the applicant or its
   affiliate:
   
   
   
   (a)	Has at least three (3) years of experience providing telecommunications
   services pursuant to authorization(s) by the FCC or a state regulatory body;
   and
   
   
   
   (b)	Derived more than $50 million in gross annual revenue from
   telecommunications services in the year immediately preceding the year in
   which the Application is filed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 2601 (April 14, 2000).
