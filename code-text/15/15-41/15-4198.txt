                                  DC REGULATIONS

WAIVER OR EXEMPTION (ยง 15-4198)

4198.1. Upon the request of any person subject to the provisions of these
   regulations or upon its own motion, the Commission, for good cause, may waive
   any of the requirements of these regulations that are not required by statute.
    No waiver granted pursuant to this provision shall apply retroactively to any
   wholesale supply agreement.
