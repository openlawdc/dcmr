                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECONNECTION OF SERVICE (ยง 15-315)

315.1. A Utility shall reconnect service within twenty-four (24) hours of cure
   of the cause for Disconnection.

   315.2. The Utility may Charge the Customer for reconnection of service in an
   amount authorized by the Commission.

   amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 10014 (September 26,

   (December 12, 2008).
