                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISCONNECTION OF UTILITY SERVICE (ยง 15-314)

314.1. If the requirement to make an attempt to contact a Customer is
   satisfied, the Utility may disconnect service to a residential Customer on the
   date specified in the notice or within a reasonable time thereafter.

   314.2. When natural gas or electric service is disconnected, the Utility shall
   leave a notice reasonably calculated to be seen by Persons residing on the
   premises stating that service has been disconnected and the address and
   telephone number of the Utility where the Customer may arrange to have service
   restored. The notice shall also state procedures to be followed where a
   medical or safety emergency exists on the premises.
