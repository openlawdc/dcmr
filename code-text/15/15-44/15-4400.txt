                                  DC REGULATIONS

PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY (ยง 15-4400)

4400.1. The purpose of this chapter is to establish standards and requirements
   for the accuracy, testing, billing, recordkeeping, and regulation of
   submetering and energy allocation equipment, and appropriate safeguards for
   the tenant, as well as to define the responsibilities of the owner.

   4400.2. Any owner of a building which is not individually metered for
   electricity or natural gas for each nonresidential rental unit may install
   submetering equipment or energy allocation equipment for the purpose of fairly
   allocating:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The cost of electrical or natural gas consumption for each nonresidential
   rental unit; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Electrical or natural gas demand and customer charges made by the utility and
   electricity and natural gas supplier.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 9521, 9522 (November
   11, 2011).
