                                  DC REGULATIONS

TESTING CAPABILITY AND METERING EQUIPMENT (§ 15-4404)

4404.1. Each owner shall engage a certified lab to perform the equipment tests
   required by these regulations; such tests being performed with instruments,
   portable standards, reference manuals, and other equipment and facilities all
   of which shall comply with standards specified in subsections 4401.9 and
   4401.10 (Section 4401, General Requirements) for submetering equipment, and
   with manufacturer’s recommended practices for energy allocation equipment.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 9521, 9527 (November
   11, 2011).
