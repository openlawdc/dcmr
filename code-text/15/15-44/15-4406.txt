                                  DC REGULATIONS

REQUEST TEST (ยง 15-4406)

4406.1. Upon request by a tenant, the owner will test the submetering or
   energy allocation equipment without charge to the tenant, provided that the
   owner shall not be required to conduct such test more than once in twenty-four
   (24) months for the same tenant.

   4406.2. The tenant, or his or her designated representative, may be present
   when the equipment is tested.

   4406.3. A written report of the results of the test shall be made available to
   the tenant within ten (10) business days after the completion of the test.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 9521, 9528 (November
   11, 2011).
