                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESERVATION OF RIGHTS (ยง 15-4410)

4410.1. Tenants, owners, operators or managers shall retain any private right
   of action resulting from any breach of the rental agreement or lease terms
   required by Section 4401 (General Requirements).
