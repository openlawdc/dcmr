TIME FOR ISSUING NOTICE (ยง 15-1402)

1402.1. A Notice of Agency Fund Requirements may be issued by the Commission
   at any time.

   1402.2. Any Notice of Agency Fund Requirements, once issued, shall be placed
   in the official docket of the Commission, and served on the affected utility,
   the Office of the People&#8217;s Counsel (&#8220;OPC&#8221;), and any party to
   the proceeding.

   1402.3. Any Notice of Agency Fund Requirements issued for a matter not
   officially docketed by the Commission shall be served on the affected utility
   and OPC and shall be maintained in the public records of the Commission.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 893, 894 (February 14, 1986).
