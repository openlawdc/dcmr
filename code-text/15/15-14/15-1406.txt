                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICATION OF OTHER CHAPTERS (ยง 15-1406)

1406.1. Unless otherwise noted in this chapter, the rules and procedures of
   the Commission as set forth in Chapter 1 of this title shall apply to all
   matters arising under this chapter.
