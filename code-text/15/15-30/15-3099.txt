                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (§ 15-3099)

3099.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the meaning ascribed:
   
   
   
   Cable Television Operator &#8211; a cable television company authorized to
   construct and operate a cable television system along the public ways in the
   District of Columbia or within specified areas in the District of Columbia.
   
   
   
   Director &#8211; the Executive Director of the Office of Cable Television
   established under D.C. Code §43-1805 (1990 Repl. Vol.).
   
   
   
   Legal Holidays &#8211; those public holidays listed in D.C. Code
   §§1-613.2(a) and (c) (1981 Ed.).
   
   
   
   Office &#8211; the Office of Cable Television established under D.C. Code
   §43-1805 (1981 Ed.).
   
   
   
   Owner &#8211; the legal owner of any dwelling, a condominium association, a
   housing cooperative or the agent authorized to manage, administer or control
   one or more single or multiple residential dwellings irrespective of the form
   of property ownership.
   
   
   
   Party &#8211; an applicant, petitioner, or a respondent seeking or responding
   to a request for relief from the Office of Cable Television pursuant to this
   chapter.
   
   
   
   Person &#8211; an individual, firm, partnership, corporation, or association.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 7175, 7183 (September 30, 1988).
