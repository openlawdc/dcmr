                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (§ 29-2499)

2499.1. The meanings ascribed to the definitions appearing in § 2399.1 of
   Chapter 23 shall apply to the terms in this chapter.

   2499.2. When used in this chapter, the following terms shall have the meaning
   ascribed:
   
   
   
   Act&#8211;the Choice in Drug Treatment Act of 2000 (D.C. Law 13-146; D.C.
   Official Code § 7-3001 et seq.), effective July 18, 2000.
   
   
   
   Addiction Recovery Fund or Fund&#8211;the fund established by § 5 of the Act.
   
   
   
   Drug Treatment Choice Program or DTCP&#8211;the substance abuse treatment
   program established by § 3 of the Act.
