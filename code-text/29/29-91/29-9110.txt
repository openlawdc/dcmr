                                  DC REGULATIONS

REIMBURSEMENT (§ 29-9110)

Medicaid reimbursement shall be available for the seven (7) categories of
services described under this chapter.  ASARS shall be reimbursed according to a
fee schedule rate for each service included in an approved treatment plan.







Separate reimbursement rates shall be established for services eligible to be
rendered in community-based, group, and family settings.







The fee schedule shall be published on the DHCF’s website at
www.dc-medicaid.com.





SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 11144 (September 28, 2012).
