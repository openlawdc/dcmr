                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 29-9499)

Medicaid Provider/Supplier Application &#8211; The general or
provider/supplier-specific application developed by DHCF, and required to
initiate participation as a D.C. Medicaid provider or supplier.



Disclosing Entity &#8211; A prospective or enrolled Medicaid provider or
supplier (other than an individual practitioner or group of practitioners), or a
fiscal agent.



Federal Health Care Program &#8211; Shall have the meaning ascribed in 42 U.S.C.
ยง 1320a-7b(f).



In-District Applicants &#8211; Prospective Medicaid providers or suppliers that
are located inside of the District of Columbia Consolidated Metropolitan
Statistical Area, as defined by the United States Census Bureau.



Out-of-District Applicants &#8211; Prospective Medicaid providers or suppliers
that are located outside of the District of Columbia Consolidated Metropolitan
Statistical Area, as defined by the United States Census Bureau.



Provider Agreement &#8211; Official enrollment document establishing roles,
responsibilities, and rights of a District Medicaid provider/supplier.







SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 10041 (July 12, 2013).
