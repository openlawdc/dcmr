DEFINITIONS (ยง 29-8099)

8099.1. When used in this chapter, the following definitions shall apply to
   this chapter. Except as specifically provided in this section, terms used in
   this chapter retain the meaning ascribed under the applicable provisions of
   the Act.
   
   
   
   Act &#8211; the Health Care Benefits Expansion Act of 1992, D.C. Law 9-114,
   effective June 11, 1992 (D.C. Official Code ยง 32-701 et seq.).
   
   
   
   Department &#8211; the District of Columbia Department of Health.
   
   
   
   Dependent child &#8211; a natural child, adopted child, stepchild, foster
   child, or child in the legal custody of a domestic partner who is unmarried
   and under twenty-two (22) years old, is unmarried under twenty-five (25) years
   old and a full-time student, or is unmarried of any age and incapable of
   self-support because of a mental or physical disability that existed before
   the age of twenty-two (22).
   
   
   
   Director &#8211; the Director of the District of Columbia Department of
   Health.
   
   
   
   Registrar &#8211; the State Center for Health Statistics Administration, Vital
   Records Registrar.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 5419 (June 14, 2002).
