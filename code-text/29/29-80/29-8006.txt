                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORDKEEPING, REPORTING, AND PUBLIC DISCLOSURE (ยง 29-8006)

8006.1. The Registrar shall keep all records related to domestic partnership
   registration secure.

   8006.2. The Director shall report annually to the Council of the District of
   Columbia on the number of Domestic Partnerships declared and terminated.

   8006.3. All information contained in a declaration of domestic partnership
   shall be open to the public for inspection, except that address information of
   the Partners shall remain confidential and shall be redacted from any copies
   provided for public inspection.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 5419 (June 14, 2002).
