                                  DC REGULATIONS

AUTHORIZATION FOR CREMATION (ยง 29-2815)

2815.1. Authorization for cremation of a decedent shall be obtained from the
   next of kin and the Chief Medical Examiner.

   2815.2. The Registrar has no jurisdiction over disposal of the ashes of a
   deceased person.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 781, 794 (January 25, 1991).
