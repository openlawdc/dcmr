DISPOSITION OF REPORTS OF FETAL DEATHS (ยง 29-2812)

2812.1. Reports of fetal death, required to be filed pursuant to D.C. Official
   Code ยง 7-213(a), are statistical reports that shall only be used for medical
   and health purposes and shall not be incorporated into the permanent official
   records of the system of vital records.

   2812.2. The Registrar is authorized to dispose of the reports of fetal death
   when all statistical processing of the reports is complete.
