                                  DC REGULATIONS

MASS DISINTERMENT (§ 29-2814)

2814.1. No mass disinterment shall be performed without the written
   authorization of the Registrar upon written application of the person
   requesting the mass disinterment.

   2814.2. The Registrar shall not authorize a mass disinterment unless he or she
   receives an order of the Court or signed permission from the next of kin
   authorizing the disinterment.

   2814.3. The Attorney General and the Director of the Department of Consumer
   and Regulatory Affairs shall review and approve each request for mass
   disinterment before the Registrar may authorize the disinterment.

   2814.4. The Registrar may issue one (1) authorization to permit mass
   disinterment and reinterment of all remains in a mass disinterment provided
   that, insofar as possible, the remains of each body is identified.

   2814.5. The Registrar’s written authorization shall serve as official
   approval for disinterment, transportation, and reinterment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 781, 793 (January 25, 1991); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 1664 (February 23, 2007).
