                                  DC REGULATIONS

CHANGE OF BASE YEAR FOR HOSPITAL FOR SICK CHILDREN (§ 29-927)

927.1. For services rendered on or after July 1, 2012, the Hospital for Sick
   Children’s Medicaid payment for inpatient hospital services is based upon
   the hospital’s audited allowable cost per diem for the base year period
   defined as the hospital’s fiscal year ending December 31, 2009.
