WHERE CARE MAY BE PROVIDED (ยง 29-917)

917.1. Nurse-midwifery services shall be provided as follows:
   
   
   
   (a)	In a hospital on an inpatient or outpatient basis;
   
   
   
   (b)	In a free-standing treatment and diagnostic clinic or free-standing
   maternity center;
   
   
   
   (c)	In the office of the nurse-midwife or the physician providing
   collaboration and referral services; or
   
   
   
   (d)	In the recipient&#8217;s home or alternative residence.

   917.2. Each facility in which labor and delivery services are provided shall
   be licensed in accordance with District of Columbia law.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 5657 (September 12, 1986).
