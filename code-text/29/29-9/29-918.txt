COLLABORATION AND PROTOCOL REQUIREMENTS (ยง 29-918)

918.1. Each nurse-midwife shall maintain at all times a current, written
   protocol for a twenty-four (24) hour collaboration and referral pursuant to
   the requirements of D.C. Law 6-99.

   918.2. Each nurse-midwife shall maintain and submit to the Department of Human
   Services a copy of the signed protocol.

   918.3. Each nurse-midwife shall inform the D.C. Medicaid Program, immediately,
   of any termination or change in the protocol required in ยง 918.1 of this
   section.
