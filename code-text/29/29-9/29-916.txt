                                  DC REGULATIONS

WHO MAY PROVIDE CARE (ยง 29-916)

916.1. The nurse-midwifery services shall meet the following requirements:
   
   
   
   (a)	Be provided by a person who is licensed as a registered nurse in the
   District of Columbia;
   
   
   
   (b)	Meet the requirements of D.C. Law 6-99; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Execute a provider agreement with the D.C. Medicaid Program.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 5657 (September 12, 1986).
