                                  DC REGULATIONS

AUDITING AND ACCESS TO RECORDS (ยง 29-977)

977.1. Each cost report submitted shall be reviewed for completeness,
   accuracy, compliance and reasonableness through a desk audit.

   977.2. On-site audits shall be conducted not less than once every four (4)
   years.

   977.3. Each facility shall allow access, during an on-site audit or review by
   Department of Health auditors, other District of Columbia government officials
   and representatives of the United States Department of Health and Human
   Services, to relevant financial records and statistical data to verify costs
   previously reported to the Medicaid Program.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 2333, 2350 (April 17, 1998).
