                                  DC REGULATIONS

CHANGE OF BASE YEAR FOR COLUMBIA HOSPITAL FOR WOMEN, PROVIDENCE HOSPITAL AND
WASHINGTON HOSPITAL CENTER (§ 29-967)

967.1. Columbia Hospital for Women, Providence Hospital and Washington
   Hospital Center’s Medicaid payment for inpatient services is based upon each
   hospital’s audited reimbursable cost per discharge for a base year period
   defined as the hospital’s fiscal year ending September 30, 1991.
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: The authority for this section is D.C. Code, 2001 Ed. §1-307.02;
   and Commissioner’s Order 70-83 (March 6, 1970), as amended by Mayor’s
   Order 79-144 (July 11, 1979).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 4044 (June 25, 1993).
   
   
   
   	EDITOR’S NOTE: This section was originally adopted as §970. It was
   renumbered by Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 2333, 2334 (April 17,
   1998).
