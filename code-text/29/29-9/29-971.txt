                                  DC REGULATIONS

CHANGE OF BASE YEAR FOR HOSPITAL FOR SICK CHILDREN (ยง 29-971)

971.1. Beginning one day after the effective date of these rules, the Hospital
   for Sick Children&#8217;s Medicaid payment for inpatient hospital services is
   based upon the hospital&#8217;s audited allowable cost per diem for the base
   year period defined as the hospital&#8217;s fiscal year ending December 31,
   1998.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 45 DCR 2333, 2340 (April 17, 1998); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 3487 (April 12, 2002).
