                                  DC REGULATIONS

USE OF INFORMATION AND DATA (§ 29-5316)

5316.1. The Department shall have a license, free of charge, to use any data
   or information system, including software, documentation and manuals,
   developed by the prepaid, capitated provider pursuant to the implementation of
   the provider’s Medicaid managed care provider agreement.

   5316.2. Data, information, and reports collected or prepared by the prepaid,
   capitated provider in the course of carrying out its obligations under the
   Medicaid managed care provider agreement shall not be used by the prepaid,
   capitated provider for any purpose not directly related to meeting the terms
   of the Medicaid managed care provider agreement without the prior written
   permission of the Department.

   5316.3. The Department may reproduce, publish and otherwise use and authorize
   others to use, for District, state or federal government purposes, any
   material developed by the prepaid, capitated provider in the course of the
   performance of the Medicaid managed care provider agreement.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 1566, 1586 (March 31, 1995).
