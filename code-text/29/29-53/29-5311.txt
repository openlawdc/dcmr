CONFIDENTIALITY OF INFORMATION &#8211; GENERAL (ยง 29-5311)

5311.1. All information, records, and data collected and maintained by the
   prepaid, capitated provider or its subcontractors that relate to enrollees
   shall be protected from unauthorized disclosure by the prepaid, capitated
   provider.

   5311.2. Except as otherwise provided by federal law or regulation, the use or
   disclosure of information concerning enrollees shall be restricted to purposes
   directly related to the administration of the Medicaid program in accordance
   with 42 C.F.R. ยง431.302.

   5311.3. The information to be safeguarded shall include all information listed
   in 42 C.F.R. ยง431.305.
