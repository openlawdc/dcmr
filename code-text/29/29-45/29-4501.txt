                                  DC REGULATIONS

INTERIM PAYMENTS (ยง 29-4501)

4501.1. Each FQHC shall receive interim payments until completion of the
   audit, as required in section 4500.2(a). The interim payment shall be
   determined in accordance with the methodology in effect on the date preceding
   the effective date of these rules.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 7724 (August 9, 2002).
