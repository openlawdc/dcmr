                                  DC REGULATIONS

EFFECT OF DIRECTOR&#8217;S DETERMINATION (ยง 29-1310)

1310.1. The determination by the Director shall be final and binding on the
   provider submitting a cost report for program reimbursement unless either of
   the following occurs:
   
   
   
   (a)	The Director revises his or her determination on the basis of additional
   documentation; or
   
   
   
   (b)	The provider requests a hearing and a decision is rendered.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 31 DCR 3870 (August 3, 1984).
