                                  DC REGULATIONS

COVERED SERVICES (ยง 29-1505)

1505.1. The demonstration program shall cover the Medicaid benefits outlined
   in the State Plan. Coverage will begin on the first day of the month of
   application. The MAA shall not authorize retroactive coverage for hospital
   services, however, the Administrator may extend retroactive coverage to other
   services.
