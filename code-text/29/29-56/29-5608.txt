                                  DC REGULATIONS

GRIEVANCE SYSTEM &#8211; MANAGED CARE PROVIDER (§ 29-5608)

5608.1. The managed care provider shall have a grievance system with
   reasonable procedures for the prompt resolution of complaints initiated by
   enrolled children and their families.

   5608.2. The managed care provider shall submit its proposed grievance system
   to the Department for written approval prior to implementation.

   5608.3. The grievance system shall include a Grievance Committee for reviewing
   enrolled children’s complaints. One (1) or more enrolled children and/or
   family members shall be members of the Committee.

   5608.4. An enrolled child may pursue a grievance with the assistance of a
   personal representative of his or her choice.

   5608.5. A written record of each grievance review shall be prepared and shall
   be maintained by the managed care provider and shall be made available to the
   Department upon written request.

   5608.6. The managed care provider shall submit to the Department an annual
   report of grievances within ninety (90) days of the close of its fiscal year.
   The annual report shall include the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	The total number of grievances initiated by enrolled children and their
   families;
   
   
   
   (b)	A summary description of the types of grievances initiated; and
   
   
   
   (c)	A summary description of any action taken to resolve grievances.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 43 DCR 4833, 4847 (September 6, 1996).
