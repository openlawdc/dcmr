                                  DC REGULATIONS

MEDICAID PROGRAM AND BENEFICIARIES HELD HARMLESS (§ 29-5617)

5617.1. The managed care provider shall hold harmless the District government,
   the Department and eligible children enrolled in the provider’s plan against
   any loss, damage, expense and liability of any kind that arises from any
   action of the managed care provider or its subcontractors in the performance
   of the Medicaid managed care provider agreement.

   5617.2. Each subcontract shall contain a provision that requires the
   subcontractor to look solely to the managed care provider for payment for
   services rendered.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 43 DCR 4833, 4834 (September 6, 1996).
