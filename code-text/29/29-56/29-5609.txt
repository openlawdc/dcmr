                                  DC REGULATIONS

GRIEVANCE SYSTEM &#8211; THE DEPARTMENT (§ 29-5609)

5609.1. Each enrolled child who is aggrieved by an individual decision of the
   Department affecting that child’s right to select a provider or receive a
   covered service through the D.C. Medicaid Managed Care Program for Disabled
   Children and Youths shall be entitled to a hearing before the Department’s
   Office of Fair Hearings as provided in D.C. Code, 2001 Ed. §4-210.01 to
   4-210.18 and 42 C.F.R. Part 431, Subpart E.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 43 DCR 4833, 4848 (September 6, 1996).
