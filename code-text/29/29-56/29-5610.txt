CONFIDENTIALITY OF INFORMATION &#8211; GENERAL (ยง 29-5610)

5610.1. All information, records, and data collected and maintained by the
   managed care provider or its subcontractors that relate to enrolled children
   shall be protected by the managed care provider from unauthorized disclosure.

   5610.2. Except as otherwise provided by federal law or regulation, the use or
   disclosure of information concerning enrolled children shall be restricted to
   purposes directly related to the administration of the Medicaid program in
   accordance with 42 C.F.R. ยง431.302.

   5610.3. The information to be safeguarded shall include all information listed
   in 42 C.F.R. ยง431.305.
