                                  DC REGULATIONS

SETTLEMENT (ยง 29-168)

168.1. The hearing examiner shall encourage settlement at every stage of the
   proceeding, if settlement is consistent with the provisions and objectives of
   the Act.

   168.2. The parties shall submit any settlement agreement to the hearing
   examiner in writing or orally on the record.

   168.3. If the parties agree to settle the case, the hearing examiner shall
   issue an order setting forth the terms of the settlement and closing the case.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 2034 (April 5, 1991); as amended
   by 54 DCR 6020 (June 22, 2007).
