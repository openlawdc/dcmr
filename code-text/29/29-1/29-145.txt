                                  DC REGULATIONS

RIGHT TO A HEARING (ยง 29-145)

145.1. Each applicant for or client of the Vocational Rehabilitation Services
   Division who is dissatisfied with any determination concerning the furnishing
   or denial of services shall be entitled to a hearing by the Office of
   Administrative Hearings of the Department.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 2034 (April 5, 1991); as amended
   by 54 DCR 6020 (June 22, 2007); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 57
   DCR 4612 (May 28, 2010).
