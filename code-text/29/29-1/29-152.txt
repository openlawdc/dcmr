                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICE OF HEARING AND OF HEARING PROCEDURES AND ISSUES (§ 29-152)

152.1. The Office of Administrative Hearings shall provide a notice of the
   time, place, and issues which are the subject of the hearing to each party and
   the party’s representative at least ten (10) days prior to the hearing by
   certified mail. A hearing may be delayed for good cause shown by a party or at
   the request of both parties.

   152.2. The Office of Administrative Hearings shall provide the applicant or
   client, and his or her designated representative, if any, with a copy of the
   hearing procedures and other relevant information necessary to enable the
   applicant or client to prepare for the hearing.
