                                  DC REGULATIONS

CLOSURE WITHOUT ELIGIBILITY DETERMINATION (ยง 29-109)

109.1. The Rehabilitation Services Administration may not close an
   applicant&#8217;s record of services prior to making an eligibility
   determination unless the applicant declines to participate in, or is
   unavailable to complete, an assessment for determining eligibility and
   priority for services, and the Rehabilitation Services Administration has made
   a reasonable number of attempts to contact the applicant or, if appropriate,
   the applicant&#8217;s representative to encourage the applicant&#8217;s
   participation.

   109.2. The closure of an applicant&#8217;s record of services without an
   eligibility determination shall be based on the written approval of the
   Administrator of the Rehabilitation Services Administration or his or her
   designee.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 27 DCR 4611 (October 17, 1980); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 6189 (August 1, 2003).
