                                  DC REGULATIONS

RIGHT TO APPEAL (ยง 29-160)

160.1. The applicant or client may appeal the final decision of the Director
   by filing a petition for review in the District of Columbia Court of Appeals
   in accordance with the rules and procedures prescribed by the court.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 2034 (April 5, 1991); as amended
   by 54 DCR 6020 (June 22, 2007).
