                                  DC REGULATIONS

RIGHT TO MEDIATION (ยง 29-140)

140.1. Pursuant to the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, as amended, an applicant or
   consumer who is dissatisfied with any determination concerning the furnishing
   or denial of vocational rehabilitation services has the right to pursue
   mediation prior to pursuing an impartial due process hearing.

   140.2. Mediation is voluntary for both parties and therefore, at any time
   during the mediation process, either party or the mediator may elect to
   terminate the mediation.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 2034 (April 5, 1991); as amended
   by 54 DCR 6020 (June 22, 2007); as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 57
   DCR 4612, 4618 (May 28, 2010).
