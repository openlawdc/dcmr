                                  DC REGULATIONS

SUBSIDY (ยง 29-6806)

6806.1. The amount of the subsidy an applicant is eligible to receive shall be
   within five percent (5%) (no less than 95% and no more than 105%) of the
   regular daily rate of the subsidy for a long-term permanent Level 1
   guardianship subsidy pursuant to Title 29 DCMR Chapter 61, less any TANF or
   Supplemental Security Income (SSI) benefits received for the child.

   6806.2. The subsidy shall expire after one (1) year unless the recipient is
   recertified in accordance with ยง 6808.
