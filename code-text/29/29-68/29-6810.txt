                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONFIDENTIALITY (ยง 29-6810)

6810.1. CFSA shall maintain the confidentiality of information and records
   concerning an application for or receipt of a subsidy. Information or records
   may only be released as permitted by applicable provisions of District or
   federal law.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 5837 (July 21, 2006); as amended
   by Final Rulemaking published at 55 DCR 12482 (December 12, 2008).
