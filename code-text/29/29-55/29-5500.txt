                                  DC REGULATIONS

PURPOSE (§ 29-5500)

5500.1. The purpose of this chapter is to establish the standards and
   procedures under which a AFDC and AFDC-related recipients are enrolled in and
   disenrolled from the District of Columbia’s Medicaid Managed Care program.
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is D.C.
   Code, 2001 Ed. * 1-307.02; and Mayor’s Order 93-218.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 1566, 1597 (March 31, 1995).
