                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 29-4399)

4399.1. When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have
   the meanings ascribed:
   
   
   
   Breast cancer &#8211; All primary, recurrent and metastatic cancers of the
   breast, including, but not limited to, infiltrating or in situ.
   
   
   
   Cervical cancer &#8211; All primary, recurrent and metastatic cancers of the
   cervix, including, but not limited to, infiltrating or in situ, as well as
   cervical dysplasia.
   
   
   
   Days &#8211; Calendar days.
   
   
   
   Emergency medical condition &#8211; A medical condition manifesting itself by
   acute symptoms or sufficient severity (including severe pain) such that the
   absence of immediate medical attention could reasonably be expected to result
   in placing the patient&#8217;s health in serious jeopardy, serious impairment
   of bodily functions or serious dysfunction of any bodily part.
   
   
   
   Project WISH &#8211; Women Into Staying Healthy, the screening component of
   the District of Columbia Breast and Cervical Cancer Early Detection Program.
   
   
   
   Provider &#8211; Any health care professional or entity providing services
   pursuant to this Chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 997 (January 31, 2003).
