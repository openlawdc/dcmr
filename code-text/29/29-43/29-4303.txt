NEED OF TREATMENT (ยง 29-4303)

4303.1. A women is determined to need treatment for breast or cervical cancer,
   if in the opinion of the women&#8217;s treating health care professional, the
   diagnostic evaluation following the clinical screening, indicates that the
   patient is in need of treatment. Women who are determined by the health care
   professional to require routine monitoring services for precancerous breast or
   cervical conditions or diseases, are not considered to need treatment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 997 (January 31, 2003).
