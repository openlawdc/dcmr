SERVICES AND SUPPORTS[COLON] STANDARDS FOR CORE REQUIREMENTS &#8212; THERAPEUTIC
ASSISTANT SERVICES FOR RESIDENTIAL FACILITIES (ยง 29-2347)

2347.1. Therapeutic assistant services, provided in a residential setting,
   shall include the following activities:
   
   
   
   (a)	Training in activities of daily living;
   
   
   
   (b)	Instruction and supervision of therapeutic recreation activities; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Protective supervision during evening, overnight, and weekend hours for
   patients who need the protection and structure of staff twenty-four (24) hours
   a day.

   2347.2. A therapeutic assistant is required to have a high school degree or
   GED, and at least twenty (20) hours of in-service training per year regarding
   rehabilitation issues for substance abuse.

   2347.3. A therapeutic assistant shall at a minimum be supervised by a Level II
   certified addictions counselor.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 9341 (November 24, 2000),
   adopting Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 7708, 7774
   (September 22, 2000).
