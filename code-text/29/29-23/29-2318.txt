                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES[COLON] MANAGEMENT AND ADMINISTRATION &#8212;
ACCESSIBILITY STANDARDS (ยง 29-2318)

2318.1. A substance abuse treatment facility or program shall provide or
   arrange for access to services free from all barriers. Specifically:
   
   
   
   (a)	The program shall provide equal opportunity to qualified handicapped
   individuals; and
   
   
   
   (b)	No barriers (architectural, communication, procedural and the like) to the
   delivery of services shall exist.

   2318.2. A substance abuse treatment facility or program shall establish
   policies and procedures that support accessibility to all qualified
   individuals. Established policies and procedures shall stipulate that
   individuals infected with the human immunodeficiency virus or acquired
   immunodeficiency syndrome have equal access to services.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 9341 (November 24, 2000),
   adopting Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 7708, 7725
   (September 22, 2000).
