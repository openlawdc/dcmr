                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICE REQUIREMENTS &#8212; RESUMING OPERATIONS (ยง 29-2312)

2312.1. If a facility or program discontinues a service provision, the
   facility or program shall apply for and obtain a new certification from the
   Department before resuming operations.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 9341 (November 24, 2000),
   adopting Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 7708, 7719
   (September 22, 2000).
