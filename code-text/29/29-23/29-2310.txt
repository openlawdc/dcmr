                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICE REQUIREMENTS &#8212; OPERATIONAL CHANGES (§ 29-2310)

2310.1. A substance abuse treatment program or facility shall notify the
   Department in writing thirty (30) days prior to implementing any of the
   following operational changes, including all aspects of the operations
   materially affected by the changes:
   
   
   
   (a)	A change in the program’s or facility’s geographic location;
   
   
   
   (b)	A change in the settings where services are performed (e.g. outpatient
   clinic to home-based);
   
   
   
   (c)	A planned sale, lease, or change in ownership;
   
   
   
   (d)	The proposed addition or deletion of major service components;
   
   
   
   (e)	A change in required staff qualifications;
   
   
   
   (f)	A proposed change in organizational structure;
   
   
   
   (g)	A change in the population served;
   
   
   
   (h)	A change in bed capacity, and;
   
   
   
   (i)	A change in program capacity.

   2310.2. Written notice of any change in the ownership of a facility or program
   owned by an individual, partnership, or association, or in the legal or
   beneficial ownership of 10% or more of the stock of a corporation that owns or
   operates a facility or program, shall be given to the Department at least
   thirty (30) calendar days prior to the change in ownership.

   2310.3. The Department, upon notification, may at its discretion require
   reinspection to ensure that the facility or program will remain in compliance
   with the provisions of the Act, this chapter, and all other applicable
   provisions of law.

   2310.4. A substance abuse treatment program or facility shall provide other
   notifications as required by applicable District and federal laws and
   regulations.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 9341 (November 24, 2000),
   adopting Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 7708, 7718
   (September 22, 2000).
