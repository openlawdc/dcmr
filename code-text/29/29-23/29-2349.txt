SERVICES AND SUPPORTS[COLON] STANDARDS FOR CORE REQUIREMENTS &#8212; GENERAL
NURSING SERVICES (ยง 29-2349)

2349.1. Only licensed registered and/or practical nurses shall provide nursing
   services that include, but are not limited to:
   
   
   
   (a)	Health assessments of patients and children, as appropriate;
   
   
   
   (b)	Health screenings and referrals for examination by a physician;
   
   
   
   (c)	Health education for participants and staff;
   
   
   
   (d)	Collection of health data;
   
   
   
   (e)	Appropriate treatment intervention;
   
   
   
   (f)	Administration of medication;
   
   
   
   (g)	Observation of medication use by individuals and proper documentation;
   
   
   
   (h)	Health care counseling, especially in the areas of high-risk sexual
   behavior and the possibility of HIV positives; and
   
   
   
   (i)	Infection control.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 9341 (November 24, 2000),
   adopting Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 7708, 7775
   (September 22, 2000).
