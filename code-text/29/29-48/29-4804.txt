                                  DC REGULATIONS

CALCULATION OF THE HOSPITAL SPECIFIC COST PER DISCHARGE OF INDIRECT MEDICAL
EDUCATION (ยง 29-4804)

4804.1. The hospital specific cost per discharge of indirect medical education
   shall be calculated as follows:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The cost per discharge adjusted for case mix shall be divided by the indirect
   medical education factor set forth in Subsection 4802.5.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   The amount established pursuant to Subsection 4804.1(a) shall be subtracted
   from the average cost per discharge adjusted for case mix.

   2012).
