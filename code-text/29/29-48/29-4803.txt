                                  DC REGULATIONS

CALCULATION OF THE PEER GROUP AVERAGE COST PER DISCHARGE (ยง 29-4803)

4803.1. The peer group average cost per discharge shall be equal to the
   weighted average of the hospital specific cost per discharge calculated
   pursuant to Section 4802 for each hospital in the peer group.
