                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECIPROCAL INFORMATION (ยง 29-1705)

1705.1. The Department shall transmit a copy of District of Columbia child
   support enforcement laws, and any subsequent changes therein, to the state
   information agency of every other state which has adopted similar laws.

   1705.2. The department shall maintain a register of the names and addresses of
   the courts having jurisdiction of child support enforcement proceedings in
   other states.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 2756, 2758 (April 15, 1988).
