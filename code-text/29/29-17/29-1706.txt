                                  DC REGULATIONS

RETURN OF OVERPAYMENTS (§ 29-1706)

1706.1. The Department shall return to the obligor any overpayment of support
   submitted to the Department within thirty (30) workings days of receipt of
   written notice and sufficient documentation of an alleged overpayment.

   1706.2. The overpayment refund shall be sent by mail to the obligor’s last
   known address.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 2756, 2758 (April 15, 1988).
