                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTIFICATION REGARDING APPLICATION (§ 29-1640)

1640.1. The agency shall notify each applicant of its decision to accept or
   deny his or her application to be a foster parent within thirty (30) days of
   its receipt.

   1640.2. A foster home shall be re-evaluated two (2) years after the date of
   the initial approval and every two (2) years thereafter.

   1640.3. If the initial foster home study was performed by the same
   child-placing agency, the foster home re-evaluation shall include:
   
   
   
   (a)	One visit to the home;
   
   
   
   (b)	Face-to-face interviews with each member of the household;
   
   
   
   (c)	Updated medical reports on all members of the household; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Any changes from the initial foster home study.

   1640.4. If the initial foster home study was performed by a different
   child-placing agency, the child-placing agency’s re-evaluation shall include
   the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Two visits with the foster parent(s), one of which shall be in the home;
   
   
   
   (b)	Face-to-face interviews with each member of the household;
   
   
   
   (c)	Updated medical reports on the members of the household; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Any changes in the family’s data from the initial home study.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3066 (May 11, 1990).
   
   
   
   	EDITOR’S NOTE: This section does not apply to foster homes to which Chapter
   60 of this title applies. See 29 DCMR 6000.3.
