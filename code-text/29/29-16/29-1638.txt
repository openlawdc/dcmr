                                  DC REGULATIONS

FOSTER CARE (§ 29-1638)

1638.1. The child-placing agency shall comply with the District of Columbia
   laws and regulations in its selection of foster family homes in the District
   of Columbia.

   1638.2. Each child-placing agency shall use only licensed foster homes for the
   placement of children.

   1638.3. Each child-placing agency shall comply with the laws governing
   placement of each jurisdiction involved in the placement of a child, and the
   rules and regulations governing foster care of each state in which a child is
   placed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3062 (May 11, 1990).
   
   
   
   	EDITOR’S NOTE: See Chapter 60 of this title for rules of the Child and
   Family Services Agency regarding foster homes.
