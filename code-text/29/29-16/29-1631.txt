                                  DC REGULATIONS

PATERNITY AFFIDAVITS (§ 29-1631)

1631.1. The child-placing agency shall obtain an affidavit which sets out the
   identity of the father from the birth mother when the identity of the birth
   father is not reasonably ascertainable and there is no information to dispute
   the affidavit.

   1631.2. The child-placing agency shall obtain an affidavit which sets out the
   identity of the father from the birth mother if a named birth father’s
   whereabouts are unknown.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3058 (May 11, 1990).
