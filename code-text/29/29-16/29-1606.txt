                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORD KEEPING (ยง 29-1606)

1606.1. Each child-placing agency shall maintain financial records of all
   receipts, disbursements, assets, and liabilities in such a manner as to
   facilitate adequate financial reporting and shall establish an accounting
   system capable of tracking all movements of funds and the actual expenditures
   for each case.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3035 (May 11, 1990).
