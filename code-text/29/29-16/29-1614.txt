                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTRACT PROFESSIONAL SERVICES (§ 29-1614)

1614.1. A child-placing agency may employ a licensed social worker to provide
   social work service on a contractual basis.

   1614.2. A social worker employed on a contractual basis shall not be
   considered a member of the child-placing agency’s social work staff.

   1614.3. A social worker employed on a contractual basis shall comply with the
   qualifications for social workers as set out in §1613 of this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3041 (May 11, 1990).
