                                  DC REGULATIONS

VOLUNTEERS (§ 29-1615)

1615.1. Each child-placing agency using volunteers to work directly with
   children on a regular basis shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Describe the duties for volunteer positions;
   
   
   
   (b)	Require a personal reference for each volunteer and a criminal records and
   child abuse clearance;
   
   
   
   (c)	Not use a volunteer to fill a staff position required by the child-placing
   agency’s service program plan;
   
   
   
   (d)	Have a designated staff member to supervise and evaluate each volunteer;
   
   
   
   (e)	Provide orientation and training for the volunteers in the philosophy of
   the agency, the needs of the children in care, and the needs of their
   families;
   
   
   
   (f)	Maintain records documenting the hours and activities in which volunteers
   work for the agency; and
   
   
   
   (g)	Require that volunteers rendering direct services to children be free of
   communicable and contagious diseases based upon medical reports completed
   within the previous twelve (12) month period.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3041 (May 11, 1990).
