                                  DC REGULATIONS

INTERSTATE PLACEMENT (ยง 29-1635)

1635.1. A child-placing agency in the District of Columbia which is
   cooperating with agencies or individuals in other states for foster or
   adoptive placement of a child in the District or in another jurisdiction shall
   comply with the interstate placement requirements of the states involved
   during all phases of the placement process.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3060 (May 11, 1990).
