                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTIFICATION REGARDING APPLICATION (ยง 29-1621)

1621.1. Each child-placing agency shall notify each applicant in writing
   within thirty (30) days of completion of the home study of the acceptance or
   denial of his or her application to be an adoptive parent.

   1621.2. When an application is denied, the child-placing agency shall inform
   the applicant of the reasons why a child cannot be placed in his or her home
   during a face-to-face interview. The child-placing agency shall offer
   counseling services to the applicant(s) to assist him or her in adjusting to
   the denial.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3049 (May 11, 1990).
