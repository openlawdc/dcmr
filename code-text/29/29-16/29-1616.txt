                                  DC REGULATIONS

STUDENT FIELD PLACEMENTS[SLASH]INTERNSHIPS (ยง 29-1616)

1616.1. Each child-placing agency which accepts students as interns for field
   placements shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Develop a written plan describing their tasks and functions;
   
   
   
   (b)	Provide a copy of the plan to each student and his or her school;
   
   
   
   (c)	Require personal references and a criminal records and child abuse
   clearance;
   
   
   
   (d)	Designate a staff member to supervise and evaluate the students; and
   
   
   
   (e)	Develop a plan for orientation and training of students in the philosophy
   of the agency, needs of the children in care, and of their families.

   1616.2. A student shall not assume the responsibilities of any paid staff
   member.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3041 (May 11, 1990).
