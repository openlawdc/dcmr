                                  DC REGULATIONS

THE RESPONSIBILITY OF THE CHILD-PLACING AGENCY IN FOSTER CARE PLACEMENTS (§
29-1643)

1643.1. Each child-placing agency’s authority to place a child in foster
   care shall be documented in the organization’s policies and procedures.

   1643.2. After a child is placed in foster care, each child-placing agency
   shall provide casework services to the child, his birth family, and the foster
   parents, as appropriate.

   1643.3. Each child-placing agency shall ensure that the foster care services
   plan includes:
   
   
   
   (a)	An estimation of the length of time the child is expected to be in foster
   care; and
   
   
   
   (b)	A plan for permanency placement for the child which shall include any
   modifications needed in that plan.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3068 (May 11, 1990).
