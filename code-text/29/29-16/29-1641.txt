                                  DC REGULATIONS

FOSTER PARENT TRAINING (§ 29-1641)

1641.1. The child-placing agency shall provide orientation and pre service
   training to the foster care system to prospective foster parents.

   1641.2. The child-placing agency shall provide training annually to foster
   parents to improve their parenting skills.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3066 (May 11, 1990).
   
   
   
   	EDITOR’S NOTE: This section does not apply to foster homes to which Chapter
   60 of this title applies. See 29 DCMR 6000.3.
