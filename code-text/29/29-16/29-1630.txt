                                  DC REGULATIONS

SEVERANCE OF PARENTAL RIGHTS (ยง 29-1630)

1630.1. Each child-placing agency shall comply with the procedures set forth
   in D.C. Code, 2001 Ed. ยง4-1406, the statutory provision on voluntary
   relinquishment.

   1630.2. When the birth parent does not speak English, or has impaired
   communication skills, a translator or interpreter shall be present prior to
   and during the period the document is being signed.  When the birth mother
   cannot read, she may be accompanied by someone who can read the document for
   her.

   1630.3. When a birth parent consents to relinquishment of parental rights, the
   child-placing agency shall accept the relinquishment and make efforts to
   inform the other birth parent of the relinquishment. The notice to the
   non-participating birth parent of the consent to relinquishment shall be sent
   to him or her at the last know address and documented in the record.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 3033, 3058 (May 11, 1990).
