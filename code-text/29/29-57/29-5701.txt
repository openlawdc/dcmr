                                  DC REGULATIONS

ENROLLMENT (§ 29-5701)

5701.1. The managed care provider shall accept and enroll each eligible child
   who chooses or is assigned to the managed care provider’s plan.

   5701.2. The following categories of children are not eligible to participate
   in the demonstration:
   
   
   
   (a)	Children eligible for a period that is retroactive;
   
   
   
   (b)	Children with restricted eligibility; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Children with dual eligibility for Medicare and Medicaid.

   5701.3. Except as provided in §5701.4, enrollment by an eligible child in the
   managed care provider’s plan shall be voluntary.

   5701.4. Eligible children who do not choose to remain in the Medicaid
   fee-for-service program within ten (10) days of being certified or recertified
   as eligible for Medicaid shall be assigned to the managed care provider using
   an automated, random assignment process.

   5701.5. By the fifteenth (15th) day of the month, the Department shall send a
   notification letter to the family or representative of each eligible child
   selected by the Department that month, advising the family or representative
   of their option to either:
   
   
   
   (a)	Enroll the child in the demonstration; or
   
   
   
   (b)	Choose to keep the child in the current Medicaid fee-for-service program.

   5701.6. The letter described will inform each family or representative that in
   the event the family or representative does not exercise the right to choose,
   the child will be assigned to the demonstration.

   5701.7. In the event that a family or representative of an eligible child does
   not exercise the right to choose within forty-five (45) days of the date of
   the notification letter, the child will be assigned to the demonstration. If a
   child has been autoassigned to the demonstration, the family or representative
   of the child may choose to keep the child in the current Medicaid
   fee-for-service program, for any reason, during the first forty-five (45) days
   of membership following autoassignment.

   5701.8. A family or representative who initially chooses to keep the child in
   the current Medicaid fee-for-service program will be allowed to enroll the
   child in the demonstration at any time.

   5701.9. The effective date of enrollment for children who are voluntarily
   enrolled or are autoassigned to the demonstration will be the first (1st) day
   of the second (2nd) month following the Department’s notification letter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 43 DCR 4833, 4855 (September 6, 1996).
   
   
   
   	EDITOR’S NOTE: This section as adopted included two subsections numbered
   ‘5701.6’. In this edition, the second §5701.6 has been renumbered
   §5701.7. Concurrently, original §5701.7 and 5701.8 have been renumbered
   §5701.8 and 5701.9, respectively.
