                                  DC REGULATIONS

PURPOSE (§ 29-5700)

5700.1. The purpose of this chapter is to establish the standards and
   procedures under which eligible children are enrolled in and disenrolled from
   the District of Columbia’s Medicaid Managed Care Program for Disabled
   Children and Youths.
