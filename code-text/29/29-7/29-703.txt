                                  DC REGULATIONS

ACTIVITIES COORDINATOR (ยง 29-703)

703.1. An Activities Coordinator shall have a minimum of one (1) or more years
   of experience working in an adult or elderly social or recreational program.

   703.2. An Activities Coordinator shall have the ability to develop and
   implement therapeutic activity programming both for specific individuals and
   for groups.

   703.3. For the developmentally disabled or for those with maladaptive
   behavior, the Activities Coordinator shall have had one (1) year of experience
   working in the specialty services being provided in the day care treatment
   program.

   703.4. The Activities Coordinator shall implement, in concert with plan of
   care goals and with other day treatment program staff, activity programs to
   meet the individual needs of each participant.

   703.5. The Activities Coordinator shall not serve as the Program Director.

   703.6. The Activities Coordinator shall also be responsible for the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Developing and scheduling educational events;
   
   
   
   (b)	Supervising activity program assistants; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Participating in the monthly review of each participant&#8217;s care plan.

   703.7. The Activities Coordinator shall enter in each participant&#8217;s
   record, at least monthly and more often if indicated, notations regarding the
   participant&#8217;s involvement and progress in activities as a part of his or
   her care plan.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 31 DCR 326 (January 27, 1984).
