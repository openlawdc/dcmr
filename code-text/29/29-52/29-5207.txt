                                  DC REGULATIONS

DAY SERVICES (ยง 29-5207)

5207.1. Day services shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Consist of a one day billable unit of service, which includes at least
   three (3) hours of client participation;
   
   
   
   (b)	Require pre-authorization from DMH for more than ninety (90) days of
   services within a twelve (12) month period;
   
   
   
   (c)	Be provided in a DMH-certified community mental health rehabilitation
   services agency; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Meet all other requirements set forth in 22 DCMR ยง 3420.

   5207.2. Day services shall not be billed during a community support or
   counseling encounter.

   5207.3. Day services shall not be billed on the same day as Assertive
   Community Treatment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 4860 (May 24, 2002).
