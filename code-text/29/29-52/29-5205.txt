COMMUNITY SUPPORT (INDIVIDUAL AND GROUP) (ยง 29-5205)

5205.1. Community support services shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Be provided in fifteen (15) minute units;
   
   
   
   (b)	Be provided in individual or group interventions;
   
   
   
   (c)	Not be subject to any annual limits;
   
   
   
   (d)	Be provided in a DMH-certified community mental health rehabilitation
   services agency, the client&#8217;s home or other community setting or a
   residential facility consisting of sixteen (16) beds or less; and
   
   
   
   (e)	Meet all other requirements set forth in 22 DCMR ยง 3418.

   5205.2. Community support shall not be billed on the same day as assertive
   community treatment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 4860 (May 24, 2002).
