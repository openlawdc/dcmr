MEDICATION[SLASH]SOMATIC TREATMENT (INDIVIDUAL AND GROUP) (ยง 29-5203)

5203.1. Medication/Somatic treatment services shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Be provided in fifteen (15) minute units;
   
   
   
   (b)	Not be subject to annual service limits;
   
   
   
   (c)	Be provided as either an individual or group intervention;
   
   
   
   (d)	Be provided in a DMH-certified community mental health rehabilitation
   services agency, the client&#8217;s home or other community setting or a
   residential facility consisting of sixteen (16) beds or less; and
   
   
   
   (e)	Meet all other requirements set forth in 22 DCMR ยง 3416.

   5203.2. Medication/Somatic treatment services shall not be billed on the same
   day as assertive community treatment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 4860 (May 24, 2002).
