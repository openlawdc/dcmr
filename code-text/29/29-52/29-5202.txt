DIAGNOSTIC[SLASH]ASSESSMENT (ยง 29-5202)

5202.1. Diagnostic/assessment services shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Consist of an assessment, which is at least three (3) hours in duration;
   
   
   
   (b)	Be rendered once every six months, unless an additional billable unit of
   service has been authorized by DMH;
   
   
   
   (c)	Be provided in a DMH-certified community mental health rehabilitation
   services agency, the client&#8217;s home or other community setting or a
   residential facility consisting of sixteen (16) beds or less; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Meet all other requirements set forth in 22 DCMR ยง 3415.

   5202.2. Diagnostic/assessment services shall not be billed on the same day as
   assertive community treatment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 4860 (May 24, 2002).
