                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 29-5299)

5299.1. When used in this chapter, the following words shall have the meanings
   ascribed:
   
   
   
   Assertive Community Treatment or &#8220;ACT&#8221; &#8211; Intensive,
   integrated rehabilitative, crisis, treatment and mental health rehabilitative
   community support provided by an interdisciplinary team to children and youth
   with serious emotional disturbance and to adults with serious and persistent
   mental illness.
   
   
   
   Client &#8211; A person eligible to receive services pursuant to this Chapter.
   
   
   
   Community-Based Intervention &#8211; Time-limited, intensive, mental health
   intervention services delivered to children, youth or adults and intended to
   prevent utilization of an out-of-home therapeutic resource by the client.
   
   
   
   Community Support &#8211; Rehabilitation supports considered essential to
   assist a client in achieving rehabilitation and recovery goals.
   
   
   
   Counseling &#8211; Individual, group, or family face-to-face services for
   symptom and behavior management, development, restoration, or enhancement of
   adaptive behaviors and skills, and enhancement or maintenance of daily living
   skills.
   
   
   
   Crisis/Emergency &#8211; Face-to-face or telephone immediate response to an
   emergency situation experienced by a client.
   
   
   
   Day Services &#8211; A structured, clinical program intended to develop skills
   and foster social role integration through a range of social,
   psychoeducational, behavioral and cognitive mental health interventions.
   
   
   
   Diagnostic/Assessment &#8211; An intensive clinical and functional evaluation
   of a client&#8217;s mental health condition that results in the issuance of a
   report with recommendations for service delivery that provides the basis for
   the development of the individualized plan of care or individualized recovery
   plan.
   
   
   
   DMH &#8211; District of Columbia, Department of Mental Health.
   
   
   
   Individualized Plan of Care &#8211; Shall have the same meaning as set forth
   in Chapter 34 of 22 DCMR.
   
   
   
   Individualized Recovery Plan &#8211; Shall have the same meaning as set forth
   in Chapter 34 of 22 DCMR.
   
   
   
   Intensive Day Treatment &#8211; A structured, intensive and coordinated acute
   treatment program which serves as an alternative to acute inpatient treatment
   or as a step-down service from inpatient care.
   
   
   
   Medical Assistance Administration (MAA) &#8211; The single state agency,
   within the District of Columbia Department of Health responsible for
   administering the District of Columbia Medicaid Program.
   
   
   
   Medication &#8211; Somatic Treatment- Medical interventions including:
   physical examinations; prescription, supervision or administration of mental
   health-related medications; monitoring and interpreting results of laboratory
   diagnostic procedures related to mental health-related medications; and
   medical interventions needed for effective mental health treatment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 4860 (May 24, 2002).
