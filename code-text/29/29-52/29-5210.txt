ASSERTIVE COMMUNITY TREATMENT (ยง 29-5210)

5210.1. Assertive Community Treatment services shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Be provided in fifteen (15) minute units;
   
   
   
   (b)	Require prior authorization from DMH;
   
   
   
   (c)	Consist of a face-to face encounter; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Meet all other requirements set forth in 22 DCMR ยง 3423.

   5210.2. Assertive community treatment shall not be billed on the same day as
   any other MHRS, except for crisis/emergency which requires retrospective
   authorization by DMH.

   5210.3. Assertive community treatment shall be provided in a DMH-certified
   community mental health rehabilitation services agency, the client&#8217;s
   home or other community setting.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 4860 (May 24, 2002).
