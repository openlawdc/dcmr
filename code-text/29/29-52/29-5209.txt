COMMUNITY-BASED INTERVENTION (ยง 29-5209)

5209.1. Community-Based Intervention services shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Consist of a face-to-face encounter;
   
   
   
   (b)	Require prior authorization from DMH;
   
   
   
   (c)	Be provided in fifteen (15) minute units; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Meet all other requirements set forth in 22 DCMR ยง 3422.

   5209.2. Community-Based Intervention shall not be billed on the same day as
   assertive community treatment, counseling or intensive day treatment.

   5209.3. Community Based Intervention shall be provided in a DMH-certified
   community mental health rehabilitation services agency, the client&#8217;s
   home or other community setting.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 4860 (May 24, 2002).
