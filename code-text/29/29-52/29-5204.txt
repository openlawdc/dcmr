COUNSELING (INDIVIDUAL ON-SITE, INDIVIDUAL OFF-SITE AND GROUP) (ยง 29-5204)

5204.1. Counseling services shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Consist of a face-to-face encounter;
   
   
   
   (b)	Be provided in fifteen (15) minute units;
   
   
   
   (c)	Be provided on-site or off-site, or in individual or group interventions;
   
   
   
   (d)	Require pre-authorization from DMH for additional units of service after a
   client has received 160 units of service;
   
   
   
   (e)	Be provided in a DMH-certified community mental health rehabilitation
   services agency, the client&#8217;s home or other community setting or a
   residential facility consisting of sixteen (16) beds or less; and
   
   
   
   (f)	Meet all other requirements set forth in 22 DCMR ยง 3417.

   5204.2. Counseling services shall not be billed on the same day as a day
   services encounter.

   5204.3. Counseling services shall not be billed on the same day as intensive
   day treatment, community-based intervention or assertive community treatment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 4860 (May 24, 2002).
