                                  DC REGULATIONS

ASSESSMENT AND REFERRAL FOR INDIVIDUAL ADULTS IN TEMPORARY SHELTER (ยง 29-2506)

2506.1. Individuals receiving temporary shelter services for individual adults
   shall be provided assessment and case management services with an
   appropriately trained, qualified, and supervised case manager.

   2506.2. Individuals residing in temporary shelters shall participate in the
   assessment and case management services provided.
