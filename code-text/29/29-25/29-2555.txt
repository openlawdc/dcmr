                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADMINISTRATIVE REVIEW RECORD (ยง 29-2555)

2555.1. The Department shall maintain a record for each administrative review
   offered or held.  Each administrative review record shall include:
   
   
   
   (a)	Documentation of the request for a fair hearing;
   
   
   
   		 (b)	Documentation of the notice of the administrative review;
   
   
   
   		 (c)	Evidence considered at the administrative review, if held;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   All status reports issued to the Office of Administrative Hearings; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   All administrative review decisions issued.

   19, 2011).
