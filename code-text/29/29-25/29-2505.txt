                                  DC REGULATIONS

INTAKE FOR TEMPORARY SHELTER FOR INDIVIDUAL ADULTS (ยง 29-2505)

Intake for temporary shelter for individual adults shall consist of an
eligibility determination and placement in a shelter.







 	An individual adult applicant shall apply directly to and be determined
eligible for temporary shelter by the Provider from whom the individual is
seeking services.
