                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADMINISTRATIVE REVIEW PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY (§ 29-2551)

2551.1. The purpose of an administrative review is to determine, in a timely
   manner, whether the service Provider&#8217;s or agency’s position is legally
   valid and, if possible, to achieve an informal resolution of the appeal.
   
   
   
   2551.2 		An administrative review shall be granted to any client or client
   representative who wishes to appeal a decision or action subject to review
   under subsection 2550.1 or subsection 2550.2 and who requests a fair hearing,
   orally or in writing, within ninety (90) days of receiving written notice of
   the adverse action or within ninety (90) days of an alleged violation.
