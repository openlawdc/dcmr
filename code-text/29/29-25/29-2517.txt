                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADDITIONAL LOW BARRIER SHELTER REQUIREMENTS (ยง 29-2517)

Providers of low barrier shelter shall meet the requirements of sections 2515
and 2516 as well as the additional requirements set forth in this section.







Providers of low barrier shelter shall ensure that all clients are offered case
management services as set forth in section 2504.







Providers of low barrier shelter shall provide clean, hot shower facilities.







Providers of low barrier shelter shall make available personal hygiene supplies,
including bath size towels, washcloth, soap, shampoo, deodorant, toothpaste, and
toothbrushes.
