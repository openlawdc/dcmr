                                  DC REGULATIONS

SCATTERED-SITES TRANSITIONAL HOUSING INITIATIVE – PURPOSE AND SCOPE (§
29-2525)

2525.1. The purpose of the Scattered-Sites Transitional Housing Initiative
   Program (STI Program) is to provide a structured program of supportive
   services along with rental assistance for less than or equal to two (2) years
   to prepare families for appropriate long-term permanent housing.

   2525.2. The STI Program shall consist of unit identification assistance,
   time-limited rental subsidy, tenant rent contribution, assessment, and case
   management, as set forth in these subsections.

   2525.3. Unless provided otherwise in sections 2525 through 2534, the STI
   Program shall be administered in accordance with the provisions of the shelter
   and supportive housing regulations found elsewhere in chapter 25 of title 29.

   2525.4. The STI Program shall be subject to annual appropriations and the
   availability of funds.

   2525.5. Nothing in these rules shall be construed to create an entitlement
   either direct or implied on the part of any individual or family to the STI
   Program.
