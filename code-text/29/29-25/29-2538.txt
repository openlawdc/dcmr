                                  DC REGULATIONS

PSH PROGRAM – ASSESSMENT AND CASE MANAGEMENT (§ 29-2538)

2538.1. The PSH Program shall offer participants a comprehensive needs
   assessment and case management.

   2538.2. Participants shall not be required to participate in the needs
   assessment or case management as a condition of receipt of the housing
   assistance or other PSH services, except that the PSH Program may require
   reasonable minimal case management requirements as set forth in the PSH
   Program Rules, in order to ensure the PSH Program provides reasonable
   oversight of its program and participants.
