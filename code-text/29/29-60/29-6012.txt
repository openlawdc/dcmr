                                  DC REGULATIONS

EXTERIOR ENVIRONMENT (ยง 29-6012)

6012.1. The exterior premises of a foster home shall be free from all safety
   hazards and from any objects or materials that constitute a danger to a foster
   child.

   6012.2. A foster home that has a swimming pool shall meet the
   jurisdiction&#8217;s requirements for the pool.

   6012.3. A foster parent shall make swimming and wading pools inaccessible to
   foster children except when supervised by an adult who can swim.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 6617 (July 27, 2001).
