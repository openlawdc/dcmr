                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEAL PROCEDURE (ยง 29-6032)

6032.1. A person aggrieved by a decision of CFSA to deny, suspend or revoke a
   license or the renewal of a license may appeal the decision in accordance with
   29 DCMR Chapter 59.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking publish at 48 DCR 6617 (July 27, 2001).
