MAINTAINING FOSTER CHILDREN&#8217;S RECORDS (ยง 29-6013)

6013.1. A foster parent shall maintain records on each foster child placed in
   the home. All information regarding a foster child shall be kept confidential
   and released only in accordance with ยง 6023.

   6013.2. A foster child&#8217;s records shall include the following
   information:
   
   
   
   (a)	The foster child&#8217;s name, birth date, and Medical Assistance number;
   
   
   
   (b)	The name, address, and telephone number(s) of each person to contact in
   case of emergency and each person authorized to give medical consent;
   
   
   
   (c)	A record of the foster child&#8217;s medical and dental appointments,
   illness and health problems, prescribed medications, and hospitalizations;
   
   
   
   (d)	Reports of the foster child&#8217;s medical tests and any information or
   counseling received regarding chronic and handicapping conditions;
   
   
   
   (e)	A copy of any service agreement for the foster child;
   
   
   
   (f)	Dates of future Court or administrative reviews, any medical or other
   appointments, and planned activities;
   
   
   
   (g)	The dates that the foster child arrived at and departed from the foster
   home;
   
   
   
   (h)	A listing of the activities in the foster child&#8217;s case plan for
   which the foster parent is responsible;
   
   
   
   (i)	School reports; and
   
   
   
   (j)	A record of the foster child&#8217;s memberships, activities, and
   participation in extracurricular activities.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 6617 (July 27, 2001).
