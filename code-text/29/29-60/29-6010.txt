                                  DC REGULATIONS

FIRE SAFETY (ยง 29-6010)

6010.1. A foster home shall meet the fire safety requirements of the
   jurisdiction in which it is located.

   6010.2. A foster home shall be free from all fire hazards, including faulty
   electric cords, unsafe appliances, and unsafe fireplaces and chimneys.

   6010.3. A foster home shall have working smoke detectors that meet the
   jurisdiction&#8217;s requirements on each floor and in hallways within ten
   (10) feet of each bedroom.

   6010.4. A foster home shall have a portable dry chemical fire extinguisher on
   every floor of the home.

   6010.5. A foster parent shall develop a fire evacuation plan and share it with
   all household members who are capable of understanding.

   6010.6. A foster parent shall store combustible items away from sources of
   heat.

   6010.7. A foster home shall have two (2) means of egress that provide
   unrestricted exit in case of fire.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 6617 (July 27, 2001).
