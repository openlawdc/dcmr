CONFIDENTIALITY (ยง 29-6023)

6023.1. A foster parent shall maintain the confidentiality of all information
   about a foster child and the foster child&#8217;s family. A foster parent may
   transmit information about a foster child or the foster child&#8217;s family
   only in accordance with District law:
   
   
   
   (a)	To CFSA and its representatives;
   
   
   
   (b)	To the agency and its representatives;
   
   
   
   (c)	The foster child&#8217;s parent pursuant to ยง 6002.2(c);
   
   
   
   (d)	To the foster child&#8217;s attorney;
   
   
   
   (e)	To the foster child&#8217;s guardian ad litem;
   
   
   
   (f)	To the parent&#8217;s attorney;
   
   
   
   (g)	As authorized by CFSA or the agency in order to provide services to the
   foster child and in accordance with ยง 6023;
   
   
   
   (h)	As necessary for the provision of medical, mental health, or educational
   services; and
   
   
   
   (i)	In accordance with a court order.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 6617 (July 27, 2001).
