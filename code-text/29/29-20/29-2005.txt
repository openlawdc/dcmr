                                  DC REGULATIONS

PAYMENT FOR DRUGS (ยง 29-2005)

2005.1. The program shall make full payment for an eligible drug for uninsured
   persons and full payment up to the amount of an insurance co-payment required
   of persons who are insured.

   2005.2. No person shall be entitled to any payment under this program if no
   appropriated funds are available for that purpose.

   2005.3. Prescriptions for an eligible drug may be filled by any pharmacy
   currently enrolled as a provider in the District of Columbia Medicaid Program.

   2005.4. A pharmacy filling a prescription for an eligible drug for an
   individual covered under this program shall submit the claim for payment to
   the Office of Health Care Financing, using the same form and following the
   same procedures as if the claim were being filed for drugs furnished to a
   District of Columbia Medicaid recipient.

   2005.5. The Administration for HIV Policy and Programs shall seek
   reimbursement from the patient&#8217;s insurance carrier or other third-party
   sources providing reimbursement of any amount chargeable to the carrier or
   third-party payor.

   2005.6. A pharmacy filling a prescription for an eligible drug for an
   individual covered under this program shall be paid the actual wholesale price
   of the drug, as determined by the Office of Health Care Financing, plus a
   dispensing fee of four dollars and twenty-five cents ($4.25) for each
   prescription filled.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 4935, 4938 (July 14, 1989); as
   Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 2360 (March 16, 2007).
