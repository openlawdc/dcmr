                                  DC REGULATIONS

DURATION OF BENEFITS (§ 29-2024)

2024.1. The Department shall continue to pay health insurance premiums until
   one (1) of the following occurs:
   
   
   
   (a)	The recipient becomes eligible for Medicare benefits;
   
   
   
   (b)	The recipient is re-employed and can obtain health benefits under the new
   employer’s group plan;
   
   
   
   (c)	Thirty (30) months from the month the employee is unable to continue
   working; or
   
   
   
   (d)	Funding identified for the program pursuant to§2020.2 is exhausted.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 4360,4362 (July 12, 1991).
