CONFIDENTIALITY OF PATIENT INFORMATION (ยง 29-2001)

2001.1. Information about applicants and recipients of assistance under this
   program shall be kept confidential. The information shall be used solely for
   the purpose of administering the program and shall not be disclosed without
   the prior written consent of the person to whom the information pertains,
   except to Department personnel who have a need to know for administrative
   purposes. The information shall be safeguarded according to the procedures
   used by the District of Columbia Medicaid Program and the criteria set forth
   in 42 CFR ยง 431.300 et seq.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 4935 (July 14, 1989); as Final
   Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 2360 (March 16, 2007).
