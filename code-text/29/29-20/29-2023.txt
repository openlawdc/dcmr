                                  DC REGULATIONS

PAYMENT OF PREMIUMS (§ 29-2023)

2023.1. The Department of Human Services shall either pay the recipient’s
   monthly premium directly to the insurer, or reimburse the recipient for the
   monthly premium if the recipient pays it.

   2023.2. The Department shall not pay or reimburse the recipient for any
   co-payments or deductibles.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 4360, 4362 (July 12, 1991).
