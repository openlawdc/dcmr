                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 29-4900)

4900.1. The purpose of this chapter is to establish standards governing
   Medicaid reimbursement for rehabilitative (&#8220;rehab&#8221;) services
   provided by the District of Columbia Child and Family Services Agency (CFSA)
   and its subcontractors (&#8220;Provider&#8221;).

   4900.2. CFSA shall enter into a provider agreement with the Department of
   Health, Medical Assistance Administration (MAA) for the provision of rehab
   services.

   4900.3. The provider agreement shall specify the services to be provided,
   methods of operation, financial and legal requirements, and identification of
   the population to be served.

   2002).
