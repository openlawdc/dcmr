ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS (ยง 29-4901)

4901.1. Persons eligible to receive rehab services shall be Medicaid
   recipients who meet the following qualifications:
   
   
   
   (a)	Are twenty (20) years of age or less;
   
   
   
   (b)	Are determined to be abused or neglected pursuant to the Prevention of
   Child Abuse and Neglect Act of 1997, effective September 23, 1997 (D.C. Law
   2-22; D.C. Official Code ยง 4-1301.1 et seq); and
   
   
   
   (c)	Are enrolled in a comprehensive health care network with the capability to
   assure the timely provision of appropriate Early and Periodic Screening,
   Diagnosis, and Treatment (EPSDT).

   4901.2. MAA may request additional information to determine whether the
   services provided are medical necessity.
