                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORDS AND CONFIDENTIALITY OF INFORMATION (ยง 29-1909)

1909.1. Each client shall be entitled to a fair hearing in accordance with 42
   CFR 431 and D.C. Official Code ยง 4-210.01 if the government:
   
   
   
   (a)	Fails to offer the client a choice of either institutional care in an
   ICF/MR or home and community-based waiver services;
   
   
   
   (b)	Denies a waiver service requested by the client;
   
   
   
   (c)	Terminates, suspends or reduces a waiver service;
   
   
   
   (d)	Fails to give a client the provider of his or her choice; or
   
   
   
   (e)	Terminates, suspends or reduces Medicaid eligibility.

   1909.2. The Department of Human Services shall be responsible for issuing each
   legally required notice to the client or client&#8217;s representative
   regarding the right to request a hearing as required in subsection 1909.1.

   1909.3. The content of the notice issued pursuant to subsections 1909.1 and
   1909.2 shall comply with the requirements set forth in 42 CFR. 431.210 and
   D.C. Official Code ยง 4-205.55.
