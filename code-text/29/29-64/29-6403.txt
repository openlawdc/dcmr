                                  DC REGULATIONS

INCOME REQUIREMENTS (ยง 29-6403)

6403.1. Each applicant shall provide documentation of income that shall
   include the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	For a gross income determination, each applicant shall provide evidence of
   any of the following documentation that is applicable:
   
   
   
   (1)	Social Security cash benefit verification;
   
   
   
   (2)	Unemployment compensation;
   
   
   
   (3)	Veteran&#8217;s benefits;
   
   
   
   (4)	Pension check stub;
   
   
   
   (5)	Any other public assistance documentation; or
   
   
   
   (6)	Any other award letter for receipt of cash benefits.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Emergency Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 5605 (May 28, 2004)
   [EXPIRED]; as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 7435 (July 30,
   2004).
