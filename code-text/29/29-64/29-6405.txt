                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECIPIENT PROVIDER ASSISTANCE (ยง 29-6405)

6405.1. A Demonstration Project recipient shall have the freedom to choose his
   or her Medicaid providers.

   6405.2. The HIV/AIDS Administration shall provide, as necessary, to all waiver
   recipients a list of the following at the time of initial enrollment:
   
   
   
   (a)	List of Ryan White case management providers; or
   
   
   
   (b)	List of Ryan White HIV-experienced physician and clinic providers and
   Board-certified infectious disease specialists.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Emergency Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 5605 (May 28, 2004)
   [EXPIRED]; as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 7435 (July 30,
   2004).
