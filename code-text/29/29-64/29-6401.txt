                                  DC REGULATIONS

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS (ยง 29-6401)

6401.1. Each individual eligible to receive services shall meet all of the
   following requirements:
   
   
   
   (a)	Is a District of Columbia resident:
   
   
   
   (b)	Has a gross income at or below one hundred percent (100%) of the Federal
   Poverty Level (FPL);
   
   
   
   (c)	Has resource limits of $2,600 for an individual and $3,000 for an
   individual and his/her spouse;
   
   
   
   (d)	Owns no property other than the home in which he or she lives and a car;
   
   
   
   (e)	Is not eligible for the traditional Medicaid program under Titles XIX and
   XXI of the Social Security Act;
   
   
   
   (f)	Is HIV-infected;
   
   
   
   (g)	Does not reside in a long-term care facility, mental health facility, or
   penal institution; and
   
   
   
   (h)	Has completed an informed consent form at the time of application.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Emergency Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 5605 (May 28, 2004)
   [EXPIRED]; as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 7435 (July 30,
   2004).
