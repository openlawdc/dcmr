                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICATION REVIEW (ยง 29-4402)

4402.1. A review panel selected by the Department shall review each
   application to determine if it meets the requirements that apply to the
   particular grant.

   4402.2. The review panel shall advise the Director of all those applications
   which satisfy the applicable grant requirements and shall recommend awards
   based on the review factors and available funding.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 40 DCR 6246, 6247 (August 27, 1993).
