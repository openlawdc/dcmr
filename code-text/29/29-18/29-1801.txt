                                  DC REGULATIONS

GRANT OFFICER (§ 29-1801)

1801.1. The Director shall appoint a Grant Officer who shall serve as the
   Department’s point of contact for grant making activities and shall forward
   the Grant Officer’s name to the Office of the City Administrator and the
   Office of Partnership and Grant Services.

   1801.2. At minimum, the Department’s Grant Officer shall be conversant with:
   
   
   
   (a)  	The federal uniform administrative requirements for grants; and
   
   
   
   (b) 		The local requirements governing grants and subgrants.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 44 DCR 481 (January 24, 1997); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 5614 (April 12, 2013); as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 15243 (November 1, 2013).
