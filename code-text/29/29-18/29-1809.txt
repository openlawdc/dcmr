                                  DC REGULATIONS

CERTIFICATION OF FUNDS AVAILABILITY (§ 29-1809)

1809.1. Before an award can be given final approval and deemed formally
   awarded, the 	Department’s Grant Officer shall obtain a Certification,
   approved by the Office of 	the Chief Financial Officer, indicating that the
   grant amount has been 	appropriated and budgeted for the fiscal year.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 15243 (November 1, 2013).
