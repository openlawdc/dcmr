                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROCEDURES FOR DETERMINING COMPETENCY (ยง 29-3205)

3205.1. The District&#8217;s competency evaluation program shall be
   administered according to federal requirements in 42 CFR 483.154.

   3205.2. The District&#8217;s competency evaluation program shall require
   acceptable performance of tasks by the individual that the individual will be
   expected to perform as a nurse aide.

   3205.3. The District&#8217;s competency evaluation program shall include a
   written examination, unless the nurse aide chooses to establish competency by
   methods other than a written examination.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 2008 (April 5, 1991).
