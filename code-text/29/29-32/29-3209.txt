PASS RATE ON THE DISTRICT&#8217;S APPROVED WRITTEN COMPETENCY EVALUATION AND
DEMONSTRATED LEVEL OF COMPETENCE (ยง 29-3209)

3209.1. Each nurse aide training program shall ensure that at least
   seventy-five (75) percent of first time candidates from its program shall
   achieve a passing score on the District&#8217;s approved competency
   evaluation.
