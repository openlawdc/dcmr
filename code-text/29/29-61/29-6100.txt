                                  DC REGULATIONS

SCOPE (ยง 29-6100)

6100.1. The purpose of this chapter of the DCMR is to establish criteria and
   procedures regarding permanent guardianship subsidies for kinship caregivers.

   6100.2. This chapter of the DCMR shall apply to all permanent guardianship
   subsidies provided through CFSA.

   6100.3. The D.C. Child and Family Services Agency is responsible for the
   implementation of this chapter of the DCMR. Copies of this chapter of the DCMR
   shall be available from CFSA upon request.
