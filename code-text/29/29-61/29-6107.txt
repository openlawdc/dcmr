                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONFIDENTIALITY (ยง 29-6107)

6107.1. CFSA shall maintain the confidentiality of information and records
   concerning an application for or receipt of a permanent guardianship subsidy.
   Information or records may only be released as provided by applicable
   provisions of District or federal law.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 3671 (April 27,
   2001) [EXPIRED]; as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10740
   (November 23, 2001).
