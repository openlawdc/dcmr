                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PUBLIC ASSISTANCE ELIGIBILITY (ยง 29-1000)

1000.1. With respect to the General Public Assistance Program, alcohol and
   drug abuse, in and of themselves shall not be the factors used to determine
   the unemployability of the user.

   1000.2. Only physical or mental ailments as described by a physician shall be
   considered by the Department&#8217;s medical review team in the determination
   of unemployability.
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: The authority for this section is Reorganization Plan No. 2 of
   1979, 3 D.C. Code, 2001 Ed. at 338.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 27 DCR 4690 (October 24, 1980).
