                                  DC REGULATIONS

ACCESS TO RECORDS (ยง 29-4116)

4116.1. Each ICF/IID shall grant full access to all records during announced
   and unannounced audits and reviews by DHCF personnel, representatives of the
   U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, and any authorized agent(s) or
   official(s) of the federal or District of Columbia government.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11590 (August 9, 2013).
