                                  DC REGULATIONS

REBASING (§ 29-4105)

4105.1. Effective October 1, 2016, final reimbursement rates for the
   residential component will be based on providers’ FY 2014 cost reports
   subject to audit and adjustment by DHCF.  Subsequent rebasing to adjust the
   residential component will occur every three (3) years thereafter.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 11590 (August 9, 2013).
