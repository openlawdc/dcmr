                                  DC REGULATIONS

LOAN APPLICATION REVIEW PROCESS (§ 29-2105)

2105.1. The Chief, Office of Health Planning and Development, shall convene a
   loan application review panel which shall be comprised of the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Chief, Office of Health Planning and Development, or his or her designee;
   
   
   
   (b)	Administrator, Alcohol and Drug Abuse Services Administration, or his or
   her designee;
   
   
   
   (c)	Controller, Office of the Controller, or his or her designee;
   
   
   
   (d)	Qualified Health Professional, in accordance with these regulations;
   
   
   
   (e)	Representative, Office of Business and Economic Development; and
   
   
   
   (f)	Non-governmental Representative.

   2105.2. The panel may be expanded up to seven (7) members to include other
   representatives agreed upon by the six (6) members cited in §2106.1.

   2105.3. The panel shall utilize criteria for determining eligibility of
   prospective borrowers as described in §2104 in determining eligibility.

   2105.4. At the first meeting of the review panel, the members shall do the
   following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Agree upon criteria to be used in addition to that delineated in §2104;
   
   
   
   (b)	Assign a numeric weight to each criterion according to this importance in
   determining eligibility for a loan; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Determine the numeric ranges for approving and denying loans using the
   weighted criteria.

   2105.5. The review panel shall convene on the first and third week of the
   month to review new applications.

   2105.6. Loan applications presented for review shall be received by the Office
   of Health Planning and Development forty-eight (48) hours prior to the next
   regularly scheduled review panel meeting.

   2105.7. The Department shall notify the prospective borrower in writing of the
   approval or denial of the loan application within five (5) working days,
   subsequent to the review panel decision.

   2105.8. The Department shall inform the prospective borrower in writing of the
   reason(s) for denial of the loan application.

   2105.9. The Office of Health Planning and Development shall notify the
   prospective borrower, and the Office of the Controller of loan approvals. Each
   office shall receive a copy of the Notice of Revolving Loan Approval.

   2105.10. The approval notice shall contain the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	The name and address of borrowers;
   
   
   
   (b)	The amount of the loan;
   
   
   
   (c)	The names of at least four (4) persons to reside in the new housing; and
   
   
   
   (d)	The name of the primary agent, as described in §2104.3, to whom the loan
   check shall be made.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 603, 607 (January 18, 1991).
