                                  DC REGULATIONS

ESTABLISHMENT (§ 29-2100)

2100.1. This chapter governs the implementation of Chapter I, §2036 of Pub.
   L. 100-690, the Anti-Drug Abuse Act of 1988, to make available start-up loans
   for self-help housing for four (4) or more recovering substance abusers.

   2100.2. This Chapter shall be administered by the Director of the Department
   of Human Services (the Department) of the Government of the District of
   Columbia.

   2100.3. The Department shall make loans to eligible groups and entities in
   amounts up to but not to exceed four thousand dollars ($4,000).
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is D.C.
   Code, 2001 Ed. §4-110(3). This chapter is enacted pursuant to the Public
   Health Service Act Title XIX, Part B, Subpart I (42 U.S.C. §300X), as amended
   by the Anti-Drug Abuse Act of 1988 Chapter I, §2036 (Pub. L. 100-690).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 603 (January 18, 1991).
