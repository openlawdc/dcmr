                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICATION REVIEW (ยง 29-4002)

4002.1. A review panel shall review each application to determine if it meets
   the requirements that apply to the particular grant.

   4002.2. The review panel shall advise the Director of all those applications
   which satisfy the applicable grant requirements and shall recommend awards
   based on the review factors and available funding.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 6302, 6304 (October 11, 1991).
