                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (§ 29-4000)

4000.1. This chapter establishes uniform requirements for transferring money
   or other things of value to recipients outside the District government, where
   the Department is authorized to make grants.

   4000.2. The Director of the Department of Human Services is authorized to make
   a grant where:
   
   
   
   (a)	A statute authorizes the Department to support or stimulate the activity
   of the recipient;
   
   
   
   (b)	The Department will not be substantially involved in the activity that is
   supported or stimulated; and
   
   
   
   (c)	The Department is not acquiring any property or service of direct use to
   the District government.

   4000.3. The Department is authorized to make grants pursuant to the Vocational
   Rehabilitation Act of 1973 as amended; the Dwight D. Eisenhower Mathematics
   and Education Act of 1988; the Stewart B. McKinney Homeless Assistance Act of
   1987 as amended; and the Community Service Block Grant Act as amended.
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: Unless otherwise noted, the authority for this chapter is the
   Elementary and Secondary Education Act, 20 U.S.C. § 6301 et seq.; the
   Vocational Rehabilitation Act of 1973, 29 U.S.C. § 720 et seq.; the Community
   Service Block Grant Act, 42 U.S.C. § 9901 et seq.; the Stewart B. McKinney
   Homeless Assistance Act, 42 U.S.C. § 11301 et seq.; and Mayor’s Order 91-97
   (June 5, 1991).
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 38 DCR 6302 (October 11, 1991).
