                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (§ 29-8499)

8499.1. The following terms and phrases shall have the meanings ascribed
   below:
   
   
   
   Agency – The D.C. Child and Family Services Agency.
   
   
   
   Ward &#8211; a child or youth who is committed to the legal custody of the
   Child and Family Services Agency.
