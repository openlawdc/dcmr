                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROVIDER QUALIFICATIONS (ยง 29-4701)

4701.1. Each Provider shall comply with the following requirements:
   
   
   
   (a)	Have the ability to access all pertinent records concerning the
   child&#8217;s needs for services, including records of the Family Division of
   the District of Columbia Superior Court and CFSA;
   
   
   
   (b)	Ensure 24-hour availability of case management services and continuity of
   those services;
   
   
   
   (c)	Have established referral systems and demonstrated linkages and referral
   ability with community resources required by the target population;
   
   
   
   (d)	Have a minimum of five years experience in providing all core elements of
   case management services to the target population;
   
   
   
   (e)	Have a financial management capacity and system that provides
   documentation of services and costs, in accordance with OMB Circular A-87
   principles;
   
   
   
   (f)	Have an administrative capacity to ensure quality of services in
   accordance with District and federal requirements;
   
   
   
   (g)	Have the capacity to document and maintain individual case records in
   accordance with District and federal requirements; and
   
   
   
   (h)	Have a demonstrated ability to comply with all District and federal laws
   and rules governing the participation of providers in the District of Columbia
   Medicaid Program, including the ability to meet District and federal
   requirements for documentation, billing and audits.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Emergency Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 5257 (June 23, 2000)
   [EXPIRED]; as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 47 DCR 7084 (September
   1, 2000).
