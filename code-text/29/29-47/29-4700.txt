                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 29-4700)

4700.1. The purpose of this chapter is to establish standards under which the
   District of Columbia Child and Family Services Agency (CFSA) and its
   subcontractors, shall qualify to participate as providers of targeted case
   management (TCM) services.

   4700.2. CFSA shall enter into a provider agreement with the Department of
   Health, Medical Assistance Administration (MAA) for the provision of TCM
   services.

   4700.3. The provider agreement shall specify the services to be provided,
   methods of operation, financial and legal requirements, and identification of
   the population to be served.
