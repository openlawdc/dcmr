                                  DC REGULATIONS

REIMBURSEMENT FOR OUT OF STATE FACILITIES (ยง 29-6516)

6516.1. If a facility is located outside the District of Columbia
   (&#8220;District&#8221;), MAA shall reimburse the facility for care rendered
   to a District Medicaid recipient in accordance with the Medicaid reimbursement
   policy of the state in which the facility is located.

   6516.2. MAA shall notify each out-of-state facility, in writing, of its
   payment rate calculated in accordance with this section.

   6516.3. An out-of-state facility is not required to file cost reports with
   MAA.

   6516.4. Each out-of-state facility shall obtain written authorization from MAA
   prior to admission of a District Medicaid recipient in accordance with the
   requirements set forth in sections 905.3 and 905.4 of Title 29 DCMR.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 1370 (February 24, 2006).
