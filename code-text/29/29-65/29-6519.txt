                                  DC REGULATIONS

ACCESS TO RECORDS (ยง 29-6519)

6519.1. Each nursing facility shall allow appropriate personnel of the
   Department of Health, representatives of the Department of Health and Human
   services and other authorized agents or officials of the District of Columbia
   government and federal government full access to all records during announced
   and unannounced audits and reviews.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 1370 (February 24, 2006).
