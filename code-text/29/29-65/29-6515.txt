                                  DC REGULATIONS

REIMBURSEMENT FOR REORGANIZED FACILITIES OR CHANGE OF OWNERSHIP (ยง 29-6515)

6515.1. A nursing facility that has been re-organized pursuant to Chapter 11
   of the United States Bankruptcy Code after September 30, 2000 shall be
   reimbursed at the same rate in effect prior to the date of filing its
   petition.

   6515.2. A nursing facility with a change of ownership after September 30, 2000
   shall be reimbursed at the same rate established for the nursing facility
   prior to the change of ownership.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 1370 (February 24, 2006).
