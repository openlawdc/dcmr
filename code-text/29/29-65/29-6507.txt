CAPITAL-RELATED COSTS PER DIEM CALULATION (ยง 29-6507)

6507.1. Each nursing facility&#8217;s capital-related cost per diem shall be
   calculated by dividing total allowable capital-related base year costs
   adjusted in accordance with subsection 6501.4 by total resident days
   determined in accordance with subsection 6512.2 for all nursing care
   residents.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 1370 (February 24, 2006).
