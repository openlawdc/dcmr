                                  DC REGULATIONS

PERIOD OF REVIEW (ยง 29-418)

418.1. The period for SHPDA or SHCC review shall begin no more than seven (7)
   days after the receipt of the application to the SHPDA.

   418.2. The SHPDA or the SHCC shall be allowed up to sixty (60) days to conduct
   a review of grant applications and categorical state plans unless the
   appropriate federal funding agency or state program agency specifies in
   writing a longer period with respect to a program or particular proposed use
   of federal funds.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 1086 (March 13, 1981).
