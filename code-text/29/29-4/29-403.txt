                                  DC REGULATIONS

NONREVIEWABLE GRANT APPLICATIONS (ยง 29-403)

403.1. Except as provided in ยง404, the SHCC and SHPDA shall not review,
   comment upon, approve, or disapprove proposed uses of federal funds which are
   either of the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Noncompeting extension grant applications; or
   
   
   
   (b)	Supplemental grant applications that are for administrative increases in
   costs.

   403.2. The SHPDA shall not review and approve or disapprove the proposed uses
   of federal funds appropriated for grants or contracts for research or
   training, as determined by the federal funding agency.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 1086 (March 13, 1981).
