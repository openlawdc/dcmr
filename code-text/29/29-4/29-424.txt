                                  DC REGULATIONS

PUBLICATION OF ANNUAL STATUS REPORT (ยง 29-424)

424.1. The SHPDA shall prepare and publish an annual report of the Agency
   reviews conducted.

   424.2. The annual report shall include a statement concerning the status of
   each review, a general statement of the findings and decisions made in the
   course of the reviews, and a list of the reviews completed by the SHPDA since
   the publication of the last report.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 28 DCR 1086 (March 13, 1981).
