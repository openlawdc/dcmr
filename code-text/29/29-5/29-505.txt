                                  DC REGULATIONS

EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICES AGENCIES[COLON] LOCATION (ยง 29-505)

505.1. An Emergency Medical Services Agency shall maintain a fixed physical
   location. The agency shall notify the Director no fewer than thirty (30) days
   prior to any change in the location before the agency moves to the new
   location.
