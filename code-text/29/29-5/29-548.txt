                                  DC REGULATIONS

COURSE RECORDS (ยง 29-548)

548.1. An education program shall maintain course records for at least five
   (5) years following course completion.  The records shall be maintained in a
   manner to prevent loss, destruction, or unauthorized use and shall contain the
   following:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A complete outline for each course given, including a brief overview,
   instructional objectives, comprehensive topical outline, method of evaluation,
   and a record of participant performance;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A record of the time, place, and date that each course is given;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A record of the number of CE hours awarded for the CE course;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A curriculum vitae or resume for each instructor; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   A roster signed by course participants that shall include the name and
   certificate number of each person who has taken any certification, refresher
   or CE course, class, or activity, and a record of any course completion
   certificate issued.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 16569 (December 6, 2013).
