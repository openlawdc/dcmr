                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICABILITY OF FEES AND PENALTIES TO DISTRICT GOVERNMENT AGENCIES (ยง 29-566)

566.1 	No District government agency shall be required to pay personnel or
organizational fees established by this chapter.



566.2 	District government agencies shall be liable for penalties that may be
imposed for violation of a provision of this chapter.



SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 16569 (December 6, 2013).
