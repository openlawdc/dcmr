                                  DC REGULATIONS

EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS[COLON] PROGRAM DIRECTOR (ยง 29-529)

529.1 	Each EMS educational institution shall have a program director who:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Has at least two (2) years of experience instructing and evaluating EMS
   students;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Has experience with administration of educational programs;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Has an instructor certification equivalent to the course level being
   instructed; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Has knowledge of the District scope of practice for Emergency Medical Services
   Providers and the National Educational Standards.
   
   
   
   529.2 	The program director shall:
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Have overall responsibility for the success of the education program,
   including continuous quality review and improvement of the education program;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Serve as the education program student and faculty liaison;
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Identify sites where students can fulfill field internship requirements; and
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   Keep the medical director informed of the program schedule, progress of
   individual student performance, student or instructor complaints, and the
   status of other program activities.

   529.3. The medical director may also act as the program director.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 16569 (December 6, 2013).
