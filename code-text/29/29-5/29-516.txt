                                  DC REGULATIONS

EMS PROVIDERS &#8211; EMERGENCY MEDICAL RESPONDER (§ 29-516)

516.1 	Each applicant for certification as an Emergency Medical Responder
   (EMR) shall submit an application to the Director.  The application shall
   include:
   
   
   
   (a) 	A copy of a valid NREMT-First Responder (or greater) certification card;
   
   
   
   (b)	A copy of a valid American Heart Association (AHA) Health Care Provider
   (or equivalent) CPR card;
   
   
   
   (c)	A completed application signed by the sponsoring EMS agency’s medical
   director; and
   
   
   
   (d)	A check or money order made out in the appropriate fee made payable to the
   D.C. Treasurer (if applicable).

   516.2. Upon receipt of a complete application, the Director shall review the
   application and, if approved, shall issue a D.C. EMR certification card.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 16569 (December 6, 2013).
