                                  DC REGULATIONS

MASS CASUALTY INCIDENTS (ยง 29-560)

560.1 	Each Emergency Medical Services Agency is required to develop a plan to
   mitigate a Mass Casualty Incident (MCI).

   560.2. Each MCI plan shall comply with the National Incident Management System
   requirements and the District MCI plan.

   560.3. Each MCI plan shall be submitted to the Director for his or her
   approval.

   560.4. Each Emergency Medical Services Agency shall use the triage system
   approved by the Director.
   
   
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 60 DCR 16569 (December 6, 2013).
