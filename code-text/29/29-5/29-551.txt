INTER-FACILITY TRANSPORTATION OF PATIENTS WHERE THE POINT OF ORIGIN IS IN THE
DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA (§ 29-551)

551.1. A patient who is transported by ambulance between facilities, where the
   point of origin is within the District of Columbia, shall only be transported
   by a certified District of Columbia Emergency Medical Services Agency
   ambulance except when the transporting agency has been requested through a
   mutual aid agreement or memorandum of understanding by the Department of
   Health.

   551.2. A transporting agency that is not District-certified shall adhere to
   the protocols of its local jurisdiction unless otherwise specified through a
   mutual aid agreement or memorandum of understanding.

   551.3. Patients shall be transported to the appropriate heath care facility as
   outlined in the agency’s protocol.
