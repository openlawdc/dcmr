                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPUTATION OF HPRP ASSISTANCE (§ 29-7604)

7604.1. The following shall apply to determinations of HPRP benefits.

   7604.2. Computation of the HPRP applicant’s or participant’s contribution
   for security deposit and first month rent (section 7607), utility arrearage
   and utility deposit (section 7608), and rental arrearage (section 7610) shall
   be calculated based on the net income and assets available to the applicant
   unit within the last thirty (30) days prior to application and may taken into
   account necessary and reasonable expenditures incurred during that time
   period.

   7604.3. Computation of the HPRP applicant’s or participant’s contribution
   for rental assistance (section 7609) shall be made in accordance with section
   7609.3.
