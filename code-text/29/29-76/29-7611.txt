FAIR HEARING AND ADMINISTRATIVE REVIEW (ยง 29-7611)

7611.1. An applicant or participating HPRP household shall have ninety (90)
   calendar days following the receipt of a notice described in subsection
   7601.14 or subsection 7601.16 to request a fair hearing, in accordance with
   the hearing provisions of section 26 of the Homeless Services Reform Act of
   2005, effective October 22, 2005 (D.C. Law 16-35, D.C. Official Code ยง
   4-764.41), for the action that is the subject of the notice.

   7611.2. Upon receipt of a fair hearing request, the Department shall offer the
   appellant or his or her authorized representative an opportunity for an
   administrative review in accordance with section 27 of the Homeless Services
   Reform Act of 2005, effective October 22, 2005 (D.C. Law 16-35, D.C. Official
   Code ยง 4-764.42), except that if an eviction is imminent, the Department
   shall take all reasonable steps to provide an expedited administrative review
   to maximize resolution of the appeal in time to resolve the housing emergency
   and prevent the eviction.
