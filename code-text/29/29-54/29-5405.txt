                                  DC REGULATIONS

GRIEVANCE SYSTEM &#8211; THE DEPARTMENT (§ 29-5405)

5405.1. Each AFDC or AFDC-related recipient enrolled in the D.C. Medicaid
   Managed Care program that is aggrieved by an individual decision of the
   Department affecting that recipient’s choice or assignment of providers, or
   receipt of a covered service through the District’s Medicaid Managed Care
   Program shall be entitled to a hearing before the Department’s Office of
   Fair Hearings as provided in D.C. Code, 2001 Ed. §4-210.01 to 4-210.18 and 42
   C.F.R. Part 431, Subpart E.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 1566, 1592 (March 31, 1995).
