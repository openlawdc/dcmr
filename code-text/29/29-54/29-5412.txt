                                  DC REGULATIONS

MEDICAID PROGRAM AND RECIPIENTS HELD HARMLESS (ยง 29-5412)

5412.1. Each fee-for-service primary care provider shall hold harmless the
   District government, the Department and AFDC or AFDC-related Medicaid
   recipients against any loss, damage, expense and liability of any kind that
   arises from any action of the provider in the performance of the Medicaid
   managed care provider agreement.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 1566, 1594 (March 31, 1995).
