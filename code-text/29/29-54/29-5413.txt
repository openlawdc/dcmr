                                  DC REGULATIONS

NON-DISCRIMINATION (ยง 29-5413)

5413.1. Each fee-for-service primary care provider shall comply with all
   applicable federal and District laws, regulations, and orders that prohibit
   discrimination on the basis of race, age, sex, marital status, personal
   appearance, sexual orientation, family composition, physical disability,
   education, political affiliation, source of income, and place of residence or
   business.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 42 DCR 1566, 1595 (March 31, 1995).
