CONFIDENTIALITY OF INFORMATION &#8211; GENERAL (ยง 29-5406)

5406.1. All information, records, and data collected and maintained by the
   fee-for-service primary care provider that relate to enrollees shall be
   protected from unauthorized disclosure by the fee-for-service provider.

   5406.2. Except as otherwise provided by federal law or regulation, the use or
   disclosure of information concerning enrollees shall be restricted to purposes
   directly related to the administration of the Medicaid program in accordance
   with 42 C.F.R. ยง431.302.

   5406.3. The information to be safeguarded shall include all information listed
   in 42 C.F.R. ยง431.305.
