                                  DC REGULATIONS

PAYMENT AND REIMBURSEMENT (§ 29-2909)

2909.1. The Department shall not seek reimbursement for its costs from adults
   or legally responsible persons receiving assistance, as specified by Title VII
   of the District of Columbia Public Assistance Act of 1982, D.C. Law 4-101,
   D.C. Code, 2001 Ed. § 4-201.01 et seq.

   2909.2. The APS may waive charges it might otherwise assess against
   self-payment adults or legally responsible persons for services provided in
   exigent circumstances.

   2909.3. Each agency or Department that charges for services, such as ambulance
   charges, shall be responsible for charging and collecting those fees.

   2909.4. The Department may require an adult, as a condition of receiving
   Medicaid, other health care assistance, or any other assistance under the Act,
   to execute a written assignment to the District of any right or claim the
   beneficiary has against a third party for assistance undertaken by the
   District, pursuant to the Health-Care Assistance Reimbursement Act of 1984,
   D.C. Law 5-86, D.C. Code, 2001 Ed. § 4-601 to 4-609.

   2909.5. Notwithstanding any other provision in this chapter, the District may,
   in appropriate circumstances, seek payment or reimbursement from each person
   responsible for an adult’s abuse, neglect or exploitation for all or any
   part of the costs authorized pursuant to D.C. Code, 2001 Ed. § 7-1910.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 6646, 6655 (October 19, 1990).
