                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECREATIONAL ACTIVITIES (ยง 29-6344)

6344.1. An independent living program shall develop and follow a written plan
   for providing recreational activities adequate to meet each resident&#8217;s
   needs, including:
   
   
   
   (a)	Sufficient supervised, organized or structured individual and group
   recreational programs, both indoors and outdoors, appropriate to the
   resident&#8217;s age, interests, and needs, and consistent with the
   resident&#8217;s ITILP;
   
   
   
   (b)	Free time for residents to pursue their individual interests, with
   protective supervision as required, unless otherwise provided in the
   resident&#8217;s ITILP;
   
   
   
   (c)	The maximum use of available community recreational resources, where
   appropriate;
   
   
   
   (d)	Reasonable access to sufficient recreational supplies, equipment and
   activities appropriate to the resident&#8217;s age, interests, and needs; and
   
   
   
   (e)	Usable leisure-time materials, including but not limited to books, games,
   and toys that are appropriate to the resident&#8217;s sex and stage of
   development, and transportation to local libraries.

   6344.2. An independent living program shall provide at least one (1)
   recreational activity per calendar quarter for the participation of all
   residents.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 1591 (February 22, 2002).
