                                  DC REGULATIONS

PERSONAL PROPERTY (§ 29-6321)

6321.1. An independent living program shall maintain an inventory of a
   resident&#8217;s personal property. The inventory shall include:
   
   
   
   (a)	A description of the property, including its condition, and serial number,
   if any; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Identification of any property whose use has been limited pursuant to §
   6321.2.

   6321.2. An independent living program may limit a resident&#8217;s use of her
   or his personal property as reasonably necessary to protect the health,
   safety, or welfare of the resident or others. Any such limitation shall be set
   forth in the resident&#8217;s initial ITILP or ITILP, pursuant to § 6341, and
   documented in the resident&#8217;s case record.

   6321.3. An independent living program shall return personal property whose use
   was limited pursuant to § 6321.2 to the resident:
   
   
   
   (a)	Upon discharge, if appropriate; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Prior to discharge, if specifically provided in the resident&#8217;s
   initial ITILP or ITILP, pursuant to § 6341.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 1591 (February 22, 2002).
