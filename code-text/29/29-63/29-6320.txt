                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONFIDENTIALITY (ยง 29-6320)

6320.1. A resident&#8217;s case record is confidential and may not be
   disclosed or used other than in the course of official independent living
   program duties to provide services to the resident and in such instance shall
   only be disclosed to the least extent possible, consistent with any court
   order, the resident&#8217;s initial ITILP and ITILP, and local and federal
   law. The independent living program, CFSA, contracting entity, and guardian ad
   litem shall have access to the resident&#8217;s case record. No other person
   shall have access to the resident&#8217;s case record unless authorized in
   writing by CFSA.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 1591 (February 22, 2002).
