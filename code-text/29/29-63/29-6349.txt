                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEAL PROCEDURE (ยง 29-6349)

6349.1. A person aggrieved by a decision of CFSA to deny, suspend, or revoke a
   license or the renewal of a license may appeal the decision in accordance with
   29 DCMR Chapter 59.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 1591 (February 22, 2002).
