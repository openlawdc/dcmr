                                  DC REGULATIONS

MONITORING (ยง 29-6331)

6331.1. An independent living program shall monitor each resident and
   residence, including through at least twice monthly inspections of each
   residence.

   6331.2. An independent living program shall conduct both announced and
   unannounced inspections.

   6331.3. Monitoring shall assess and evaluate:
   
   
   
   (a)	The residence&#8217;s general upkeep;
   
   
   
   (b)	The resident&#8217;s ability to maintain the residence;
   
   
   
   (c)	The resident&#8217;s ability to maintain adequate and appropriate
   houseware, pantry items, and food supplies;
   
   
   
   (d)	The resident&#8217;s maintenance of a balanced diet;
   
   
   
   (e)	 The resident&#8217;s ability to maintain an adequate and appropriate
   wardrobe;
   
   
   
   (f)	The resident&#8217;s proper personal care, hygiene, and grooming;
   
   
   
   (g)	The resident&#8217;s ability to do her or his laundry;
   
   
   
   (h)	The storage of poisonous, toxic, or flammable substances in accordance
   with the manufacturer&#8217;s instructions and in accordance with ยง 6336;
   
   
   
   (i)	The resident&#8217;s awareness of the fire safety plan; and
   
   
   
   (j)	The resident&#8217;s ability to access public transportation.

   6331.4. Each inspection shall be documented in the resident&#8217;s case
   record. Both the resident and the independent living program staff member who
   conducted the inspection shall sign the inspection report.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 1591 (February 22, 2002).
