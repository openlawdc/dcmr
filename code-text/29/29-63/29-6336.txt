                                  DC REGULATIONS

TOXIC, POISONOUS, AND FLAMMABLE SUBSTANCES (ยง 29-6336)

6336.1. A main facility and each residence shall have on-site only those
   poisonous, toxic, or flammable substances that are required to maintain the
   main facility or residence.

   6336.2. All poisonous, toxic, or flammable substances shall be stored in
   accordance with the manufacturer&#8217;s instructions.

   6336.3. All toxic or poisonous substances shall be labeled as to the contents
   and antidote.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 1591 (February 22, 2002).
