6317.1. An independent living program shall cooperate with monitoring and
   evaluation efforts undertaken by CFSA or the contracting entity, including by
   providing or assisting in obtaining any information requested to evaluate the
   adequacy, appropriateness, effectiveness, cost-effectiveness, and quality of
   the program&#8217;s operation and services.
