                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESIDENTS&#8217; COUNCIL (ยง 29-6345)

6345.1. An independent living program shall establish and maintain a
   residents&#8217; council.

   6345.2. The purpose of the residents&#8217; counsel is to make recommendations
   to the independent living program to improve the quality of the independent
   living program and to protect residents&#8217; rights.

   6345.3. The residents&#8217; council shall be composed of residents.

   6345.4. The residents&#8217; council shall meet at least monthly.

   6345.5. An independent living program shall provide the space for, and
   conspicuously post notice of, residents&#8217; council meetings.

   6345.6. The independent living program shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Give due consideration to all recommendations made by the residents&#8217;
   council;
   
   
   
   (b)	Respond in writing to all recommendations; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Maintain a record of all recommendations and responses.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 1591 (February 22, 2002).
