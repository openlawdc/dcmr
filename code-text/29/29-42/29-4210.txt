                                  DC REGULATIONS

REIMBURSEMENT RATES[COLON] PERSONAL EMERGENCY RESPONSE SERVICES (ยง 29-4210)

4210.1. The reimbursement rate for Personal Emergency Response Services shall
   be forty dollars ($40.00) for one (1) installation and twenty-eight dollars
   and fifty cents ($28.50) per month for the rental fee.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 9025 (October 24, 2003).
