                                  DC REGULATIONS

REIMBURSEMENT RATES[COLON] CASE MANAGEMENT SERVICES (ยง 29-4208)

4208.1. Case management services shall be reimbursed at the following levels:
   
   
   
   (a)	For initial assessments, five hundred dollars ($500.00) shall be
   reimbursed by MAA;
   
   
   
   (b)	For monthly assessments, one hundred and twenty-five dollars ($125.00)
   shall be reimbursed by the MAA;
   
   
   
   (c)	For annual re-assessments, two hundred and twenty-five dollars ($225.00)
   shall be reimbursed by the MAA.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 9025 (October 24, 2003).
