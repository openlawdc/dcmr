                                  DC REGULATIONS

CASE MANAGEMENT SERVICES REQUIRED (ยง 29-4201)

4201.1. As a condition of waiver participation in the home- and
   community-based waiver services program, each recipient or applicant must
   receive case management services for each service(s) provided.

   4201.2. No recipient shall receive case management services unless the
   recipient or an authorized representative of the recipient signs the
   &#8220;Beneficiary Freedom of Choice Form&#8221; provided by the
   recipient&#8217;s case manager to elect to receive home and community-based
   waiver services.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 9025 (October 24, 2003).
