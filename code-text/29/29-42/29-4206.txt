                                  DC REGULATIONS

ACCESS TO RECORDS (§ 29-4206)

4206.1. Each provider of waiver services shall allow access to a
   recipient&#8217;s records during announced or unannounced audits or reviews by
   designated MAA staff and federal representatives and as set forth in §§
   4237.3 and 4237.4 of this chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 50 DCR 9025 (October 24, 2003).
