                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEALS (ยง 29-6705)

6705.1. Decisions made by MAA and communicated in the formal response
   described in subsections 6704.5 and 6704.6, may be appealed, within thirty
   (30) days of the date of MAA&#8217;s letter notifying the personal
   representative or attorney of record of the decision, to the Office of
   Administrative Hearings.

   6705.2. Filing an appeal shall not stay the District&#8217;s ability to file a
   claim against the decedent&#8217;s estate.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 6353 (August 4, 2006).
