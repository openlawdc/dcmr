                                  DC REGULATIONS

HOSPITAL INTAKE PROCEDURES FOR A SURRENDERED NEWBORN (ยง 29-8303)

8303.1. When a newborn is surrendered to a hospital or transported to another
   hospital after being surrendered, each hospital shall treat and provide care
   for the newborn while the newborn is in its custody.

   8303.2. The hospital where the newborn was surrendered shall:
   
   
   
   	(a)  	Provide CFSA with a copy of the authorized receiving facility report on
   or 	before CFSA assumes physical custody of the newborn; and
   
   
   
   (b) 	Contact the CFSA Post Permanency Unit within one (1) hour after receiving
   the newborn or as soon as possible.

   8303.3. CFSA shall not assume physical custody of the newborn until it
   receives the authorized receiving facility report.
