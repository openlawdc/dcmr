                                  DC REGULATIONS

ANONYMITY OF PARENT (ยง 29-8301)

8301.1. A parent who surrenders a newborn eligible for surrender under the Act
   at an authorized receiving facility has the right to remain anonymous and
   shall not be required to disclose his or her name or address.
