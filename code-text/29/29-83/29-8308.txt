                                  DC REGULATIONS

SIGNAGE (ยง 29-8308)

8308.1. CFSA shall post a sign in a conspicuous place on the exterior of each
   authorized receiving facility in the District.  The sign shall state in plain
   terms that a newborn may be surrendered at the facility and shall provide
   information about how a person can determine whether a particular newborn has
   been surrendered.
