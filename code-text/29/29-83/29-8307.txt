                                  DC REGULATIONS

IMMUNITY FROM LIABILITY (ยง 29-8307)

8307.1. An authorized receiving facility and its staff shall be immune from
   civil or criminal liability for the good faith performance of their
   responsibilities under the Act and this chapter.
