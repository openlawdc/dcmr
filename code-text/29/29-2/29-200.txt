                                  DC REGULATIONS

ISSUANCE OF LICENSES (§ 29-200)

200.1. Licenses to operate vending facilities within the Blind Vendors Program
   (also referred to in this chapter as the &#8220;Program&#8221;) shall be
   issued only to blind persons who meet the following criteria:
   
   
   
   (a)	Residency in the District at the time of licensing; and
   
   
   
   (b)	United States citizenship.

   200.2. Licenses shall be issued only to persons who are certified by the
   licensing agency as qualified to operate a vending facility.

   200.3. Primary preference shall be given to blind persons who are jobless and
   in need of employement.
   
   
   
   	AUTHORITY: Authority for §§ 200-204, 206-217, 218-252, and 299 of this
   chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: DCRR Title 6C, as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 27 DCR
   4611 (October 17, 1980).
