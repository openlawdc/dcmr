                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESPONSE TO MOTIONS (ยง 29-231)

231.1. Each party or intervenor upon whom a motion is served shall have ten
   (10) calendar days from the date of service of the motion to submit a
   response.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
