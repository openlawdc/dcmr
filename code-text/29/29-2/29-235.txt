                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICE OF HEARING AND OF HEARING PROCEDURES AND ISSUES (§ 29-235)

235.1. The Office of Fair Hearings shall give a notice of the time, place, and
   issues which are the subject of the hearing to each party, the party’s
   representative and intervenors at least ten (10) days prior to the hearing by
   certified mail, except under exceptional circumstances or by agreement of each
   party.

   235.2. The Office of Fair Hearings shall provide the complainant a copy of the
   hearing procedures and other relevant information necessary to enable the
   complainant to prepare for the hearing.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
