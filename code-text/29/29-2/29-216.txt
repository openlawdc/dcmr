                                  DC REGULATIONS

ACCESS TO PROGRAM AND FINANCIAL INFORMATION (ยง 29-216)

216.1. Each vendor shall be provided access to all program and financial data
   of the Department relevant to the operation of the Program, including
   quarterly and annual financial reports, if that disclosure does not violate
   applicable federal or District laws pertaining to the disclosure of
   confidential information.

   216.2. Insofar as practicable, data shall be made available in braille or
   recorded tape and, at the request of the vendor, the Department shall arrange
   a convenient time to assist in the interpretation of data.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: DCRR Title 6C, as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 27 DCR
   4611 (October 17, 1980).
