                                  DC REGULATIONS

RIGHT TO APPEAL (§ 29-243)

243.1. The complainant may appeal the final decision to the United States
   Secretary of Education, pursuant to 20 U.S.C. § 107d 1 and 34 C.F.R. §
   395.13, within fifteen (15) working days of the complainant’s receipt of the
   decision.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
