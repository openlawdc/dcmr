                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADMINISTRATIVE REVIEW RECORD (ยง 29-227)

227.1. Documentation of a written request for an administrative review, oral
   and documentary evidence introduced at the administrative review, and all
   decisions resulting therefrom shall be maintained as part of the official
   record of the administrative review process.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
