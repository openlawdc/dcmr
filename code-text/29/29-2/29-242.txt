                                  DC REGULATIONS

FINAL DECISION (§ 29-242)

242.1. After a review of the proposed decision, and after affording the
   parties an opportunity to file written exceptions pursuant to D.C. Code, 2001
   Ed. § 2-509(d), the Director shall issue a decision in accordance with the
   requirements of § 240.2. This decision shall be final and binding upon the
   parties, unless otherwise specifically indicated in the decision.

   242.2. The Director shall issue the final decision within fifteen (15) working
   days after receipt of the proposed decision, and may affirm, modify, remand,
   or reverse the proposed decision in accordance with D.C. Code, 2001 Ed. §
   2-509.

   242.3. The Director shall promptly transmit a copy of the final decision to
   each party and to each party’s representative.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
