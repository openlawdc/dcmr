                                  DC REGULATIONS

REQUEST FOR HEARING; TIMELINESS (§ 29-232)

232.1. Each request for a hearing shall be submitted in writing to the Office
   of Fair Hearings.

   232.2. The written request for a hearing shall be submitted personally or by
   certified mail, return receipt requested. This request may be transmitted
   through the State Committee of Blind Vendors.

   232.3. The request for hearing shall be signed by the complainant or on the
   complainant’s behalf by an authorized representative.

   232.4. Each complainant shall request a full evidentiary hearing within
   fifteen (15) working days after an adverse decision based on an administrative
   review or, in the absence of an administrative review, within fifteen (15)
   working days of the occurrence of the action upon which the complaint is
   based.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
