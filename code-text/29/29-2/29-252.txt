                                  DC REGULATIONS

EX PARTE COMMUNICATION (§ 29-252)

252.1. The hearing examiner in a proceeding shall not communicate directly or
   indirectly regarding any fact or issue of law with any party or party’s
   representative, except on notice and with an opportunity for each party or
   each party’s representative to participate.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
