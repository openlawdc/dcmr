                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSCRIPT (ยง 29-240)

240.1. A transcript shall be made of the oral evidence and shall be made
   available to the parties. The Administration shall pay all transcript costs
   and shall provide the blind vendor with at least one copy of the transcript.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
