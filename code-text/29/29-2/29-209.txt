                                  DC REGULATIONS

RELOCATION OF VENDORS (ยง 29-209)

209.1. If fifty percent (50%) or more of the government employees are moved
   from the premises in which a vending facility is located to new premises in
   which a vending facility is to be established, the vendor of the facility in
   the old premises shall have the privilege of transferring to the new location
   only if the relocated employees constitute fifty percent (50%) or more of the
   government personnel in the new permises.

   209.2. If the provisions of ยง209.1 do not apply, the vending facility vacancy
   in a new premises shall be filled in accordance with the usual promotion and
   transfer system.
