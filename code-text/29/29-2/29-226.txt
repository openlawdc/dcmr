                                  DC REGULATIONS

DECISION OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE REVIEW PANEL (§ 29-226)

226.1. The Administrative Review Panel shall render a written decision which
   sets forth its evaluation and resolution of the complaint and describes the
   actions required of the Administration, as well as those required of the
   licensee, in implementing the decision.

   226.2. The Administrative Review Panel shall issue a written decision within
   fifteen (15) working days after its review of a complaint.

   226.3. Each written decision of the Administrative Review Panel shall notify
   the licensee of the licensee’s right to request a full evidentiary hearing
   within fifteen (15) working days after the issuance of the Administrative
   Review Panel’s decision.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
