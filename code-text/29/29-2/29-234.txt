                                  DC REGULATIONS

PLACE FOR HEARING; READER SERVICES (ยง 29-234)

234.1. Each hearing shall be conducted at the Office of Fair Hearings within
   fifteen (15) working days of the receipt of the request for the hearing,
   unless the Administration and the licensee mutually, in writing, agree to hold
   the hearing at a later date.

   234.2. The Administration shall arrange reader services or other communication
   services, if the complainant or any other party or intervenor requests these
   services for the hearing in a timely manner.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 8538 (December 9, 1988).
