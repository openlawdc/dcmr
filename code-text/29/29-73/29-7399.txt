                                  DC REGULATIONS

DEFINITIONS (ยง 29-7399)

When used in this chapter, the following terms and phrases shall have the
following meanings:



Immigrant child- Any child who is ineligible for Medicaid by virtue of the
child&#8217;s immigration status.



Medical Assistance- Health care services provided by a health maintenance
organization (HMO) in accordance with the terms of a contract between the
District government and the HMO to provide managed care health services on a
capitated basis.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8971 (September 28, 2001); Errata
Notice published at 57 DCR 5049 (June 11, 2010).
