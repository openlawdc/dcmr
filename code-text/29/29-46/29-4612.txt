                                  DC REGULATIONS

RISK MANAGEMENT (ยง 29-4612)

4612.1. Each Provider shall develop and review annually a risk management
   outpatient plan consistent with 50000.115.IF of the CMHS Performance Plan,
   that is directed at minimizing the following as it relates to MCOTT clients:
   
   
   
   (a)	Accidents;
   
   
   
   (b)	Infections;
   
   
   
   (c)	Fires;
   
   
   
   (d)	Suicides and unusual deaths; and
   
   
   
   (e)	Unfavorable service-related outcome, including discharges that are not
   mutually agreed upon.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 46 DCR 6122 (July 23, 1999).
