                                  DC REGULATIONS

ESTABLISHMENT (§ 29-1100)

1100.1. The Office of Energy shall administer the Complementary Energy
   Assistance Program (CEAP) which provides energy assistance Grants to needy
   households referred by the Vocational Rehabilitation Program, the Subsidized
   Adoption Program, the Foster Care Program, and the Aid to Families with
   Dependent Children (AFDC) Program who meet the requirements as set forth in
   §§ 1101.1 and 1101.2 of this chapter.

   1100.2. In addition, any needy District resident may receive an energy
   assistance grant who also meets the requirements as set forth in §§ 1101.1
   and 1101.2 of this chapter.

   1100.3. This assistance shall be in the form of cash payments to assist such
   recipients with household energy and utility costs. Recipients shall be held
   harmless against any other forms of energy assistance he/she may receive from
   other public or private sources.

   1100.4. This assistance shall not duplicate any assistance paid by the D.C.
   Department of Human Services in its capacity as the State Agency.
