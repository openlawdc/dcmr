SCOPE AND PURPOSE (ยง 29-5900)

5900.1. The purpose of these rules is to establish procedures that shall apply
   to each fair hearing provided by CFSA.

   5900.2. Copies of these rules shall be available from CFSA upon request.

   5900.3. If a chapter of the DCMR concerning a CFSA program or service for
   which a fair hearing is provided under ยง 5901.1(a) establishes a procedure or
   time frame different from that established by this Chapter 59, the
   requirements of the other chapter control.
