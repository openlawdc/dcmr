CONTINUATION OF SERVICE DURING FAIR HEARING PROCESS (ยง 29-5904)

5904.1. Except as provided in ยง 5904.2, CFSA, in its discretion, may continue
   or reinstate service during the fair hearing process. Service that is
   continued or reinstated shall be maintained until the fair hearing decision,
   and may not be modified, reduced, suspended, or terminated unless or until:
   
   
   
   (a)	The appellant is notified that CFSA has denied or dismissed the request
   for a fair hearing under ยง 5905;
   
   
   
   (b)	The Hearing Examiner decides at the fair hearing that the sole issue
   raised by appellant concerns the plain language of a District statute,
   regulation or policy;
   
   
   
   (c)	The Hearing Examiner upholds the action of CFSA;
   
   
   
   (d)	A change in appellant&#8217;s circumstances occurs while the appeal is
   pending and adversely affects the delivery of service.
   
   
   
   (e)	The appellant specifically requests that service not be continued or
   reinstated;
   
   
   
   (f)	The appellant and CFSA agree; or
   
   
   
   (g)	Continuing the service poses an immediate or serious and continuing danger
   to the health, safety or welfare of a child.

   5904.2. Service may not be reinstated or continued if reinstating or
   continuing the service poses an immediate or serious and continuing danger to
   the health, safety or welfare of a child.
