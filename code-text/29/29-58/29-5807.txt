                                  DC REGULATIONS

POST ELIGIBILITY ASSESSMENT AND INDIVIDUAL RESPONSIBILITY PLAN (ยง 29-5807)

5807.1. Unless otherwise exempt under subsection 5810.3, each TANF applicant
   or recipient shall be assessed in-depth as to their prior work experience,
   employability, barriers to employment and employment goals. Participants shall
   be advised of the location and time of the assessment.

   5807.2. Each TANF applicant or recipient may have a representative of his or
   her choosing present at the assessment and at all discussions related to the
   Individual Responsibility Plan.

   5807.3. Based on the preliminary assessment identified in this section, each
   TANF recipient shall develop an Individual Responsibility Plan
   (&#8220;IRP&#8221;) with the Director or his or her designee which:
   
   
   
   (a)	Sets forth the actions required of the recipient to achieve
   self-sufficiency;
   
   
   
   (b)	Identifies the resources, training, and other services that the Director
   or his or her designee may provide to the recipient to assist in attaining
   self-sufficiency;
   
   
   
   (c)	Recognizes individual preferences of the TANF applicant or recipient to
   the extent possible; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Ensures a TANF applicant&#8217;s or recipient&#8217;s safety, as required
   by Section 5811.

   5807.4. Neither an applicant or recipient shall be required to participate in
   activities that are not designed to help him or her prepare for, find, or
   retain employment.

   5807.5. The Director or his or her designee and the applicant or recipient
   shall discuss the terms of the IRP and each shall sign it as evidence of their
   agreement. Each recipient shall be given a copy of the IRP and shall have the
   opportunity to have it reviewed periodically, but at least once a year.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10251 (November 9, 2001).
