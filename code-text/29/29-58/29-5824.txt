                                  DC REGULATIONS

WORK PARTICIPATION REQUIREMENT AND STANDARDS FOR ACCEPTING CLAIMS OF DOMESTIC
VIOLENCE (ยง 29-5824)

5824.1. In determining whether domestic violence took place, the Director or
   his or her designee shall rely on the following evidence:
   
   
   
   (a)	Police, government agency, or court records; documentation from a shelter
   worker, legal, clerical, medical, or other professionals from whom the
   applicant or recipient has sought assistance; statements from other
   individuals with knowledge of the circumstances; physical evidence of domestic
   violence, or any other evidence supporting the allegations; and
   
   
   
   (b)	In the absence of evidence in paragraph 5824.1(a) of this subsection,
   allegations of the victim made under oath, including an applicant&#8217;s
   petition for a Civil Protection Order, shall be sufficient to substantiate a
   claim.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10251 (November 9, 2001).
