                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADVERSE ACTION OR DETERMINATION BY NON-GOVERNMENTAL ENTITIES (ยง 29-5805)

5805.1. An individual against whom any adverse finding or recommendation has
   been made or taken by a non-governmental entity shall have the right to have
   the adverse finding or recommendation reviewed pursuant to the District of
   Columbia Public Assistance Act of 1982, as amended.

   5805.2. When a non-governmental entity changes the work or training activity,
   or location where that work or training occurs, as long as the change comports
   with the plan of self-sufficiency as contained in the Individual
   Responsibility Plan, the change shall not be considered an adverse finding or
   recommendation against an individual under this section.

   5805.3. For purposes of this section, &#8220;adverse action&#8221; includes,
   but is not limited to, the recommendation for sanction for non-participation
   in a required activity.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10251 (November 9, 2001).
