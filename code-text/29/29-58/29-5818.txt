                                  DC REGULATIONS

LUMP SUM PAYMENT OR SETTLEMENT (ยง 29-5818)

5818.1. Under the District of Columbia Public Assistance Act of 1982, as
   amended, a lump sum payment or settlement shall not include a personal injury
   award, worker&#8217;s compensation payment or similar award if the payment or
   award is unavailable to the assistance unit either:
   
   
   
   (a)	Because it is earmarked and used for the purpose for which it is paid,
   such as medical expenses; or
   
   
   
   (b)	Because it is an award that is beyond the control of the family such as,
   but not limited to, irrevocable trust funds, which are provided as
   compensation for injury or medical disability.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10251 (November 9, 2001).
