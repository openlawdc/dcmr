                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICES REGARDING DOMESTIC VIOLENCE GOOD CAUSE WAIVERS (ยง 29-5819)

5819.1. The Director or his or her designee shall provide a notice to an
   applicant or recipient of his or her right to request a domestic violence good
   cause waiver from the work requirements and/or child support cooperation
   requirements. The notices shall be provided orally and in writing, at the time
   of application and before any sanction is imposed.

   5819.2. The notice shall inform each applicant or recipient that:
   
   
   
   (a)	He or she has a right to request a domestic violence good cause waiver of
   the work requirements and the child support cooperation requirements at any
   point in time;
   
   
   
   (b)	The Director shall refer victims of domestic violence to appropriate
   services;
   
   
   
   (c)	Disclosure of domestic violence is voluntary and will not adversely affect
   or delay the ability to receive aid; and
   
   
   
   (d)	Disclosed information will be confidential; however, disclosed information
   about child abuse, child neglect or elder abuse must be reported to Child
   Protective Services or Adult Protective Services.

   5819.3. For purposes of this chapter, the definition of domestic violence is
   the same as that of &#8220;battered or subject to extreme cruelty&#8221; as
   set forth in Section 511a (e) of the District of Columbia Public Assistance
   Act of 1982, as amended.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10251 (November 9, 2001).
