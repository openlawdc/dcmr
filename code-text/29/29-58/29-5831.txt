POWER &#8211; GOOD CAUSE (ยง 29-5831)

5831.1. Notice shall be given orally and in writing to each applicant or
   recipient of good cause for failure to comply with POWER program requirements.

   5831.2. For purposes of subsection 5831.1 of this section, &#8220;good
   cause&#8221; for failure to comply with POWER program requirements, in any
   form, as directed by the Director or his or her designee, shall include one
   (1) or more of the reasons listed in subsection 5810.3.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10251 (November 9, 2001).
