                                  DC REGULATIONS

DOCUMENTATION REQUIRED TO VERIFY THE COST OF A COVERED NEED (ยง 29-5834)

5834.1. In order for a Diversion Payment to be issued for a covered need, the
   applicant shall verify the cost of the covered need. The Department shall
   instruct the applicant of the documentation required to reasonably verify the
   cost of the covered need. This documentation shall include one, or more, of
   the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	A bill for the amount of motor vehicle insurance, rental arrearage,
   utility arrearage, professional licenses and fees, work clothes and work
   tools;
   
   
   
   (b)	Two (2) written estimates, where feasible, for motor vehicle repair, work
   clothes, work tools, etc. if the amount of the covered need is over three
   hundred dollars ($ 300.00); and
   
   
   
   (c)	One (1) written estimate for motor vehicle repair, work clothes, work
   tools, etc. if the amount of the covered need is under three hundred dollars
   ($ 300.00).

   5834.2. When a bill is submitted to document the cost of a covered need, the
   Department shall verify or estimate the amount of the bill.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10251 (November 9, 2001).
