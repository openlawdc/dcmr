                                  DC REGULATIONS

VOLUNTARY REQUEST FOR WAIVERS (ยง 29-5822)

5822.1. A decision not to seek services or waivers due to domestic violence
   shall not bar the applicant or recipient from seeking services or waivers at a
   later date, or from future referrals to services.

   5822.2. An applicant or recipient is free to decline an offer of a waiver or
   to voluntarily terminate an existing waiver at any time without penalty. A
   waiver shall never be implemented against the will of the applicant or
   recipient.

   5822.3. Granting of a waiver shall not restrict otherwise qualified applicants
   and recipients from voluntary participation in approved work, job training,
   educational job readiness, work placement assistance, or community service
   programs.

   5822.4. An applicant or recipient who is denied a domestic violence good cause
   waiver of the work requirements and/or child support cooperation has the
   option of withdrawing his or her application for benefits.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10251 (November 9, 2001).
