                                  DC REGULATIONS

SANCTIONS IN THE POWER PROGRAM (ยง 29-5830)

5830.1. Each POWER recipient who, without good cause, fails to assist in the
   development of his or her plan of self-sufficiency or to comply with the terms
   contained in that plan shall be subject to sanctions by DHS, as set forth in
   subsections 5812.2 and 5812.3.

   5830.2. For the purposes of section 575 of the District of Columbia Public
   Assistance Act of 1982, as amended, other factors to be considered when the
   head of the assistance unit&#8217;s incapacity no longer substantially
   precludes the head of assistance unit&#8217;s ability to work or to
   participate in job search or job readiness activities shall include, but not
   be limited, to:
   
   
   
   (a)	Education;
   
   
   
   (b)	Work Experience; and
   
   
   
   (c)	Age.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10251 (November 9, 2001).
