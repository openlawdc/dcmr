                                  DC REGULATIONS

REASSESSMENT OF WAIVERS (ยง 29-5826)

5826.1. The Director or his or her designee shall request that the applicant
   or recipient attend a reassessment interview at least once every six (6)
   months.

   5826.2. The Director or his or her designee shall notify the applicant or
   recipient in writing of the reassessment interview at least thirty (30) days
   prior to the expiration of the current waiver.

   5826.3. The waiver shall continue as long as the program requirements would
   continue to make it more difficult for the applicant or recipient to escape
   family violence, put the applicant or recipient at further risk of violence,
   or where failure to comply with work requirements is a result of family
   violence.

   5826.4. A decision by the Director or his or her designee not to continue the
   domestic violence good cause waiver may be appealed pursuant to Section 5827.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 10251 (November 9, 2001).
