                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECRUITMENT AND PLACEMENT (ยง 29-3105)

3105.1. Participating agencies may employ Corps trainees pursuant to an
   agreement signed by the agency and the Director.

   3105.2. The Director shall be responsible for referring all trainees to
   participating District Government agencies, and for assuring that these
   persons meet all eligibility requirements.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 8290, 8294 (December 21, 1990).
