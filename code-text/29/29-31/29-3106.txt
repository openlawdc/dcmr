                                  DC REGULATIONS

USE OF FUNDS (ยง 29-3106)

3106.1. The financial assistance provided shall include tuition and may
   depending on a needs analysis include the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Reasonable cost of textbooks and supplies;
   
   
   
   (b)	Reasonable transportation costs to and from classes;
   
   
   
   (c)	Reasonable costs of child care while attending classes; and
   
   
   
   (d)	A stipend to defray the reasonable cost of living expenses for full-time
   independent students.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 8290, 8294 (December 21, 1990).
