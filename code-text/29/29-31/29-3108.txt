                                  DC REGULATIONS

REPORTING (ยง 29-3108)

3108.1. An educational institution receiving money under this program shall
   agree to inform the Director whenever a significant change in trainee status
   occurs.

   3108.2. The participating agencies shall provide the following reports to the
   Director:
   
   
   
   (a)	Notice of failure to obtain state license within the designated period of
   time; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Notice of termination of employment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 8290, 8295 (December 21, 1990).
