                                  DC REGULATIONS

ADMINISTRATION (ยง 29-3101)

3101.1. The Corps shall be administered by the Director of the Department of
   Human Services.

   3101.2. The Director shall confirm the status of trainees in the Corps Program
   at least annually, or more frequently if required.

   3101.3. Recipients of aid, shall cooperate in providing the information
   necessary for the Director to perform administrative functions under this
   Chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 8290 (December 21, 1990).
