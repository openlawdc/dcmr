TERMINATION OF FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE (ยง 29-3107)

3107.1. The Director may terminate financial assistance when the Director
   determines that the following occurs:
   
   
   
   (a)	The trainee has substantially failed to comply with these rules;
   
   
   
   (b)	The trainee fails to meet the conditions set forth in the District of
   Columbia Nurses Training Corps Program Agreement; or
   
   
   
   (c)	The Director determines that the trainee is no longer pursuing a course of
   study leading to a certificate or degree in practical or professional nursing.

   3107.2. When the Director has reason to believe that financial assistance
   should be terminated, the Director shall:
   
   
   
   (a)	Notify the trainee in writing of non-compliance with program requirements,
   stating the facts or documents relied upon for the determination and proposed
   action; and
   
   
   
   (b)	Advise the participant that he or she may appeal (in writing), any
   determination made by the Director, within thirty (30) days of the date that
   the Director issued the notification, as follows:
   
   
   
   (1)	Submit the appeal to the Office of Fair Hearings; and
   
   
   
   (2)	Include any documentation considered necessary for certifying that he or
   she is in compliance with program requirements.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 8290, 8295 (December 21, 1990).
