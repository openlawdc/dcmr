                                  DC REGULATIONS

DAILY LIVING EXPERIENCES (ยง 29-6269)

6269.1. The facility shall develop, conspicuously post in the facility, and
   follow a written plan of basic daily routines, tasks and work assignments for
   residents which are consistent with the resident&#8217;s age and level of
   development.

   6269.2. Whenever practicable, residents shall participate in planning daily
   routines, tasks and work assignments.

   6269.3. Daily routines, tasks and work assignments shall be consistent with
   each resident&#8217;s age and level of development.

   6269.4. Daily routines, tasks, and work assignments shall not conflict with a
   resident&#8217;s ISP or serve as a substitute for sufficient facility staff.

   6269.5. The facility shall make the plan of basic daily routines, tasks and
   work assignments for residents available to all residents and staff.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675 (September 21, 2001).
