                                  DC REGULATIONS

LAUNDRY FACILITIES (ยง 29-6246)

6246.1. The facility shall have operable laundry facilities, a contract with a
   vendor of such services, or access to off-site laundry facilities. The
   facility shall not charge residents for the use of laundry facilities or
   services.

   6246.2. All equipment located at the facility shall be in good repair and have
   protective safety devices and shall be located in an area which may be readily
   supervised by staff.

   6246.3. The facility shall provide necessary laundry supplies.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675, 8719 (September 21, 2001).
