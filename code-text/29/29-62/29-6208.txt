                                  DC REGULATIONS

FACILITIES IN OTHER JURISDICTIONS (ยง 29-6208)

6208.1. At least once (1) a year, the applicable licensing agencies shall
   conduct site visits of all facilities caring for District of Columbia children
   outside the District of Columbia to ensure that each of these facilities is in
   substantial compliance with these rules and all other applicable laws, as
   required by section 6 of the Act (D.C. Code ยง 3-805(a)(2)).

   6208.2. All facilities caring for District of Columbia children outside the
   District of Columbia shall provide the licensing agency with a current copy of
   a valid license from the jurisdiction in which the facility is operating.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675, 8687 (September 21, 2001).
