                                  DC REGULATIONS

REPAIRS AND MAINTENANCE (ยง 29-6235)

6235.1. The facility shall keep records of routine maintenance and cleaning in
   all areas.

   6235.2. The facility shall replace or repair broken, run-down or defective
   furnishings, carpeting, and equipment. Outside doors, windows and other
   features of the structure necessary for security shall be repaired within
   twenty-four (24) hours.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675, 8714 (September 21, 2001).
