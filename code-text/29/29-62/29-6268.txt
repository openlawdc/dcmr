                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECREATIONAL ACTIVITIES (ยง 29-6268)

6268.1. The facility shall develop and follow a written plan for providing
   recreational and leisure activities adequate to meet each resident&#8217;s
   needs, including:
   
   
   
   (a)	Designated staff for supervising the provision of recreational activities;
   
   
   
   (b)	Sufficient supervised, organized or structured individual and group
   recreational and leisure programs, both indoors and outdoors, appropriate to
   the resident&#8217;s age, interests, and needs, and consistent with the
   resident&#8217;s ISP;
   
   
   
   (c)	Free time for residents to pursue their individual interests, with
   protective supervision as required, unless otherwise provided in the
   resident&#8217;s ISP;
   
   
   
   (d)	The maximum use of available community recreational resources, where
   appropriate;
   
   
   
   (e)	Reasonable access to sufficient recreational supplies, equipment and
   activities appropriate to the resident&#8217;s age, interests, and needs; and
   
   
   
   (f)	Usable leisure-time material, including but not limited to books, games,
   and toys that are appropriate to the resident&#8217;s sex and stage of
   development, and transportation to local libraries.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675, 8732 (September 21, 2001).
