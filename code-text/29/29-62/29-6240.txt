                                  DC REGULATIONS

CLOTHING (ยง 29-6240)

6240.1. The facility shall coordinate with the contracting entity and, where
   applicable, the CSSD to ensure that each resident has his or her own adequate
   and appropriate seasonable clothing and footwear.

   6240.2. Where the facility provides residents a clothing allowance, the
   facility shall provide the resident a clothing allowance on a regular and
   timely basis and notify the resident when the clothing allowance will be
   provided.

   6240.3. The facility shall involve the resident in the selection of clothing,
   as appropriate to the resident&#8217;s age and level of development.

   6240.4. The facility shall coordinate with the contracting entity and, where
   applicable, the CSSD to permit each resident to take his or her clothing upon
   discharge from the facility.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675, 8717 (September 21, 2001).
