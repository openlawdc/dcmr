                                  DC REGULATIONS

RESIDENTS&#8217; COUNCILS (ยง 29-6271)

6271.1. Each facility shall establish and maintain a residents&#8217; council
   composed of residents.

   6271.2. The residents&#8217; council shall meet on a monthly basis.

   6271.3. Each facility shall provide the space for, and post notice of,
   residents&#8217; council meetings.

   6271.4. The residents&#8217; council may make recommendations to the facility
   to ensure and improve the quality of the facility and its staff and to ensure
   the protection of residents&#8217; rights.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675, 8733 (September 21, 2001).
