                                  DC REGULATIONS

LIVING ROOMS, ADMINISTRATIVE AND COUNSELING SPACE (ยง 29-6242)

6242.1. The facility shall provide a common living room or day area for the
   use of all residents, which is equipped with comfortable furnishings, for
   general relaxation and entertainment which is appropriate to the
   resident&#8217;s age and level of development.

   6242.2. The facility shall provide:
   
   
   
   (a)	Each resident a work area with appropriate lighting that is quiet and
   conducive to studying;
   
   
   
   (b)	Administrative office space separate from the residents&#8217; living
   areas, unless specifically waived by the licensing agency; and
   
   
   
   (c)	A designated space to allow private discussions and counseling sessions
   between individual residents and staff, or between individual residents and
   their parents, counselors, social workers, guardians ad litem or other
   advocates.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675, 8718 (September 21, 2001).
