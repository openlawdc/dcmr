                                  DC REGULATIONS

FIRST AID (ยง 29-6255)

6255.1. The facility shall maintain accessible first aid kits in the facility
   and in the vehicles used to transport residents.

   6255.2. The facility shall have accessible a first aid kit during all
   recreational activities.

   6255.3. The facility shall store all first aid supplies in closed containers
   that are accessible to staff at all times. These kits shall be checked and
   restocked regularly.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675, 8722 (September 21, 2001).
