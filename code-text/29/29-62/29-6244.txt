                                  DC REGULATIONS

WATER (ยง 29-6244)

6244.1. The facility shall provide a continuous supply of safe and potable
   water in all water outlets which is sufficient in quantity and pressure to
   meet the conditions of peak demand.

   6244.2. Hot and cold or tempered water shall be provided in each sink, bath
   and shower.

   6244.3. Hot water temperature shall not exceed one hundred twenty (120)
   degrees Fahrenheit at outlets accessible to residents.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675, 8718 (September 21, 2001).
