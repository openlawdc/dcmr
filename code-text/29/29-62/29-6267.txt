                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSPORTATION SERVICES (ยง 29-6267)

6267.1. The facility shall provide or arrange for transportation necessary to
   implement each resident&#8217;s ISP and health care plan, including but not
   limited to relative visitation, medical and counseling appointments, and
   school and extracurricular activities.

   6267.2. The facility shall provide vehicles for the transportation of
   residents and staff in their work with the residents. Such vehicles shall
   include all safety devices required by law.

   6267.3. All persons acting on behalf of the facility who drive vehicles used
   to transport residents shall be locally licensed to operate that class of
   vehicle.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675 (September 21, 2001).
