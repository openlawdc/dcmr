                                  DC REGULATIONS

INDEPENDENT LIVING SKILLS (ยง 29-6270)

6270.1. As early as practicable, but no later than a resident&#8217;s
   fourteenth (14th) birthday, the facility shall develop and follow a written
   plan to teach independent living skills that are individualized,
   age-appropriate, and in accordance with each resident&#8217;s ISP, including
   knowledge, practices and skills in the following areas:
   
   
   
   (a)	General health, general safety and fire safety;
   
   
   
   (b)	Reproductive health and contraception;
   
   
   
   (c)	Parenting skills, including child development, health, and safety;
   
   
   
   (d)	HIV and sexually transmitted disease;
   
   
   
   (e)	Problem solving and decision-making;
   
   
   
   (f)	Conflict resolution, negotiation, team building, peer support, and
   relationship;
   
   
   
   (g)	Work readiness and employment;
   
   
   
   (h)	Stress reduction;
   
   
   
   (i)	Money management;
   
   
   
   (j)	Meal purchase, preparation and storage;
   
   
   
   (k)	Nutrition;
   
   
   
   (l)	Shopping;
   
   
   
   (m)	Personal grooming and hygiene;
   
   
   
   (n)	Transportation; and
   
   
   
   (o)	Household maintenance.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 48 DCR 8675, 8732 (September 21, 2001).
