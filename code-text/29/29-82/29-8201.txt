SCOPE (ยง 29-8201)

8201.1. The purpose of this chapter is to establish uniform requirements for
   transferring money or other things of value to recipients outside the District
   government, where the Agency is authorized to make grants.

   8201.2. The Director of the Child and Family Services Agency, or his or her
   designee, is authorized to make a grant where:
   
   
   
   (a)	A statute authorizes the Agency to support or stimulate the activity of
   the recipient;
   
   
   
   (b)	The Agency will not be substantially involved in the activity so supported
   or stimulated; and
   
   
   
   (c)	The Agency is not acquiring any property or service of direct use to the
   District government.

   8201.3. The Agency is authorized to make grants pursuant to the D.C. Code
   ยง4-1303.3 the Child and Family Services Establishment Act.

   8201.4. Copies of this chapter shall be available from CFSA upon request.
