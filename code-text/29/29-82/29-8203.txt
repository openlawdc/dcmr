                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPLICATION REVIEW (ยง 29-8203)

8203.1. A review panel selected by the Agency shall review each application to
   determine if it meets the requirements that apply to the particular grant.

   8203.2. The review panel shall advise the Director of all those applications
   which satisfy the applicable grant requirements and shall recommend awards
   based on the review factors and available funding.
