                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORD RETENTION AND ACCESS (ยง 29-8213)

8213.1. The grantee shall retain all records for a period of at least three
   (3) years following final close-out of the grant, if no other period is
   specified in the grant agreement.

   8213.2. The grantee shall make all records available to the Director for audit
   by the Grant Officer, other District officials, and Federal officials as
   necessary.

   DCR 120 (January 5, 2007).
