                                  DC REGULATIONS

PROTECTION OF EMPLOYEE RIGHTS (ยง 7-2009)

2009.1. Employees have a right, to the maximum extent possible, to a safe and
   healthful working environment.

   2009.2. Any employee in exercising this right may, without fear of reprisal,
   report an unsafe or unhealthful working condition or practice which may lead
   to a subsequent investigation.
