                                  DC REGULATIONS

MOTOR VEHICLE SAFETY (ยง 7-2006)

2006.1. Each agency shall be responsible for the maintenance of a motor
   vehicle safety program which will include the following:
   
   
   
   (a)	Driver identification cards;
   
   
   
   (b)	Physical examinations;
   
   
   
   (c)	Driver training, including annual refresher and remedial courses; and
   
   
   
   (d)	A preventive maintenance program to ensure that vehicles are in safe
   condition.
