                                  DC REGULATIONS

GENERAL PROVISIONS (ยง 7-600)

600.1. The District of Columbia On-Job Training Program for Adults With
   Dependents is established by ยง2(a)(4) of the Youth Employment Act of 1979.

   600.2. The purpose of the program is to provide financial incentives to
   private and public employers who hire, train and retain as new employees
   persons who are unemployed adult residents of the District with dependents, so
   that these persons may acquire fully productive work and occupational skills
   to insure their economic self sufficiency through meaningful career and work
   opportunities.

   600.3. Special emphasis under the program is to be directed at encouraging the
   participation of employers who are minority business firms and non-profit
   organizations, so that these employers may benefit from the program by
   utilizing program financial incentives to train employees, and thus through
   improved employee productivity become more competitive in the market place.
