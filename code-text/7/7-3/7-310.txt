                                  DC REGULATIONS

JUDICIAL REVIEW OF FINAL DECISIONS (ยง 7-310)

310.1. After the final decision of the Director, an aggrieved party may
   petition the District of Columbia Court of Appeals for review of the final
   administrative action.

   310.2. A petition for review shall be filed within fifteen (15) days after the
   actual delivery of the decision or after the mailing of the decision to the
   appellant&#8217;s last known address.
