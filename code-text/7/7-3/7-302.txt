                                  DC REGULATIONS

OFFICE OF UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION (ยง 7-302)

302.1. In accordance with Reorganization Plan No. 1 of 1980, the Director
   administers the Act through the Office of Unemployment Compensation.

   302.2. Office hours shall be established by the Director and shall be posted
   and made known to all employers and employees subject to the provisions of the
   Act.

   302.3. The Director shall consider a document timely filed when the
   transmitting envelope bears a postmark of not later than midnight of the day
   specified for the filing.
