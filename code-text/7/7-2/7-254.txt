                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONFLICT OF INTEREST (ยง 7-254)

254.1. During any period in which the Chief Administrative Appeals Judge and
   other members serve on the Board, they shall be subject to conflict of
   interest and other appropriate ethical restraints.

   254.2. Notice of any objection which a party may have to any Board member
   participating in a proceeding governing the disposition of the party&#8217;s
   appeal shall be made by such party at the earliest opportunity but in no event
   later than the date that the case is assigned to a Review Panel under section
   262.1 of this Chapter.

   254.3. The Board member shall consider such objection and shall, in his or her
   discretion, either proceed with the case or withdraw.
