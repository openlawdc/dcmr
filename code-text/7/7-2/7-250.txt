250.1. Sections 250 to 260 describe the establishment and organizational
   structure of the Compensation Order Review Board (Board), section 261 sets
   forth general rules applicable to the operation of the Board, and, sections
   265 through 271, provide the Board&#8217;s rules of practice and procedure.

   250.2. These regulations supersede and replace 7 DCMR section 118, governing
   administrative appeals under the CMPA (Public Sector Act) and 7 DCMR section
   230 governing administrative appeals under the District of Columbia
   Workers&#8217; Compensation Act of 1979 (WCA), effective July 1, 1980, D.C.
   Law 3-77, D.C. Official Code ยง 32-1501 et seq. (2001) (Private Sector Act)
   (collectively, the Acts).

   250.3. Except as otherwise specified herein, the definitions set forth at 7
   DCMR section 199 shall apply to all Public Sector Act appeals, and the
   definitions set forth at 7 DCMR section 299 shall apply to all Private Sector
   Act appeals.
