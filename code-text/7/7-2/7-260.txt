                                  DC REGULATIONS

STAYS OF COMPENSATION (ยง 7-260)

260.1. The filing of an Application for Review shall not stay the
   effectiveness of a compensation order nor the payment of any amount ordered by
   it pending review by the Board.

   260.2. Upon application by the employer the Board may stay a compensation
   order.

   260.3. The Board shall only stay a compensation order on the grounds that the
   employer would suffer irreparable injury by complying with it.
