                                  DC REGULATIONS

OCCUPATIONAL DISEASE (§ 7-227)

227.1. In accordance with §11 of the Act [§36-310, D.C. Code, 1981 ed.] and
   §2(1) of the Act [§36-301(12), D.C. Code, 1981 ed.], the employer with the
   last known exposure shall be liable to pay compensation for occupational
   disease.

   227.2. The employer shall not be liable for any occupational disease which
   results from a hazard to which the worker has had greater exposure outside of
   the employment.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 3213 (June 3, 1994).
