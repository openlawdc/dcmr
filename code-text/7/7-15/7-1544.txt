GENERAL RULES OF CONSTRUCTION (ยง 7-1544)

1544.1. Words importing gender apply as appropriate to the other gender as
   well.

   1544.2. Any edited contracts, agreement, rules\ and regulations, and
   legislation reflecting changes made thereto, except that documents submitted
   to the Office of Documents shall be in the form required by that Office.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10811 (November 29,
   2002).
