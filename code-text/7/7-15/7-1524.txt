                                  DC REGULATIONS

PERSONAL PRIVILEGE (ยง 7-1524)

1524.1. Any Board member may, as a matter of personal privilege, speak for a
   period not longer than ten (10) minutes upon matters that may collectively
   affect the Board, its rights, its dignity, and the integrity of its
   proceedings or the rights, reputation, and conduct of its individual members,
   but only in connection with members&#8217; representative capacities.
