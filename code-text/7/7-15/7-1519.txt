                                  DC REGULATIONS

APPEALS (ยง 7-1519)

1519.1. An appeal may be taken from any decision of the Chairman or
   Parliamentarian. The Board member appealing shall state his or her reason for
   appeal. The Chairman or Parliamentarian may then respond. The appeal shall be
   voted upon immediately. An affirmative vote of a majority of the Board members
   present and voting shall be required to override the Chairman or
   Parliamentarian.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10804 (November 29,
   2002).
