                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORDS OF MEETINGS (ยง 7-1529)

1529.1. Copies of the written transcript or minutes of public meetings shall
   be made available upon request to the public at reasonable cost. A written
   transcript or minutes shall be kept for all meetings at which official action
   of any kind is taken, and shall be made available to the public during the
   normal business hours of the Board, except as provided in ยง1528.

   1529.2. All Board meetings, regular, additional, committee, or special
   meetings shall be recorded on tape.
