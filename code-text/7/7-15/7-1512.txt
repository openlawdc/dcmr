                                  DC REGULATIONS

TRANSACTION OF BUSINESS (ยง 7-1512)

The Board shall take official action or transact other business only at meetings
duly called under the provisions of this chapter, unless, at a prior meeting the
Board specifically authorized that action be taken in another manner or at
another time; or in the event at a subsequent meeting the Board determined that
an emergency situation warranted immediate action be taken in another manner and
ratified such action by a majority vote.



	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10803 (November 29,
2002).
