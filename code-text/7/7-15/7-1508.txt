                                  DC REGULATIONS

STAFF (§ 7-1508)

1508.1. Assignments to, removal from, and the remuneration of the professional
   and clerical staff of the Board shall be determined by the Executive Director,
   consistent with applicable provisions of the Reform Act and Comprehensive
   Merit Personnel Act.(D.C. Code §§1- 601.01 et seq. (2001)).

   Rulemaking published at49 DCR 10801 (November 29, 2002).
