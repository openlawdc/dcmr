                                  DC REGULATIONS

POINTS OF ORDER (ยง 7-1518)

1518.1. Points of order shall be debatable only at the discretion of the
   Chairman. If the Chairman permits debate, he or she shall have the authority
   to limit it.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 49 DCR 10804 (November 29,
   2002).
