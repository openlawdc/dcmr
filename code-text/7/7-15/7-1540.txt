                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORDS (ยง 7-1540)

1540.1. The Executive Director shall maintain accurate and up-to-date records
   of all official Board actions, including but not limited to proposed rules,
   regulations, and resolutions; amendments to rules, regulations, and
   resolutions; committee reports on rules, regulations, and resolutions, and
   amendments thereto; and rules, regulations, and resolutions adopted by the
   Board.
