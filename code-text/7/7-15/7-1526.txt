DECORUM OF MEMBERS OF THE PUBLIC (ยง 7-1526)

1526.1. The Chairman shall maintain order in the meeting room, and if in the
   Chairman&#8217;s opinion the removal of any member of the public is necessary
   for the maintenance of order, the Chairman may, after warning, order the
   Sergeant-At-Arms to remove any disorderly person.
