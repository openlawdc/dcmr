                                  DC REGULATIONS

REPORTING REQUIREMENTS (ยง 7-413)

413.1. The Department shall submit to the Mayor the following reports:
   
   
   
   (a)	The YES status summary including participant characteristics as of July
   30, and August 30, 1980 components (e.g., Vocational Exploration Program (VEP)
   shall be included in this report); and
   
   
   
   (b)	The YES Financial Status Report as of July and August 30,
   (Special/Component Financial Reports shall be included).

   413.2. Reports in this section shall be submitted to the Mayor no later than
   fifteen (15) days after the end of the report period.
