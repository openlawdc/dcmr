                                  DC REGULATIONS

LOBBYING ACTIVITIES (ยง 7-419)

419.1. No funds provided under the Act may be used to influence a Member of
   Congress, a State or local legislator or elected official to favor or oppose
   any legislation or appropriation of the legislators.

   419.2. Communications and consultation with State and local legislators or
   elected officials for purposes of providing information, such as matters
   necessary to provide compliance with the Act, shall not be considered
   lobbying.
