                                  DC REGULATIONS

POLITICAL PATRONAGE (ยง 7-416)

416.1. No recipient, contractor, program, component may select, reject or
   promote a participant based on that individual&#8217;s political affiliation
   or beliefs. The selection or advancement of employees as a reward for
   political services or as a form of political patronage is prohibited.

   416.2. There shall be no selection of participants, YES employees,
   contractors, vendors, sub-recipients based on political patronage or
   affiliation.
