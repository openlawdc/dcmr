                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTRACTING OFFICERS (ยง 7-1606)

1606.1. Contracting Officers have only such authority delegated to them
   pursuant to ยง 1605.2.

   1606.2. Contracting Officers have discretion to determine the method of
   procurement, project delivery and type of contract to use for each
   requirement, unless this function is excluded from the delegation of
   contracting authority.

   1606.3. Contracting Officers shall determine responsive bids and responsible
   offerors.  A responsive bid is a response to a solicitation which conforms in
   all material respects to the solicitation.

   1606.4. A responsible offeror has the capability in all respects to perform
   fully the contract requirements, and the integrity and reliability which will
   assure good faith performance.
