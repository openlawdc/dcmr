                                  DC REGULATIONS

CONTRACTING OFFICER&#8217;S TECHNICAL REPRESENTATIVE (§ 7-1607)

1607.1  	Contracting Officers may appoint, in writing, a Contracting
   Officer’s Technical Representative (“COTR”) to provide such management
   oversight and technical direction for a particular procurement or contract as
   the Contracting Officer shall determine is necessary or useful.

   1607.2. The COTR shall maintain an arm’s length relationship with the
   contractor. COTRs have no authority to modify or amend contracts.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   	Source: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 12594, 12598 (December
