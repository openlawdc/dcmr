EXCEPTIONS TO CALCULATIONS OR PROVISION OF PAID LEAVE (ยง 7-3202)

An employee who is exempt from overtime payment by reason of section 213(a)(1)
of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938, approved June 25, 1938 (52 Stat. 1060;
29 U.S.C. ยง 201 et seq.) shall not accrue leave pursuant to this chapter for
hours worked beyond a forty (40) hour work week.







An individual who works for an employer both as an employee and in a non-covered
employment position shall accrue paid leave for the hours worked as an employee.







If the employee does not suffer a loss of income when absent from work for the
number of days of paid leave provided in ยง 3201, the employer shall not be
required to provide paid leave to the employee as would have been otherwise
required by the Act.
