                                  DC REGULATIONS

RELEASE OF INFORMATION (§ 7-3209)

Nothing in the Act or this chapter shall require a health care professional to
disclose information in violation of section 1177 of the Social Security Act,
effective August 21, 1996 (110 Stat. 2029; 42 U.S.C. § 1320d-6) or the
regulations promulgated pursuant to section 264(c) of the Health Insurance
Portability and Accountability Act of 1996, effective August 21, 1996 (110 Stat.
2033; 42 U.S.C §1320d-2 note).







Information provided to an employer pursuant to §§ 3206, 3207, or 3208 shall
not be disclosed by the employer, except when such disclosure is:







Requested or consented to by the employee;







Ordered by a court or administrative agency; or







Otherwise required by federal or local law. 







SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 5231, 5236 (June 18, 2010).
