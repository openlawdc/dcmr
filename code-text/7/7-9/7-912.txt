                                  DC REGULATIONS

POSTINGS (ยง 7-912)

912.1. Every employer who is subject to any provision of this Chapter shall
   keep a copy or summary of this Chapter and any applicable postings or
   literature in a form prescribed or approved by the Department of Employment
   Services, posted in a conspicuous and accessible place in or about the
   premises at which any employee covered is employed.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 187 (January 14,
   1994).
