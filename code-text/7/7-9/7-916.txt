                                  DC REGULATIONS

PRESERVATION OF EXISTING RIGHTS AND LIABILITIES (ยง 7-916)

Rights accrued and liabilities incurred prior to October 1, 1993, under Wage
Orders issued by the Wage-Hour Board, shall be governed by provisions of the
respective Wage Order issued by the Wage-Hour Board.



	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 187 (January 14, 1994).
