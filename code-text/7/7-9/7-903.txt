                                  DC REGULATIONS

GRATUITIES (ยง 7-903)

903.1. The employer shall advise the employee of provisions as they relate to
   determining wages based on gratuities and an employer taking a gratuity
   allowance from the wage of an employee shall have the burden of proving the
   employee received gratuities at least as much as the gratuity allowance taken.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 187 (January 14,
   1994).
