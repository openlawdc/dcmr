                                  DC REGULATIONS

TOOLS (ยง 7-910)

910.1. In addition to the wages required by this Chapter, the employer shall
   pay the cost of purchasing and maintaining any tools required of the employee
   in the performance of the business of the employer.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 187 (January 14,
   1994).
