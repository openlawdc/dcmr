                                  DC REGULATIONS

SPLIT SHIFTS (ยง 7-906)

906.1. In addition to the wages required by this Chapter, the employer shall
   pay the employee for one additional hour at the minimum wage for each day
   during which the employee works a split shift. This provision is not
   applicable to an employee who lives on the premises of the employer.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 41 DCR 187 (January 14,
   1994).
