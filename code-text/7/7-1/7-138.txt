                                  DC REGULATIONS

NOTICE OF RETURN TO WORK (ยง 7-138)

138.1 	In all cases reported to the Program, the official superior shall be
required to notify the Program immediately when the claimant returns to work or
when the injury ceases.

138.2 	The official superior shall notify the Program if, after the claimant
returns to work, the same injury causes the claimant to stop work again.





SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 59 DCR 8766, 8808 (July 27, 2012).
