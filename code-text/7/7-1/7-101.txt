                                  DC REGULATIONS

FORMS (§ 7-101)

101.1 	Any notices, claims, requests, applications, or certificates that the Act
or this chapter requires to be made shall be on approved forms.



101.2 	All approved forms shall be obtained from the Program, including forms
for requesting a hearing to appeal a decision of the Program regarding a claim
for compensation.  If a form pertains to requests for hearings or attorney
representation pursuant to §§ 129-132 of this chapter, forms shall be obtained
from the Office of Hearings and Adjudication (OHA) of the Department of
Employment Services.
