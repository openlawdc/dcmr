                                  DC REGULATIONS

INVESTIGATIONS (§ 7-102)

102.1 	The Program shall conduct any investigation that is necessary to make
   an ID of eligibility for benefits under this chapter.

   102.2. The Program shall investigate the claim throughout the life of the
   claim to confirm that a claimant or employee is still entitled to benefits
   under the Act, including, when necessary, to make an eligibility determination
   (ED).

   102.3. Claimants and employees are required to cooperate with all aspects of
   the Program’s investigation, including participating in “alive and well
   checks,” attending physical examinations, and providing documentation of all
   medical services, earnings information, and dependent and marital status.
