                                  DC REGULATIONS

MONITORING (ยง 7-805)

805.1. The Director shall provide monitoring of and technical assistance to
   participating service deliverers utilizing departmental staff in carrying out
   the Department&#8217;s responsibilities under the Training and Retraining for
   Employment Program (TREP).

   805.2. Monitoring shall include the periodic visitation and review of service
   deliverers programs.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 6950 (November 2,
   1990).
