                                  DC REGULATIONS

PARTICIPANT RECRUITMENT, CERTIFICATION AND REFERRAL (ยง 7-803)

803.1. The Department or any participating service deliverer (at the
   discretion of the Department) shall recruit program participants.

   803.2. The Department shall be responsible for certifying the program
   eligibility of applicants and referring them to participating service
   deliverers.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 6949 (November 2,
   1990).
