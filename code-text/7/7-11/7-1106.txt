                                  DC REGULATIONS

REGISTRATION OF INDIVIDUAL APPRENTICESHIP AGREEMENTS REQUIRED (ยง 7-1106)

1106.1. No apprentice shall be employed under a registered apprenticeship
   program unless an individual apprenticeship agreement for that apprentice has
   been registered with the Director.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 6005(September 14,
   1990).
