                                  DC REGULATIONS

REINSTATEMENT OF APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAMS (ยง 7-1105)

1105.1. Each sponsor whose apprenticeship program has been deregistered
   pursuant to this Chapter may be reinstated by applying to the Apprenticeship
   Council and demonstrating that the apprenticeship program is operating in
   accordance with this Chapter.

   1105.2. Before denying an application for reinstatement, the Apprenticeship
   Council shall afford a sponsor the opportunity for a hearing in accordance
   with the provisions of section 1104 of this Chapter.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 37 DCR 6005(September 14,
   1990).
