                                  DC REGULATIONS

DCPS CONTESTED RESIDENCY CASES &#8211; FINAL ORDERS (ยง 1-2908)

2908.1. The presiding Administrative Law Judge shall issue a final order in
   all contested residency cases, which shall include the statement of appeal
   rights required by Subsection 2830.1.
