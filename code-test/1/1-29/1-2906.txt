DCPS CONTESTED RESIDENCY CASES &#8211; BEGINNING A CASE (ยง 1-2906)

2906.1. DCPS shall refer a contested residency case to OAH by filing a copy of
   the request for review that it received, along with a statement that DCPS
   requests OAH to hear and to decide the case.
