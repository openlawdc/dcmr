DCPS CASES &#8211; CONFIDENTIALITY OF THE RECORD (§ 1-2909)

2909.1. The OAH record in any case referred by DCPS is confidential.  Only the
   following persons may have access to that record:
   
   
   
   (a)	The adult student;
   
   
   
   (b)	The minor student’s parent, guardian, or representative;
   
   
   
   (c)	Any person who has the written consent of the adult student or the minor
   student’s parent or guardian; and
   
   
   
   (d)	School officials with a legitimate interest.
   
   
   
   SOURCE: Notice of Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 58 DCR 11122
   (December 23, 2011)[EXPIRED]; as amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking
   published at 59 DCR 3128, 3131 (April 20, 2012).
