                                  DC REGULATIONS

RENTAL HOUSING CASES &#8211; HEARINGS (ยง 1-2930)

2930.1 	A petition received by OAH will be treated as a request for a hearing. 
OAH will schedule a status conference, a hearing, or mediation.  OAH shall
notify the parties of the hearing date and of their right to obtain a lawyer at
least fifteen (15) calendar days before a hearing.



2930.2 	An Administrative Law Judge may dismiss any petition or any claim in a
petition without holding a hearing if the Rental Housing Act does not provide
relief for the claim(s).  The Administrative Law Judge shall first give the
parties notice and an opportunity to respond.



	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 53 DCR 5674, 5678 (July 14, 2006); as
amended by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCR 12541, 12579 (December
31, 2010).
