                                  DC REGULATIONS

SERVICE OBLIGATIONS (§ 1-2406)

2406.1. Each participant receiving an initial one (1) year loan under the
   Program shall, as a service obligation, complete one (1) year of eligible
   employment. Each subsequent one (1) year renewal of the loan shall require the
   completion of an additional one (1) year of eligible employment.

   2406.2. The initial loan and each renewal shall be forgiven upon the
   participant’s completion of the specific service obligation associated with
   the initial loan or the applicable renewal.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 1025 (February 2, 2007).
