                                  DC REGULATIONS

SANCTIONS (ยง 1-2826)

2826.1. Before issuing an order imposing any sanctions under the Act, the
   presiding Administrative Law Judge shall allow the party subject to the
   sanction an opportunity to be heard.  Any order imposing a sanction shall be
   in writing.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 6399 (June 25, 2004); as amended
   by Notice of Final Rulemaking published at 57 DCMR 12541, 12562 (December 31,
   2010).
