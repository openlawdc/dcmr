                                  DC REGULATIONS

AGENCY CASELOAD PROJECTIONS (§ 1-2839)

2839.1. To measure changes in an agency’s caseload as required by Section
   16(e) of the Act, the agency shall compare the number of cases reported in the
   Chief Administrative Law Judge’s annual summary to the number of cases it
   anticipates filing at OAH in the current or following fiscal year.
