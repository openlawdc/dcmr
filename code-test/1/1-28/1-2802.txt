                                  DC REGULATIONS

BEGINNING A CASE AT OAH (ยง 1-2802)

2802.1. The Government may begin a case at OAH by filing a Notice of
   Infraction or Notice of Violation as described in Section 2803.

   2802.2. Any party also may begin a case at OAH by filing a request for a
   hearing as described in Section 2808.

   2802.3. Rules for how to begin rental housing, public benefits and
   unemployment insurance cases are in Chapter 29.
