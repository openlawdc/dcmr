                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECORDS OR DONATED HISTORICAL MATERIALS IN THE ARCHIVES (ยง 1-1521)

1521.1. Upon accessioning into the Archives, jurisdiction over the records
   shall pass from the agency that created, maintained, or received the records,
   to the Archives.

   1521.2. The Archivist shall be responsible for the custody, use, withdrawal,
   and loan of records and donated historical materials transferred to the
   Archives.

   1521.3. The Archivist shall establish procedures for access to and use of
   records in the research room and elsewhere in the Archives.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 34 DCR 7001, 7018 (October 30, 1987).
