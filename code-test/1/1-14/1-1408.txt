                                  DC REGULATIONS

NON-AFFILIATION WITH THE GOVERNMENT (ยง 1-1408)

1408.1. The Office of Property Management reserves the right to advise the
   public, through signs or announcements, of the presence of any permittees and
   of their non-affiliation with the Government of the District of Columbia.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 36 DCR 1316, 1322 (February 17, 1989).
