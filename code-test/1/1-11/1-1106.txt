                                  DC REGULATIONS

COMPENSATION (ยง 1-1106)

Members of the Board shall serve without compensation (beyond their salaries as
employees of the District government), except that a Member may be reimbursed
for reasonable expenses incurred in the authorized execution of official Board
duties, if approved in advance by the CFO or his/her designee.



	SOURCE: Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 7968 (August 13,
2004) [EXPIRED]; as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 4402 (May
11, 2007).
