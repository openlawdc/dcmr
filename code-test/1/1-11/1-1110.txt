INVESTIGATIVE ASSISTANCE (ยง 1-1110)

The Board shall seek investigative assistance, as necessary, from the Office of
the Inspector General (OIG), the OCFO, the OAG, and other agencies of the
District government in the course of the Board&#8217;s review of a reported
violation.
