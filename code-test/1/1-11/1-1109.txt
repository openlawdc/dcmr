                                  DC REGULATIONS

LEGAL ADVICE (ยง 1-1109)

1109.1. The Board may consult with and seek advice from the Office of the
   Attorney General (&#8220;OAG&#8221;), in coordination with the Office of
   General Counsel for the Chief Financial Officer, in the course of the
   Board&#8217;s review of each reported violation.

   1109.2. In making recommendations for disciplinary action, the Board may
   consult with and seek advice from the District of Columbia Office of Personnel
   and/or the applicable personnel authority for the subject agency.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Emergency and Proposed Rulemaking published at 51 DCR 7968 (August
   13, 2004) [EXPIRED]; as amended by Final Rulemaking published at 54 DCR 4402
   (May 11, 2007).
