                                  DC REGULATIONS

ORGANIZATION (ยง 1-1107)

1107.1. The Board may establish committees as needed, including standing
   committees. The Board shall determine who shall be members of any such
   committees. Committees may include District government employees who are not
   Members, provided that each committee is chaired by a Member. Unless specified
   by the Board, the individual committees shall determine the dates, times, and
   locations of meetings and whether or not to keep records of committee
   meetings.

   1107.2. The Board may establish its own bylaws and rules of procedure.
