DISTRICT SUBROGATED TO CLAIMS BY POLICEMAN OR FIREMAN AGAINST THIRD PARTY (ยง
1-2004)

2004.1. As soon as the District begins providing care to a policeman or
   fireman, it shall become subrogated to any right or claim that the policeman
   or fireman has against any third party for the care it has undertaken to
   provide or pay for.

   2004.2. In addition to the legal subrogation effected under ยง 2004.1, the
   policeman or fireman shall execute a written assignment of that same right or
   claim, upon written request by the Corporation Counsel.
