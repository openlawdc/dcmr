                                  DC REGULATIONS

REQUIREMENTS FOR PARTICIPATION IN HEARING (ยง 1-2205)

2205.1. To participate in a proceeding before the Board, a person shall file
   with the D.C. Energy Office, at a time prior to the deadline set forth in the
   notice of public hearing, the following information:
   
   
   
   (a)	Name, address and telephone number;
   
   
   
   (b)	Whether he or she will appear as a proponent or opponent of the
   application; and
   
   
   
   (c)	A statement setting forth the grounds upon which he or she supports or
   opposes the application.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 35 DCR 5101, 5104 (July 1, 1988).
