                                  DC REGULATIONS

DISHONORED CHECKS (§ 1-809)

809.1. In accordance with D.C. Code § 1-357 (1999 Repl.), a charge of fifteen
   dollars ($15) shall be imposed for dishonored checks received in payment of an
   outstanding advance or any portion thereof.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 32 DCR 1337, 1343 (March 8, 1985).
   
   
   
   	EDITOR’S NOTE: Pursuant to the authority vested in the Mayor under D.C.
   Code §1-357(a), the fee for dishonored checks has been increased to fifty
   dollars ($50.00). See section 108 of this title.
