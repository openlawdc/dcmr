                                  DC REGULATIONS

RECEIPT OF ADVANCE (ยง 1-806)

806.1. If an advance payment for travel is not collected by the traveler at
   his or her official duty station, the payment shall be mailed to the address
   designated by the traveler on the payment voucher.

   806.2. The traveler shall ensure that the address indicated on the voucher is
   correct.

   806.3. In no case shall a request to use imprest fund monies for advances be
   honored.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 32 DCR 1337, 1342 (March 8, 1985), as
   amended by Final Rulemaking published at 33 DCR 7296, 7297 (November 21,
   1986).
