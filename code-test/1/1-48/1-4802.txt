                                  DC REGULATIONS

LIMITATION ON AMOUNT (ยง 1-4802)

4802.1. No financial institution shall at any time have investments of public
   funds in an amount exceeding twenty-five percent (25%) of the total assets,
   exclusive of public funds, of that financial institution.
   
   
   
   	SOURCE: Final Rulemaking published at 32 DCR 5098, 5100 (September 6, 1985).
